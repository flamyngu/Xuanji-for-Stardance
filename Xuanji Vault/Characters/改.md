## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集下〉【攴字部】頁468第11【唐韻】古亥切【集韻】【韻會】已亥切【正韻】居亥切，𠀤音輆。【說文】更也。【註】李陽冰曰：已有過，攴之卽𢻰。【五經文字】𢻰从戊己之已。【易·益卦】有過則改。又【井卦】改邑不改井。　又姓。【廣韻】秦有大夫攺產。　又【韻補】叶荀起切。【張衡·思𤣥賦】私湛憂而深懷兮，思繽紛而不理。願竭力以守誼兮，雖貧窮而不改。【說文】从攴作𢻰。攺、𢻰。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, lower; Rap radical 攴; p.468 #11)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 古亥. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 已亥. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 居亥 — sounding like 輆 (gǎi).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to change, alter (更)." Note: Li Yangbing says, "having a fault (已), one raps (攴) it — that is 𢻰 [改]." Wujing wenzi: "𢻰 follows the 已 of 戊己." Yijing, *Yi*: "having a fault, then change (改)." Jing: "one may change (改) the town but not change the well."
- *A surname.* *Guangyun*: "Qin had the grandee Gai Chan."

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 荀起.* Zhang Heng, *Sixuan fu*: "...I would spend my strength to keep the right, though poor and in want I would not change (改)."

**On the graph.** *Shuowen*: "it follows 攴, written 𢻰".
