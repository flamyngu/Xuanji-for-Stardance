## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1023第12    〔古文〕：𦱡、𦱶、𧁇【唐韻】而灼切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】日灼切，𠀤音弱。【說文】若，擇菜也。　又【玉篇】杜若，香草。【楚辭·九歌】采芳洲兮杜若。【夢溪筆談】杜若，卽今之高良薑。　又順也。【書·堯典】欽若昊天。【傳】敬順也。【詩·小雅】曾孫是若。　又汝也。【儀禮·士昏禮】勗帥以敬先妣之嗣，若則有常。【晉語】晉文公謂勃鞮曰：爾爲惠公從余于渭濵，命曰三日，若宿而至。　又如也。【書·盤庚】若網在綱。　又乃也。【周語】必有忍也，若能有濟也。　又語辭。【儀禮·士相見禮】君若降送之，則不敢顧。【疏】若者，不定之辭也。　又【前漢·武帝紀】民年九十以上，爲復子若孫。【註】若者，豫及之辭也。　又若若，垂貌。【前漢·石顯傳】印何纍纍綬若若耶。　又【莊子·秋水篇】向若而歎。【註】若，海神。　又歲名。【爾雅·釋天】太歲在丑曰赤奮若。　又若木。【淮南子·地形訓】若木在建木西。【楚辭·天問】若華何光。【註】若木何能有明赤之光華乎。　又水名。【水經注】若水東南流，鮮水注之。　又姓。【正字通】漢下邳相若章。　又【廣韻】人者切，音惹。乾草也。　又般若，梵語謂智慧也。【晉書·曇霍傳】霍持一錫杖，令人跪，曰：此是波若眼。　又【韻會】浮屠所居，西域謂之蘭若。【柳宗元文】蘭若眞公。【註】官賜額者爲寺，私造者爲招提、蘭若。　又人賒切，音婼。蜀地名。【前漢·地理志】若屬南郡，春秋傳作鄀。【唐韻古音】讀汝三略，尊𤰞相若，强弱相虜。古人讀若字爲汝，故傳記之文，多有以若爲汝者。《史記·項羽本紀》云：吾翁卽若翁，《漢書》云吾翁卽汝翁，此可據也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1023 #12)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𦱡, 𦱶, 𧁇.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 而灼. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 日灼 — sounding like 弱 (ruò).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "若 means to pick vegetables (擇菜)."

**Senses.**
- *Also* *Yupian*: "杜若 (duruo), a fragrant herb." Chuci, *Jiu ge*: "I pluck duruo (杜若) on the fragrant islet." *Mengxi bitan*: "duruo is today's galangal (高良薑)."
- *To comply, obey (順).* Shu, *Yao dian*: "reverently complying with (欽若) vast Heaven"; tradition: "respectfully obeying." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the descendant complies (是若)."
- *You, thou (汝).* Yili, *Shi hun li*: "exhort and lead her in reverence to carry on the late mother's place; let you (若) be constant." *Discourses of Jin*: "Duke Wen of Jin said to Bo Di: 'you served Duke Hui in following me to the Wei shore; the order was for three days, and you (若) came after a night.'"
- *Like, as (如).* Shu, *Pan geng*: "like (若) the net on its cord."
- *Then, and so (乃).* *Discourses of Zhou*: "there must be forbearance, and then (若) there can be success."
- *A particle.* Yili, *Shi xiang jian li*: "if (若) the lord comes down to see him off, he dares not look back"; subcommentary: "若 is a word of uncertainty."
- *Also* Former Han, *Annals of Emperor Wu*: "for the people ninety years and over, exempt their sons or (若) grandsons"; note: "若 is a word reaching forward to include."
- *若若, a dangling look.* Former Han, *Biography of Shi Xian*: "what trailing seals, what dangling cords (綬若若)!"
- *Also* Zhuangzi, *Qiu shui*: "he sighed facing Ruo (若)"; note: "Ruo is the sea-god."
- *A year-name.* Erya, *Shi tian*: "the Grand Year in 丑 is called Chifenruo (赤奮若)."
- *若木 (the Ruo tree).* Huainanzi, *Di xing*: "the Ruo tree (若木) is west of the Jian tree." Chuci, *Tian wen*: "the Ruo's bloom (若華), what light?"; note: "how could the Ruo tree have bright red light and bloom?"
- *A water-name.* Shuijing zhu: "the Ruo water (若水) flows southeast, and the Xian water joins it."
- *Surname.* *Zhengzitong*: "Ruo Zhang, Chancellor of Xiapi under the Han."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: fanqie 人者, sounding like 惹 (rě). Dry grass (乾草).
- *般若 (prajñā), Sanskrit for wisdom (智慧).* Jinshu, *Biography of Tan Huo*: "Huo held a tin staff and bade a man kneel, saying: 'this is the eye of prajñā (波若).'"
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: "a Buddhist dwelling, in the Western Regions called a 蘭若 (āraṇya)." Liu Zongyuan's prose: "Lanruo Zhengong (蘭若眞公)"; note: "those granted a name-board by the state are temples (寺); those privately built are zhaoti or lanruo (蘭若)."
- *Also* fanqie 人賒, sounding like 婼 (ruò). A Shu place-name. Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "Ruo (若) belongs to Nan commandery"; the *Chunqiu zhuan* writes 鄀. *Tang yun guyin*: read 汝 (rǔ). The *San lüe*: "high and low ranking each other (相若), strong and weak enslaving each other"; the ancients read the graph 若 as 汝, hence in records 若 is often used for 汝. Shiji, *Annals of Xiang Yu*: "my father is your (若) father"; the Hanshu: "my father is your (汝) father" — this is the proof.
