## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集上〉【日字部】頁501第24    〔古文〕：𣊥【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤苦謗切，音壙。【說文】明也。【後漢·竇融傳】則曠若發曚。【註】曠，明也。　又【書·臯陶謨】無曠庶官。【傳】曠，空也。【前漢·賈山傳】曠日十年。【註】師古曰：曠，空也，廢也。【劉楨·贈五官中郞將詩】彌曠十餘旬。【註】《倉頡篇》曰：曠，疎曠也。　又【博雅】曠，遠也。曠曠，大也。【篇海】久也，豁也。【廣韻】又姓。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, upper; Sun radical 日; p.501 #24)*

**Ancient graph.** 𣊥.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 苦謗 — sounding like 壙 (kuàng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "bright (明)." Later Han, *Biography of Dou Rong*: "then it grew bright (曠) as the lifting of a fog"; note: "曠 means bright."
- *Empty, vacant (空).* Shu, *Gaoyao mo*: "leave not the many offices vacant (曠)"; tradition: "曠 means empty." Former Han, *Biography of Jia Shan*: "empty (曠) days for ten years"; note: Yan Shigu says, "曠 means empty, neglected." Liu Zhen, *Poem presented to the General of the Five Offices*: "the more void (曠), more than ten decades"; note: the Cangjie pian says, "曠 means open and wide."
- *Far; vast; long.* *Boya*: "曠 means far; 曠曠 means vast." *Pianhai*: "long-lasting; open." *Guangyun*: also a surname.
