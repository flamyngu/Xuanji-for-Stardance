**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Knife radical 刀; p.136 #04)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 府文. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 方文 — sounding like 餴 (fēn).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "To divide (別)." It follows 八 (eight) and 刀 (knife), the knife being what divides things. Yijing, *Xi ci*: "things by kinds divide (羣分)."

**Senses.**
- *Also* *Zengyun*: "to split (裂); to part (判)."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "to apportion (賦); to bestow (施)." *Zengyun*: "to give (與)."
- *Also* *Yupian*: "to separate (隔)."
- *Also* Former Han, *Treatise on the Pitch-pipes and Calendar*: "the breadth of one millet-grain is one 分; the 分 grows from three minutes (微) into something manifest, and can be distinguished."
- *Half (半).* Gongyang zhuan, Zhuang yr 2: "the army lost half (分) of it." Xunzi, *Zhongni*: "with the half-portion (分) of Qi, to offer it was yet not enough."
- *Throughout, all round (徧).* Zuozhuan, Ai yr 1: "those with cooked food shared it round (分), and only then dared eat."
- *Also* interchangeable with 紛. Xunzi, *Ru xiao*: "thronging (分分), it has its end and beginning." Huainanzi, *Miucheng xun*: "calamity in its rising comes thronging (分分)"; note: "like 紛紛."
- *Also* Zhouli, *Tian guan*: "to provide for the state's apportioned grants (匪頒)"; note: "匪 is read as 分."
- *Also* *Tang Yun*: fanqie 扶問. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 符問 — the departing tone of 汾 (fèn). A station, an allotted lot (名分). Liji, *Li yun*: "ritual being attained, the lots (分) are fixed."
- *Even, a measured dose (均, 分劑).* Liji, *Qu li*: "in sharing (分), do not seek the larger part."
- *One's station and duty (分位).* Zhuge Liang, *Memorial on Going to War* (Han): "this is how your servant would repay the late Emperor and do his duty (職分) to Your Majesty."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 方問 — the departing tone of 紛 (fèn). Even (均). Zuozhuan, Xi yr 1: "to relieve trouble and share (分) disaster."
- *Also* *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 府吻, sounding like 粉 (fěn). Erya, *Shi qi*: "the pitch-pipe is called the 分"; note: "分 is sounded 粉; the pitch-tube can apportion (分) the breaths."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 丰 (fēng), fanqie 膚容.* Cao Zhi, *Qi qi*: "at the Grand Ultimate's first, chaos yet undivided (未分); the myriad things in their tangle rose together with the Way."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 膚眠 — sounding near 徧.* Ban Gu, *Xi du fu*: "the nine markets open their grounds, the goods sorted, the rows divided (隊分); a man cannot turn his head, a cart cannot wheel about."

**Textual note.** The graph 災 (disaster) in the original block-print is formed with 𡿧.
