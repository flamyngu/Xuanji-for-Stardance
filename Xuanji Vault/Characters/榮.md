> 《康熙字典》〈辰集中〉【木字部】頁544第17【唐韻】永兵切【集韻】【正韻】于平切【韻會】於營切，𠀤音營。【說文】桐木也。見桐字註。　又屋梠之兩頭起者爲榮。【禮·喪大記】升自東榮，降自西北榮。【註】榮屋翼也。　又榮華。【爾雅·釋草】木謂之華，草謂之榮。不榮而實者謂之秀，榮而不實者謂之英。　又【淮南子·時則訓】秋行夏令爲華，行春令爲榮。　又榮者，辱之反。【老子·道德經】知其榮，守其辱。　又人以血爲榮，以氣爲衞。【內經】榮衞不行，五臟不通。　又【諡法】寵祿光大曰榮。　又州名。【前漢·地理志】屬犍爲郡，唐置榮州。　又杜榮，草也。《爾雅》莣，杜榮。註似茅，皮可以爲繩索履屩。　又姓。【史記·聖門弟子傳】榮旂。【莊子】榮啓期。　又【集韻】維傾切，音營。義同。　又叶以中切，音融。【越絕書】種留封侯，不知令終。二賢比德，種獨不榮。【楊愼云】東韻宜收榮字。　又叶爲命切，音詠。【揚雄·太𤣥經】宗其高年，鬼待敬也。牽羊于叢，不足榮也。鼎血之蕕，信王命也。    考證：〔又杜榮，忘憂艸也。【郭璞·爾雅註】一名莣，今芒草，可爲索。〕　謹按杜榮非忘憂草，今改又杜榮，草也。爾雅莣，杜榮。註似茅，皮可以爲繩索履屩。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, middle; Wood radical 木; p.544 #17)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 永兵. *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 于平. *Yun Hui*: fanqie 於營 — sounding like 營 (yíng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the paulownia tree (桐木)" (see the 桐 entry).
- *The eaves' upturned ends.* The two raised ends of the eaves-board are 榮. Liji, *Sang da ji*: "ascend by the east 榮, descend by the northwest 榮"; note: "榮, the wings of the roof."
- *Blossoming (榮華).* Erya, *Shi cao*: "of trees it is called 華 (flower), of plants 榮 (bloom); what fruits without blooming is 秀, what blooms without fruiting is 英."
- Huainanzi, *Shize xun*: "in autumn to follow summer's ordinances brings 華; to follow spring's brings 榮."
- *Glory, opposite of disgrace (辱).* Laozi: "know its glory (榮), keep to its disgrace (辱)."
- *In the body: the nutritive principle (榮), opposed to the defensive (衞).* Neijing: "when 榮 and 衞 do not move, the five viscera do not communicate."
- *Posthumous title.* "Favour and emolument, bright and great, is 榮."
- *A prefecture.* Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: belonging to Qianwei commandery; the Tang set up Rongzhou.
- *杜榮, a plant.* Erya: "莣 is 杜榮"; note: "like cogon-grass; its bark can make ropes and sandals."
- *Surname.* Shiji, *Disciples*: Rong Qi. Zhuangzi: Rong Qiqi.
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 維傾, sounding like 營 (yíng); same sense.

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 融 (róng), fanqie 以中.* *Yuejue shu*: "…[Wen] Zhong alone did not flourish (榮)"; Yang Shen: "in the 東 rhyme one should include 榮."
- *Rhymes as 詠 (yǒng), fanqie 爲命.* Yang Xiong, *Taixuanjing*: "…not enough to glory in (榮)… [it] trusts the king's command."

**Textual note.** The 杜榮 gloss is corrected: 杜榮 is not the "forget-care" herb; read "又杜榮，草也" with the Erya gloss as given.
