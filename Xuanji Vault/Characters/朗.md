**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls иҫ°, upper; Moon radical жңҲ; p.505 #23)*

**Ancient graphs.** рҘҮ‘, и„ј.

**Pronunciation.** *Ji Yun*: "the same as жң–."

**Etymology and senses.** Shijing, *Daya*: "lofty and bright (жң—), with a fair end"; tradition: "жң— means bright."
- *A surname.* *Guangyun*: "it appears in the Xingyuan."
- *Yunbu*: rhymes, fanqie зӣ§з•¶. Wang Yi, *Jiu si*: "the vast heaven, clear and cool; the dark vapour, lofty and bright (жң—); the north wind keen and chill; grass and trees sallow and yellow."
