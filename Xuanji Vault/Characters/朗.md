## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集上〉【月字部】頁505第23    〔古文〕：𥇑、脼【集韻】同朖。【詩·大雅】高朗令終。【傳】朗，明也。　又姓。【廣韻】出姓苑。　又【韻補】叶盧當切。【王逸·九思】昊天兮淸涼，𤣥氣兮高朗。北風兮潦冽，草木兮蒼黃。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, upper; Moon radical 月; p.505 #23)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𥇑, 脼.

**Pronunciation.** *Ji Yun*: "the same as 朖."

**Etymology and senses.** Shijing, *Daya*: "lofty and bright (朗), with a fair end"; tradition: "朗 means bright."
- *A surname.* *Guangyun*: "it appears in the Xingyuan."
- *Yunbu*: rhymes, fanqie 盧當. Wang Yi, *Jiu si*: "the vast heaven, clear and cool; the dark vapour, lofty and bright (朗); the north wind keen and chill; grass and trees sallow and yellow."
