**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, lower; Death/Bare-bone radical 歹; p.580 #25)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 市朱. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 慵朱. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 尚朱 — sounding like 殳 (shū).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "To die (死)." A Han statute: "When a Man-Yi chief is guilty of a crime, he is to be 殊'd (cut down / executed)." Zhuangzi, *Zai you*: "those put to death (殊死) were within sight of one another"; note: *Guangya*: "殊, to sever (斷)"; Sima [Biao]: "to determine"; one sense, "to execute (誅)"; *Zilin*: "to die."
- *Capital execution.* Former Han, edict of Emperor Xuan: "pardon those [condemned to] 殊死 (death) and below."

**Senses.**
- *To cut off, sever utterly (絕).* Former Han, edict of Xuan: "kin of flesh and bone, though parted, are not severed (殊)"; Yan Shigu: "粲, bright; 殊, severed — clear in benevolence, not parting or cutting off." *Biography of Han Xin*: "the whole army fought 殊死 (to the death)"; Shigu: "殊, to sever — meaning, resolved to die."
- *To cut asunder (斷絕).* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 23: "they cut the trees behind them but did not sever (殊) them."
- *Wounded but not yet dead.* Shiji, *Biography of Su Qin*: a Qi grandee, vying for favour, sent a man to stab Su Qin; not killed, "殊而走" — badly wounded, he fled.
- *Different, distinct (別, 異).* Yijing, *Xici*: "the world comes to one end by different (殊) roads." Liji, *Da chuan*: "differ in titles, differ in implements."
- *To exceed, be past (過).* Hou Han, *Biography of Liang Song*: "his mother was 殊 (past) seventy"; note: "殊 is like 過."
- *An emphatic particle.* Shijing, *Wei feng*: "how unlike (殊異) the lord's chariot!"
- *殊庭, the court of the immortals of Penglai.* Former Han, *Treatise on the Feng and Shan*: "to perform the distant sacrifice to such as Penglai, nearly reaching the immortal court (殊庭)."
- *Rhymes as 酬 (chóu), fanqie 時流.* Chen Lin's poem: "sunk among the common throng, with the age I have nothing strange (殊); knotted and grieving, bound in sorrow — by what way shall I unfold?"
