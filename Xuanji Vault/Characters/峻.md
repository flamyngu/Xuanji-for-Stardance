## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈寅集中〉【山字部】頁312第12【廣韻】私閏切【集韻】【正韻】祖峻切【韻會】須閏切，𠀤音浚。【說文】高也。本作𡺲。或省作峻。　又【廣韻】險也，長也。【集韻】大也。【大學】克明峻德。　又峭也，速也，嚴急也。【史記·酷吏傳】吏務爲嚴峻。　又【韻會】通作駿。【詩·大雅】駿極于天。【傳】駿，大也。【正字通】集韻或作𡍵𡼕，非。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 寅, middle; Mountain radical 山; p.312 #12)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 私閏. *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 祖峻. *Yun Hui*: fanqie 須閏 — sounding like 浚 (jùn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "high." Originally written 𡺲; sometimes abbreviated to 峻.
- *Steep; tall (險, 長).* *Guangyun.* *Ji Yun*: "great (大)." Daxue: "able to make bright his lofty (峻) virtue."
- *Sheer; swift; strict and severe (峭, 速, 嚴急).* Shiji, *Biographies of the Harsh Officials*: "the officers strove to be strict and severe (嚴峻)."
- *Interchangeable with 駿.* *Yun Hui.* Shijing, *Daya*: "lofty (駿), reaching to heaven"; tradition: "駿 means great."

**On the graph.** *Zhengzitong*: "Ji Yun's forms 𡍵 and 𡼕 are wrong."
