## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁399第34【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤良據切，音鑢。【說文】謀思也。从思，虍聲。思有所圖曰慮，慮，猶縷也。【增韻】憂也，疑也。【書·太甲】弗慮胡獲。【註】欲其謹思之也。【大學】安而后能慮。【朱註】謂處事精詳。　又度也。【揚子·太𤣥經】立督慮也。【註】督，正也。慮，度也。運以正度也。　又總計曰亡慮。【前漢·書註】舉凡之辭，言不待計慮而知其大凡也。　又軍前所持幡曰慮無。【左傳·宣十二年】前茅慮無。【註】慮有無也。【正義】明爲思慮其所無之事，使知而爲之備也。　又【釋名】慮，旅也，旅，衆也。【易·繫辭】一致而百慮。【註】慮及衆物，以一定之也。　又姓。【左傳】南蒯臣慮癸。　又【廣韻】力居切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】凌如切，𠀤音閭。【正韻】思慮也。【淮南子·原道訓】恬然無思，澹然無慮。以天爲蓋，以地爲輿。　又木名。【爾雅·釋木】諸慮，山櫐也。　又地名。隆慮，在河內。無慮，在遼東。取慮，在臨淮。且慮，在遼西。昌慮，在海東。隆音林，取音趣，且音苴。　又【古今字考】兩舉切，音呂。【箕山歌】日月運照，靡不記睹。游放其閒，何所却慮。　又【正字通】盧谷切，音錄。【唐書·百官志】大理寺掌折獄詳𠛬，凡繫囚，五日一慮。【前漢·雋不疑傳】每行縣，錄囚徒還。【師古註】錄囚，今云慮囚，本錄聲之去者耳。近俗不曉其意，訛爲思慮之慮，失其源矣。◎按師古此言近於識字，而實未通韻。惟未通韻，亦未爲識字之源也。蓋每字原具四聲，如慮字從平聲起韻，閭呂慮錄，則閭字爲慮字之平，呂字爲慮字之上，錄字爲慮字之入也。慮本訓謀思，然兼有詳審之義，故漢書錄囚亦卽慮囚也。慮字原具入聲，有錄音，豈必專屬去聲，爲得字之源乎。　又叶郞古切，音魯。【楚辭·九章】惟佳人之獨懷兮，折芳椒以自處。曾歔欷之嗟嗟兮，獨隱伏而思慮。〇按本从思，《說文》玉篇都入思部。字彙倂入心部，取其便考。    考證：〔【大學】安而后能慮。【朱註】處事精詳也。〕　謹照原文改爲謂處事精詳。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.399 #34)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 良據, sounding like 鑢 (lǜ).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "To plan and think (謀思)." It follows 思 (to think) with 虍 as phonetic; when thought has something it schemes at, it is called 慮 — 慮 is like 縷 (a thread, to run through). *Zengyun*: "to worry (憂); to doubt (疑)." Shu, *Tai jia*: "if you do not ponder (慮), how will you grasp it?"; note: "wishing him to think on it carefully." *Daxue*: "at rest, one can then deliberate (慮)"; Zhu Xi's note: "it means to handle affairs with fine thoroughness."

**Senses.**
- *To measure, gauge (度).* Yang Xiong, *Taixuan jing*: "to set up oversight and measure (慮)"; note: "督 means to set right; 慮 means to measure; to operate by correct measure."
- *亡慮, the sum total, "in all."* Former Han, note: "a term of summing-up — meaning that without waiting to reckon (計慮) one knows the gross of it."
- *A pennant carried before the army is called 慮無.* Zuozhuan, Xuan yr 12: "the van bore the reed-pennant and the 慮無"; note: "慮無 means to provide against what is lacking (慮有無)"; *Correct Meaning*: "clearly, to think over (思慮) the matters one lacks, so as to know them and make ready against them."
- *Also* *Shiming*: "慮 means 旅, and 旅 means a multitude (衆)." Yijing, *Xi ci*: "one goal and a hundred thoughts (百慮)"; note: "thought (慮) reaching to the host of things, fixing them by one."
- *Surname.* Zuozhuan: Nan Kuai's retainer Lü Gui (慮癸).

**Further readings (lǘ).**
- *Guangyun*: fanqie 力居. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 凌如 — sounding like 閭 (lǘ). *Zheng Yun*: "to think, to ponder (思慮)." Huainanzi, *Yuan dao*: "calm, without thought; placid, without care (慮); taking Heaven for canopy and Earth for chariot."
- *A tree-name.* Erya, *Shi mu*: "諸慮 is the mountain-lei vine (山櫐)."
- *Place-names.* Longlü (隆慮) in Henei; Wulü (無慮) in Liaodong; Qulü (取慮) at Linhuai; Julü (且慮) in Liaoxi; Changlü (昌慮) east of the sea. (Here 隆 is sounded 林 lín, 取 is sounded 趣 qù, and 且 is sounded 苴 jū.)
- *Gujin zikao*: fanqie 兩舉, sounding like 呂 (lǚ). *Song of Mount Ji*: "sun and moon move and shine, and nothing goes unrecorded; roaming free in their midst, what care (慮) need one put away?"
- *Zhengzitong*: fanqie 盧谷, sounding like 錄 (lù). Tang shu, *Treatise on Officials*: "the Court of Judicial Review managed the deciding of cases and the reviewing of punishments; for all detained prisoners there was a review (慮) every five days." Former Han, *Biography of Jun Buyi*: "on each circuit of the counties he reviewed (錄) the prisoners and returned"; Yan Shigu's note: "錄囚 (reviewing prisoners) is what is now called 慮囚 — originally just the departing-tone reading of 錄. Recent custom, not grasping its sense, has corrupted it into the 慮 of 思慮 (to ponder), losing its origin."

**Editorial note.** A note: Yan Shigu's remark here comes near to knowing the graph, yet in truth he had not mastered the rhymes. Each graph originally possesses all four tones; thus 慮, beginning its rhyme-series from the level tone — 閭, 呂, 慮, 錄 — then 閭 is the level of 慮, 呂 is the rising of 慮, and 錄 is the entering of 慮. 慮 properly trains as "to plan and think," yet it also carries the sense of careful examination, so the *reviewing of prisoners* in the Hanshu is indeed the *reviewing (慮) of prisoners*. The graph 慮 originally has an entering tone, with the reading 錄 — why must it belong solely to the departing tone to be the origin of the graph?

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 魯 (lǔ), fanqie 郞古.* Chuci, *Jiu zhang*: "the fair one alone cherishing her longing, plucks the fragrant pepper to dwell apart; sighing and sobbing again and again, alone she hides and broods in thought (思慮)."

**On the graph.** A note: it properly follows 思, and the *Shuowen* and *Yupian* both place it under the Think radical (思部); the *Zihui* merged it into the Heart radical (心部), for convenience of lookup.

**Textual note.** *Daxue*, "at rest one can then deliberate," Zhu Xi's note given as "處事精詳也" — checked against the original, it should read "謂處事精詳" ("it means to handle affairs with fine thoroughness").
