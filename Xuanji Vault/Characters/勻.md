## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集下〉【勹字部】頁150第20【唐韻】羊倫切【集韻】【韻會】兪倫切，𠀤音云。【說文】少也。从勹从二。指事也。一曰均也。【玉篇】齊也。【廣韻】徧也。【杜甫·麗人行】肌理細膩骨肉勻。又【集韻】規倫切，音君。與均同。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Wrap radical 勹; p.150 #20)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 羊倫. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 兪倫 — sounding like 云 (yún).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "few (少)." It follows 勹 (wrap) and 二 — a self-indicating graph. One says: even (均). *Yupian*: "even (齊)." *Guangyun*: "all over (徧)." Du Fu, *Song of the Fair Ladies*: "the flesh fine and sleek, bone and flesh evenly formed (勻)."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 規倫, sounding like 君 (jūn). Same as 均.
