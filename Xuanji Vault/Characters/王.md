## Shuowen Jiezi (說文解字) — original
> 《王部》王：天下所歸往也。董仲舒曰：「古之造文者，三畫而連其中謂之王。三者，天、地、人也，而參通之者王也。」孔子曰：「一貫三為王。」凡王之屬皆从王。

## Guangyun (廣韻) — original
> 《廣韻·下平聲·陽·王》王：大也，君也，字林云三者天地人一貫三爲王天下所法又姓出太原琅邪周靈王太子晉之後北海陳留齊王田和之後東海出自姫姓髙平京兆魏信陵君之後天水東平新蔡新野山陽中山章武東萊河東者殷王子比千爲紂所害子孫以王者之後号曰王氏金城廣漢長沙堂邑河南共二十一望又漢複姓五氏左傳晉有樂王鮒小王桃甲賈執英賢傳云東莞有五王氏史記云出齊威王至建王五王之後風俗通云漢有中郎威王弼出自楚威王後漢有新豐令王史音。雨方切，又雨誑切，四。

## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈午集上〉【玉字部】頁727第02【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤雨方切，音徨。【廣韻】大也，君也，天下所法。【正韻】主也，天下歸往謂之王。【易·坤卦】或從王事。又【隨卦】王用享于西山。【書·洪範】無偏無黨，王道蕩蕩。【詩·小雅】宜君宜王。【註】君，諸侯也。王，天子也。◎按秦漢以下，凡諸侯皆稱王，天子伯叔兄弟分封于外者亦曰王。　又諸侯世見曰王。【詩·商頌】莫敢不來王。【箋】世見曰王。　又凡尊稱亦曰王。【爾雅·釋親】父之考爲王父，父之妣爲王母。　又法王，象王，皆佛號。【華嚴偈】象王行處落花紅。【岑參詩】况値廬山遠，抽簪禮法王。【註】法王，佛尊號也。　又姓。　又【諡法】仁義所往曰王。　又王屋，山名。【書·禹貢】至于王屋。【疏】正義曰：王屋在河東垣縣東北。　又弓名。【周禮·冬官考工記】弓人，往體寡，來體多，謂之王弓之屬。　又王連，遠志也。見【博雅】夫王，芏草也。見【爾雅·釋草疏】。　又王鴡，鳥名。【爾雅·釋鳥】鴡鳩，王鴡。【註】鵰類，今江東呼之爲鶚。　又王鮪，魚名。【周禮·天官·人】春獻王鮪。【註】王鮪，鮪之大者。　又蛇名。【爾雅·釋魚】蟒，王蛇。【註】蟒，蛇最大者，故曰王蛇。　又蟲名。【爾雅·釋蟲】王蛈蜴。註：卽螲蟷，似䵹鼄，在穴中，有蓋。今河北人呼蛈蜴。《博雅》虎，王蝟也。　又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤于放切，音旺霸王也。【正韻】凡有天下者，人稱之曰王，則平聲。據其身臨天下而言曰王，則去聲。【詩·大雅】王此大邦。【箋】王，君也。【釋文】王，于况反。【前漢·高帝紀】項羽背約而王君王於南鄭。【師古註】上王字，于放反。　又【廣韻】盛也。【莊子·養生主】神雖王，不善也。【註】謂心神長王。【釋文】王，于况反。　又音往。【詩·大雅】昊天曰明，及爾出王。【傳】王，往也。【朱註】音往。〇按王本古文玉字。註詳部首。    考證：〔【爾雅·釋親】父之母曰王母。〕　謹照原文改父之妣爲王母。〔【周禮·冬官考工記】王弓。【註】往體寡來體多曰王。〕　謹照原文改弓人，往體寡，來體多，謂之王弓之屬。〔又蟲名。【爾雅·釋蟲】虎，王蝟也。〕　謹於又蟲名爾雅釋蟲下增王蛈蜴。註：卽螲蟷，似䵹鼄，在穴中，有蓋。今河北人呼蛈蜴。二十二字。又按虎王蝟也非爾雅文，查係博雅，謹照原書增博雅二字。〔【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤于放切，徨去聲。霸王。〕　謹按王于放切，徨胡光切，王非徨之去聲，今將徨去聲霸王五字改爲音旺霸王也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, upper; Jade radical 玉; p.727 #02)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 雨方 — sounding like 徨 (wáng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Guangyun*: "great (大); a sovereign (君); he whom the world takes as its model." *Zheng Yun*: "a master (主); he to whom the world turns and goes (歸往) is called 王." Yijing, *Kun*: "one may take up the king's service (王事)." Also *Sui*: "the king makes offering on the Western Mountain." Shu, *Hongfan*: "without bias, without faction, the kingly way (王道) is broad and level." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "fit to be lord, fit to be king"; note: "君 is a feudal lord, 王 the Son of Heaven." A note: from Qin and Han onward, all feudal lords were styled 王 (king), and the Son of Heaven's uncles and brothers enfeoffed abroad were also called 王.
- *Also, a feudal lord's hereditary audience is called 王.* Shijing, *Shangsong*: "none dared not to come to the royal audience (王)"; Jian: "the hereditary audience is called 王."
- *Also, any honorific is called 王.* Erya, *Shiqin*: "a father's deceased father is the 王父 (grandfather), a father's deceased mother the 王母 (grandmother)."
- *Also 法王 (Dharma King) and 象王 (Elephant King) are both titles of the Buddha.* Huayan gatha: "where the Elephant King (象王) walks, the fallen blossoms are red." Cen Shen's poem: "the more so, meeting Yuan of Mount Lu afar, I draw out my hairpin and do homage to the Dharma King (法王)"; note: "法王 is an honorific title of the Buddha."
- *Also a surname.*
- *Also, the rule for posthumous titles:* "he to whom benevolence and righteousness turn is called 王."
- *Also 王屋, a mountain-name.* Shu, *Yu gong*: "as far as Wangwu (王屋)"; subcommentary, the *Correct Meaning*: "Wangwu lies northeast of Yuan county in Hedong."
- *Also a bow-name.* Zhouli, *Dongguan, Kaogong ji*: "the bowyer: when the outward curve is slight and the inward curve great, this is called the class of the royal bow (王弓)."
- *Also,* 王連 is the milkwort (遠志); see the Boya. 夫王 is the tu-grass (芏); see the subcommentary to Erya, *Shicao*.
- *Also 王鴡, a bird-name.* Erya, *Shiniao*: "the juju (鴡鳩) is the wangju (王鴡)"; note: "of the eagle kind; in Jiangdong today it is called the osprey (鶚)."
- *Also 王鮪, a fish-name.* Zhouli, *Tianguan, Yuren*: "in spring they present the wangwei (王鮪)"; note: "the 王鮪 is the largest of the sturgeons (鮪)."
- *Also a snake-name.* Erya, *Shiyu*: "the python (蟒) is the king-snake (王蛇)"; note: "the python is the largest of snakes, hence 王蛇."
- *Also an insect-name.* Erya, *Shichong*: "王蛈蜴"; note: "this is the dietang (螲蟷, the trapdoor spider), like the zhizhu spider, living in a hole with a lid; the people of Hebei today call it tieyi (蛈蜴)." Boya: "the tiger is the king-hedgehog (王蝟)."
- *Also* *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 于放 — sounding like 旺 (wàng): to reign as king (霸王). *Zheng Yun*: "whoever possesses the realm, when men call him 王, it is in the level tone; but spoken of his person reigning over the realm, 王 is in the departing tone." Shijing, *Daya*: "reign over (王) this great state"; Jian: "王 means to rule as lord"; Shiwen: "王, fanqie 于况." Former Han, *Annals of Emperor Gao*: "Xiang Yu broke the covenant and made our sovereign king (王) at Nanzheng"; Yan Shigu's note: "the first 王, fanqie 于放."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: flourishing (盛). Zhuangzi, *Yangsheng zhu*: "though the spirit flourishes (王), it is not good"; note: "it means the heart-spirit ever flourishing"; Shiwen: "王, fanqie 于况."
- *Also pronounced 往 (wǎng).* Shijing, *Daya*: "vast Heaven is bright, and goes forth (王) with you"; tradition: "王 means to go (往)"; Zhu Xi's note: "pronounced 往." A note: 王 was originally the old graph for 玉 (jade); see the radical-head for details.

**Textual notes.** The Erya, *Shiqin* citation should read "a father's deceased mother is the 王母." The Zhouli, *Kaogong ji* citation should read "the bowyer: when the outward curve is slight and the inward curve great, this is called the class of the royal bow." Under "also an insect-name, Erya, *Shichong*," twenty-two graphs ("王蛈蜴。註：卽螲蟷⋯今河北人呼蛈蜴") are to be added; and since "the tiger is the king-hedgehog" is not Erya text but belongs to the Boya, the two graphs 博雅 are supplied. A note: 王 is fanqie 于放 and 徨 is fanqie 胡光, so 王 is not the departing tone of 徨; the reading "徨 in the departing tone, 霸王" is therefore corrected to "sounding like 旺, to reign as king."
