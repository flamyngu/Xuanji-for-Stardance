## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集下〉【車字部】頁1244第09    〔古文〕：𨌷、𨓷【廣韻】去盈切【集韻】【韻會】牽盈切【正韻】丘京切，𠀤音卿。【說文】輕車也。【廣韻】重之對也。　又去聲。【廣韻】虛正切【集韻】【韻會】牽正切【正韻】丘正切，𠀤音慶。【集韻】疾也。【左傳·桓十二年】絞小而輕，輕則寡謀。又【僖三十三年】秦師輕而無禮。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, lower; Cart radical 車; p.1244 #09)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𨌷, 𨓷.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 去盈. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 牽盈. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 丘京 — sounding like 卿 (qīng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a light chariot." *Guangyun*: "the opposite of heavy (重)."
- *Departing tone.* *Guangyun*: fanqie 虛正. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 牽正. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 丘正 — sounding like 慶 (qìng). *Ji Yun*: "quick, rash (疾)." Zuozhuan, Huan yr 12: "Jiao is small and rash (輕); being rash, it lacks counsel." Also Xi yr 33: "the army of Qin was reckless (輕) and without decorum."
