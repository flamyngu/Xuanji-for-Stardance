## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁398第32    〔古文〕：𢟽【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤莫故切，音暮。【說文】習也，愛而習翫模範之也。【史記·司馬相如傳】慕藺相如之爲人，更名相如。　又思也。【禮·檀弓】其往也如慕。　又係戀不忘也。【孟子】大孝終身慕父母。　又姓。又慕容，複姓。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.398 #32)*

**Ancient graph.** 𢟽.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 莫故 — sounding like 暮 (mù).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to imitate fondly (習); to love and study a model, taking it as a pattern." Shiji, *Biography of Sima Xiangru*: "he admired (慕) the character of Lin Xiangru, and changed his own name to Xiangru."
- *To long for (思).* Liji, *Tan Gong*: "in going to the grave, the mourner is as one yearning (慕)."
- *To cleave to in unforgetting love.* Mengzi: "the greatest filial piety is to long for (慕) one's parents all one's life."
- *A surname.* Also Murong (慕容), a compound surname.
