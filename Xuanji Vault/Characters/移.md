**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls еҚҲ, lower; Grain radical зҰҫ; p.853 #04)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie ејӢж”Ҝ. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie дҪҷж”Ҝ. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie е»¶зҹҘ вҖ” sounding like еҢң (yГӯ).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Grain leaning and shifting upon one another (зҰҫзӣёеҖҡз§»)." *Liushu gu*: "to transplant seedlings (з§»з§§); in planting rice, after the seedling, one moves (з§») it." Also *Shuowen*: "one account: a grain-name."

**Senses.**
- *To remove, shift (йҒ·).* *Yun Hui*: "today's йҒ·еҫҷ (to move) borrows иҝ», written з§»." Shu, *Duo shi*: "I move (з§») you to the far distance."
- *To change; to draw out (жҳ“, е»¶).* *Guangyun*. Boya: "to turn (иҪү)." Shu, *Bi ming*: "the age changes and the customs shift (йўЁз§»)." Liji, *Da zhuan*: "for a kin-line cut off, the mourning is not extended (з§»жңҚ)"; subcommentary: "what is at the side and reaches over is з§» вҖ” meaning it does not draw out to reach it." Also *Yu zao*: "in hurrying fast one would start forward, but hands and feet do not sway (з§»)"; subcommentary: "з§» means to sway and stir."
- *To let slip, lose (йҒә).* *Guangyun*. Boya: "з§» means to slip and lose (и„«йҒә)."
- *Where the bureaus and ducal offices are not under one another's authority, there is the з§»-document вҖ” of the note, memorial, and like kinds.* Former Han, *Biography of Gongsun Hong*: "Hong then sent in a sick-leave notice (з§»з—…) and was dismissed home"; note: "a document declaring illness." Later Han, *Annals of Guangwu*: "thereupon he had his staff draw up a circular (ж–Үз§»)"; note: "a document circulated to the dependent counties."
- *Surname.* *Fengsu tong*: "Yi Liang of the Han, Grand Administrator of Hongnong."
- *A mountain-name.* Shuijing zhu: "the Yuan water rises from Mount Xu and flows into the Xiong stream; the Xiong stream is bordered south by Mount Yi (з§»еұұ); the mountain was north of the water, but one evening, in wind and rain, by morning it had moved (з§») south of the water вҖ” hence the name."
- *Interchangeable with ж–Ҫ.* Former Han, *Biography of Wei Wan*: "the sword is a thing men exchange (ж–Ҫжҳ“) вҖ” has it lasted to now alone?"; Ru Chun's note: "ж–Ҫ read as з§»; it means the sword is what men prize, hence often exchanged (з§»жҳ“) and traded."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie д»Ҙиұү, sounding like з•° (yГ¬). Liji, *Jiao te sheng*: "to make the people envious (з§»ж°‘)"; note: "з§» speaks of envy (зҫЁ)"; subcommentary: "at the za-sacrifice all is rich and abundant, all drunk and sated with food and drink, making men long for it." Also Cao Zhi, *Yaoque fu*: "the sparrow, hearing the hawk's words, was greatly unmoved (дёҚз§»); its eyes like split peppercorns, it leapt and beat its two wings"; note: "з§» means to remove."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie ж•һр ҮҚ, sounding like дҫҲ (chЗҗ). Liji, *Biao ji*: "with robes to make them ample (з§»д№Ӣ)"; note: "з§» read like the з§» of ж°ҙжұңз§»; it is like 'broad and great.'"

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as дҝ„ (Г©), fanqie зүӣдҪ•.* Chuci, *Qi jian*: "clear and cold it is cut off and destroyed, turbid and murky it grows day by day; the owls and night-jars have made their flocks, and the dark cranes furl their wings and screen off, withdrawing (еұҸз§»)."
