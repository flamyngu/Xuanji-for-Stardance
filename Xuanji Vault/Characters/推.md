**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, middle; Hand radical 手; p.439 #08)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 他回. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 通回 — the level tone of 退, i.e. **tuī**.

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to push (排)." *Zengyun*: "to shove; to press; one says to put forward." Liji, *Yueling*: "in early spring he ploughs the holy field in person: the Son of Heaven pushes [the plough] thrice (三推), the Three Dukes five times, the ministers and lords nine times." Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 14: "some pulled it, some pushed (推) it"; note: "to draw from the front is 輓, to push from behind is 推."
- *To shift, remove (移).* Shijing, *Daya*: "the drought being too sore, it cannot be pushed off (推)"; subcommentary: "it cannot be made to remove; 推 is a word for putting far away."
- *To yield to another.* Shiji, *Biography of the Marquis of Huaiyin*: "he took off his robe to clothe me, pushed over (推) his food to feed me." Later Han, *Annals of Guangwu*: "he pushes (推) his loyal heart into others' breasts."
- *To decline; to shift onto another.*
- *To move on in sequence.* *Tang Yun*: fanqie 昌錐; *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 川錐 — sounding like 䜅 (chuī). Yijing, *Xici*: "cold and heat push each other on (相推), and the year is made."
- *To choose; to commend; to promote.* *Zengyun.* Shu, *Zhouguan*: "promote (推) the worthy and yield to the able, and the many offices are harmonious." Liji, *Ruxing*: "from above unpulled, from below unpushed (推)"; note: "推 is like 'to advance'"; subcommentary: "not recommended by those below." Former Han, *Biography of Han Xin*: "poor and of no [good] conduct, he could not be put forward (推擇) for office"; note: "no good conduct by which to be recommended and chosen."
- *To trace out, infer (尋繹).* Former Han, appraisal in the *Biography of Liu Xiang*: "with purpose he traced it to its root (推本)"; note: "he probed the very root, with deep intent; one says, to press to the bottom." Shiji, *Biographies of the Harsh Officials*: "Luo Bi of Tianshui [used] interrogation (推減)"; note: "推, fanqie 直追, means to press and hold [a suspect] to make a case; 減 is in one text 成.
