**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls жҲҢ, upper; Gate radical й–Җ; p.1338 #26)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie зҫҪйқһ. *Ji Yun*: fanqie дәҺйқһ вҖ” sounding like йҹӢ (wГ©i).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a gate within the palace." Liji, *Zaji*: "the lady arriving, she enters by the side-gate (й—Ҳй–Җ)"; *Shiwen*: "й—Ҳ sounds йҹӢ, a gate within the palace." Note to Zuozhuan, Min yr 2: "a small gate within the palace is called й—Ҳ"; *Shiwen*: sounds йҹӢ. Shiji, *House of the Grand Duke of Qi*: "they attacked the side-gate (й—Ҳ) and the main gate"; note: "a gate within the palace is й—Ҳ." Former Han, *Author's Postface*: "all reaching the gates and doors (й–Җй—Ҳ) of the lord of the time."
- *A place-name.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 23: "Yin Xin took Xiwei (иҘҝй—Ҳ)"; note: "Xiwei, a Zhou place"; *Shiwen*: "й—Ҳ sounds йҹӢ."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie еҗҒйҹӢ; *Yun Hui*: fanqie еҚҖйҹӢ вҖ” sounding like жҡү (huД«). Liji, *Zaji*: "she enters by the side-gate (й—Ҳй–Җ)"; *Shiwen*: "Liu Changzong reads it жҡү." Note to Zuozhuan, Min yr 2: "a small gate within the palace is called й—Ҳ"; *Shiwen*: "also sounds жҡү."

**On the graph.** Sometimes written её·: note to Liji, *Zaji* on й—Ҳй–Җ, "й—Ҳй–Җ is sometimes её·й–Җ." The vulgar form рЁөҫ is wrong.
