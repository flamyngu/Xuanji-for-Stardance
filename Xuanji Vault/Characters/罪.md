## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈未集中〉【网字部】頁948第13    〔古文〕：辠、𡈚【廣韻】徂賄切【集韻】粗賄切，𠀤音㠑。【說文】捕魚竹罔。【易·解卦】君子以赦過宥罪。【書·大禹謨】罪疑惟輕。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 未, middle; Net radical 网; p.948 #13)*

**Ancient graphs.** 辠, 𡈚.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 徂賄. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 粗賄 — sounding like 㠑 (zuì).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a bamboo net for catching fish."
- *Crime, guilt.* Yijing, *Jie* hexagram: "the gentleman thereby pardons faults and is lenient with crimes (罪)." Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "when the crime (罪) is doubtful, deal with it lightly."
