## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集下〉【女字部】頁261第22【廣韻】卽夷切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】津私切，𠀤音咨。【說文】態也。【唐書·太宗紀】太宗生四歲，有書生見之，曰：龍鳳之姿。【後魏·宋弁傳】聲姿淸亮。　又【集韻】資四切，音恣。媚也。【韓愈詩】羲之俗書媿姿媚。【歐陽氏曰】性姿，天姿，與資義同。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, lower; Woman radical 女; p.261 #22)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 卽夷. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 津私 — sounding like 咨 (zī).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "bearing, mien (態)." Tang shu, *Annals of Taizong*: "when Taizong was four years old, a scholar saw him and said, 'a dragon-and-phoenix bearing (姿).'" Hou Wei shu, *Biography of Song Bian*: "his voice and bearing (聲姿) clear and resonant."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 資四, sounding like 恣 (zì). "Charming (媚)." Han Yu's poem: "Xizhi's common script is shamed by mere charm and grace (姿媚)." Master Ouyang says: "in 性姿 (natural endowment) and 天姿 (innate gifts), 姿 is the same in sense as 資.
