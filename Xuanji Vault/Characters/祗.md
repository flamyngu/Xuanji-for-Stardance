## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈午集下〉【示字部】頁841第20    〔古文〕：祬【廣韻】旨夷切。【集韻】【韻會】烝夷切【正韻】旨而切，𠀤音脂。【爾雅·釋詁】敬也。【書·大禹謨】祗承于帝。【周禮·春官·大司樂】以樂德敎國子，中和祗庸孝友。【正譌】从示，氐聲。見神示則敬。會意。【正字通】與祇通。郝敬曰：祗从氏下一，韻書別出，其實同。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, lower; Spirit radical 示; p.841 #20)*

**Ancient graph.** 祬.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 旨夷. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 烝夷. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 旨而 — sounding like 脂 (zhī).

**Etymology and senses.** *Erya, Shigu*: "reverence (敬)." Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "reverently (祗) serving the Sovereign." Zhouli, *Chunguan, Da sile*: "by the virtues of music he taught the sons of the state — the mean, harmony, reverence (祗), the constant, filiality, and friendship."

**On the graph.** The Liushu zhengwei: "it follows 示 (spirit) with 氐 as phonetic; seeing the spirits one is reverent — a meaning-compound." *Zhengzitong*: "interchangeable with 祇. Hao Jing says: 祗 follows 氏 with a stroke below; the rhyme-books list it separately, but in truth they are the same."
