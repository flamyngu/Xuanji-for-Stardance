**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁408第17    〔古文〕：褱【唐韻】戸乖切【集韻】【韻會】乎乖切，𠀤音槐。【說文】念思也。从心褱聲。【論語】君子懷德。又歸也。【書·大禹謨】黎民懷之。【註】歸之也。　又來也。【詩·齊風】曷又懷止。又【周頌】懷柔百神。【註】懷，來也。　又安也。【書·秦誓】邦之榮懷。【詩·王風】懷哉懷哉。　又包也。【書·堯典】蕩蕩懷山襄陵。　又藏也。【論語】懷其寶，而迷其邦。【禮·曲禮】其有核者，懷其核。　又慰也。【詩·檜風】懷之好音。　又懷抱，胷臆也。【左傳·成十七年】瓊瑰盈吾懷乎。【論語】然後免於父母之懷。　又傷也。【詩·邶風】願言則懷。【毛傳】懷，傷也。　又私也。【詩·小雅】每懷靡及。【箋】懷，私也。　又【爾雅·釋詁】至也。【註】齊魯之會郊曰懷。【詩·小雅】懷允不忘。【箋】至也。至信不忘。　又【爾雅·釋詁】止也。【疏】至止也。　又【釋名】懷，回也。本有去意，回來就已也。亦言歸也，來歸已也。　又【諡法】慈仁哲行曰懷。又地名。【書·禹貢】覃懷底績。　又州名。春秋時野王邑，漢河內郡，唐置懷州。　又姓。三國吳尚書郞懷敘。　又通作褢。【前漢·許后傳】褢誠秉忠。　又叶胡隈切，音回。【詩·周南】陟彼崔嵬，我馬虺隤。我姑酌彼金罍，維以不永懷。　又叶呼回切，音揮。【楚辭·九歌】長太息兮將上，心低回兮顧懷。羌生色兮娛人，觀者憺兮忘歸。【魏文帝·苦寒行】延頸長太息，遠行多所懷。我心何怫鬱，思欲一東歸。　又叶苦禾切，音窠。【淮南子·時則訓】平而不阿，明而不苛。包裹覆露，無不囊懷。【周伯琦曰】褱，藏挾於衣中也。从衣，眔聲。心之所恖念藏貯亦曰褱。隷作懷。    考證：〔【楚辭·九歌】長太息兮將上，心低回兮顧懷。羌生色以娛人，觀者憺以忘歸。〕　謹照原文兩以字𠀤改爲兮字。〔【淮南子·五位篇】平而不阿，明而不苛。包裹覆露，無不囊懷。〕　謹照原書，五位篇改爲時則訓。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.408 #17)*

**Ancient graph.** 褱.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 戸乖. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 乎乖 — sounding like 槐 (huái).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Longing thought (念思)." It follows 心 with 褱 as phonetic. Lunyu: "the gentleman cherishes (懷) virtue."

**Senses.**
- *To turn toward, return (歸).* Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "the black-haired people turn to (懷) him"; note: "they return to him."
- *To come (來).* Shijing, *Qi feng*: "when, oh, will he come (懷)?"; *Zhou song*: "he soothes and draws near (懷柔) the hundred spirits"; note: "懷, to come."
- *To be at peace (安).* Shu, *Qin shi*: "the state's glory and peace (懷)"; Shijing, *Wang feng*: "be at peace, be at peace (懷哉懷哉)!"
- *To enfold, embrace (包).* Shu, *Yao dian*: "vast, it embraces (懷) the mountains and overtops the hills."
- *To conceal, keep within (藏).* Lunyu: "to hide (懷) one's treasure while letting one's state go astray." Liji, *Qu li*: "if [the fruit] has a pit, keep (懷) the pit [in your bosom]."
- *To comfort (慰).* Shijing, *Kuai feng*: "comfort (懷) him with sweet notes."
- *The bosom, the breast (懷抱, 胷臆).* Zuozhuan, Cheng yr 17: "the jade-gems filled my bosom (懷)." Lunyu: "only thereafter freed from the embrace (懷) of father and mother."
- *To wound, grieve (傷).* Shijing, *Bei feng*: "to speak of it grieves (懷) me"; Mao tradition: "懷, to grieve."
- *Private (私).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "ever private (懷) and falling short"; gloss: "懷, private."
- *To reach, arrive (至).* Erya, *Shi gu*; note: "in Qi and Lu a meeting at the suburb was called 懷." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "[with] sincerity (懷) not to be forgotten"; gloss: "至, to reach — utmost faith not forgotten."
- *To stop (止).* Erya, *Shi gu*; subcommentary: "to reach and halt."
- *To turn back (回).* *Shiming*: "懷 is 'to turn back': having had an intent to leave, one turns back to oneself; also said to be 'returning,' coming home to oneself."
- *Posthumous title.* "Kind, benevolent and wise in conduct is 懷."
- *Place name.* Shu, *Yu gong*: "at Tan-huai he achieved his work."
- *A prefecture.* In the Spring-and-Autumn the town of Yewang; under the Han the Henei commandery; the Tang set up Huaizhou.
- *Surname.* In Three-Kingdoms Wu, the Secretariat Gentleman Huai Xu.
- *Interchangeable with 褢.* Former Han, *Biography of Empress Xu*: "cherishing (褢) sincerity and holding to loyalty."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 回 (huí), fanqie 胡隈.* Shijing, *Zhou nan*: "I climb that craggy ridge, my horse worn out… I pour from that bronze flagon, only that I may not grieve forever (懷)."
- *Rhymes as 揮 (huī), fanqie 呼回.* Chuci, *Jiu ge*: "with a long sigh I am about to rise, my heart sinks and circles, looking back with longing (懷)…" Wei Wendi, *Bitter Cold*: "I crane my neck and sigh long; on the far march much fills my heart (懷)…"
- *Rhymes as 窠 (kē), fanqie 苦禾.* Huainanzi, *Shize xun*: "level yet not partial, bright yet not harsh; wrapping and sheltering, there is nothing it does not gather in its bosom (懷)." Zhou Boqi: "褱 means to store and tuck within one's robes; from 衣 (clothing) with 眔 phonetic; what the heart thinks and stores up is likewise called 褱 — in clerical script written 懷."

**Textual notes.** In the *Jiu ge* citation the two 以 should read 兮; the *Huainanzi* passage is from *Shize xun*, not *"Wuwei pian."*
