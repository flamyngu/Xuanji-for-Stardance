## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈午集下〉【禸字部】頁848第13    〔古文〕：𢄕【唐韻】【集韻】巨今切【韻會】【正韻】渠今切，𠀤音琴。【爾雅·釋鳥】二足而羽謂之禽，四足而毛謂之獸。【白虎通】禽，鳥獸總名，言爲人禽制也。【孔穎達云】王用三驅失前禽，則驅走者亦曰禽。【于越志】臨川吳澄問東隅吳正道禽獸二字，正道曰：禽卽獸也。曰：兩翼爲禽，四足爲獸，何以言。曰：禮不云乎，猩猩能言，不離禽獸。　又【韻會】鳥獸未孕曰禽。　又戰勝執獲曰禽。【左傳·僖三十三年】外僕髠屯禽之以獻。通作擒。　又姓。【左傳】魯大夫禽鄭。【高士傳】禽慶。　又叶渠容切，音窮。【易林】吉日車攻，田弋獲禽。宣王飮酒，以告嘉功。　又【字彙補】龍支切，音離。【魏志·和洽傳】諡𥳑侯子禽。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, lower; Track radical 禸; p.848 #13)*

**Ancient graph.** 𢄕.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*: fanqie 巨今. *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 渠今 — sounding like 琴 (qín).

**Etymology and senses.** Erya, *Shiniao*: "two-footed and feathered is called 禽 (fowl); four-footed and furred is called 獸 (beast)." Baihu tong: "禽 is the general name for birds and beasts; it says they are taken and mastered (禽制) by man." Kong Yingda says: "'the king uses the three drives and lets the fore-game (前禽) escape' — so what is driven and runs is also called 禽." Yuyue zhi: "Wu Cheng of Linchuan asked Wu Zhengdao of Dongyu about the two graphs 禽 and 獸. Zhengdao said, '禽 is just 獸.' Wu Cheng said, 'two wings is 禽, four feet is 獸 — why so?' Zhengdao said, 'does not the Liji say, "the orangutan can speak, yet is not removed from the birds and beasts (禽獸)"?'"
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: birds and beasts not yet pregnant are called 禽.
- *Also, to win in battle and take captive is called 禽.* Zuozhuan, Xi yr 33: "the outer servant Kun Tun captured (禽) him and presented him." Interchangeable with 擒.
- *Also a surname.* Zuozhuan: "the Lu grandee Qin Zheng (禽鄭)." Gaoshi zhuan: "Qin Qing (禽慶)."
- *Rhymes as* fanqie 渠容, sounding like 窮 (qióng). Yilin: "on a lucky day, the chariots arrayed, hunting and fowling they took game (禽); King Xuan drank wine, to proclaim the fine deed."
- *Also* Zihui bu: fanqie 龍支, sounding like 離 (lí). Wei zhi, *Biography of He Qia*: "he was given the posthumous title Marquis Jian (簡侯); his son was Qin (禽)."
