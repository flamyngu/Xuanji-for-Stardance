## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁389第13【唐韻】徒到切【集韻】【韻會】大到切【正韻】杜到切，𠀤音導。【廣韻】傷也。【詩·邶風】中心是悼。　又憐愛也。【禮·曲禮】七年曰悼，雖有罪，不加𠛬，憐其知未及，而非故犯也。　又懼也。【揚子·方言】𨻰楚謂懼曰悼。　又【諡法】中年早夭曰悼。【春秋】晉悼公。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.389 #13)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 徒到. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 大到. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 杜到 — sounding like 導 (dào).

**Etymology and senses.** *Guangyun*: "to grieve (傷)." Shijing, *Beifeng*: "in my inmost heart I grieve (悼)."
- *To pity, hold dear (憐愛).* Liji, *Quli*: "a child of seven is called 悼; though he commit a crime, no punishment is laid on him, in pity that his understanding has not yet reached, and the offence was not deliberate."
- *To fear (懼).* Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "in Chen and Chu, to fear is called 悼."
- *Posthumous-title rule:* "to die untimely in middle years is called 悼." Chunqiu: "Duke Dao (悼公) of Jin."
