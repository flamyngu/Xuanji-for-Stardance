## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集中〉【貝字部】頁1209第35    〔古文〕：𧶤【唐韻】【集韻】才線切【正韻】在線切，𠀤音羨。【說文】賈少也。【玉篇】𤰞下也，不貴也。【廣韻】輕賤。【易·屯卦】以貴下賤，大得民也。【書·旅獒】不貴異物，賤用物。【前漢·食貨志】糴甚貴傷民，甚賤傷農。　又姓。【風俗通】漢北平太守賤瓊。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, middle; Shell radical 貝; p.1209 #35)*

**Ancient graph.** 𧶤.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*: fanqie 才線. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 在線 — sounding like 羨 (jiàn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "low in price (賈少)." *Yupian*: "low and mean; not dear." *Guangyun*: "lowly, contemptible." Yijing, *Zhun*: "the noble placing himself below the base (賤), he greatly wins the people." Shu, *Lü'ao*: "prize not strange things, and hold cheap (賤) things of use." Former Han, *Treatise on Food and Money*: "grain too dear hurts the people; too cheap (賤), it hurts the farmer."
- *A surname.* Fengsu tong: "the Han had Jian Qiong, governor of Beiping.
