## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁385第02【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤資四切，資去聲。【說文】縱也。从心次聲。【漢明德馬皇后詔】寵貴橫恣。又【詩·檜風·小序】疾恣也。【鄭箋】恣，謂狡㹟淫戲，不以禮也。　又【五音集韻】千咨切，音趑。恣睢，自得貌。秦刻石文作資。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.385 #02)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 資四 — the departing tone of 資 (zì).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to let loose, give rein to (縱)." It follows 心 (heart) with 次 as phonetic. Edict of the Han Empress Mingde Ma: "favoured and honoured, wanton and unrestrained (橫恣)."
- *also.* Shijing, *Kuaifeng*, Lesser Preface: "it deplores wantonness (恣)"; Zheng Xuan's Jian: "恣 means cunning dalliance and lewd sporting, not by the rites."
- *Wuyin jiyun*: fanqie 千咨, sounding like 趑 (cī). 恣睢, the look of self-satisfaction; the Qin stone inscriptions write it 資.
