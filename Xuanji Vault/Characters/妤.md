## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集下〉【女字部】頁257第04【廣韻】羽諸切【集韻】羊諸切【正韻】雲居切，𠀤音余。婕妤，女官。【師古曰】婕，言接幸于上。妤，美稱。【史記·外威世家】褚先生曰：婕妤，秩比列侯。通作倢伃。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, lower; Woman radical 女; p.257 #04)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 羽諸. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 羊諸. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 雲居 — sounding like 余 (yú).

**Etymology and senses.** 婕妤 (*jieyu*), a female palace office. Yan Shigu: "婕 means to be received in favour (接幸) by the sovereign; 妤 is a term of beauty." Shiji, *Hereditary Houses of the Maternal Relatives*: "Master Chu said, 'the *jieyu* (婕妤) ranked equal to a full marquis.'" Used interchangeably with 倢伃.
