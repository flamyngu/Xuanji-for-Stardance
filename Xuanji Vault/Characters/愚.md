## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁395第12【唐韻】麌俱切【集韻】【韻會】元俱切【正韻】牛俱切，𠀤音虞。戇也，闇也，蒙也，昧也，蠢也，鈍也，愗也，滯也，固也，蔽也，冥也。一曰愚之言寓也，無所爲，若寄寓然。【荀子·修身篇】非是是非之謂愚。　又【山海經】天愚，神名。居苦山東堵山。　又姓。又叶魚侯切，音吽。【華竅自責文】聖恩雨注，哀棄其尤。猥命草對，潤被下愚。从心从禺。禺，猴屬，獸之愚者。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.395 #12)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 麌俱. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 元俱. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 牛俱 — sounding like 虞 (yú).

**Etymology and senses.** Blockish (戇); benighted (闇); muddled (蒙); blind (昧); dull (蠢); blunt (鈍); witless (愗); sluggish (滯); obstinate (固); shut-off (蔽); dark (冥). One account: 愚 (stupid) speaks of 寓 (lodging) — doing nothing, like one who merely lodges as a guest. Xunzi, *Xiu shen*: "to call the right wrong and the wrong right is what is meant by stupidity (愚)."

**Senses.**
- *Also* Shanhai jing: "Tianyu (天愚), a god's name; it dwells on Mount Du, east of Mount Ku."
- *Surname.*
- *Also* fanqie 魚侯, sounding like 吽 (hōu). *Huaqiao zize wen*: "the sage's grace pours like rain, in pity casting off its faults; humbly bidden to draft replies, its bounty reaches even the lowest fool (下愚)."

**On the graph.** It follows 心 (heart) and 禺. 禺 is a kind of monkey, the stupid one among beasts.
