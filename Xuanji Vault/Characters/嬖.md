## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集下〉【女字部】頁272第07【廣韻】博計切【集韻】【韻會】必計切，𠀤音閉。【說文】便辟也。愛也。【增韻】便幸，左右近習者也。【春秋傳】賤而得幸曰嬖。【左傳·昭九年】飮外嬖嬖叔。　又官名。【左傳·昭七年】宣子爲子產之敏也，使從嬖大夫。【註】下大夫也。　又【集韻】𤰞義切，音臂。義同。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, lower; Woman radical 女; p.272 #07)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 博計. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 必計 — sounding like 閉 (bì).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "fawning, obsequious (便辟); to be loved." *Zengyun*: "a favourite — one of the intimate attendants at one's side." The Chunqiu tradition: "one base-born yet favoured is called 嬖." Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 9: "he gave drink to the outer favourite, the favourite Shu (嬖叔)."
- *An office-name.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 7: "Xuanzi, because of Zichan's quickness, had him rank among the *bi* grandees (嬖大夫)"; note: "the lower grandees."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 𤰞義, sounding like 臂 (bì); same sense.
