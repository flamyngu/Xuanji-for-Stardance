**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, lower; Ox radical 牛; p.699 #09)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 文弗. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 文拂 — sounding like 勿 (wù).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the myriad things; the ox is a great thing; the reckoning of Heaven and Earth begins at [the asterism] Oxherd, hence it follows 牛 (ox), with 勿 as phonetic." *Yupian*: "all that is born between Heaven and Earth is called 物." Yijing, *Qian*: "the kinds of things (品物) flow into shape." Wuwang: "the former kings, matching the seasons in abundance, reared the myriad things (萬物)." Zhouli, *Tianguan, Da zai*: "of the nine tributes, the ninth is the goods-tribute (物貢)"; note: "sundry goods — fish, salt, oranges, pomelos."
- *An affair, matter (事).* *Yupian.* Yijing, *Jiaren*: "the gentleman has substance (物) in his words and constancy in his deeds"; subcommentary: "物 means a matter." Liji, *Aigong wen*: "Confucius answered, 'not to exceed the bounds of things (物)'"; note: "物 is like 'affairs.'" Zhouli, *Diguan, Da situ*: "by the three matters (三物) of the districts he teaches the people, and recommends them as guests."
- *Coat-colour, kind (毛物).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "matched in kind (物), the four black steeds"; tradition: "物 means coat-colour"; and: "thirty by their colours (物), your victims are complete." Zhouli, *Chunguan, Jiren*: "in charge of the fowl-victims, distinguishing their kind (物)"; note: "coat-colour." Xiaguan, *Jiaoren*: "in all military affairs, he sorts the horses by colour (物) and assigns them"; subcommentary: "物 is just colour." The Chu yu: "the coat shows the kind (物)."
- *To assay, survey (相).* Zhouli, *Diguan, Kuangren*: "...then he assays (物) the ground, maps it, and assigns it"; note: "to assay the ground is to judge its form and colour, knowing salt from fresh." The Caoren "assays the ground (物地) to judge its fitness." Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 32: "he surveyed (物) the lie of the soil"; note: "物 means to inspect — to take the aspect of the ground."
- *A kind, sort (類).* *Yupian.* Zuozhuan, Huan yr 6: "the duke said, 'his birth is of the same kind (物) as mine'"; note: "物 means kind — the same [cyclical] day."
- *Stuff, material (財).* Zhouli, *Tianguan, Jiuzheng*: "he distinguishes the stuff (物) of the three wines"; subcommentary: "物 here is 'material' — since the wines take time to make, the material is given out in advance."
- *不物, out of the ordinary.* Zhouli, *Diguan, Simen*: "he inspects those going in and out who are 'out of the ordinary' (不物)"; note: "those whose dress and bearing are unlike the common run, or whose goods do not match the regulation."
- *The proper measure.* The Zhouyu: "when a god appears, it does not exceed its measure (物)"; note: "物 means the measure of things."
- *A banner-name.* *Guangyun.* Zhouli, *Chunguan, Sichang*: "mixed silk makes the *wu*-banner (物)."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes, departing tone, fanqie 符沸 (Tang yun zheng).* Yangzi's *Taixuan jing*: "man by man, thing by thing (物), each by its own kind." Ruan Ji, *Dongping fu*: "...planted with marker-emblems (表物), the four seasons modelling its forms, yin and yang spreading its *qi*."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 微律.* Ban Gu, *Dongdu fu*: "...joy not pushed to riot, the kill not exhausting the game (物)."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 微月.* Su Shi, *Inscription for the Sida Studio*: "what is like this quiet? — void and empty, with no thing (物)."

**Textual notes.** "辦其物" should read "辨其物"; "義不極盤" should read "樂不極盤".
