## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈戌集中〉【面字部】頁1383第07【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤彌箭切，音偭。【說文】本作𠚑，顏前也。从𦣻，象人面形。【書·益稷】汝無面從，退有後言。　又見也。【禮·曲禮】夫爲人子者，出必告，反必面。【註】反言面者，從外來，宜知親之顏色安否。【儀禮·聘禮】𢷤者出請事，賔面如覿幣。【註】面，亦見也。【周禮·秋官·司儀】私面。【註】私覿也。　又向也。【書·周官】不學牆面。【疏】人而不學，如面向牆。【禮·曲禮】天子當依而立，諸侯北面而見天子，曰覲。又【玉藻】唯君面尊。【註】面，猶鄕也。【周禮·冬官考工記·匠人】面朝後市。　又【廣韻】前也。【儀禮·士冠禮】覆之面葉。【註】面，前也。　又【韻會】方面，當四方之一面也。【書·顧命】大輅在賔階面，綴輅在阼階面。【周禮·冬官考工記】或審曲面勢。【註】審察五材曲直方面形勢之宜。【史記·留侯世家】獨韓信可屬大事，當一面。　又【韻會】相背曰面。【史記·項羽紀】馬童面之。【註】如淳曰：面，不正視也。【前漢·項羽傳註】師古曰：如淳說非也。面謂背之，不面向也。面縛亦謂反背而縛之，杜元凱以爲但見其面，非也。　又【前漢·張敞傳】自以便面拊馬。【註】師古曰：便面，扇之類也。亦曰屛面。    考證：〔【說文】本作𠚑，顏前也。从自〕　謹照原文自改𦣻。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 戌, middle; Face radical 面; p.1383 #07)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 彌箭 — sounding like 偭 (miàn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "properly written 𠚑, the front of the face (顏前). It follows 𦣻 (head) and pictures a man's face." Shu, *Yi Ji*: "do not assent to my face (面) and then, retiring, have words behind my back."
- *Also, to see, to present oneself (見).* Liji, *Quli*: "as for one who is a son, going out he must announce it, returning he must present himself (面)"; note: "to say 'present himself' on returning — coming from outside, he should know whether his parents' looks are well or not." Yili, *Pin li*: "the usher comes out to ask the business; the guest presents himself (面) as for the offering of gifts"; note: "面 too means to present oneself." Zhouli, *Qiuguan, Siyi*: "the private presentation (私面)"; note: "a private visit (私覿)."
- *Also, to face (向).* Shu, *Zhou guan*: "not to learn is to face a wall (牆面)"; subcommentary: "a man who does not learn is like one facing a wall." Liji, *Quli*: "the Son of Heaven stands by the screen, and the feudal lords face north (北面) to see him — this is called the audience (覲)." Also *Yuzao*: "only for the ruler does the wine-jar face (面) him"; note: "面 is like 鄉 (to face toward)." Zhouli, *Dongguan, Kaogong ji, Jiangren*: "the court in front (面朝), the market behind."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "the front (前)." Yili, *Shi guan li*: "covered, with the leaf to the front (面葉)"; note: "面 is the front."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: 方面 (a quarter), one side of the four directions. Shu, *Gu ming*: "the Great Carriage at the front of the guest-steps (賔階面), the Adorned Carriage at the front of the eastern steps (阼階面)." Zhouli, *Dongguan, Kaogong ji*: "some examine the curve and aspect (面勢)"; note: "examining the fitness of the bends and straights, the squares and aspects, of the five materials." Shiji, *Hereditary House of the Marquis of Liu*: "only Han Xin can be entrusted with great affairs and hold a whole sector (一面)."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: turning the back is called 面. Shiji, *Annals of Xiang Yu*: "Lü Matong turned away from (面) him"; note: Ru Chun says: "面 means not looking straight at." Former Han, *Biography of Xiang Yu*, note: Yan Shigu says: "Ru Chun's view is wrong; 面 means to turn one's back to him, not to face him; 面縛 (binding with face turned) likewise means to bind with the back reversed. Du Yu held it to mean merely showing the face — this is wrong."
- *Also* Former Han, *Biography of Zhang Chang*: "he would screen his face (便面) and pat his horse"; note: Yan Shigu says: "便面 is of the fan kind; also called a face-screen (屛面)."

**Textual notes.** In the Shuowen citation, 从自 should read 从𦣻 (per the original).
