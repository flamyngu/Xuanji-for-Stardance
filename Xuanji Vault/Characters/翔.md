## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈未集中〉【羽字部】頁956第40【廣韻】似羊切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】徐羊切，𠀤音詳。【說文】回飛也。【爾雅·釋鳥】鳶烏醜，其飛也翔。【註】布翅翱翔。【易·豐卦】天際翔也。【詩·鄭風】河上乎翱翔。　又【禮·曲禮】室中不翔。【註】行而張拱曰翔。　又【禮·玉藻】朝廷濟濟翔翔。【註】莊敬貌。　又【禮·三年問】過其故鄉回翔焉。　又【周禮·冬官考工記·矢人】前弱則俛，後弱則翔。【註】翔，回顧也。【集韻】通作鴹。    考證：〔【爾雅·釋鳥】鳶鳥醜，其飛也翔。〕　謹照原文鳶鳥醜改鳶烏醜。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 未, middle; Feather radical 羽; p.956 #40)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 似羊. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 徐羊 — sounding like 詳 (xiáng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to wheel in flight (回飛)." Erya, *Shiniao*: "the kite, the crow, and their kind — their flying is a soaring (翔)"; note: "spreading the wings and wheeling." Yijing, *Feng*: "soaring (翔) at the rim of heaven." Shijing, *Zhengfeng*: "wheeling (翱翔) above the river."
- *To walk with arms spread.* Liji, *Quli*: "within a chamber, do not 'soar' (翔)"; note: "to walk with arms spread and clasped is 翔."
- *翔翔, a look of solemn reverence.* Liji, *Yuzao*: "in court, grave and stately (翔翔)"; note: "a look of solemn reverence."
- *To wheel and circle.* Liji, *Sannian wen*: "passing its old haunts, it wheels and circles (回翔)."
- *To veer (of an arrow).* Zhouli, *Dongguan kaogong ji*, Arrow-maker: "if the fore is weak it dips, if the aft is weak it veers (翔)"; note: "翔 is to swing back." *Ji Yun*: "interchangeable with 鴹."

**Textual note.** "鳶鳥醜" should read "鳶烏醜".
