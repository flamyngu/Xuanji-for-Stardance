## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集下〉【邑字部】頁1277第30【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤直正切，音㽀。國名。【釋名】鄭，町也，地多平町町然也。【正字通】鄭本西都畿內地，周宣王封其弟友，是爲鄭。桓公寄孥與賄于虢鄶，其子武公，定平王于東都，因徙其封，施舊號于新邑，是爲新鄭。今河南開封府鄭州是也。　又南鄭，縣名。今屬陝西漢中府，桓公封邑在畿內，爲西鄭，漢中在京兆之南，故稱南鄭以別之。【一統志】南鄭，古褒國附庸之邑。桓公歿，其民南奔居此，因曰南鄭。　又【廣韻】鄭重，殷勤之意。【前漢·王莽傳】非皇天所以鄭重降符命之意。　又姓。【集韻】鄭滅于韓，子孫因以國爲氏。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, lower; Settlement radical 邑; p.1277 #30)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 直正 — sounding like 㽀 (zhèng).

**Etymology and senses.** A state-name. *Shiming*: "鄭 means level-fields (町): the land had many level fields, flat and even." *Zhengzitong*: "Zheng was originally land within the metropolitan region of the western capital. King Xuan of Zhou enfeoffed his younger brother You — this was Zheng, Duke Huan. Duke Huan lodged his family and goods with Guo and Kuai; his son Duke Wu settled King Ping in the eastern capital, and so moved his fief, giving the old name to the new town — this was New Zheng (新鄭). It is the present Zhengzhou (鄭州) in Kaifeng prefecture, Henan."
- *Also 南鄭 (Nanzheng), a county-name*, now belonging to Hanzhong prefecture in Shaanxi. Duke Huan's fief-town lay within the metropolitan region and was the Western Zheng (西鄭); Hanzhong lies south of the capital region, hence it is called Nanzheng (南鄭) to distinguish it. Yitong zhi: "Nanzheng was anciently a dependent town of the state of Bao. When Duke Huan died, his people fled south and settled here, and so it was called Nanzheng."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: 鄭重, in the sense of earnest solicitude. Former Han, *Biography of Wang Mang*: "this is not the meaning by which August Heaven so earnestly (鄭重) sends down its tokens of mandate."
- *Also a surname.* *Ji Yun*: "Zheng was destroyed by Han, and its descendants accordingly took the state-name as their clan-name."
