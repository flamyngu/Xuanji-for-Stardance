## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集下〉【辵字部】頁1254第29【集韻】【韻會】演爾切【正韻】養里切，𠀤音以。與迆同。【爾雅】註：迤邐，旁行連延也。　又【集韻】余支切【正韻】延知切，𠀤音移。委迤，自得貌。　又【集韻】【正韻】𠀤唐何切，音駝。逶迤，行貌。本作迱。    考證：〔【爾雅·釋訓】迤邐，旁行也。〕　謹按此爾雅釋邱註非釋訓文，謹照原文釋訓改作註，於旁行下增連延二字。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, lower; Walk radical 辵; p.1254 #29)*

**Pronunciation.** *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 演爾. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 養里 — sounding like 以 (yǐ). The same as 迆.

**Etymology and senses.** Erya, note: "迤邐 (winding), going aslant and stretching on in linked succession."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 余支. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 延知 — sounding like 移 (yí). 委迤, the look of self-satisfied ease.
- *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 唐何, sounding like 駝 (tuó). 逶迤, the look of going windingly. Originally written 迱.

**Textual notes.** The citation "Erya, *Shixun*: 迤邐，旁行也" is in fact a note to the *Shiqiu* (釋邱) chapter, not the *Shixun* text; "釋訓" corrected to "note," and the two graphs 連延 added after 旁行.
