## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集中〉【木字部】頁549第15【唐韻】附袁切，音煩。【說文】作𤕩，鷙不行也。从𠬜从棥。【徐曰】鷙，猶縶也。鷹隼之屬，見籠不得出，以左右攀引外也。【廣韻】樊，籠也。【莊子·養生主】澤雉十步一啄，百步一飮，不期畜于樊中。　又通棥。【詩·小雅】止于樊。【說文】作棥。【孫炎曰】樊圃之樊也。謂樊籬。【莊子·山木篇】莊子遊乎雕陵之樊，睹一蟬得美䕃而忘其身。　又紛雜貌。【莊子·齊物論】樊然殽亂。又國名。【詩·大雅】生仲山甫。【毛傳】仲山甫，樊侯也。　又地名。【左傳·隱十一年】王取田于鄭，而與鄭人以溫、原、絺、樊。　又姓。【姓苑】仲山甫之後，因國爲氏。　又【正韻】符艱切。義同。　又通鞶。【周禮·春官·巾車】掌王之五路。一曰玉路，鍚樊纓。鄭註：樊讀如鞶，馬大帶也。《釋文》步干反。《左傳》作繁纓。　又【高誘曰】扶萬切，音飯。樊桐，山名。【淮南子·地形訓】樊桐在崑崙閶闔之中。　又叶汾沿切，讀若楩。【左思·贈妹詩】才麗漢班，明朗楚樊。默識若記，下筆成篇。【白居易·中隱詩】大隱住朝市，小隱住丘樊。不如作中隱，隨月有俸錢。○按《說文》𠬜部收樊，下不从大，今誤入。    考證：〔【周禮·春官·巾車】玉路，錫樊纓。〕　謹照原文錫樊纓改鍚樊纓。〔【鄭註】鞶，馬大帶也。步干切，與鞶通。【左傳】作曲縣繁纓。〕　謹按步干切以下非鄭註也。謹照原文改鄭註樊讀如鞶，馬大帶也。釋文步干反。左傳作繁纓。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, middle; Tree radical 木; p.549 #15)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 附袁 — sounding like 煩 (fán).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "written 𤕩; a bird of prey that cannot fly. It follows 𠬜 and 棥." Xu [Kai] says: "鷙 (to seize) is like 縶 (to tether); birds of the hawk and falcon kind, seeing the cage, cannot get out, and pull at left and right toward the outside." *Guangyun*: "樊 is a cage (籠)." Zhuangzi, *Yangsheng zhu*: "the marsh pheasant pecks once in ten steps, drinks once in a hundred, but would not wish to be kept within a cage (樊)."
- *Also interchangeable with 棥.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "it settles on the fence (樊)." *Shuowen*: written 棥. Sun Yan says: "the 樊 of 樊圃 (the garden-fence) — that is, a hedge (樊籬)." Zhuangzi, *Shanmu*: "Zhuangzi wandered by the hedge (樊) of Diaoling and saw a cicada that, finding fine shade, forgot itself."
- *Also, the look of being tangled and confused.* Zhuangzi, *Qiwu lun*: "tangled and jumbled (樊然) in disorder."
- *Also a state-name.* Shijing, *Daya*: "Heaven gave birth to Zhongshan Fu"; Mao's tradition: "Zhongshan Fu was the Marquis of Fan (樊侯)."
- *Also a place-name.* Zuozhuan, Yin yr 11: "the king took fields from Zheng and gave the men of Zheng Wen, Yuan, Chi, and Fan (樊)."
- *Also a surname.* Xingyuan: "descendants of Zhongshan Fu, who took the state-name as their clan."
- *Also* *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 符艱; same sense.
- *Also interchangeable with 鞶.* Zhouli, *Chunguan, Jinche*: "[the master] has charge of the king's five carriages. The first is the Jade Carriage, with frontlet, girth-band (樊), and breast-tassels"; Zheng [Xuan]'s note: "樊 is read like 鞶, the horse's great belt"; Shiwen: "fanqie 步干." The Zuozhuan writes it 繁纓.
- *Also* Gao You says: fanqie 扶萬, sounding like 飯 (fàn). 樊桐 (Fantong), a mountain-name. Huainanzi, *Dixing*: "Fantong (樊桐) lies within the Changhe gate of the Kunlun."
- *Rhymes as* fanqie 汾沿, read like 楩 (pián). Zuo Si, poem presented to his sister: "in talent and grace a Ban of the Han, in clarity and brightness a Fan of Chu (楚樊); silently knowing as if recording, she sets brush to paper and completes the piece." Bai Juyi, *Zhongyin shi*: "the great recluse dwells in court and market, the lesser recluse dwells by mound and hedge (丘樊); better to be a middling recluse, with a monthly stipend coming in."

**On the graph.** A note: in the *Shuowen*, 樊 is filed under the 𠬜 radical, and below it does not follow 大; here it has been mistakenly entered under 木.

**Textual notes.** In the Zhouli, *Jinche* citation, 錫樊纓 should read 鍚樊纓 (錫, "tin," corrected to 鍚, "frontlet"). And the text from "fanqie 步干" onward is not part of Zheng's note; corrected per the original to read: "Zheng's note: 樊 is read like 鞶, the horse's great belt; Shiwen: fanqie 步干; the Zuozhuan writes it 繁纓."
