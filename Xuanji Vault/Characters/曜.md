## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集上〉【日字部】頁501第11    〔古文〕：𣇪、𤒂【廣韻】弋照切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】弋笑切，𠀤音燿。日光也。【釋名】光明照耀也。【詩·檜風】日出有曜。【前漢·韋𤣥成傳】光曜晻而不宣。【玉篇】亦作燿。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, upper; Sun radical 日; p.501 #11)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𣇪, 𤒂.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 弋照. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 弋笑 — sounding like 燿 (yào).

**Etymology and senses.** "Sunlight (日光)." *Shiming*: "brightness, shining and dazzling." Shijing, *Kuai feng*: "the sun comes out with radiance (曜)." Former Han, *Biography of Wei Xuancheng*: "the light (曜) dimmed and was not displayed." *Yupian*: also written 燿.
