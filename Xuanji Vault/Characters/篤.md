**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls жңӘ, upper; Bamboo radical з«№; p.894 #29)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie еҶ¬жҜ’. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie йғҪжҜ’ вҖ” sounding like зқЈ (dЕ«).

**Etymology and senses.** *Guangyun*: "deep, generous (еҺҡ)." Yijing, *Da xu*: "firm and strong, solid and substantial (зҜӨеҜҰ), its radiance daily renewing its virtue." Shu, *Luo gao*: "deepen (зҜӨ) the accomplished achievement of our forebears." Shijing, *Daya*: "and so deepens (зҜӨ) his blessing." Erya, *Shigu*: "зҜӨ means firm (еӣә)"; subcommentary: "what is substantial is firm."
- *Also, pure, wholehearted (зҙ”).* Liji, *Ruxing*: "earnest (зҜӨ) in conduct and never wearying"; note: "like зҙ”; conduct pure and single."
- *Also a horse's ailment.* *Shuowen*: "a horse going haltingly and slow."
- *Also a man's grave illness is called зҜӨ.* Shiji, *Biography of Fan Ju*: "the Marquis of Ying then took sick, pleading that it was grave (зҜӨ)."
- *Also a place-name.* Shuijing zhu: "Pingyuan county has the Duma River (зҜӨйҰ¬жІі), which flows northeast into the sea over five hundred and twenty li."
- *Rhymes as* fanqie еҫ’е°Қ. Yang Xi, *Zan Zhao Dao*: "Zhennan founded the frontier, the Army-Inspector was ever steadfast (зҜӨ); both shared the martial charge, their charge from within the realm."

**On the graph.** *Zheng Yun*: also written з«ә.
