## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集中〉【戈字部】頁412第38【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤倉歷切，音磩。【正字通】戉類。【六書精薀】戉之白者，爲之錫劑，以文之不專用武也。【司馬法】殷執白戚。【詩·大雅】干戈戚揚。【註】戚，斧也。【釋名】戚，慼也。斧以斬斷，見者慼懼也。　又親也。【詩·大雅】戚戚兄弟。【傳】戚戚，內相親也。正義曰：戚戚，猶親親也。　又哀也。【論語】喪與其易也寧戚。【註】哀戚也。　又憂也。【論語】小人長戚戚。【註】戚戚，憂貌。　又惱也。【書·金滕】未可以戚我先王。【蔡註】戚，憂惱之意。　又憤也。【禮·檀弓】慍斯戚。【註】戚，憤恚也。　又醜疾人曰戚施。【詩·邶風】得此戚施。【箋】戚施，面柔下人以色，不能仰者也。　又【小爾雅】戚，近也。　又地名。【春秋·文元年】公孫敖會晉侯于戚。【註】戚，衞邑。　又姓。漢有臨轅侯戚緦。　又【集韻】【韻會】𠀤趨玉切。同促。【周禮·冬官考工記】不微至，無以爲戚速也。　又【集韻】【韻會】𠀤昨木切，音族。縣名。在東海。　又【韻補】叶子六切，音蹙。【詩·小雅】歲聿云暮，采蕭穫菽。心之憂矣，自貽伊戚。    考證：〔【釋名】戚，蹙也。斧以斬斷，見者蹙懼也。〕　謹照釋名釋兵原文兩蹙字均改慼。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, middle; Halberd radical 戈; p.412 #38)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui* all: fanqie 倉歷 — sounding like 磩 (qī).

**Etymology and senses.** *Zhengzitong*: "a kind of battle-axe (戉)." The Liushu jingyun: "a bright [white] battle-axe, made with a tin alloy, to civilize it and not use force alone." The Sima fa: "the Yin bore the white axe (白戚)." Shijing, *Daya*: "shield and dagger-axe, war-axe (戚) and great-axe"; note: "戚 is an axe." Shiming: "戚 is 'to grieve' (慼): the axe cuts and severs, and those who see it grieve and fear."
- *Kin, intimate (親).* Shijing, *Daya*: "close-bound (戚戚) brothers"; tradition: "戚戚, inwardly drawn to one another"; the Correct Meaning: "戚戚 is like 'loving one's kin.'"
- *Grief (哀).* Lunyu: "in mourning, rather than easy formality, let there be grief (戚)"; note: "sorrowing grief."
- *Worry (憂).* Lunyu: "the small man is forever anxious (戚戚)"; note: "戚戚, the look of worry."
- *To trouble, vex (惱).* Shu, *Jin teng*: "we may not yet grieve (戚) our former kings"; Cai's note: "戚 means to worry and trouble."
- *Indignation (憤).* Liji, *Tan Gong*: "vexation, then resentment (戚)"; note: "戚, angry resentment."
- *An ugly, ailing person, qishi (戚施).* Shijing, *Beifeng*: "she got this hunchback (戚施)"; Jian: "戚施, one who fawns on others with a flattering face and cannot look up."
- *Near (近).* Xiao Erya: "戚 means near."
- *A place-name.* Chunqiu, Wen yr 1: "Gongsun Ao met the Marquis of Jin at Qi (戚)"; note: "戚, a town of Wei."
- *A surname.* The Han had Qi Si (戚緦), Marquis of Linyuan.
- *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 趨玉; same as 促 (urgent). Zhouli, *Kaogong ji*: "if the wheel does not just barely touch the ground, there is no way to make it press on swiftly (戚速)."
- *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 昨木, sounding like 族 (zú). A county-name, in Donghai.
- *Yunbu*: rhymes as 蹙 (cù), fanqie 子六. Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the year is drawing to its close, I gather the mugwort and reap the beans; my heart is heavy with care, I have brought this grief (戚) on myself."

**Textual notes.** In Shiming, "戚，蹙也…見者蹙懼也" — checked against the *Shi bing* chapter of the Shiming, both instances of 蹙 should read 慼.
