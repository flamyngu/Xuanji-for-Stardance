**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls еҚҜ, upper; Heart radical еҝғ; p.396 #47)*

**Ancient graphs.** рЎҙң, зңҳ, гҘІ, жҳҡ.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie жҷӮеҲғ вҖ” the departing tone of иңғ (shГЁn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to be careful, circumspect (и¬№)." Shu, *Yi Ji*: "be careful (ж„ј) in your office"; the Correct Meaning: "you should be circumspect in the position you hold."
- *Also, truly, sincerely (иӘ ).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "be careful (ж„ј) of your words." Also: "be earnest (ж„ј) in your ease"; Jian: "both glossed as 'sincere.'"
- *Also* Erya, *Shigu*: "still, calm (йқң)"; subcommentary: "ж„ј is careful and calm."
- *Also* Erya, *Shigu*: "to think (жҖқ)"; subcommentary: "the Fangyan says, in Qin and Jin it is ж„ј; every look of thinking is called ж„ј."
- *Also a word of injunction.* Shiji, *Biography of Liu Pi, King of Wu*: "the emperor said to Pi, 'be sure (ж„ј) not to revolt.'"
- *Also еҸёж„ј (Sishen), the name of a heavenly spirit.* Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 11: "Sishen (еҸёж„ј) and Simeng (еҸёзӣҹ)"; note: "the two Si are names of heavenly spirits."
- *Also a surname*; the Han grandee Shen Dao (ж„јеҲ°). Also Gongshen (е…¬ж„ј), a compound surname.
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie д№ӢеҲғ, sounding like йңҮ (zhГЁn). Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "Shenyang (ж„јйҷҪ) in Runan commandery"; Ru Chun says: "pronounced йңҮ." Kan Yin says: "originally written ж»Ү; in the fifth year of Yongping the seal was lost and recut, and so the water radical was changed to the heart radical."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie дёһзңһ, sounding like иҫ° (chГ©n). Note to Zhouli, *Xiaguan, Da sima*: "a beast of five years is a ж„ј."

**On the graph.** Liushu jingyun: "the human heart has its false part; cultivate it in the bright and open, give it rein in the dark and hidden вҖ” only ж„ј (the careful) is the true heart." Xu Kai says: "a true heart (зңһеҝғ) makes ж„ј: it is not careless."
