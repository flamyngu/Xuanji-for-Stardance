**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.396 #47)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𡴜, 眘, 㥲, 昚.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 時刃 — the departing tone of 蜃 (shèn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to be careful, circumspect (謹)." Shu, *Yi Ji*: "be careful (愼) in your office"; the Correct Meaning: "you should be circumspect in the position you hold."
- *Also, truly, sincerely (誠).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "be careful (愼) of your words." Also: "be earnest (愼) in your ease"; Jian: "both glossed as 'sincere.'"
- *Also* Erya, *Shigu*: "still, calm (靜)"; subcommentary: "愼 is careful and calm."
- *Also* Erya, *Shigu*: "to think (思)"; subcommentary: "the Fangyan says, in Qin and Jin it is 愼; every look of thinking is called 愼."
- *Also a word of injunction.* Shiji, *Biography of Liu Pi, King of Wu*: "the emperor said to Pi, 'be sure (愼) not to revolt.'"
- *Also 司愼 (Sishen), the name of a heavenly spirit.* Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 11: "Sishen (司愼) and Simeng (司盟)"; note: "the two Si are names of heavenly spirits."
- *Also a surname*; the Han grandee Shen Dao (愼到). Also Gongshen (公愼), a compound surname.
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 之刃, sounding like 震 (zhèn). Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "Shenyang (愼陽) in Runan commandery"; Ru Chun says: "pronounced 震." Kan Yin says: "originally written 滇; in the fifth year of Yongping the seal was lost and recut, and so the water radical was changed to the heart radical."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 丞眞, sounding like 辰 (chén). Note to Zhouli, *Xiaguan, Da sima*: "a beast of five years is a 愼."

**On the graph.** Liushu jingyun: "the human heart has its false part; cultivate it in the bright and open, give it rein in the dark and hidden — only 愼 (the careful) is the true heart." Xu Kai says: "a true heart (眞心) makes 愼: it is not careless."
