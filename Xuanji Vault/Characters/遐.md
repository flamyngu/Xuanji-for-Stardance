## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集下〉【辵字部】頁1261第25【唐韻】胡加切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】何加切，𠀤音霞。【說文】遠也。【書·太甲】若陟遐必自邇。【楚辭·遠遊】氾容與而遐舉兮。　又與何通。【詩·小雅】遐不謂矣。又【大雅】遐不作人。　又叶寒歌切，音何。【左思·魏都賦】閑居隘巷，室邇心遐。富仁寵義，職競弗羅。　又叶洪孤切，音胡。【揚子·太𤣥經】缺船跋車，其害不遐。    考證：〔【楚辭·九章】氾容與而遐舉兮。〕　謹照原書九章改遠遊。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, lower; Walk radical 辵; p.1261 #25)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 胡加. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 何加 — sounding like 霞 (xiá).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "far (遠)." Shu, *Taijia*: "as in climbing to the far (遐), one must start from the near." Chuci, *Yuanyou*: "drifting at ease, I soar afar (遐舉)."
- *Interchangeable with 何 (why not).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "why not (遐) say so?" Also *Daya*: "why not (遐) make men [of them]?"

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 寒歌, sounding like 何 (hé).* Zuo Si, *Weidu fu*: "dwelling at ease in a narrow lane, the chamber near but the heart far (遐); rich in benevolence, prizing right, vying in duty and not ensnared."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 洪孤, sounding like 胡 (hú).* Yangzi's *Taixuan jing*: "a leaky boat, a stuck cart — the harm is not far off (不遐)."

**Textual note.** The "氾容與而遐舉" line is from the *Yuanyou* (遠遊), not the *Jiuzhang* (九章).
