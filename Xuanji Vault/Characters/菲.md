**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls з”і, upper; Grass radical иүё; p.1039 #22)*

**Pronunciation.** *Ji Yun*: fanqie еҰғе°ҫ. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie ж•·е°ҫ вҖ” sounding like ж–җ (fДӣi).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** A vegetable-name. *Shuowen*: "the wu (иҠҙ)." Shijing, *Bei feng*: "they gather the feng and gather the fei (йҮҮи‘‘йҮҮиҸІ)"; subcommentary: "Guo Pu says: the fei-herb grows in low damp ground, like the turnip, its flower purple-red, and is edible."

**Senses.**
- *Also* Lunyu: "he was sparing (иҸІ) in his own food and drink, yet did utmost filial duty to the spirits"; He Yan's note: "thin, scant (и–„)."
- *Also* Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "иҸІ means downcast and rueful (жғ„жӮө)."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie зҲ¶жІё, sounding like зӢ’ (fГЁi). The sense the same.
- *Also* interchangeable with еұқ. Liji, *Zengzi wen*: "no staff, no straw sandals (иҸІ), no mourning-hut"; note: "иҸІ is a straw sandal." Former Han, *Treatise on Punishments*: "straw sandals (иҸІеұҘ) and russet robe, unhemmed."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie иҠіеҫ®, sounding like йңҸ (fД“i). A look of luxuriant growth (иҚүиҢӮиІҢ). *Guangyun*: "fragrant and flourishing (иҠіиҸІ)."
- *Also* *Boya*: "иҸІиҸІ, fragrant (йҰҷ)." Sima Xiangru, *Shanglin fu*: "richly, fragrantly (йғҒйғҒиҸІиҸІ)."
- *Also* Yang Xiong, *Tai xuan jing*: "white and black all mingled (иҸІиҸІ)"; note: "mixed (йӣң)." Later Han, *Biography of Liang Hong*: "his will now rises, now sinks (иҸІиҸІ)"; note: "high and low, unsettled."

**Textual note.** Later Han, *Biography of Liang Hong*, "his will now rises, now sinks (еҝ—иҸІиҸІдәҺеҚҮйҷҚ)" вҖ” checked against the original, дәҺ is corrected to е…®.
