## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1039第22【集韻】妃尾切【正韻】敷尾切，𠀤音斐。菜名。【說文】芴也。【詩·邶風】采葑采菲。【疏】郭璞曰：菲草生下濕地，似蕪菁，華紫赤色，可食。　又【論語】菲飮食而致孝乎鬼神。【何晏註】薄也。　又【揚子·方言】菲惄悵也。　又【集韻】父沸切，音狒。義同。　又與屝通。【禮·曾子問】不杖、不菲、不次。【註】菲，草履。【前漢·刑法志】菲履赭衣而不純。　又【集韻】芳微切，音霏。草茂貌。【廣韻】芳菲也。　又【博雅】菲菲，香也。【司馬相如·上林賦】郁郁菲菲。　又【揚子·太𤣥經】白黑菲菲。【註】雜也。【後漢·梁鴻傳】志菲菲兮升降。【註】高下不定也。    考證：〔【後漢·梁鴻傳】志菲菲于升降。〕　謹照原文于改兮。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1039 #22)*

**Pronunciation.** *Ji Yun*: fanqie 妃尾. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 敷尾 — sounding like 斐 (fěi).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** A vegetable-name. *Shuowen*: "the wu (芴)." Shijing, *Bei feng*: "they gather the feng and gather the fei (采葑采菲)"; subcommentary: "Guo Pu says: the fei-herb grows in low damp ground, like the turnip, its flower purple-red, and is edible."

**Senses.**
- *Also* Lunyu: "he was sparing (菲) in his own food and drink, yet did utmost filial duty to the spirits"; He Yan's note: "thin, scant (薄)."
- *Also* Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "菲 means downcast and rueful (惄悵)."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 父沸, sounding like 狒 (fèi). The sense the same.
- *Also* interchangeable with 屝. Liji, *Zengzi wen*: "no staff, no straw sandals (菲), no mourning-hut"; note: "菲 is a straw sandal." Former Han, *Treatise on Punishments*: "straw sandals (菲履) and russet robe, unhemmed."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 芳微, sounding like 霏 (fēi). A look of luxuriant growth (草茂貌). *Guangyun*: "fragrant and flourishing (芳菲)."
- *Also* *Boya*: "菲菲, fragrant (香)." Sima Xiangru, *Shanglin fu*: "richly, fragrantly (郁郁菲菲)."
- *Also* Yang Xiong, *Tai xuan jing*: "white and black all mingled (菲菲)"; note: "mixed (雜)." Later Han, *Biography of Liang Hong*: "his will now rises, now sinks (菲菲)"; note: "high and low, unsettled."

**Textual note.** Later Han, *Biography of Liang Hong*, "his will now rises, now sinks (志菲菲于升降)" — checked against the original, 于 is corrected to 兮.
