> 《康熙字典》〈寅集下〉【广字部】頁346第21【唐韻】特丁切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】唐丁切，𠀤音亭。【說文】宮中也。【玉篇】庭，堂階前也。【易·節卦】不出戸庭，无咎。【周禮·天官·閽人】掌埽門庭。　又官名。【周禮·秋官】庭氏。【註】主射夭鳥，令國中淸潔如庭者也。　又【爾雅·釋詁】直也。【疏】庭條，直也。【詩·小雅】播厥百穀，旣庭且碩。　又州名。【唐書·地理志】庭州，貞觀十四年置，長安二年，爲北庭都護府，有後庭縣。【廣韻】卽漢車師後王庭之地，本烏孫國土，其前王庭，卽交河縣也。　又天庭，星名。【石氏星傳】龍星左角曰天田，右角曰天庭。　又洞庭，湖名。【楚辭·九歌】洞庭波兮木葉下。　又山名。【前漢·地理志】太湖中有包山，山下有洞庭穴道，濳行水底去，無所不通，號爲地脉。　又山庭。【任昉王文憲集序】山庭異表。【註】論語摘輔像曰：子貢山庭斗繞口，謂面有山庭，言山在中，鼻高有異相也。　又【集韻】他定切，音聽。【增韻】逕庭，隔遠貌。【莊子·逍遙遊】大有逕庭。　又叶徒陽切，音近長。【韓愈·此日足可惜詩】馳辭對我策，章句何煒煌。禮終樂亦闋，相拜送於庭。    考證：〔【周禮·秋官】庭氏。【註】主射天鳥，令國中淸潔如庭者也。〕　謹照原文天改夭。〔【韓愈·此日足可惜詩】馳辭對我策，章句何煌煌。〕　謹照原詩煌煌改煒煌。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 寅, lower; Shelter radical 广; p.346 #21)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 特丁. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 唐丁 — sounding like 亭 (tíng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "within the palace (宮中)." *Yupian*: "庭 is the area before the hall-steps." Yijing, *Jie*: "he does not go out of the door-court (戸庭) — no blame." Zhouli, *Tianguan, Hunren*: "he has charge of sweeping the gate-courtyard (門庭)."
- *Also an office-name.* Zhouli, *Qiuguan*: the Ting masters (庭氏); note: "in charge of shooting ill-omened birds, keeping the capital clean as a courtyard (庭)."
- *Also* Erya, *Shigu*: "straight (直)"; subcommentary: "庭條 means straight." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "he sows the hundred grains, both straight (庭) and great."
- *Also a prefecture-name.* Tang shu, *Treatise on Geography*: "Tingzhou (庭州), set up in the fourteenth year of Zhenguan; in the second year of Chang'an it became the Beiting Protectorate, with Houting county." *Guangyun*: "it is the land of the Han's rear royal court of Cheshi (車師後王庭), originally Wusun territory; its former royal court is the present Jiaohe county."
- *Also 天庭 (Tianting), a star-name.* Shi's *Xingzhuan*: "of the Dragon-stars, the left horn is the Heavenly Field (天田), the right horn the Heavenly Court (天庭)."
- *Also 洞庭 (Dongting), a lake-name.* Chuci, *Jiu ge*: "the waves of Dongting (洞庭), and the leaves fall."
- *Also a mountain-name.* Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "in Lake Tai is Mount Bao, beneath which is the Dongting cave-passage (洞庭穴道), running unseen along the water's bottom, reaching everywhere — called the earth's veins."
- *Also 山庭 (the mountain-court of the face).* Ren Fang, *preface to the collected works of Wang Wenxian*: "a mountain-court (山庭) of remarkable aspect"; note: "the Lunyu zhaifu xiang says, 'Zigong had a mountain-court and a dipper-encircled mouth' — it means the face had a mountain-court, that is, a mountain in the middle, the nose high, an extraordinary physiognomy."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 他定, sounding like 聽 (tìng). *Zengyun*: "逕庭 is the look of wide separation." Zhuangzi, *Xiaoyao you*: "his words were greatly at variance (逕庭)."
- *Rhymes as* fanqie 徒陽, sounding near 長 (cháng). Han Yu, *"Ci ri zu ke xi" poem*: "racing his words to answer my essay, his phrases how brilliant! The rites ended, the music too ceased, and bowing they saw me off in the courtyard (庭)."

**Textual notes.** In the Zhouli, *Qiuguan* citation, 天鳥 should read 夭鳥 (ill-omened birds), per the original. And in the Han Yu poem, 煌煌 should read 煒煌.
