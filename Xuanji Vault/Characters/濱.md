## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈巳集上〉【水字部】頁657第20    〔古文〕：𣴩、𩔤、𨽗【唐韻】必鄰切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𤰞民切，𠀤音賓。水際也。【書·禹貢】海濱廣斥。　又地近亦曰濱。【史記·貨殖傳】鄒魯濱洙泗。　又【韻會】毗賓切。與頻通。【詩·大雅】池之竭矣，不云自頻。【箋】頻作濱，厓也。　又【韻補】叶𤰞眠切，音邊。【蘇轍詩】送我出重嶺，長揖淸江濱。我老益不堪，惟有二頃田。【集韻】或作瀕。    考證：〔古文𩔤〕　謹按𣥿古涉字，中从三。今濱下引古文𩔤字，應照古涉字改偏旁爲𩔤。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.657 #20)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𣴩, 𩔤, 𨽗.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 必鄰. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 𤰞民 — sounding like 賓 (bīn).

**Etymology and senses.** "The water's edge (水際)." Shu, *Yu gong*: "the sea-shore (海濱), a broad salt-flat."
- *Also, land lying near is likewise called 濱.* Shiji, *Treatise on the Money-makers*: "Zou and Lu border (濱) the Zhu and Si."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: fanqie 毗賓; interchangeable with 頻. Shijing, *Daya*: "the pool is dried up — does it not begin from the brink (頻)?"; Jian: "頻 is written 濱, the bank (厓)."
- *Rhymes* (per the *Yunbu*) as fanqie 𤰞眠, sounding like 邊 (biān). Su Zhe's poem: "they see me out past the layered ridges, with a long bow at the clear river's edge (濱); I, grown old and ever less able, have only two qing of field."

**On the graph.** *Ji Yun*: sometimes written 瀕.

**Textual notes.** A note on the ancient graph: since 𣥿 is the old form of 涉 (to ford), with three strokes in the middle, the ancient graph cited here under 濱 should have its radical-element corrected to match the old 涉 form.
