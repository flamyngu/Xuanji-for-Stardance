## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈戌集下〉【頁字部】頁1405第18    〔古文〕：𩔤【唐韻】符眞切【集韻】【韻會】毗賔切，𠀤音顰。【玉篇】急也。【廣雅】比也。【詩·大雅】國步斯頻。【傳】頻，急也。【箋】頻猶比也。國家之政，行此禍害比比然。　又姓。【風俗通】漢有酒泉太守頻暢。　又【說文】水厓。【徐鉉曰】今俗別作水濵，非是。【詩·大雅】池之竭矣，不云自頻。【傳】頻，厓也。【箋】頻，當作濵。　又與顰同。【易·復卦】頻復厲无咎。【註】謂頻蹙之貌。　又果名。【前漢·司馬相如·上林賦】仁頻，幷閭。【註】仁頻，賔榔也。頻或作賔。    考證：〔【詩·大雅】地之竭矣，不云自頻。〕　謹照原文地改池。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 戌, lower; Head radical 頁; p.1405 #18)*

**Ancient graph.** 𩔤.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 符眞. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 毗賔 — sounding like 顰 (pín).

**Etymology and senses.** *Yupian*: "urgent (急)." *Guangya*: "in close succession (比)." Shijing, *Daya*: "the state's pace is thus pressed (頻)"; tradition: "頻 means urgent"; Jian: "頻 is like in close succession — the state's governance brings these disasters one after another."

**Senses.**
- *Surname.* *Fengsu tong*: "the Han had Pin Chang, Grand Administrator of Jiuquan."
- *Also* *Shuowen*: "a water's bank (水厓)." Xu Xuan: "the common writing 水濵 is wrong." Shijing, *Daya*: "the pool is dried up — does it not start from the bank (頻)?"; tradition: "頻 is a bank"; Jian: "頻 should be 濵."
- *Same as 顰 (to knit the brows).* Yijing, *Fu* hexagram: "knitted return, perilous but no fault (頻復)"; note: "it means a frowning look (頻蹙)."
- *A fruit-name.* Former Han, Sima Xiangru, *Shanglin fu*: "the renpin (仁頻) and the bing-lü"; note: "renpin is the betel-palm (賔榔); 頻 is sometimes written 賔."

**Textual note.** Shijing, *Daya*, "the earth (地) is dried up, it starts not from the bank" — checked against the original, "地" should read "池" (pool).
