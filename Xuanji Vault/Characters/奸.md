## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集下〉【女字部】頁255第04【唐韻】古寒切【集韻】【韻會】居寒切，𠀤音干。【說文】犯也。【增韻】犯非禮也。【玉篇】亂也。【左傳·成十三年】奸絕我好。【前漢·溝洫志】使神人各得其所，而不相奸。　又【集韻】居顏切，音菅。私也，僞也。　又與干通。【莊子·天運篇】以奸者七十二君。【史記·齊世家】尚以漁釣奸周。　又讀若堅。【史記·龜筴傳】寒氣不和，賊氣相奸。同歲異節，其時使然。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, lower; Woman radical 女; p.255 #04)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 古寒. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 居寒 — sounding like 干 (gān).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "To offend, violate (犯)." *Zengyun*: "to offend against propriety (犯非禮)." *Yupian*: "disorder (亂)." Zuozhuan, Cheng yr 13: "you violated and cut off (奸絕) our goodwill." Former Han, *Treatise on Ditches and Canals*: "to let gods and men each gain their place, and not encroach (奸) on one another."

**Senses.**
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 居顏, sounding like 菅 (jiān). "Private (私); false (僞)."
- *Interchangeable with 干 (to seek, intrude on).* Zhuangzi, *Tian yun*: "he sought office of (奸) seventy-two lords." Shiji, *Hereditary House of Qi*: "Shang sought out (奸) Zhou by his fishing."
- *Read like 堅 (jiān).* Shiji, *Biography of the Tortoise and Milfoil*: "when the cold breath is out of harmony, the noxious breath encroaches (奸) on it; the same year but a different season — its timing makes it so."
