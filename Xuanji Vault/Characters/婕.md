## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集下〉【女字部】頁264第28【廣韻】卽葉切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】卽涉切，𠀤音接。婕妤，婦官。【張衡·西京賦】增昭儀于婕妤。　又【集韻】七接切，音妾。又疾葉切，音捷。𠀤美貌。【漢書】作倢伃。義詳妤字註。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, lower; Woman radical 女; p.264 #28)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 卽葉. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 卽涉 — sounding like 接 (jié).

**Etymology and senses.** 婕妤 (*jieyu*), a female office. Zhang Heng, *Fu on the Western Capital*: "he added the Zhaoyi rank above the *jieyu* (婕妤)."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 七接, sounding like 妾 (qiè); also fanqie 疾葉, sounding like 捷 (jié) — both "a comely look." The Han shu writes it 倢伃. For the sense, see the note under [[妤]].
