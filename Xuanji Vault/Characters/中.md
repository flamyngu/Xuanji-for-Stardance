**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, upper; Line radical 丨; p.79 #03)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𠁧, 𠁩.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 陟弓. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 陟隆 — sounding like 忠 (zhōng).

**Etymology and senses.** Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "sincerely hold to the Mean (中)." Zhouli, *Diguan, Da situ*: "by the five rites he guards against the people's falsehood and teaches them the mean (中)." Zuozhuan, Cheng yr 13: "Master Liu said, 'the people receive the equilibrium (中) of Heaven and Earth to live.'"
- *The mid-month [solar] term.* Zuozhuan, Wen yr 1: "fixing [the calendar] true by the mid-points (中), the people are not confused"; note: "taking the mid-month terms (中氣)."
- *司中, a star in the Taiwei enclosure.* Zhouli, *Chunguan, Da zongbo*: "with the blazing pyre he sacrifices to Sizhong, Siming, the Wind-Master, and the Rain-Master."
- *The yang-mid and yin-mid.* Former Han, *Treatise on the Pitch-pipes and Calendar*: "spring is the yang-mid (陽中), and the myriad things are born; autumn the yin-mid (隂中), and they are completed."
- *中央, the centre of the four directions.* Shu, *Zhaogao*: "the king came to succeed God on high, and took his place at the land's centre (土中)"; note: "Luo is the centre of Heaven and Earth." Zhang Heng, *Dongjing fu*: "dwelling at the centre (中), round and great."
- *Right, correct (正).* Liji, *Ruxing*: "the scholar's dress and cap are correct (中)." Zhouli, *Qiuguan, Sici*: "by these three laws he seeks the people's truth and judges them aright (中)"; note: "judging guilt so that light and heavy hit the mean."
- *The heart, inward (心).* Shiji, *Biography of Han Anguo*: "deep within (中), generous and kind."
- *Inside (內).* Yijing, *Kun*: "yellow skirt, supremely auspicious — the pattern lies within (中)." Laozi, *Daodejing*: "much speech soon fails; better keep to the centre (中)."
- *Half (半).* Liezi, *Liming*: "to gain is halfway (中), to lose is halfway too." Weizhi, *Biography of Guan Lu*: "one drum-watch half through (鼓一中)"; note: "like saying half the drum[-watch]."
- *Completion (成).* Liji, *Liqi*: "from the famous mountain he sent up the report of completion (中) to Heaven"; note: "中 is like 'completion'; burning faggots to sacrifice to Heaven, reporting the lords' achievements."
- *Full (滿).* Former Han, *Table of Officials*: "ranked at a full (中) two-thousand *shi*"; note: "a full two-thousand *shi*"; Suoyin: "by the Han system, for the nine ministers and above, the yearly stipend is a full two-thousand *shi*."
- *To pierce through (穿).* Zhouli, *Dongguan kaogong ji*: "pierce (中) its shaft"; note: "to bore through it."
- *A counter-holder.* Liji, *Touhu*: "the host bears the arrows, the Master of Archery bears the counter-holder (中)"; note: "the gentleman's is a deer-counter, the grandee's a rhino-counter, wood carved as a crouching rhino or deer, with an upright ring on its back to hold the rods."
- *中衣, an inner robe.* Note to Liji, *Shenyi*: "a garment with an outer is called the 中衣"; interchangeable with 衷.
- *不中, "not allowable" (colloquial).* The Xiao Can Xitong lu cites Zuozhuan Cheng 2, "unable to serve"; Du Yu's note: "not fit (不中) to be made to serve."
- *Winter, to store.* Liji, *Xiang yinjiu yi*: "winter (冬) means 中 (within); 中 is to store away."
- *A surname.* The Han had Zhong Jing, Minister of the Privy Treasury. Also Zhonghang, Zhongying, Zhongliang, Zhonglei, Zhongye, all compound surnames.

**Departing tone (zhòng).** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 陟仲, sounding like 妕 (zhòng). "An arrow reaching the mark is 中 (to hit)."
- *To hit the mark.* Shiji, *Annals of Zhou*: "Yang Youji, a hundred paces from the willow-leaf, shot it — a hundred shots, a hundred hits (中)."
- *To be struck within.* Zhuangzi, *Dasheng*: "struck (中) in the body at the heart, one falls ill" — as the medical books say 'struck by wind (中風)' or 'struck by heat (中暑).'"
- *The essential, the gist (要).* Zhouli, *Chunguan*: "the governing records (治中) of the offices, districts, and towns he receives and stores"; note: "the essentials of the administrative registers."
- *To answer to (應).* Liji, *Yueling*: "the pitch answers to (中) Taicu"; note: "中 is like 'to answer.'"
- *To suit, hit (合).* Zuozhuan, Ding yr 1: "Jisun said, 'Zijiazi has often spoken to me, and never failed to hit (中) my wish.'"
- *Interchangeable with 仲.* *Leipian*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 直衆. Liji, *Yueling*: "Zhonglü (中呂) is Zhonglü (仲呂)."
- *Read as 得.* Zhouli, *Diguan*: "the Preceptor is in charge of the state's gains (得) and losses"; note: "the old text writes 中 for 得"; Lu Deming: "中, Du['s] reading is 得."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 陟良, sounding like 章 (zhāng).* Yan Shigu: "anciently 中 was read 章." Wuzhi, Hu Zong, *Yellow-Dragon Great-Banner fu*: "...the Yellow Dragon dwells at the centre (中); ringed with sun and moon, it is called the Taichang."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 諸仍, sounding like 征 (zhēng).* The Liu Gongfu Shihua: "in Guanzhong 中 is read 𤇏." Shijing, *Daya*: "the spring runs dry — does it not [fail] from within (中)?" (rhyming above with 頻). Ban Gu, *Stele of the Sishui Pavilion of Gaozu*: "by Heaven's term and fortune he received rank in Hanzhong (漢中)" (rhyming below with 秦). Anciently the *dong* rhyme-group was interchangeable with *geng* and *yang*.

**Editorial note.** A note: by the colloquial reading, 中 in 中酒 (drunk) is the departing tone, in 中與 the level tone. The Weizhi, *Biography of Xu Miao*: Miao, in a time of prohibition, drank in secret to drunkenness, and when asked of state affairs said "I am [with] the sage (中聖人)" — clear wine being then called 'the sage,' the muddy 'the worthy'; Su Shi's poem, "you only do not yet know its savour; I now again now and then 'hit' (中) it" — so 中酒 may also be read level. The Tongjian: "King Xuan of Zhou won the name of 中與 (restoration)"; note: "to be equal to it (當)"; Du Fu: "this morning the Han altars newly reckon the year of 中與" — so 中與 may also be read departing.

**Textual notes.** "班固·東都賦 宅中圓大" should be "張衡·東京賦"; the Sici citation is from the 秋官, not 春官, and reads "以此三法者求民情"; "美在中也" should be "文在中也"; "子家" should add 子; "周禮·天官" should be "春官".
