## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈午集上〉【玉字部】頁741第05【廣韻】似宣切【集韻】旬宣切，𠀤音旋。【廣韻】同琁。【山海經】王母之山，有璇瑰瑤碧。【註】璇瑰，亦玉名。　又星名。【史記·天官書北斗七星註】春秋運斗極云：斗第二璇。　又【揚子·方言】簙謂之蔽。或謂之𠥙璇。【註】竹器所以整頓簿者。　又人名。【後漢·楊璇傳】楊璇字機平。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, upper; Jade radical 玉; p.741 #05)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 似宣. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 旬宣 — sounding like 旋 (xuán).

**Etymology and senses.** *Guangyun*: "the same as 琁." Shanhaijing: "the Mountain of the Queen Mother has 璇瑰, 瑤, and 碧 [fine jades]"; note: "璇瑰 is also a jade-name."
- *A star-name.* Shiji, *Treatise on the Heavenly Offices*, note to the Seven Stars of the Northern Dipper: "the Chunqiu yundouji says: the second star of the Dipper is Xuan (璇)."
- *A gaming implement.* Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "the gaming-board (簙) is called 蔽; or it is called *qiongxuan* (𠥙璇)"; note: "a bamboo implement, used to set the gaming-pieces in order."
- *A personal name.* Later Han, *Biography of Yang Xuan*: "Yang Xuan (楊璇), styled Jiping."
