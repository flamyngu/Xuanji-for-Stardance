> 《康熙字典》〈丑集下〉【女字部】頁254第22    〔古文〕：𠨰【唐韻】【正韻】尼呂切【集韻】【韻會】碾與切，𠀤茹上聲。【博雅】女，如也。言如男子之敎，人之陽曰男，隂曰女。【易·繫辭】坤道成女。【詩·小雅】乃生女子，載寢之地，載衣之裼，載弄之瓦。【禮·內則】女子生，設帨於門右。【淮南子·地形訓】土地各以類生，澤氣生女。　又已嫁曰婦，未字曰女。【禮·曾子問】嫁女之家，三夜不息燭，思相離也。　又星名。須女四星，主布帛，爲珍寶藏，一名婺女。十二月旦在北，二月旦中。又織女三星，天女也，主苽果絲帛，𠀤見【星經】。　又水名。【山海經】嶧臯之山，其水出焉，東流注于激女之水。　又𤣥女，九天妃也。黃帝與蚩尤戰，不勝，歸太山，遇一婦人，曰：吾所謂𤣥女者。見【𤣥女戰法】。　又金女，西王母也，厥姓緱氏，見【集仙錄】。　又靑女，霜神也，至秋三月地氣下藏，靑女乃出，以降霜雪，見【淮南子·天文訓】。　又奼女，丹汞也。河上奼女，得火則飛，見【參同契】。　又國名。女子國，在巫咸北。【郭璞·圖贊】女子之國，浴于黃水。乃媷乃字，生男則死。又東女國，女主號賔就。見【唐書·西域傳】。又扶桑東千里有女國，其人容貌端正，身體有毛，見【通考】。　又姓。湯賢臣女鳩，女房，漢賢良女敦，晉大夫女叔寬。　又梵言阿摩，此言女，言母。蘇弗室利，此言善女。　又鸚䳇名雪衣女，見【漢武外傳】。鵲名神女，見【古今注】。蜆名縊女，見【爾雅·釋蟲】。螟蛉名戎女，見【毛詩·註疏】。　又【集韻】【韻會】𠀤忍與切。同汝，對我之稱。　又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤尼據切，茹去聲。以女妻人曰女。【書·堯典】女于時。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, lower; Woman radical 女; p.254 #22)*

**Ancient graph.** 𠨰.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 尼呂. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 碾與 — the rising tone of 茹, i.e. **nǚ**.

**Senses.** *Boya*: "女 is 如 ('like' / 'accordingly') — as a man is instructed; a person's *yang* is male (男), the *yin* female (女)." Yijing, *Xici*: "the way of Kun forms the female (女)." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "and so a girl is born, laid upon the ground, clad in a wrapper, given a tile to play with." Liji, *Nei ze*: "when a girl is born, a kerchief is hung at the right of the door." Huainanzi, *Dixing xun*: "each land begets by kind; marsh-*qi* begets females."
- *Married, she is 婦; unbetrothed, 女.* Liji, *Zengzi wen*: "the house marrying off a daughter does not put out the lamp for three nights, mindful of the parting."
- *Star names.* 須女 (the Maiden), four stars, governing cloth and silk and the treasure-store, also called 婺女; at dawn in the 12th month it lies north, in the 2nd month at the meridian. Also 織女 (the Weaving Maiden), three stars, the Heavenly Maiden, governing melons, fruit, and silk (Xingjing).
- *A river.* Shanhaijing: from Mount Yigao the waters issue, flowing east into the 激女 waters.
- *𤣥女, the Dark Maiden,* consort of the Nine Heavens. When the Yellow Emperor fought Chiyou and could not prevail, he withdrew to Mount Tai and met a woman who said, "I am she they call the Dark Maiden" (Xuannü zhanfa).
- *金女, the Golden Maiden* — the Queen Mother of the West, surnamed Gou (Jixian lu).
- *靑女, the Azure Maiden,* goddess of frost; in autumn's last three months, when the earth-*qi* sinks and stores, she comes forth to send down frost and snow (Huainanzi, *Tianwen xun*).
- *奼女, the Maiden* — in alchemy, cinnabar-mercury: "the River-side Maiden, given fire, flies off" (Cantong qi).
- *A state.* 女子國 (Land of Women), north of Wuxian. Guo Pu, *Tuzan*: "the Land of Women bathe in the Yellow Water; they weed and conceive; if a boy is born he dies." Also 東女國 (Eastern Women's State), queen titled Binjiu (Tang shu, *Western Regions*). A thousand *li* east of Fusang is another Women's State, its people comely but hairy-bodied (Tongkao).
- *Surname.* The Tang worthies 女鳩, 女房; the Han worthy 女敦; the Jin grandee 女叔寬.
- *Sanskrit.* 阿摩 means "woman / mother"; 蘇弗室利 means "good woman."
- *Animal names.* A parrot named 雪衣女 (Snow-robe Maiden) (Han Wu waizhuan); a magpie named 神女 (Gujin zhu); a clam-creature named 縊女 (Erya, *Shi chong*); a caterpillar named 戎女 (Maoshi commentary).
- *= 汝 ("you").* *Ji Yun, Yun Hui*: fanqie 忍與 — same as 汝, the term for "you" set against "I (我)."
- *Departing tone (to marry off).* *Guangyun, Ji Yun, Yun Hui*: fanqie 尼據, departing tone of 茹 — "to give a woman in marriage is 女." Shu, *Yao dian*: "he gave [his daughters] in marriage at that time (女于時)."
