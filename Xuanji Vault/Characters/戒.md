**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls еҚҜ, middle; Halberd radical жҲҲ; p.412 #05)*

**Ancient graph.** рўҰ¬.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie еҸӨжӢң. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie еұ…жӢң вҖ” sounding like д»Ӣ (jiГЁ).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to be on guard, to warn (иӯҰ)." Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "be watchful and wary (иӯҰжҲ’) when there is no apparent cause."
- *Also, to admonish, to instruct (и«ӯ).* Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "admonish (жҲ’) them with the promise of blessing."
- *Also, to inform, to notify (е‘Ҡ).* Yili, *Shi guan li*: "the host notifies (жҲ’) the guests"; note: "to inform." *Pin li*: "he notifies (жҲ’) the chief assistant likewise"; note: "like a command."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "to be careful (ж„ј); to make ready (е…·)." Also: to provide against (еӮҷ). Yijing, *Cui*: "guard (жҲ’) against the unforeseen"; note: "to provide against the unforeseen."
- *Also* Yijing, *Xici*: "the sage thereby purifies and warns himself (йҪҠжҲ’)"; note: "to cleanse the heart is йҪҠ, to guard against trouble is жҲ’"; Zhu Xi's *Benyi*: "limpid and wholly single is called йҪҠ, solemnly watchful and wary is called жҲ’."
- *Also, to keep guard (е®Ҳ).* Zhouli, *Xiaguan, Zhanggu*: "by night, the three watch-drums to call the guard (иҷҹжҲ’)"; note: "it means beating the drum on the night-rounds to keep watch." Also Sima fa: "the drum sounded three rolls at midnight is called the dawn-watch (жҷЁжҲ’)."
- *Also interchangeable with иӘЎ.* Yijing, *Xici*: "a small chastening and a great warning (иӘЎ)." Former Han, *Biography of Jia Yi*: "the front cart overturns, the rear cart takes warning (иӘЎ)."
- *Also the same as з•Ң (boundary).* Shiji, *Tianguan shu*: "a comet at the River's bound (жІіжҲ’)." Also Tang shu, *Treatise on Astronomy*: "the Yangtze and the Yellow River make the two bounds, north and south (е…©жҲ’)."
- *Rhymes* (per the *Yunbu*) as fanqie еұ…еҗҸ, sounding like иЁҳ (jГ¬). Liutao: "if the general is not ever on guard (жҲ’), then the three armies lose their readiness."
- *Rhymes as* fanqie зҙҖеҠӣ, sounding like дәҹ (jГӯ). Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "are we not daily on guard (жҲ’)? the Xianyun press hard."

**Textual notes.** In the Yijing, *Xici* citation, йҪӢ should read йҪҠ; and in the Sima fa citation, зҷјжҲ’ should read жҷЁжҲ’.
