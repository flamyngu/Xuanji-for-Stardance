**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集上〉【口字部】頁188第11【唐韻】烏開切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】於開切，𠀤音唉。【說文】閔也。【玉篇】哀傷也。【書·大誥】允蠢鰥寡哀哉。【詩·豳風】哀我人斯。　又【爾雅·釋訓】哀哀悽悽，懷報德也。【詩·小雅】哀哀父母。　又憐也，愛也。【呂氏春秋】人主胡可以不務哀士。　又【莊子·德充符】衞有惡人焉，曰哀駘它。【註】哀駘，醜貌；它，其名。　又【諡法】恭仁短折曰哀。　又姓。【風俗通】魯哀以後，因諡爲姓。【前漢·王莽傳】梓潼人哀章。【姓譜】宋有哀長吉。【正字通】明嘉靖進士哀貞，上命攺哀爲衷。　又國名。【後漢·南蠻傳】哀牢人皆穿鼻儋耳。　又叶於希切，音衣。【詩·小雅】行道遲遲，載渴載饑。我心傷悲，莫知我哀。【又】山有蕨薇，隰有𣏌桋。君子作歌，維以告哀。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, upper; Mouth radical 口; p.188 #11)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 烏開. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 於開 — sounding like 唉 (āi).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to grieve, pity (閔)." *Yupian*: "哀 is sorrow." Shu, *Dagao*: "truly are the widowed and orphaned stirred to grief (哀)!" Shijing, *Binfeng*: "pity (哀) us, these men."
- *哀哀, cherishing the wish to requite kindness.* *Erya, Shixun*: "哀哀 and 悽悽 [express] cherishing the wish to requite kindness." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "alas, alas (哀哀) for my father and mother."
- *To pity, to love.* Lüshi chunqiu: "how can a ruler fail to make it his care to cherish (哀) his gentlemen?"
- *A personal name.* Zhuangzi, *Dechongfu*: "in Wey there was an ugly man named Ai Tai Tuo (哀駘它)"; note: "哀駘 is the look of ugliness, 它 his name."
- *A posthumous title.* "Reverent and kind, cut off young" is titled 哀 (the Pitiable).
- *A surname.* Fengsu tong: "after Duke Ai of Lu, [his line] took the posthumous title as surname." Former Han, *Biography of Wang Mang*: "Ai Zhang, a man of Zitong." Xingpu: "the Song had Ai Changji." Zhengzitong: "the Ming Jiajing-era graduate Ai Zhen — the Emperor ordered 哀 changed to 衷."
- *A state-name.* Later Han, *Account of the Southern Man*: "the Ailao (哀牢) people all pierce their noses and stretch their ears."

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 於希, sounding like 衣 (yī).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the road I walk is long and slow, now thirsty and now famished; my heart is wounded with grief, and none knows my sorrow (哀)." Also: "on the hill are bracken and osmund, in the marsh the medlar and *yi*; the gentleman makes this song only to tell his sorrow (哀).
