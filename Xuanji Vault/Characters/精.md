**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls жңӘ, upper; Rice radical зұі; p.910 #10)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie еӯҗзӣҲ. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie е’ЁзӣҲ вҖ” sounding like жҷ¶ (jД«ng).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "To select, sift (ж“Ү)." *Guangyun*: "refined (зҶҹ); fine (зҙ°); single, undivided (е°ҲдёҖ)." Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "be refined (зІҫ), be single." Yijing, *Xi ci*: "to refine (зІҫ) the sense to the point of the divine, and so bring it to use."

**Senses.**

- *Numinous; the true vital force (йқҲ, зңһж°Ј).* Yijing, *Xi ci*: "the refined force (зІҫж°Ј) makes things"; subcommentary: "the numinous force of yin and yang, dense and gathering, becomes the myriad things." Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 7: "Zichan said: 'when the things used are refined and many (зІҫеӨҡ), then the soul is strong, and so there is keen spirit (зІҫзҲҪ) reaching to the divine and bright.'" Also (Zuozhuan,) Zhuang yr 25, subcommentary on the eclipse: "the sun is the yang-essence (йҷҪзІҫ), the moon the yin-essence (йҡӮзІҫ)." Also Xiang yr 28, subcommentary on "spring without water": "the Five Stars are the essences (зІҫ) of the Five Phases вҖ” the wood-essence is the Year-star (Jupiter), the fire-essence is Yinghuo (Mars), the earth-essence is the Quelling-star (Saturn), the metal-essence is Taibai (Venus), the water-essence is the Chen-star (Mercury)."
