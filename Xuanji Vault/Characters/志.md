> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁376第21    〔古文〕：𢗍【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】職吏切【正韻】支義切，𠀤音鋕。【說文】从心之聲。志者，心之所之也。【論語】志於道。【詩序】在心爲志。　又【廣韻】意慕也。【儀禮·大射儀】不以樂志。【註】志者，意所擬度也。【禮·少儀】問卜筮曰：義歟，志歟。義則可問，志則否。【註】義，正事也。志，私意也。　又準志也。【書·盤庚】若射之有志。【疏】如射之有所準志，志之所主，欲得中也。　又章志也。【禮·檀弓】孔子之喪，公西赤爲志焉。子張之喪，公明儀爲志焉。【疏】故爲盛禮，以章明志識也。　又本志也。【左傳·襄元年】謂之宋志。【註】言宋本志，在攻取彭城也。　又【左傳·昭二十五年】以制六志。【註】爲禮，以制好惡喜怒哀樂六志。又記也。與誌同。或作識。【周禮·春官】小史掌邦國之志。【前漢書】有十志。【師古曰】志，記也。積記其事也。【後漢·劉駿傳】博見彊志。　又【集韻】昌志切。與幟通。旗也。【史記·張丞相傳】沛公以周昌爲職志。　又箭鏃也。【爾雅·釋器】金鏃翦羽謂之鍭，骨鏃不翦羽謂之志。【註】鏃，今之錍箭。志，今之骨骲。　又叶眞而切，音支。【楚辭·九章】昔君與我成言兮，曰黃昏以爲期。羌中道而回畔兮，反旣有此他志。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.376 #21)*

**Ancient graph.** 𢗍.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 職吏. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 支義 — sounding like 鋕 (zhì).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "From 心 (heart) with 之 as sound. 志 is that toward which the heart goes (心之所之)." Lunyu: "set the will (志) on the Way." Preface to the *Shijing*: "in the heart it is intent (志)."

**Senses.**
- *To long for (意慕).* *Guangyun.* Yili, *Da she yi*: "不以樂志"; note: "志 is what the intent gauges and aims at." Liji, *Shaoyi*: "in divining one asks, 'is it duty (義) or private wish (志)?' Duty may be asked, private wish may not"; note: "義, a proper matter; 志, private intent."
- *To aim at a mark (準志).* Shu, *Pan geng*: "like an archer having his aim (志)"; subcommentary: "as archery has its target — what the intent fixes on is to hit the centre."
- *To make manifest (章志).* Liji, *Tan gong*: "at Confucius's funeral Gongxi Chi made the display (志); at Zizhang's, Gongming Yi made the display (志)"; subcommentary: "they made grand rites, to make manifest the marker and intent."
- *The original intent (本志).* Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 1: "this is called Song's intent (志)"; note: "Song's original intent was to attack and take Pengcheng."
- *The six dispositions.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 25: "to regulate the six 志"; note: "by rites to govern the six dispositions — liking, disliking, joy, anger, grief, delight."
- *To record (記); same as 誌, sometimes written 識.* Zhouli, *Chunguan*: "the Lesser Scribe manages the records (志) of the states." Former Han: "has ten Treatises (志)"; Yan Shigu: "志, to record — accumulating a record of events." Hou Han, *Biography of Liu Jun*: "widely seen and strongly remembering (彊志)."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 昌志 — same as 幟 (a banner). Shiji, *Biography of Zhou Chang*: "the Lord of Pei made Zhou Chang his banner-officer (職志)."
- *An arrowhead.* Erya, *Shi qi*: "a metal head with trimmed feathers is a 鍭; a bone head with untrimmed feathers is a 志"; note: "志 is today's bone-tipped arrow."
- *Rhymes as 支 (zhī), fanqie 眞而.* Chuci, *Jiu zhang*: "of old you and I made our vow, that dusk should be the time; but midway you turned away, having now this other intent (志)."
