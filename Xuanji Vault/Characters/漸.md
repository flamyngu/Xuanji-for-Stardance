## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈巳集上〉【水字部】頁647第14【唐韻】慈冉切【集韻】【韻會】疾染切【正韻】秦冉切，𠀤𥕌上聲。水名。【說文】水出丹陽黟南蠻中，東入海。　又漸次也，進也，稍也，事之端先覩之始也。【易·漸卦正義】漸者，不速之名。凡物有變移，徐而不速，謂之漸。　又通作蔪。【書·禹貢】草木漸包。【釋文】如字，本又作蔪。【字林】才冉反，草之相包裹也。　又【集韻】鋤銜切，音鑱。與巉通。高也。或作嶄。【詩·小雅】漸漸之石，維其高矣。　又側銜切，音𪗂。流貌。【楚辭·九章】涕漸漸兮。　又【廣韻】子廉切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】將廉切，𠀤音尖。流入也。【書·禹貢】東漸于海。　又漬也，濕也。【詩·衞風】漸車帷裳。　又浸也，染也。【前漢·董仲舒傳】漸民以仁。　又臺名。【水經注】太液池中有漸臺，三十丈。　又星名。【甘氏星經】漸臺四星，屬織女東足。　又【集韻】子艷切，音𡄑。漸洳，溼貌也。　又之列切，音折。同浙。江名。　又【類篇】慈鹽切，音潛。涉水也。與潛通。【書·洪範】沉潛剛克。【左傳】【史記】皆作沉漸。    考證：〔通作蔪。【書·禹貢】草木漸包。【傳】如字，本又作蔪。〕　謹照原書傳字改釋文。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.647 #14)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 慈冉. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 疾染. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 秦冉 — the rising tone of 𥕌 (jiàn). A river-name. *Shuowen*: "a river that issues from among the Man tribes south of Yi in Danyang, and flows east into the sea."

**Senses.**
- *Gradual, by degrees (漸次); to advance (進); little by little (稍) — the start of a thing's sprouting, first glimpsed.* Yijing, *Jian* hexagram, *Correct Meaning*: "漸 is a name for the unhurried. Whenever a thing changes and shifts slowly and not fast, it is called 漸."
- *Interchangeable with 蔪.* Shu, *Yu gong*: "the grasses and trees grow thick and wrapping (漸包)"; *Shiwen*: "read as written, also written 蔪"; *Zilin*: "fanqie 才冉, grasses wrapping one another."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 鋤銜, sounding like 鑱 (chán) — same as 巉, "high"; also written 嶄. Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "craggy and high (漸漸) the rocks, how lofty they are."
- *Also* fanqie 側銜, sounding like 𪗂 (zhàn). A flowing look. Chuci, *Jiu zhang*: "my tears flow streaming down (漸漸)."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: fanqie 子廉. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 將廉 — sounding like 尖 (jiān). "To flow into (流入)." Shu, *Yu gong*: "in the east it reaches into the sea (東漸于海)."
- *To soak; to wet (漬, 濕).* Shijing, *Weifeng*: "wetting (漸) the carriage-curtains."
- *To steep; to dye, suffuse (浸, 染).* Former Han, *Biography of Dong Zhongshu*: "to steep the people in benevolence (漸民以仁)."
- *A terrace-name.* Shuijing zhu: "in the Taiye pond there is the Jian Terrace (漸臺), thirty zhang high."
- *A star-name.* Gan's *Star Canon*: "the Jian-tai (漸臺), four stars, belong to the east foot of the Weaving-Maid."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 子艷, sounding like 𡄑 (jiàn). 漸洳, a damp look.
- *Also* fanqie 之列, sounding like 折 (zhé) — same as 浙, a river-name.
- *Also* *Leipian*: fanqie 慈鹽, sounding like 潛 (qián). "To wade water (涉水)" — interchangeable with 潛. Shu, *Hong fan*: "the sunk and submerged (沉潛) is mastered by firmness"; the Zuozhuan and Shiji both write 沉漸.

**Textual note.** "Interchangeable with 蔪. Shu, *Yu gong*, 'the grasses and trees grow thick and wrapping,' tradition (傳): 'read as written, also written 蔪'" — checked against the original work, "tradition (傳)" should read "*Shiwen* (釋文)."
