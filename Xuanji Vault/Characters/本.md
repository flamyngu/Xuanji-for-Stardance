## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集中〉【木字部】頁509第07    〔古文〕：楍、㮺、𡴡【唐韻】【正韻】布忖切【集韻】【韻會】補衮切，𠀤奔上聲。【說文】木下曰本。从木，一在其下，草木之根柢也。【左傳·昭元年】木水之有本原。【班固·西都賦】元元本本，殫見洽聞。　又【玉篇】始也。　又【廣韻】舊也，下也。【禮·禮器】反本修古，不忘其初。【爾雅·釋器疏】柢本也，凡物之本，必在底下。　又【左傳註】豫爲後地曰張本。　又【曲禮】韭曰豐本，菖蒲根曰昌本。　又【集韻】【類篇】𠀤逋昆切。同奔。喻德宣譽曰本走。　又叶方典切，音匾。【班婕妤·擣素賦】調非常律，聲無定本。或連躍而更投，或暫舒而常斂。    考證：〔【曲禮註】韭曰豐本。〕　謹按韭曰豐本係經文，非註文，謹省註字。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, middle; Wood radical 木; p.509 #07)*

**Ancient graphs.** 楍, 㮺, 𡴡.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 布忖. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 補衮 — the rising tone of 奔 (běn).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "The lower part of a tree is 本; it follows 木 (tree) with 一 below it — the root and base of plants and trees." Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 1: "as trees and water have their root and source (本原)." Ban Gu, *Xidu fu*: "primal and rooted (元元本本), exhausting all sight and broad in hearing."

**Senses.**
- *The beginning (始).* *Yupian*.
- *Old; low (舊, 下).* *Guangyun*. Liji, *Li qi*: "returning to the root (本) and following the old, not forgetting one's origin." Erya, *Shi qi*, subcommentary: "柢 is the root (本); the root of any thing must be at the bottom."
- *Also* Zuozhuan note: "to lay the ground for what comes after is called 張本 (laying a foundation)."
- *Also* Liji, *Qu li*: "leeks are called 豐本 (rich-rooted); the sweet-flag root is called 昌本."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*, *Leipian*: fanqie 逋昆 — same as 奔 (to run). To extol virtue and proclaim fame is 本走.

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 匾 (biǎn), fanqie 方典.* Ban Jieyu, *Daosu fu*: "the tuning not by the usual measure, the sound without fixed key (本); now linked it leaps and is cast anew, now briefly spread and ever drawn in."

**Textual note.** "Liji, *Qu li*, note: leeks are called 豐本" — a note: "leeks are called 豐本" is the classic's text, not a note; the word 註 (note) is dropped.
