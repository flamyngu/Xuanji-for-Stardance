**Original entry (verbatim):**

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 寅, upper; Child radical 子; p.278 #16)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 莫更, sounding like 夢 (mèng).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "The eldest (長)." *Li wei*: "the eldest of the principal wife is 伯, the eldest of a concubine is 孟." Shu, *Kang gao*: "the king spoke thus: 'Senior Prince (孟侯), my younger brother, little one Feng.'" *Shu zhuan*: "the Son of Heaven's son, at eighteen, is styled Senior Prince (孟侯)." Also, a woman's elder brother is likewise called 孟.

**Senses.**
- *The first, the beginning (始).* *Yupian*. The first month of each of the four seasons is the 孟 month. Former Han, *Biography of Li Xun*: "the 寅, the 孟 month."
- *To strive, exert (勉).* *Guangyun*. Ban Gu, *You tong fu*: "why not strive and advance (孟晉) to reach the throng? the hour flits past and comes not twice"; note: "孟晉 means to strive and press forward."
- *Great (大).* Guanzi, *Ren fa*: "lofty words and great deeds (孟行), exceeding one's true nature."
- *A prefecture-name*, the Han Henei commandery, the ancient Mengjin (孟津); the Tang set up Mengzhou (孟州).
- *Also* Shu, *Yu gong*: "covering Mengzhu (孟豬)"; the Erya writes 孟諸, the Zhouli writes 望諸; Zheng's note: "a marsh-mere is 望諸, which is Mengzhu."
- *Surname*: the Zhongsun clan of Lu was the 孟 of the Three Huan, hence called 孟.
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 母朗, sounding like 莽 (mǎng). Meaning without aim or choice.
- *Also* fanqie 莫浪, the departing tone of 𥁃 (màng). An imprecise, careless look. Zhuangzi, *Qi wu lun*: "reckless, careless words (孟浪之言)."
