**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls еҜ…, upper; Child radical еӯҗ; p.278 #16)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie иҺ«жӣҙ, sounding like еӨў (mГЁng).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "The eldest (й•·)." *Li wei*: "the eldest of the principal wife is дјҜ, the eldest of a concubine is еӯҹ." Shu, *Kang gao*: "the king spoke thus: 'Senior Prince (еӯҹдҫҜ), my younger brother, little one Feng.'" *Shu zhuan*: "the Son of Heaven's son, at eighteen, is styled Senior Prince (еӯҹдҫҜ)." Also, a woman's elder brother is likewise called еӯҹ.

**Senses.**
- *The first, the beginning (е§Ӣ).* *Yupian*. The first month of each of the four seasons is the еӯҹ month. Former Han, *Biography of Li Xun*: "the еҜ…, the еӯҹ month."
- *To strive, exert (еӢү).* *Guangyun*. Ban Gu, *You tong fu*: "why not strive and advance (еӯҹжҷү) to reach the throng? the hour flits past and comes not twice"; note: "еӯҹжҷү means to strive and press forward."
- *Great (еӨ§).* Guanzi, *Ren fa*: "lofty words and great deeds (еӯҹиЎҢ), exceeding one's true nature."
- *A prefecture-name*, the Han Henei commandery, the ancient Mengjin (еӯҹжҙҘ); the Tang set up Mengzhou (еӯҹе·һ).
- *Also* Shu, *Yu gong*: "covering Mengzhu (еӯҹиұ¬)"; the Erya writes еӯҹи«ё, the Zhouli writes жңӣи«ё; Zheng's note: "a marsh-mere is жңӣи«ё, which is Mengzhu."
- *Surname*: the Zhongsun clan of Lu was the еӯҹ of the Three Huan, hence called еӯҹ.
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie жҜҚжң—, sounding like иҺҪ (mЗҺng). Meaning without aim or choice.
- *Also* fanqie иҺ«жөӘ, the departing tone of рҘҒғ (mГ ng). An imprecise, careless look. Zhuangzi, *Qi wu lun*: "reckless, careless words (еӯҹжөӘд№ӢиЁҖ)."
