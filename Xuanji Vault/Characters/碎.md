## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈午集下〉【石字部】頁832第05【廣韻】蘇內切【集韻】【韻會】蘇對切，𠀤音誶。【說文】䃺也。　又散也，細破也。【史記·趙世家】臣頭與璧俱碎於柱。　又煩碎也。【後漢·黃霸傳】米鹽靡密，初若煩碎。又【王通中·說事君篇】謝莊王融，古之纖人也，其文碎。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, lower; Stone radical 石; p.832 #05)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 蘇內. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 蘇對 — sounding like 誶 (suì).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to grind to bits (䃺)."
- *To scatter; to break small.* Shiji, *Hereditary House of Zhao*: "my head and the jade disc shall be smashed (碎) together against the pillar."
- *Petty and fussy.* Later Han, *Biography of Huang Ba*: "his administration, minute as rice and salt, at first seemed fussy and trivial (煩碎)."
- *Fragmentary.* Wang Tong, *Zhongshuo, Shi junzhang*: "Xie Zhuang and Wang Rong were petty men of old; their writing is fragmentary (碎).
