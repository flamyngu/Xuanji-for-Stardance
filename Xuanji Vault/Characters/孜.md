## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈寅集上〉【子字部】頁278第10【唐韻】子之切【集韻】【韻會】津之切【正韻】津私切，𠀤音咨。【說文】汲汲也。一曰力篤愛也，勤也。【書·益稷】予思日孜孜。又【君𨻰】惟日孜孜，無敢逸豫。經傳通作孳。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 寅, upper; Child radical 子; p.278 #10)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 子之. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 津之. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 津私 — sounding like 咨 (zī).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "anxiously striving (汲汲)." One says: earnest in devotion and love (力篤愛); diligent (勤).
- *Diligent, unflagging.* Shu, *Yi Ji*: "I ponder it, day after day, diligently and diligently (孜孜)."
- *also.* Shu, *Jun Chen*: "only day by day diligent (孜孜), never daring ease or idleness."

**On the graph.** In the classics and their traditions it is used interchangeably with 孳.
