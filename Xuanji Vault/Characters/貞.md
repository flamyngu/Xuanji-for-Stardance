## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集中〉【貝字部】頁1204第03    〔古文〕：𠨀【唐韻】陟盈切【集韻】【韻會】知盈切，𠀤音禎。【說文】卜問也。从卜，貝，以爲贄。【徐曰】《周禮》有大貞禮，謂卜人事也。　又【易·乾卦】元亨利貞。【疏】貞，正也。又【文言】貞者，事之幹也。【書·太甲】一人元良，萬邦以貞。【疏】天子有大善，則天下得其正。　又【書·洪範】曰貞，曰悔。【傳】內卦曰貞，外卦曰悔。　又【禮·檀弓】故謂夫子貞惠文子。【疏】諡法，外內用情曰貞。　又【釋名】貞，定也。精定不動惑也。　又木名。【本草】女貞，木名。蘇頌曰：女貞，負霜蔥翠，故貞女慕其名。一名冬靑。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, middle; Cowrie radical 貝; p.1204 #03)*

**Ancient graph.** 𠨀.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 陟盈. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 知盈 — sounding like 禎 (zhēn).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "To divine and inquire (卜問). From 卜 (divination) and 貝 (cowrie), used as the offering." Xu [Kai]: "the Zhouli has the rite of 大貞 (Great Divination), meaning the diviner's business."

**Senses.**
- *Upright, correct (正).* Yijing, *Qian*: "originating, pervading, profiting, upright (元亨利貞)"; subcommentary: "貞, upright." *Wenyan*: "貞 is the trunk of affairs." Shu, *Tai jia*: "one man supremely good, and the myriad states are thereby set right (貞)"; subcommentary: "when the Son of Heaven has great goodness, the world gains its uprightness."
- *The inner trigram.* Shu, *Hong fan*: "it says 貞, it says 悔"; tradition: "the inner trigram is 貞, the outer is 悔."
- *Posthumous title.* Liji, *Tan gong*: "hence he was styled Master Zhen-hui-wen (貞惠文子)"; subcommentary: "by the title-law, 'using true feeling within and without is 貞.'"
- *Fixed, steadfast (定).* *Shiming*: "貞, fixed — essence settled, unmoving, undeceived."
- *A tree.* *Bencao*: "女貞 (privet), a tree." Su Song: "the privet bears the frost green and lush, so the chaste maiden admires its name; another name is 冬靑 (winter-green)."
