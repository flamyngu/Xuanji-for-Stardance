## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1060第21【唐韻】【集韻】【正韻】𠀤無非切，音微。【玉篇】菜也。【說文】似藿菜之微者也。【詩·召南】言采其薇。【儀禮·公食大夫禮】鉶芼：牛藿、羊苦、豕薇皆有滑。　又白薇，藥名。　又蘼蕪，一名薇蕪。【張衡·南都賦】薇蕪蓀萇。　又花名。【拾遺記】元熙元年，詔民閒園囿皆植紫薇，以爲壓勝。【唐書·百官志】中書知制浩，開元號紫薇省。　又薔薇，見薔字註。　又【唐韻】武悲切，音眉。【爾雅·釋草】薇垂水。【註】生於水邊。　又薇銜，藥名。【集韻】或作𦵨。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1060 #21)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 無非 — sounding like 微 (wēi).

**Etymology and senses.** *Yupian*: "a vegetable." *Shuowen*: "like the *huo* bean-leaf, but a slighter kind."
- *Osmund, the wild bean.* Shijing, *Zhaonan*: "I gather the osmund (薇)." Yili, *Gong shi dafu li*: "for the seasoned broths: beef with bean-leaf, mutton with bitter-herb, pork with osmund (薇), all with a smoothing relish."
- *White wei (白薇), a drug-name.*
- *Miwu, also called weiwu (薇蕪).* Zhang Heng, *Fu on the Southern Capital*: "weiwu, sun-herb, and changchu."
- *A flower-name (the crape-myrtle).* The Shiyi ji: "in the first year of Yuanxi, an edict bade the people plant crape-myrtle (紫薇) in their gardens, as a charm against ill." Tang shu, *Treatise on Officials*: "the Secretariat's office of drafting edicts was, in the Kaiyuan era, named the Ziwei (紫薇) Department."
- *The rose (薔薇); see the note under 薔.*
- *Tang Yun*: fanqie 武悲, sounding like 眉 (méi). Erya, *Shicao*: "薇 hangs over the water"; note: "it grows by the water's edge."
- *Weixian (薇銜), a drug-name.* *Ji Yun*: sometimes written 𦵨.
