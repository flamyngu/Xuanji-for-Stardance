> 《康熙字典》〈丑集上〉【口字部】頁205第01    〔古文〕：𡂥【唐韻】【集韻】他案切【正韻】他晏切，𠀤音炭。【說文】吞歎也。一曰太息也。與歎同。　又【廣韻】【集韻】他干切【正韻】他丹切，𠀤音灘。義同。【詩·王風】嘅其嘆矣，遇人之艱難矣。【釋文】嘆，本亦作歎，吐丹反，協韻也。又【大雅】而無永嘆。【釋文】歎，他安反，字或作嘆。　又叶他絹切，音𤩴。【曹植賦】入空室而獨倚，對牀幃而切歎。歎叶上見。　又叶他涓切，音天。【曹大家·東征賦】陟封丘而踐路兮，慕京師而竊嘆。小人性之懷土兮，自書傳而有焉。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, upper; Mouth radical 口; p.205 #01)*

**Ancient graph.** 𡂥.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*: fanqie 他案. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 他晏 — sounding like 炭 (tàn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to swallow a sigh (吞歎); one sense, a deep sigh (太息)." Same as 歎.
- *Tang Yun, Ji Yun*: fanqie 他干; *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 他丹 — sounding like 灘 (tān); same sense. Shijing, *Wang feng*: "she sighs (嘆) her sigh, having met a man of hardship"; *Shiwen*: "嘆 also written 歎, fanqie 吐丹, to suit the rhyme." Also *Daya*: "and no long sighing (永嘆)"; *Shiwen*: "歎 fanqie 他安, the graph sometimes written 嘆."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 𤩴 (juàn), fanqie 他絹.* Cao Zhi's rhapsody: "I enter the empty chamber and lean alone, facing the bed-curtains in keen sighing (歎)" (歎 rhyming with the preceding 見).
- *Rhymes as 天 (tiān), fanqie 他涓.* Cao Dagu (Ban Zhao), *Rhapsody on the Eastward Journey*: "I climb Fengqiu and tread the road, longing for the capital and secretly sighing (嘆)…"
