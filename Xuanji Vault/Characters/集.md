## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈戌集中〉【隹字部】頁1365第15    〔古文〕：雦【唐韻】【廣韻】【韻會】【正韻】秦入切【集韻】【類篇】籍入切，𠀤音箿。【說文】本作雧。羣鳥在木上也。【詩·周南】集于灌木。　又【廣韻】就也，成也。【書·武成】大統未集。【傳】大業未就。【詩·小雅】我行旣集。【箋】集，猶成也。　又【韻會】雜也。【孟子】是集義所生者。【註】集，雜也。　又【廣韻】衆也。　又【廣韻】安也。【史記·曹參世家】問所以安集百姓。　又【玉篇】合也。【廣韻】聚也，會也，同也。【史記·秦始皇紀】天下雲集響應。【前漢·鼂錯傳】動靜不集。【註】師古曰：集，齊也。【史記·司馬相如傳】鱗集仰流。　又【左傳·昭二十三年】險其走集。【註】走集，邊境之壘辟也。　又【前漢·藝文志】劉歆總羣書，而奏其七略，故有輯略。【註】師古曰：輯與集同。謂諸書之總要。【韻會】文集，文所聚也。唐有子史經集四庫。　又州名。【廣韻】漢宕渠縣，梁恭帝爲集州。　又【廣韻】姓也。漢有集壹。　又【韻補】叶疾救切，音就。【詩·小雅】我龜旣厭，不我告猶。謀夫孔多，是用不集。猶，于救切。　又叶昨合切，音雜。【詩·大雅】天監在下，有命旣集。文王初載，天作之合。    考證：〔【左傳·昭二十三年】險其走集。【註】集，謂邊境之壘辟也。〕　謹按註中集上脫走字下多謂字。今照原文改走集邊境之壘辟也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 戌, middle; Short-tailed-bird radical 隹; p.1365 #15)*

**Ancient graph.** 雦.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Guangyun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 秦入. *Ji Yun*, *Leipian*: fanqie 籍入 — sounding like 箿 (jí).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "properly written 雧; a flock of birds upon a tree." Shijing, *Zhounan*: "they settle (集) on the bushy trees."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "to accomplish (就); to complete (成)." Shu, *Wu cheng*: "the great succession was not yet achieved (集)"; tradition: "the great work was not yet accomplished." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "my journey is now done (集)"; Jian: "集 is like 'completed.'"
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: "mixed, accumulated (雜)." Mengzi: "this is what is born of the accumulation of righteousness (集義)"; note: "集 means accumulated."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "many (衆)."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "to settle, to pacify (安)." Shiji, *Hereditary House of Cao Can*: "he asked how to settle (安集) the people."
- *Also* *Yupian*: "to join (合)." *Guangyun*: "to gather (聚); to assemble (會); to be alike (同)." Shiji, *Annals of the First Emperor*: "the realm gathered like clouds (雲集) and answered like echoes." Former Han, *Biography of Chao Cuo*: "movement and rest were not in concert (不集)"; note: Yan Shigu says: "集 means even, in accord (齊)." Shiji, *Biography of Sima Xiangru*: "they gather like fish-scales (鱗集), facing upstream."
- *Also* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 23: "they made dangerous their frontier-forts (走集)"; note: "走集 are the ramparts and outworks of the border."
- *Also* Former Han, *Treatise on Literature*: "Liu Xin gathered all the books and presented his Seven Epitomes, hence there is the 'Collected Epitome' (輯略)"; note: Yan Shigu says: "輯 is the same as 集; it means the gathered essentials of the books." *Yun Hui*: "a 文集 (literary collection) is where writings are gathered. The Tang had the four repositories: Masters, Histories, Classics, and Collections (子史經集)."
- *Also a prefecture-name.* *Guangyun*: "the Han's Dangqu county; Emperor Gong of Liang made it Jizhou (集州)."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: a surname; the Han had Ji Yi (集壹).
- *Rhymes* (per the *Yunbu*) as fanqie 疾救, sounding like 就 (jiù). Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "my tortoise-shells are wearied and tell me no more plans; the counselors are very many, and so nothing is achieved (集)"; 猶, fanqie 于救.
- *Rhymes as* fanqie 昨合, sounding like 雜 (zá). Shijing, *Daya*: "Heaven looks down below, and the mandate is now settled (集); King Wen at his first bearing, Heaven made for him his mate."

**Textual notes.** In the Zuozhuan note, 走 was dropped before 集 and an extra 謂 added; corrected per the original to "走集邊境之壘辟也."
