*(Kangxi: rolls еҚҜ, lower; Rap radical ж”ҙ; p.473 #35)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui* all: fanqie еұ…ж…¶, sounding like з«ҹ (jГ¬ng).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Reverent (иӮ…)." *Shiming*: "ж•¬ means to be on guard (иӯҰ); ever keeping oneself grave and watchful." *Yupian*: "respectful (жҒӯ); heedful (ж„ј)."
