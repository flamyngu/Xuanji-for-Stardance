**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls еҚҲ, lower; Grain radical зҰҫ; p.859 #24)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie еӯҗжҳ”. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie иіҮжҒҜ вҖ” sounding like и·Ў (jД«).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "To gather, heap up (иҒҡ)." *Zengyun*: "to pile up (зҙҜ); to heap and stack (е ҶрӨҙҒ)." Yijing, *Da you*: "the great cart bears the load, what is heaped within (з©Қдёӯ) does not fail." Also *Sheng* hexagram: "by heaping the small (з©Қе°Ҹ) it grows high and great." Shijing, *Zhou song*: "they heaped it up (з©Қд№Ӣ), grain upon grain." Liji, *Yue ling*: "in mid-autumn, the officers are charged to urge the people to heap up much store (з©ҚиҒҡ)." Former Han, *Treatise on Food and Money*: "stores laid up (з©ҚиІҜ) are the great lifeline of the realm."

**Senses.**
- *Also* Yili, *Shi guan li*: "the leather cap and the plain pleated skirt (зҙ з©Қ)"; Zheng's note: "з©Қ is like to pleat (иҫҹ) вҖ” using plain silk for the skirt, gathered and pleated at the waist."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie еүҮжӯ·, sounding like зёҫ (jГ¬). Also "to gather."
- *Also* *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie еӯҗжҷә. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie иіҮеӣӣ вҖ” sounding like жҒЈ (zГ¬). To gather; to store up (е„Іи“„). Shijing, *Daya*: "then they stored (з©Қ), then they granaried." Zhouli, *Tian guan, Xiao zai*: "he managed the pen-provisions and the stockpiles (е§”з©Қ)"; note: "е§”з©Қ means the penned beasts, grain, firewood, and fodder supplied for guests' road-use." Also *Da situ*: "he had the wilds repair the roads and lay up stores (е§”з©Қ)"; note: "the little is е§”, the much is з©Қ, both to supply guests."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as еӯҗ (zЗҗ), fanqie жҒЈжӯӨ.* Shijing, *Zhou song*: "full and solid are its stores (з©Қ), myriads and millions and more."
