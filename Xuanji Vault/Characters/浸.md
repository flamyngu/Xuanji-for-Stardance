## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈巳集上〉【水字部】頁625第15    〔古文〕：𥧲【唐韻】【集韻】【正韻】𠀤子鴆切，音祲。漬也。【詩·曹風】浸彼苞稂。　又潤也。【詩·小雅】浸彼稻田。　又漸也。【易·臨卦】剛浸而長。　又沒也。【史記·趙世家】城不浸者三版。　又涵也，沈也，澤之總名也。【莊子·逍遙遊】大浸稽天。【周禮·夏官·職方氏】揚州其浸五湖。　又通作湛。【禮·內則】湛諸美酒。　又【廣韻】七林切【集韻】千尋切，𠀤音侵。浸淫，漸漬也。【王褒·洞簫賦】浸淫叔子遠其類。或作濅寖。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.625 #15)*

**Ancient graph.** 𥧲.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 子鴆, sounding like 祲 (jìn).

**Etymology and senses.** To steep, soak (漬). Shijing, *Cao feng*: "it soaks (浸) those clustered weeds."

**Senses.**
- *To moisten (潤).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "it waters (浸) those rice-fields."
- *Gradually (漸).* Yijing, *Lin* hexagram: "the firm gradually (浸) grows."
- *To submerge (沒).* Shiji, *Hereditary House of Zhao*: "the city not yet submerged (浸) measured three planks."
- *To soak through; to sink; a general name for a marsh (涵, 沈, 澤之總名).* Zhuangzi, *Xiaoyao you*: "the great flood (大浸) reaches the sky." Zhouli, *Xia guan, Zhifang shi*: "Yangzhou — its marshes (浸) are the Five Lakes."
- *Used for 湛.* Liji, *Nei ze*: "steep (湛) them in fine wine."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: fanqie 七林. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 千尋 — sounding like 侵 (qīn). 浸淫 (jinyin), to soak gradually (漸漬). Wang Bao, *Dongxiao fu*: "spreading and seeping (浸淫), Shuzi outdoes his kind." Also written 濅 or 寖.
