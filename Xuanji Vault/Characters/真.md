## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈午集中〉【目字部】頁804第18    〔古文〕：𡙊【唐韻】側鄰切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】之人切，𠀤軫平聲。【說文】仙人變形而登天也。【司馬子坐忘樞翼篇】鍊形爲氣，名曰眞人。　又【玉篇】不虛假也。【韻會】實也。僞之反也。【正韻】神也，淳也，精也，正也。【莊子·漁父篇】眞者，精誠之至也。【前漢·宣帝詔】使眞僞毋相亂。【後漢·馬援傳】帝王自有眞也。　又【南齊書·顧歡傳】佛號正眞，眞會無生。【天隱子神解篇】本一性而言，謂之眞如。　又【舊唐書·明皇紀】莊子文子列子庚桑子所著書，改爲眞經。　又天曰眞宰。【莊子·齊物論】若有眞宰，而特不得朕。　又天眞，天乙，始生之眞元也。【黃帝素問】有上古天眞論。　又畫像曰寫眞。【張君房麗情集】蒲女崔徽寫眞，寄裴敬中。　又星名。【晉書·天文志】京房風角集星章，妖星，一曰眞若。歲星所生也。　又茶名。【博物志】飲眞茶，令人少眠。任昉云：眞，香茗，出巴東。　又香名。【洪芻香譜】降眞香，生南海諸山。　又金名。【本草·釋名】陶弘景曰：仙方名金爲太眞。　又玉名。【抱朴子僊藥篇】𤣥眞者，玉之別名也。　又州名。漢廣陵地，宋置眞州。又四川化外，唐置眞州。　又姓。漢長史眞祐，宋學士眞德秀。又複姓。唐將軍眞郭待封。　又【南齊書·魏虜傳】北魏呼官吏爲眞，如直眞，烏矮眞之類。　又叶之然切，音旃。【陸雲·陸常侍誄】於惟君德，齊聖廣淵。羣彥景附，漸化濯眞。【六書正譌】人受氣以生，目最先，神之所聚，無非實也。故从目从匕。匕，化也。从兀，氣之狀也。會意。【孫季昭曰】六經之中無眞字。𠤛，古文眞。    （真）同眞。俗字。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, middle; Eye radical 目; p.804 #18)*

**Ancient graph.** 𡙊.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 側鄰. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 之人 — the level tone of 軫 (zhēn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "an immortal transforming his shape and ascending to heaven." Sima [Chengzhen], *Zuowang shuyi*: "refining the form into breath — this is called the True Man (眞人)."
- *Real, genuine, not false.* *Yupian*: "not empty or false." *Yun Hui*: "real (實); the opposite of the false." *Zheng Yun*: "divine (神); pure (淳); essential (精); upright (正)." Zhuangzi, *Yufu*: "the true (眞) is the utmost of pure sincerity." Former Han, edict of Emperor Xuan: "let the true (眞) and the false not be confounded." Later Han, *Biography of Ma Yuan*: "the true emperor has of himself a genuineness (眞)."
- *The True (Buddhist/Daoist).* Nan Qi shu, *Biography of Gu Huan*: "the Buddha is titled the Correct and True (正眞); the True realizes the unborn." The Tianyinzi, *Shenjie*: "spoken of as the one nature at root, it is called Suchness (眞如)."
- *True Classics.* Jiu Tang shu, *Annals of Minghuang*: "the books written by Zhuangzi, Wenzi, Liezi, and Gengsangzi were renamed True Classics (眞經)."
- *The True Lord (眞宰), i.e. Heaven.* Zhuangzi, *Qiwu lun*: "it is as if there were a True Lord (眞宰), yet one gets no sign of it."
- *tianzhen (天眞), the heavenly first* — the original genuineness of life's first arising. Huangdi Suwen: "it has the *Treatise on the High-Antiquity Heavenly Genuineness* (上古天眞論)."
- *A portrait (寫眞).* Zhang Junfang, *Liqing ji*: "the woman of Pu, Cui Hui, had her portrait (寫眞) made and sent it to Pei Jingzhong."
- *A star-name.* Jin shu, *Treatise on Astronomy*: "Jing Fang's *Fengjiao jixing* chapter on baleful stars — one is called Zhenruo (眞若), born of Jupiter."
- *A tea-name.* Bowu zhi: "drinking *zhen*-tea (眞茶) makes a man sleep little." Ren Fang says: "眞 is a fragrant tea, from Badong."
- *An incense-name.* Hong Chu, *Xiangpu*: "Jiangzhen incense (降眞香) grows on the mountains of the South Sea."
- *A name for gold.* Bencao, *Shiming*: "Tao Hongjing says, 'the immortals' recipes name gold *taizhen* (太眞).'"
- *A name for jade.* Baopuzi, *Xianyao*: "*xuanzhen* (𤣥眞) is another name for jade."
- *A prefecture-name.* The Han Guangling region; the Song set up Zhenzhou (眞州). Also beyond the pale of Sichuan, the Tang set up a Zhenzhou.
- *A surname.* The Han Chief Clerk Zhen You; the Song academician Zhen Dexiu. Also a compound surname: the Tang general Zhenguo Daifeng (眞郭待封).
- *An official term.* Nan Qi shu, *Account of the Wei Caitiffs*: "the Northern Wei called officials 眞, as in *zhizhen* (直眞), *wu'aizhen* (烏矮眞), and the like."
- *Rhymes as 旃 (zhān), fanqie 之然.* Lu Yun, *Dirge for Privy Counsellor Lu*: "ah, the lord's virtue, sage and vast and deep; the host of the talented attached themselves like shadows, gradually transformed and washed pure and true (眞)."

**On the graph.** The Liushu zhengwei: "man receives breath and is born; the eye comes first, where the spirit gathers — nothing of it but is real; hence it follows 目 (eye) and 匕. 匕 means 'to transform.' It follows 兀, the shape of breath — a meaning-compound." Sun Jizhao says: "in the Six Classics there is no graph 眞." 𠤛 is the ancient form of 眞. [Under 真:] the same as 眞; a vulgar form.
