## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集下〉【八字部】頁128第11    〔古文〕：𥡝【唐韻】古甜切【集韻】【韻會】堅嫌切【正韻】古嫌切，𠀤音縑。【說文】幷也。从手禾。兼持二禾也。【徐曰】會意。秉持一禾，兼持二禾。可兼持者，莫若禾也。【易·繫辭】兼三才而兩之。【前漢·王莽傳】縣宰缺者，數年守兼。【註】師古曰：不拜正官，令人守兼也。又姓。【韻會】衞公子兼之後。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Eight radical 八; p.128 #11)*

**Ancient graph.** 𥡝.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 古甜. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 堅嫌. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 古嫌 — sounding like 縑 (jiān).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to combine, hold both (幷)." It follows 手 (hand) and 禾 (grain): holding two stalks of grain at once. Xu [Kai]: "a meaning-compound. To grasp one stalk is 秉; to grasp two is 兼; nothing is so apt for grasping-together as grain." Yijing, *Xici*: "it embraces the Three Powers (兼三才) and doubles them." Former Han, *Biography of Wang Mang*: "where a county magistracy fell vacant, for years it was held jointly in trust (守兼)"; note: Yan Shigu: "no regular officer was appointed, but someone was made to hold it jointly."
- *A surname.* *Yun Hui*: "descendants of Prince Jian (兼) of Wei."
