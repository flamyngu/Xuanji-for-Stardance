## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集下〉【衣字部】頁1112第14    〔古文〕：𠆂、𠌺【唐韻】所危切【集韻】雙隹切，𠀤音䙑。小也，減也，殺也。【類篇】浸微也。【韻會】弱也，耗也。　又【集韻】初危切，音夂。【玉篇】等衰也。【齊語】相地而衰征，則民不移。　又【集韻】倉回切，音崔。【類篇】同縗，喪服也。【禮·喪服小記】斬衰括髮以麻，齊衰惡筓以終喪。　又邑名。【晉語】公子濟河，召令狐臼，衰、桑、泉皆降。【註】三者皆晉邑。　又【集韻】蓑本字。【說文】艸雨衣。秦謂之萆。【詩·小雅】何衰何笠。【石經】作蓑。　又【韻補】所類切，音帨。【東方朔·七諫】駑駿雜而不分兮，服疲牛而驂驥。年滔滔而日遠兮，壽冉冉而愈衰。【說文】作𠆂。【類篇】作𠌺。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, lower; Clothes radical 衣; p.1112 #14)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𠆂, 𠌺.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 所危. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 雙隹 — sounding like 䙑 (shuāi).

**Etymology and senses.** "To lessen, to diminish, to abate." *Leipian*: "gradually faint." *Yun Hui*: "weak; wasting."
- *Graded decrease (cuī).* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 初危, sounding like 夂 (cuī). *Yupian*: "graded decrease (等衰)." The Qiyu: "to assess the land and tax it by grades (衰征), so the people do not move away."
- *Mourning dress (cuī).* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 倉回, sounding like 崔 (cuī). *Leipian*: "same as 縗, mourning dress." Liji, *Sangfu xiaoji*: "for the unhemmed mourning (斬衰) one binds the hair with hemp; for the hemmed mourning (齊衰) one uses a plain hairpin, to the end of the mourning."
- *A town-name.* The Jinyu: "when the prince crossed the River, he summoned Linghu Jiu, and Cui (衰), Sang, and Quan all submitted"; note: "the three are all Jin towns."
- *The original graph for 蓑 (a straw rain-cape).* *Ji Yun.* *Shuowen*: "a grass rain-coat; in Qin called *bi* (萆)." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "[wearing] what cape (衰) and what hat"; the Stone Classics write 蓑.

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 所類, sounding like 帨 (shuì).* Dongfang Shuo, *Qijian*: "...the years roll on and daily recede, my span creeps by and ever wanes (衰)."

**On the graph.** The Shuowen writes 𠆂; the Leipian writes 𠌺.
