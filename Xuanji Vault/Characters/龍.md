## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈亥集下〉【龍字部】頁1536第33    〔古文〕：竜、𦱉、𢅛、𠊋、㰍、𥫈、龒、䰱【唐韻】【集韻】力鍾切【韻會】【正韻】盧容切，𠀤音籠。【說文】龍，鱗蟲之長，能幽能明，能細能巨，能短能長，春分而登天，秋分而潛淵。【廣雅】有鱗曰蛟龍，有翼曰應龍，有角曰虯龍，無角曰螭龍，未升天曰蟠龍。【本草註】龍耳虧聰，故謂之龍。【易·乾卦】時乗六龍以御天。　又星名。【左傳·僖五年】龍尾伏辰。【疏】角亢氐房心尾箕爲蒼龍之宿。又【襄二十八年】龍，宋鄭之星也。　又山名。龍門，在河東，見【禹貢】。龍山，見【山海經】。封龍，見【括地志】。　又邑名。【左傳·成二年】齊侯伐我北鄙，三日取龍。【註】龍，魯邑。在泰山博縣西南。又【前漢·地理志】燉煌郡有龍勒縣。　又官名。【左傳·昭十七年】太皡氏以龍紀，故爲龍師，而龍名。　又句龍。【左傳·昭二十九年】共工氏有子曰句龍。　又馬名。《周禮·廋人》馬八尺以上爲龍。【禮·月令】駕蒼龍。　又龍輔，玉名。【左傳·昭二十九年】公賜公衍羔裘，使獻龍輔於齊侯。　又草名。【詩·鄭風】隰有游龍。【陸璣·草木疏】一名馬蓼，生水澤中，今人謂之小葒草。　又神名。【山海經】有神名燭龍。【屈原·離騷】日安不到，燭龍何照。　又姓。漢有龍且。又複姓。夏關龍逢，卽豢龍氏後。漢御史擾龍羣，卽劉累之後。　又人名。奢龍，黃帝臣。【管子·五行篇】奢龍辨乎東方，故使爲土師。又舜臣名。【書·舜典】帝曰：龍命汝作納言，夙夜出納朕命。　又【廣雅】龍，君也。　又【廣韻】通也。　又【玉篇】竉也。【詩·商頌】何天之龍。我龍受之。【毛傳】讀如字。【朱傳】寵也。　又【玉篇】和也，萌也。　又【正韻】與寵同。【詩·商頌·何天之龍釋文】鄭讀作寵，榮名之謂也。○按朱傳作叶音。　又【正韻】音曨。【孟子】有私龍斷焉。　又【集韻】【韻會】𠀤莫江切，音厖。【集韻】黑白雜色也。【周禮·冬官考工記】玉人上公用龍。【註】謂雜色，非純玉也。　又叶蒲光切，音龐。【易·坤卦】故稱龍焉。叶上嫌於無陽。【揚雄·解嘲】以鴟梟而笑鳳凰，執蝘蜒而嘲龜龍。【說文】从肉飛之形，童省聲。【徐鉉曰】象宛轉飛動之貌。    考證：〔【爾雅·釋畜】馬高八尺爲龍。〕　謹按爾雅作駥不作龍。今改周禮廋人馬八尺以上爲龍。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 亥, lower; Dragon radical 龍; p.1536 #33)*

**Ancient graphs.** 竜, 𦱉, 𢅛, 𠊋, 㰍, 𥫈, 龒, 䰱.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*: fanqie 力鍾. *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 盧容 — sounding like 籠 (lóng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the dragon, chief of scaled creatures; it can be hidden or bright, small or huge, short or long; at the spring equinox it mounts to heaven, at the autumn equinox it dives into the deep." *Guangya*: "scaled, it is the *jiao*-dragon; winged, the *ying*-dragon; horned, the *qiu*-dragon; hornless, the *chi*-dragon; not yet ascended, the coiled (*pan*) dragon." Note to the Bencao: "the dragon's ears are dull of hearing, hence it is called 龍 [punning 聾, deaf]." Yijing, *Qian*: "in season he rides the six dragons to drive across the sky."
- *A star-name.* Zuozhuan, Xi yr 5: "the Dragon's Tail lies hidden by the sun (龍尾伏辰)"; subcommentary: "Jiao, Kang, Di, Fang, Xin, Wei, Ji are the mansions of the Azure Dragon (蒼龍)." Xiang yr 28: "the Dragon is the star of Song and Zheng."
- *Mountain-names.* Longmen (龍門), in Hedong (see the Yugong); Longshan (see the Shanhaijing); Fenglong (see the Kuodi zhi).
- *A town-name.* Zuozhuan, Cheng yr 2: "the Marquis of Qi attacked our northern marches and in three days took Long (龍)"; note: "Long, a Lu town, southwest of Bo county in Taishan." Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "Dunhuang commandery has Longle county."
- *An office-name.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 17: "the Taihao clan reckoned by the dragon, hence their officers were Dragon-Masters (龍師), named after the dragon."
- *句龍 (Gou-Long).* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 29: "the Gonggong clan had a son named Goulong."
- *A horse-name.* Zhouli, *Souren*: "a horse of eight *chi* or more is a 'dragon' (龍)." Liji, *Yueling*: "he yokes the azure dragons (蒼龍)."
- *龍輔, a jade-name.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 29: "the duke gave Gongyan a lamb-fur coat, and had him present the Longfu jade to the Marquis of Qi."
- *A plant-name.* Shijing, *Zhengfeng*: "in the marsh is the spreading *long* (游龍)"; Lu Ji, *Caomu shu*: "also called horse-knotweed (馬蓼), growing in marshes; people now call it the little *hong*-plant."
- *A god-name.* Shanhaijing: "there is a god named Torch-Dragon (燭龍)." Qu Yuan, *Lisao*: "where the sun does not reach, what does the Torch-Dragon light?"
- *A surname.* The Han had Long Ju. Compound surnames: Guanlong Feng of Xia (descendant of the dragon-rearer clan); the Han censor Raolong Qun (descendant of Liu Lei).
- *Personal names.* Shelong, a minister of the Yellow Emperor: Guanzi, *Wuxing*: "Shelong was discerning of the east, so was made Master of the Land." A minister of Shun: Shu, *Shundian*: "the Emperor said, 'Long, I charge you to be the Voice [of the throne], to issue and receive my commands morning and night.'"
- *Lord (君).* *Guangya.*
- *To penetrate (通).* *Guangyun.*
- *Favour (竉, 寵).* *Yupian.* Shijing, *Shangsong*: "[bearing] the favour (龍) of Heaven; we receive (龍) it"; Mao's tradition reads it as written; Zhu's tradition: "favour (寵)"; Shiwen: "Zheng reads it 寵, meaning glory and renown." Also *Yupian*: "harmonious; sprouting."
- *Sounding like 曨 (lǒng).* Mengzi: "he privately took the vantage-rise (龍斷) [monopoly]."
- *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 莫江, sounding like 厖 (máng). *Ji Yun*: "a mottled black-and-white colour." Zhouli, *Dongguan kaogong ji*: "the jade-worker: a senior duke uses 'dragon' (龍) [jade]"; note: "meaning mottled colour, not pure jade."

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 蒲光, sounding like 龐 (páng).* Yijing, *Kun*: "hence it speaks of the dragon (龍)" (rhyming above with 嫌於無陽). Yang Xiong, *Jiechao*: "with owl and screech-owl they mock the phoenix, with lizard in hand they jeer at tortoise and dragon (龍)."

**On the graph.** *Shuowen*: "from the shape of flesh flying, with 童 contracted as phonetic." Xu Xuan: "it images the look of writhing, winding flight."

**Textual note.** The "馬高八尺爲龍" is the Erya, *Shichu*, but the Erya writes 駥, not 龍; corrected to Zhouli, *Souren*, "馬八尺以上爲龍".
