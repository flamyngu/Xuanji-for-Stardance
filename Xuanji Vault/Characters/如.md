**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, lower; Woman radical 女; p.255 #21)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 人諸. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 人余 — sounding like 鴽 (rú).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to follow, comply (从隨)." One says: "as if (若); the same as (同)." Shu, *Shundian*: "[he did] as (如) with the five tokens, as with the Dai rites, as at first." Also "thus, -ly": Yijing, *Li*: "abruptly (突如) it comes (來如)." Former Han, *Biography of Yang Xiong*: "Xiong's estate was no more than ten *jin*, yet he was content (晏如)."
- *To go to, to reach.* "The duke went to (如) Tang, went to Qi"; see the Zuozhuan. Liu Ling, *Hymn to the Virtue of Wine*: "Heaven his tent, earth his mat, he follows his fancy wherever it goes (如)."
- *A month-name.* Erya, *Shisui*: "the second month is 如."
- *A particle.* Lunyu: "earnest and sincere (恂恂如)." Mengzi: "glad and joyful (驩虞如); broad and content (皥皥如)."
- *凉如, a county-name.* Former Han, *Treatise on the Feng and Shan*: "the Emperor toured north to Shuofang, and on return disbanded the troops at Liangru (凉如)."
- *肥如, an ancient state-name (in Liaoxi).* Ying Shao: "the Viscount of Fei fled to Yan, and Yan enfeoffed him here."
- *A surname.* The Ruluo clan, changed to the Ru clan; see the Tongpu.
- *Buddhist senses.* "Original awareness is 如, present awakening is 來 [the Tathāgata]"; see the Daoyuan ji. 眞如 (True Suchness) has the marks of change; "Suchness (如如) abides constant and shifts not"; both in the Chandeng lu.
- *Beast and bird names.* The *churu* (𤣎如), a beast; the *quru* (瞿如), a bird; both in the Shanhaijing.
- *Interchangeable with 而.* Former Han, *Treatise on the Five Phases*, citing the Zuozhuan: "the stars fell as (如) rain"; note: "如 is 而 (and): the stars fell and it rained too."
- *Guangyun*: fanqie 人恕; *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 如倨 — sounding like 茹 (rù). Dongfang Shuo, *Qijian*: "...soaring through the void, whither (如) shall I go?"; note: read in the departing tone.
- *Ji Yun*, *Leipian*: fanqie 乃箇, sounding like 那 (nà); also "as if.
