## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈巳集上〉【水字部】頁632第08    〔古文〕：𣶒、囦、𠝃【唐韻】烏圓切【集韻】【正韻】營圓切【韻會】幺圓切，𠀤音𢏮。【說文】回水也。从水，象形。左右岸也，中象水貌。【管子·度地篇】水出地而不流者，命曰淵。　又深也。【詩·衞風】秉心塞淵。　又潭名。【水經注】伊水東爲淵潭。　又淵淵，鼓聲。【詩·小雅】伐鼓淵淵。　又姓。世本有齊大夫淵㰬。　又【集韻】一均切，音蜵。亦深也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.632 #08)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𣶒, 囦, 𠝃.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 烏圓. *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 營圓. *Yun Hui*: fanqie 幺圓 — sounding like 𢏮 (yuān).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Swirling water (回水). It follows 水 (water), pictographically — the left and right are the banks, the middle images the look of water." Guanzi, *Du di*: "water that comes out of the ground but does not flow is called an abyss (淵)."

**Senses.**
- *Deep (深).* Shijing, *Weifeng*: "holding the heart steadfast and deep (塞淵)."
- *A pool-name.* Shuijing zhu: "the Yi river, eastward, becomes the Yuan pool (淵潭)."
- *淵淵, the sound of drums.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "they beat the drums, boom-boom (淵淵)."
- *Surname.* The *Shiben* has the Qi grandee Yuan Hong (淵㰬).
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 一均, sounding like 蜵 (yuān). Also "deep."
