## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集中〉【木字部】頁514第11    〔古文〕：㮤、梥、庺【唐韻】詳容切【集韻】思恭切【正韻】息中切，𠀤音淞。木也。【字說】松，百木之長，猶公，故字从公。【禮·禮器】如松柏之有心也，故貫四時而不改柯易葉。【史記·龜筴傳】千歲之松，上有兔絲，下有茯苓。【摭遺】吳鄧夫人傷臉，以琥珀屑滅痕。【註】琥珀，松脂入地千年所化。　又甘松，香草名。【廣志】細葉，蔓生，可合諸香。又長松。【本草】一名仙茆，能治風。　又州名。【韻會】本河關之西南羌也，唐置松州。　又江名。松江，《禹貢》三江之一。或作淞。    考證：〔【禮·禮器】松柏之有心也，貫四時而不改柯易葉。〕　謹照原文松上增如字。貫上增故字。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, middle; Wood radical 木; p.514 #11)*

**Ancient graphs.** 㮤, 梥, 庺.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 詳容. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 思恭. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 息中 — sounding like 淞 (sōng). A tree.

**Etymology and senses.** *Zishuo*: "the pine (松) is the chief of the hundred trees, like a duke (公), hence the graph follows 公." Liji, *Li qi*: "as pine and cypress have a core (心), so they run through the four seasons without changing bough or shedding leaf." Shiji, *Biography of the Tortoise and Milfoil*: "a thousand-year pine (松) has dodder above and fu-ling fungus below." *Zhi yi*: "Lady Deng of Wu hurt her cheek, and erased the scar with powdered amber"; note: "amber is pine-resin (松脂) gone into the earth and transformed over a thousand years."

**Senses.**
- *甘松 (gansong), the name of a fragrant herb.* *Guangzhi*: "fine-leaved, creeping; it can be blended into all manner of incense." Also 長松 (changsong): *Bencao*: "also called the immortal's reed (仙茆), it can cure wind-ailments."
- *A prefecture-name.* *Yun Hui*: "originally the southwestern Qiang of Heguan; the Tang set up Songzhou (松州)."
- *A river-name.* The Song River (松江), one of the Three Rivers of the *Yu gong*; also written 淞.

**Textual note.** Liji, *Li qi*, "pine and cypress have a core, and run through the four seasons without changing bough or shedding leaf" — checked against the original, the word 如 is added before 松, and the word 故 before 貫.
