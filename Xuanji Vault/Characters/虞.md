## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集中〉【虍字部】頁1074第24    〔古文〕：𠈌、吳【唐韻】遇俱切【集韻】【韻會】元俱切，𠀤音愚。【說文】騶虞也。白虎黑文，尾長于身，仁獸，食自死之肉。【詩·周南】吁嗟乎騶虞。　又度也。【書·大禹謨】儆戒無虞。【左傳·桓十七年】疆埸之事，愼守其一，而備其不虞。　又安也。【儀禮·士虞禮註】士旣葬其父母，迎精而返，日中而祭之于𣩵宮以安之。　又誤也。【詩·魯頌】無貳無虞，上帝臨女。【疏】言天下歸周，無有貳心，無有疑誤。　又備也。【晉語】衞文公有郉翟之虞。　又樂也。【孟子】霸者之民，驩虞如也。【趙岐註】霸者行善䘏民，恩澤暴見易知，故民驩虞樂之也。　又【博雅】助也，望也，擇也。　又【玉篇】有也，專也。　又【正韻】慮也，測也。　又官名。【易·屯卦】卽鹿無虞。【註】謂虞官。【周禮·天官·大宰】虞衡，作山澤之材。【疏】掌山（澤者謂之虞）　又國名。【詩·大雅】虞芮質厥成。【左傳註】虞國，在河東大陽縣。又縣名。【晉書·地理志】虞縣屬梁國。　又姓。【潛夫論】帝舜姓虞。【左傳·昭三年】箕伯，直柄，虞遂，伯戲。【註】四人皆舜後。【通志·氏族略】禹封商均之子于虞城爲諸侯，後以國爲氏。　又虞淵，地名。【淮南子·天文訓】日至于虞淵，是爲高舂。　又【韻會】元具切，音遇。【揚雄·長楊賦】奉太尊之烈，遵文武之度，復三王之田，反五帝之虞。　又與㕦同。【史記·孝武帝紀】不虞不驁。索隱讀話。　又通吾。吾丘壽王。【水經注】作虞丘壽王。【王應麟·詩攷】鄒虞，或作騶吾。見《劉芳·詩義疏》。【直音】俗作虞。    考證：〔【揚雄·長楊賦】復三王之日。〕　謹照原文日改田。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, middle; Tiger radical 虍; p.1074 #24)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𠈌, 吳.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 遇俱. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 元俱 — sounding like 愚 (yú).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the zouyu (騶虞): a white tiger with black markings, its tail longer than its body, a benevolent beast that eats only the flesh of creatures already dead." Shijing, *Zhounan*: "ah, alas, the zouyu (騶虞)!"
- *Also, to anticipate, to reckon (度).* Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "be watchful and wary when there seems no cause for concern (無虞)." Zuozhuan, Huan yr 17: "in matters of the border, carefully guard your own and provide against the unforeseen (不虞)."
- *Also, to set at rest (安).* Note to Yili, *Shi yu li*: "when an officer has buried his father or mother, he welcomes the soul back, and at noon sacrifices to it in the funeral chamber to set it at rest (安之)."
- *Also, to err, to misgive (誤).* Shijing, *Lusong*: "without disloyalty, without misgiving (虞), God watches over you"; subcommentary: "it says all the world turns to Zhou, with no divided heart, no doubt or error."
- *Also, a matter requiring readiness (備).* Jin yu: "Duke Wen of Wey had the threat (虞) of Xing and the Di."
- *Also, joy (樂).* Mengzi: "the people of a hegemon are merry and content (驩虞)"; Zhao Qi's note: "the hegemon does good and cares for the people, his kindness plainly seen and easily known, so the people are glad and rejoice (驩虞) in it."
- *Also* Boya: "to help (助); to look toward (望); to choose (擇)."
- *Also* *Yupian*: "to possess (有); to control (專)."
- *Also* *Zheng Yun*: "to ponder (慮); to gauge (測)."
- *Also an office-name.* Yijing, *Zhun*: "going after deer without a forester (虞)"; note: "it means the forester-official (虞官)." Zhouli, *Tianguan, Da zai*: "the foresters and weights-officers (虞衡) prepare the materials of hill and marsh"; subcommentary: "those who have charge of hills and marshes are called 虞 (foresters)."
- *Also a state-name.* Shijing, *Daya*: "Yu and Rui (虞芮) brought their dispute to settlement." Zuozhuan note: "the state of Yu (虞) was in Daiyang county of Hedong." Also a county-name. Jin shu, *Treatise on Geography*: "Yu county (虞縣) belonged to the principality of Liang."
- *Also a surname.* Qianfu lun: "the Emperor Shun's surname was Yu (虞)." Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 3: "Jibo, Zhibing, Yusui (虞遂), Boxi"; note: "the four men were all descendants of Shun." Tongzhi, *Shizu lüe*: "Yu enfeoffed Shangjun's son at Yucheng (虞城) as a feudal lord, and his descendants took the state-name as their clan."
- *Also 虞淵 (Yuyuan), a place-name.* Huainanzi, *Tianwen*: "when the sun reaches Yuyuan (虞淵), this is the 'high-pounding' hour."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: fanqie 元具, sounding like 遇 (yù). Yang Xiong, *Changyang fu*: "upholding the glory of the Supreme Honored One, following the measures of Wen and Wu, restoring the hunting-grounds of the Three Kings and returning to the parks (虞) of the Five Emperors."
- *Also the same as 㕦.* Shiji, *Annals of Emperor Wu*: "neither boastful (虞) nor proud"; the Suoyin reads it 話.
- *Also interchangeable with 吾.* Wuqiu Shouwang (吾丘壽王) — the Shuijing zhu writes Yuqiu Shouwang (虞丘壽王). Wang Yinglin, *Shi kao*: "Zouyu (鄒虞) is sometimes written Zouwu (騶吾); see Liu Fang's Shiyi shu." The Zhiyin: vulgarly written 虞.

**Textual notes.** In the Yang Xiong, *Changyang fu* citation, 日 should read 田 (per the original).
