**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls з”і, upper; Grass radical иүё; p.1060 #12)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie еӮҚеҗ„, sounding like жіҠ (bГі). A thicket (жһ—и–„). Chuci note: "where forest-grass does not interlace is called и–„." Yang Xiong, *Ganquan fu*: "the new pheasants set out in the thicket (жһ—и–„)"; note: "grass growing in clusters is called и–„."

**Senses.**
- *A blind, a screen (з°ҫ).* Liji, *Qu li*: "outside the curtain and blind (её·и–„) one does not hasten." Shiji, *Hereditary House of Zhou Bo*: "Bo lived by weaving silkworm-trays (и–„жӣІ)"; Suoyin: "weaving silkworm-trays (и ¶и–„)."
- *Thin, the opposite of thick (еҺҡи–„).*
- *Little, scant (е°‘).* Shijing, *Zhou nan*: "lightly (и–„) I wash my robe."
- *A modest particle (иҒҠ).* Shijing, *Zhou nan*: "lightly (и–„) I gather it."
- *Slight, light (иј•).* Former Han, *Biography of Dong Zhongshu*: "grieving at the world's custom of laxity and slightness (йқЎи–„)."
- *To disdain, think little of (е«Ң).* Former Han, *Biography of Zhang Anshi*: "to slight me and forget old ties (и–„жң•еҝҳж•…)."
- *To strive, exert (еӢү).* Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "to strive; in Qin and Jin called йҮ—, or и–„, hence their colloquial и–„еҠӘ (to make effort), like еӢүеҠӘ."
- *Also* Boya: "weeds, foulness (и–ү)."
- *To gather, alight (йӣҶ).* Sima Xiangru, *Shanglin fu*: "they swarm and alight (еҘ„и–„) on the water-islets."
- *Also* Shiji, *Biography of Su Qin*: "the heart shaking like a hung pennant, with nowhere at last to settle (зөӮи–„)."
- *To reach, cover (иў«).* Shu, *Yi ji*: "outward it reaches (и–„) the four seas."
- *A water-name.* Shanhai jing: "Mount Chongwei вҖ” the Bo water (и–„ж°ҙ) issues there."
- *Surname.* Shiji, *Hereditary Houses of the Imperial Affines*: "Empress Dowager Bo's father, a man of Wu, of the Bo (и–„) surname."
- *A pavilion-name.* Later Han, *Treatise on Commanderies and Kingdoms*: "Xingyang has the Bo pavilion (и–„дәӯ)."
- *A plant-name.* и–„иҚ· (mint); see in full under the graph иҚ·.
- *Interchangeable with дәі.* Liji, *Jiao te sheng*: "the Bo altar (и–„зӨҫ) with north window"; note: "the Yin altar." Xunzi, *Yi bing*: "Tang by Bo (и–„), Wu by Hao."
- *Also* fanqie иҝ«еҗ„, sounding like еҚҡ (bГі). "To press (е»№)." Yijing, *Shuo gua*: "thunder and wind press on each other (зӣёи–„)." Zuozhuan, Xi yr 24: "to draw near (и–„) and look at it."
- *Approaching nightfall is и–„жҡ® (dusk).*
- *To encroach (дҫө).* Xunzi, *Tian lun*: "before cold and heat have pressed (и–„), there is sickness."
- *Also* fanqie еҢ№еҗ„, sounding like зІ• (pГІ). Shijing, *Qi feng*: "they drove fast, rumbling (и–„и–„)"; tradition: "the sound of fast driving."
- *Also* fanqie и–„йқ© вҖ” same as ж¬Ӯ, a wall-pillar. Erya, *Shi gong*: "the thin part above a roof is called the зӯ„."
- *Also* *Tang yun guyin*: read и’І (pГә). The Shu preface: "King Cheng having trodden down Yan, he was about to remove its lord to Pugu (и’Іе§‘)"; the Zuozhuan and Shiji write и–„е§‘.

**Textual note.** Xunzi, "*Tian lun* (еӨ©еҖ«): before cold and heat have pressed, there is sickness" вҖ” checked against the original work, it should read "*Tian lun* (еӨ©и«–).
