## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1060第12【唐韻】傍各切，音泊。林薄也。【楚辭註】林草不交錯曰薄。【揚雄·甘泉賦】列新雉于林薄。【註】草叢生曰薄。　又簾也。【禮·曲禮】帷薄之外不趨。【史記·周勃世家】勃以織薄曲爲生。【索隱曰】織蠶薄也。　又厚薄。　又少也。【詩·周南】薄澣我衣。　又聊也。【詩·周南】薄言采之。　又輕也。【前漢·董仲舒傳】愍世俗之靡薄。　又嫌也。【前漢·張安世傳】薄朕忘故。　又【揚子·方言】勉也。秦晉曰釗，或曰薄。故其鄙語曰薄努，猶勉努也。　又【博雅】薉也。　又集也。【司馬相如·上林賦】奄薄水渚。　又【史記·蘇秦傳】心搖搖如懸旌，而無所終薄。　又被也。【書·益稷】外薄四海。　又水名。【山海經】蟲尾之山，薄水出焉。　又姓。【史記·外戚世家】薄太后父，吳人，姓薄氏。　又亭名。【後漢·郡國志】滎陽有薄亭。　又草名。薄荷，見荷字註。　又通亳。【禮·郊特牲】薄社北牖。【註】殷社。【荀子·議兵篇】湯以薄，武以鎬。　又迫各切，音博。廹也。【易·說卦傳】雷風相薄。【左傳·僖二十四年】薄而觀之。　又迫晚曰薄暮。　又侵也。【荀子·天論篇】寒暑未薄而疾。　又匹各切，音粕。【詩·齊風】載驅薄薄。【傳】疾驅聲也。　又薄革切，同欂。壁柱也。【爾雅·釋宮】屋上薄謂之筄。　又【唐韻古音】讀蒲。《書》序：成王旣踐奄，將遷其君於蒲姑。《左傳》《史記》作薄姑。    考證：〔【荀子·天倫篇】寒暑未薄而疾。〕　謹照原書改天論篇。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1060 #12)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 傍各, sounding like 泊 (bó). A thicket (林薄). Chuci note: "where forest-grass does not interlace is called 薄." Yang Xiong, *Ganquan fu*: "the new pheasants set out in the thicket (林薄)"; note: "grass growing in clusters is called 薄."

**Senses.**
- *A blind, a screen (簾).* Liji, *Qu li*: "outside the curtain and blind (帷薄) one does not hasten." Shiji, *Hereditary House of Zhou Bo*: "Bo lived by weaving silkworm-trays (薄曲)"; Suoyin: "weaving silkworm-trays (蠶薄)."
- *Thin, the opposite of thick (厚薄).*
- *Little, scant (少).* Shijing, *Zhou nan*: "lightly (薄) I wash my robe."
- *A modest particle (聊).* Shijing, *Zhou nan*: "lightly (薄) I gather it."
- *Slight, light (輕).* Former Han, *Biography of Dong Zhongshu*: "grieving at the world's custom of laxity and slightness (靡薄)."
- *To disdain, think little of (嫌).* Former Han, *Biography of Zhang Anshi*: "to slight me and forget old ties (薄朕忘故)."
- *To strive, exert (勉).* Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "to strive; in Qin and Jin called 釗, or 薄, hence their colloquial 薄努 (to make effort), like 勉努."
- *Also* Boya: "weeds, foulness (薉)."
- *To gather, alight (集).* Sima Xiangru, *Shanglin fu*: "they swarm and alight (奄薄) on the water-islets."
- *Also* Shiji, *Biography of Su Qin*: "the heart shaking like a hung pennant, with nowhere at last to settle (終薄)."
- *To reach, cover (被).* Shu, *Yi ji*: "outward it reaches (薄) the four seas."
- *A water-name.* Shanhai jing: "Mount Chongwei — the Bo water (薄水) issues there."
- *Surname.* Shiji, *Hereditary Houses of the Imperial Affines*: "Empress Dowager Bo's father, a man of Wu, of the Bo (薄) surname."
- *A pavilion-name.* Later Han, *Treatise on Commanderies and Kingdoms*: "Xingyang has the Bo pavilion (薄亭)."
- *A plant-name.* 薄荷 (mint); see in full under the graph 荷.
- *Interchangeable with 亳.* Liji, *Jiao te sheng*: "the Bo altar (薄社) with north window"; note: "the Yin altar." Xunzi, *Yi bing*: "Tang by Bo (薄), Wu by Hao."
- *Also* fanqie 迫各, sounding like 博 (bó). "To press (廹)." Yijing, *Shuo gua*: "thunder and wind press on each other (相薄)." Zuozhuan, Xi yr 24: "to draw near (薄) and look at it."
- *Approaching nightfall is 薄暮 (dusk).*
- *To encroach (侵).* Xunzi, *Tian lun*: "before cold and heat have pressed (薄), there is sickness."
- *Also* fanqie 匹各, sounding like 粕 (pò). Shijing, *Qi feng*: "they drove fast, rumbling (薄薄)"; tradition: "the sound of fast driving."
- *Also* fanqie 薄革 — same as 欂, a wall-pillar. Erya, *Shi gong*: "the thin part above a roof is called the 筄."
- *Also* *Tang yun guyin*: read 蒲 (pú). The Shu preface: "King Cheng having trodden down Yan, he was about to remove its lord to Pugu (蒲姑)"; the Zuozhuan and Shiji write 薄姑.

**Textual note.** Xunzi, "*Tian lun* (天倫): before cold and heat have pressed, there is sickness" — checked against the original work, it should read "*Tian lun* (天論).
