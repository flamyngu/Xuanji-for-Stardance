## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1067第11【唐韻】【韻會】𠀤子皓切，音早。【說文】水草也。【詩·召南】于以采藻。【箋】藻之言澡也。【陸璣云】生水底。　又【班固·東都賦】鋪鴻藻。【陸機·文賦序】故作《文賦》，以述先士之盛藻。【註】《孔安國·尚書傳》曰：藻，水草之有文者，以喻文焉。　又【後漢·劉陶傳】武旅有鳧藻之士。【註】鳧得水藻，言喜悅也。　又【韻會】今屋上覆橑，謂之藻井。【風俗通】宮室象東井，刻荷蔆水草，所以厭火也。【張衡·西京賦】蒂倒茄于藻井。　又藻藉，所以薦玉者。詳藉字註。　又姓。【正字通】南北朝有藻重。　又【集韻】側絞切，音爪。義同，或作薻、𧅂。【干祿字書】俗作𦸼。又𧀉。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1067 #11)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 子皓 — sounding like 早 (zǎo).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a water-plant." Shijing, *Shaonan*: "wherewith to gather the water-weed (藻)"; Jian: "藻 is so called as if to wash clean (澡)." Lu Ji says: "it grows on the water's bottom."
- *Also* Ban Gu, *Dongdu fu*: "spreading out grand ornament (鴻藻)." Lu Ji, preface to the *Wen fu*: "so I composed the Wen fu, to set forth the splendid eloquence (盛藻) of the former masters"; note: "Kong Anguo's tradition to the Shang shu says: 藻 is a patterned water-plant, used to figure literary adornment."
- *Also* Later Han, *Biography of Liu Tao*: "the army had men joyful as ducks among the weed (鳧藻)"; note: "a duck getting water-weed — it means delight."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: "the covered rafters atop a building today are called the zaojing (藻井, the coffered ceiling)." Fengsu tong: "the palace chambers image the Eastern Well constellation, carved with lotus, water-chestnut, and water-plants, to ward off fire." Zhang Heng, *Xijing fu*: "an inverted lotus-stalk hangs from the coffered ceiling (藻井)."
- *Also 藻藉 (the jade-mat), that on which jade is presented*; see the note under the graph 藉.
- *Also a surname.* *Zhengzitong*: "the Northern and Southern Dynasties had Zao Zhong (藻重)."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 側絞, sounding like 爪 (zhǎo); same sense; sometimes written 薻, 𧅂.

**On the graph.** Ganlu zishu: vulgarly written 𦸼; also 𧀉.
