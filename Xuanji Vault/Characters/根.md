## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集中〉【木字部】頁524第07【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤古痕切，音跟。【說文】木株也。【左傳·隱六年】農夫之去草，絕其本根，勿使能殖。　又【廣韻】根，柢也。【老子·道德經】重爲輕根。【管子·地形篇】地者，萬物之本原，諸生之根菀。　又【博雅】始也。　又天根星也。《周語》天根見而水涸。註亢氐之間。　又金根，車名。【後漢·輿服志】天子車金根。　又門之鋪首銅鍰曰倉琅根。【前漢·五行志】木門倉琅根。　又竹根，杯名。【晉庾信報惠酒詩】山杯捧竹根。　又雲根，山名。【宋孝武登作樂山詩】積水溺雲根。　又姓。【姓苑】周人根牟子，善著書。　又叶經天切，音堅。【三略軍讖】侵侮下民，國內譁諠。臣蔽不言，是謂亂根。    考證：〔勿使能植。〕　謹照左傳原文植改殖。〔天根，氐星也。【左傳·桓十四年】天根見而水涸。〕　謹左傳無天根見而水涸之語，查係周語。今據改天根星也。周語天根見而水涸。註亢氐之間。〔【前漢·五行志】木名倉琅根。〕　謹照原文名改門。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, middle; Wood radical 木; p.524 #07)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 古痕, sounding like 跟 (gēn).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "The stem of a tree (木株)." Zuozhuan, Yin yr 6: "the farmer, in clearing weeds, cuts off the very root (本根), so that they cannot grow again."

**Senses.**
- *A root, base (柢).* *Guangyun*. Laozi, *Dao de jing*: "the heavy is the root (根) of the light." Guanzi, *Di xing*: "the earth is the source of the myriad things, the root from which all that lives springs (根菀)."
- *The beginning (始).* Boya.
- *天根 (the Heaven-root), a star.* *Discourses of Zhou*: "when the Heaven-root (天根) appears, the waters dry up"; note: "between the Kang and Di lodges."
- *金根 (the golden-root), a carriage-name.* Later Han, *Treatise on Carriages and Robes*: "the Son of Heaven's carriage is the golden-root (金根)."
- *The bronze knocker-ring of a door is called 倉琅根.* Former Han, *Treatise on the Five Phases*: "the wooden door's cang-lang root (倉琅根)."
- *竹根 (bamboo-root), a cup-name.* Jin, Yu Xin, *Poem thanking for a gift of wine*: "the mountain cup lifts the bamboo-root (竹根)."
- *雲根 (cloud-root), a mountain-name.* Song, Emperor Xiaowu, *Poem climbing Zuoyue Mountain*: "the gathered waters drown the cloud-root (雲根)."
- *Surname.* *Xingyuan*: "Gen Mouzi of the Zhou, skilled at writing books."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 堅 (jiān), fanqie 經天.* *San lüe junchen*: "to oppress and insult the lowly people, and the realm rings with clamour; ministers screen and do not speak — this is called the root of disorder (亂根)."

**Textual notes.** "do not let them grow (植)" — checked against the Zuozhuan original, "植" should read "殖." · "the Heaven-root is a Di-star; Zuozhuan, Huan yr 14: when the Heaven-root appears, the waters dry up" — the Zuozhuan has no such words; it belongs to the *Discourses of Zhou*, and is corrected to: "天根星也. *Discourses of Zhou*: when the Heaven-root appears, the waters dry up; note: between the Kang and Di lodges." · Former Han, *Treatise on the Five Phases*, "the wooden name (名) cang-lang root" — checked against the original, "名" should read "門" (door).
