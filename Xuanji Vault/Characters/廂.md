## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈寅集下〉【广字部】頁348第32【唐韻】【正韻】息良切【集韻】【韻會】思將切，𠀤音箱。【說文】廊也。【玉篇】東西序也。【史記·周昌傳】呂后側耳於東廂聽。【註】殿東堂也。索隱曰：正寢之東西室，皆號曰廂，言似箱篋之形。　又通作箱。【儀禮·公食大夫禮】公揖退于箱。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 寅, lower; Shelter radical 广; p.348 #32)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 息良. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 思將 — sounding like 箱 (xiāng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a corridor, a side-gallery." *Yupian*: "the eastern and western wings (序)." Shiji, *Biography of Zhou Chang*: "Empress Lü, in the eastern wing (東廂), turned her ear to listen"; note: "the eastern hall of the palace"; the Suoyin: "the eastern and western chambers of the main hall are both called 廂, said to resemble the shape of a box or hamper (箱篋)."
- *Interchangeable with 箱.* Yili, *Gong shi dafu li*: "the duke bows and withdraws to the wing (箱).
