## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集上〉【丿字部】頁82第07    〔古文〕：虖【廣韻】戸吳切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】洪孤切，𠀤音湖。【說文】兮語之餘也。从兮，象聲上越揚之形。【徐曰】凡名兮皆上句之餘聲。【廣韻】極也。　又疑辭。【詩·邶風】胡爲乎中露。【戰國策】彈鋏歸來乎。　又呼聲。如魯論，參乎，使乎之類。　又荒烏切。與呼同。【詩·大雅】於乎小子。【陸德明·音義】於音烏，乎音呼。【吳越春秋】越王夫人歌曰：徊復翔兮，游飇去復反兮。於乎，今經史於戲，於虖，嗚虖，嗚嘑，於乎相通，皆歎辭。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, upper; Slash radical 丿; p.82 #07)*

**Ancient graph.** 虖.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 戸吳. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 洪孤 — sounding like 湖 (hú).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the remnant of the [particle] 兮 in speech; it follows 兮, imaging the shape of the sound rising and lifting." Xu [Kai]: "all particles called 兮 are the trailing sound of the preceding phrase." *Guangyun*: "the utmost [pitch]."
- *An interrogative particle.* Shijing, *Beifeng*: "why (乎) in the dew?" Zhanguo ce: "strumming my sword-hilt, 'let us go home (乎)!'"
- *A vocative cry.* As in the Lu Lunyu: "Shen (參乎)!", "what an envoy (使乎)!" and the like.
- *Fanqie 荒烏 — same as 呼 (to sigh, exclaim).* Shijing, *Daya*: "ah (於乎), young man!"; Lu Deming, *Yinyi*: "於 sounds 烏, 乎 sounds 呼." The Wu Yue chunqiu: the Yue king's lady sang, "wheeling and circling, the gusting wind goes and returns." In the classics and histories 於戲, 於虖, 嗚虖, 嗚嘑, and 於乎 are all interchangeable, all exclamations of sighing.
