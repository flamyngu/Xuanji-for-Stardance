## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈戌集中〉【阜字部】頁1358第17【唐韻】古核切【集韻】【韻會】各核切【正韻】各頟切，𠀤音膈。【說文】障也。【玉篇】塞也。【史記·秦始皇紀】防隔內外。【前漢·常惠傳】欲隔絕漢。　又通作鬲。【荀子·大略篇】鬲如也。【註】謂鬲絕於上。【史記·大宛傳】鬲漢道焉。【前漢·薛宣傳】西州鬲絕。又【陸厥與沈約書】一家之文，工拙壤鬲。　又【韻會小補】叶訖得切，音祴。【柳宗元·懲咎賦】讒妬結而不戒兮，猶斷斷於所執。勢危疑而多詐兮，逢天地之否隔。○按正字通引柳賦云，質韻音屹，固非。而韻會小補叶訖得切，亦謬。得在職韻，執在緝韻，職緝不相通，以隔叶執，不當作訖得切。　又與融通。【史記·秦始皇紀】昭隔內外，靡不淸淨，施於後嗣。【註】隔，一作融。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 戌, middle; Mound radical 阜; p.1358 #17)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 古核. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 各核. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 各頟 — sounding like 膈 (gé).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to bar, screen off (障)." *Yupian*: "to block (塞)."
- *To wall off, separate.* Shiji, *Annals of the First Emperor of Qin*: "to wall off and separate (隔) inner and outer." Former Han, *Biography of Chang Hui*: "wishing to cut off (隔絕) the Han."
- *Used interchangeably with 鬲.* Xunzi, *Dalüe*: "barred off (鬲), as it were"; note: "it means cut off (鬲) above." Shiji, *Account of Dayuan*: "they blocked (鬲) the road to Han." Former Han, *Biography of Xue Xuan*: "the western province was cut off (鬲絕)." Lu Jue, *Letter to Shen Yue*: "in one man's writing, the skilled and the clumsy are as far apart (壤鬲) as soil from heaven."
- *Yun Hui xiaobu*: rhymes, fanqie 訖得, sounding like 祴 (gé). Liu Zongyuan, *Fu on Punishing My Faults*: "slander and envy knot and take no warning, yet I hold firm and firm to what I grasp; the times being perilous, doubtful, and full of deceit, I meet the closure (否隔) of Heaven and Earth."
- *Used interchangeably with 融.* Shiji, *Annals of the First Emperor of Qin*: "brightly pervading (隔) inner and outer, nothing not pure and clean, handed on to later heirs"; note: "隔 is in one text written 融."

**Editorial notes.** A note: the Zhengzitong, citing Liu's fu, gives "質 rhyme, sounding like 屹" — this is certainly wrong; and the Yun Hui xiaobu's "fanqie 訖得" is mistaken too. 得 is in the 職 rhyme, 執 in the 緝 rhyme; 職 and 緝 do not interchange, so to rhyme 隔 with 執 one ought not to write "fanqie 訖得."
