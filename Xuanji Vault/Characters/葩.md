## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1045第32【唐韻】普巴切，帊平聲。【說文】華也。【張衡·西京賦】披紅葩之狎獵。　又【韻補】叶滂禾切，音坡。【張衡·思𤣥賦】天地絪縕，百卉含葩。叶下和字韻。【類篇】一作苩，一作皅。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1045 #32)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 普巴 — the level tone of 帊 (pā).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a flower (華)." Zhang Heng, *Fu on the Western Capital*: "spreading the red blossoms (紅葩) in close-ranked profusion."
- *Yunbu*: rhymes as 坡 (pō), fanqie 滂禾. Zhang Heng, *Sixuan fu*: "Heaven and Earth in teeming mingling, the hundred plants holding their blossoms (葩)" — rhyming with 和 below.

**On the graph.** Leipian: "written in one form 苩, in another 皅."
