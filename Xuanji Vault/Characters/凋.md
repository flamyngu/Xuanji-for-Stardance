## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集下〉【冫字部】頁132第44【唐韻】都聊切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】丁聊切，𠀤音貂。【說文】半傷也。【木華·海賦】爲凋爲瘵。又【韻會】通作彫。【論語】歲寒，然後知松柏之後彫。又【正韻】通作雕。【晉書·李重傳】雕弊之迹。又【玉篇】力盡貌。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Ice radical 冫; p.132 #44)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 都聊. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 丁聊 — sounding like 貂 (diāo).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "half-withered, half-blighted." Mu Hua, *Fu on the Sea*: "it makes things wither (凋) and sicken."
- *Interchangeable with 彫.* *Yun Hui.* Lunyu: "only when the year turns cold do we know the pine and cypress are the last to wither (彫)."
- *Interchangeable with 雕.* *Zheng Yun.* Jin shu, *Biography of Li Zhong*: "the traces of decay and ruin (雕弊)."
- *The look of strength spent.* *Yupian.*
