## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集下〉【止字部】頁578第07    〔古文〕：𤾤【唐韻】舉韋切【集韻】居韋切，𠀤音騩。還也，入也。【詩·小雅】薄音旋歸。　又還所取之物亦曰歸。【春秋·定十年】齊人來歸鄆、讙、龜隂田。又【禮·祭義】父母全而生之，予全而歸之。【孟子】久假而不歸。皆還復之義。　又【春秋·隱元年】歸惠公仲子之賵。【杜註】歸者，不反之辭。【桓七年】突歸于鄭。【穀梁傳】歸，易辭也。　又依歸也。【詩·曹風】于我歸處。【毛傳】歸，依歸也。　又歸附也。【穀梁傳·莊二年】王者，民之所歸往也。【詩·大雅】豈弟君子，民之攸歸。　又【說文】女嫁也。【詩·周南】之子于歸。【禮·禮運】男有分，女有歸。又【穀梁傳·隱二年】婦人謂嫁曰歸，反曰來歸。【註】嫁而曰歸，明外屬也。反曰來歸，明從外至也。【左傳·莊二十七年】凡諸侯之女歸寧曰來，出曰來歸。夫人歸寧曰如某，出曰歸于某。　又投也，委也。【左傳·襄三年】請歸死于司敗。又【前漢·申屠嘉傳】鼂錯恐自歸景帝。【註】師古曰：自首于天子。　又與也，許也。【論語】天下歸仁焉。　又合也。【禮·緇衣】私惠不歸德。【註】謂不合於德義。　又終也。【左傳·宣十一年】以討召諸侯，而以貪歸之。　又歸妹，卦名。　又三歸，臺名。【史記註】三歸，取三姓女也。　又指趨曰歸。【易·繫辭】殊途而同歸。【史記·李斯傳】覩指而識歸。　又道家有八歸。【參同契】九還、七返、八歸、六居。【註】八歸者，天三生木，地八成汞，戊己一合，木汞之眞，歸煉鼎中，故曰八歸。　又【謝察微·算經】有歸法，歸已入之數也。　又歸藏，黃帝《易》名。一曰殷《易》。【周禮·春官】大卜掌三《易》之灋，二曰歸藏。【註】歸藏者，萬物莫不歸而藏之于中。此《易》以純坤爲首，故名。　又【爾雅·釋親】女子謂晜弟之子爲姪，謂姪之子爲歸孫。　又饋也。【論語】歸孔子豚。【晉語】不腆敝邑之禮，敢歸諸下執政。　又山名。【山海經】太行之山，其首曰歸山，其上有金玉，其下有碧。　又州名。【廣韻】本春秋夔子國，武德初，割夔州之秭歸巴東二縣，置州，取歸國爲名也。【廣輿記】今屬荆州府。　又姓。　又歸邪，星氣名。【前漢·天文志】如星非星，如雲非雲，名曰歸邪。歸邪出，必有歸國者，邪音虵。　又忘歸，矢名，見【公孫子】。　又姊歸，鳥名。當歸，藥名。　又【集韻】求位切，音匱。同饋。【說文】餉也。亦讀如字。義見上。籀省作㱕。漢𥓓作歸。    考證：〔【周禮·春官】大占掌三易之法。〕　謹照原文占改卜。法改灋。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, lower; Stop radical 止; p.578 #07)*

**Ancient graph.** 𤾤.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 舉韋. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 居韋 — sounding like 騩 (guī).

**Etymology and senses.** "To return; to go in." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "and so I turn and go home (歸)."
- *To give back what one has taken.* Chunqiu, Ding yr 10: "the men of Qi came and restored (歸) the fields of Yun, Huan, and Guiyin." Liji, *Jiyi*: "father and mother gave him birth whole, and he gives it back (歸) whole." Mengzi: "long borrowing and never returning (歸)" — all the sense of giving back.
- *A term for what is not returned.* Chunqiu, Yin yr 1: "[Zai of Zhou] sent (歸) the funeral-gifts for Duke Hui and Zhongzi"; Du's note: "歸 is a term for what is not returned." Huan yr 7: "Tu returned (歸) to Zheng"; Guliang zhuan: "歸 is a term of [restoration made] easy."
- *To rely on, turn to.* Shijing, *Caofeng*: "with me he finds his home (歸處)"; Mao's tradition: "歸 means to turn to and rely on."
- *To submit, give allegiance.* Guliang zhuan, Zhuang yr 2: "the king is he to whom the people turn (歸往)." Shijing, *Daya*: "the genial gentleman, he to whom the people turn (歸)."
- *A woman's marrying.* *Shuowen.* Shijing, *Zhounan*: "this girl goes to her home (于歸)." Liji, *Liyun*: "men have their portions, women their homes (歸)." Guliang zhuan, Yin yr 2: "a woman's marrying is 歸, her return [divorced] 來歸"; note: "marrying is 歸, marking her belonging outside; the return is 來歸, marking her coming from outside." Zuozhuan, Zhuang yr 27: "of a lord's daughter, going home to visit is 來, being sent away 來歸; of a married lady, visiting is 'going to So-and-so,' being put away 'returned to So-and-so.'"
- *To cast oneself upon, entrust.* Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 3: "I beg to deliver myself (歸) to death before the Minister of Justice." Former Han, *Biography of Shentu Jia*: "Chao Cuo, in fear, gave himself up (自歸) to Emperor Jing"; note: "to surrender oneself to the Son of Heaven."
- *To grant, assent (與, 許).* Lunyu: "the world will ascribe benevolence (歸仁) to him."
- *To accord with (合).* Liji, *Ziyi*: "private favours do not accord (歸) with virtue"; note: "they do not accord with virtue and right."
- *To end in (終).* Zuozhuan, Xuan yr 11: "he summoned the lords in the name of chastisement, and ended (歸) it in greed."
- *歸妹, a hexagram-name.*
- *三歸, a terrace-name.* Note to the Shiji: "三歸 means taking women of three surnames."
- *The drift, tendency.* Yijing, *Xici*: "different roads but the same goal (歸)." Shiji, *Biography of Li Si*: "seeing the gist, one knows the drift (歸)."
- *八歸, the Daoist 'eight returns.'* The Cantong qi: "the nine recoveries, the seven reversions, the eight returns (八歸), the six abidings"; note: "Heaven's three begets Wood, Earth's eight completes mercury; *wu* and *ji* once united, the true of wood and mercury returns to be smelted in the cauldron, hence 'eight returns.'"
- *歸法, the dividing method.* Xie Chawei's Suanjing: "the 'dividing method (歸法)' — dividing a number already entered."
- *歸藏, a name of the Yellow Emperor's Yi (some say the Yin Yi).* Zhouli, *Chunguan*: "the Grand Diviner keeps the methods of the Three Yi; the second is the Guizang"; note: "the myriad things all return (歸) and are stored (藏) within; this Yi takes pure Kun as its head, hence the name."
- *歸孫, a return-grandson.* Erya, *Shiqin*: "a woman calls her brothers' sons 姪 (nephews), and a nephew's son a 歸孫."
- *Same as 饋 (to give food).* Lunyu: "[Yang Huo] sent Confucius a [steamed] pig (歸)." The Jinyu: "the poor courtesy of our town — I venture to present (歸) it to your officers."
- *A mountain-name.* Shanhaijing: "of the Taihang range, the first is Gui Mountain (歸山); above it gold and jade, below it green-stone."
- *A prefecture-name.* *Guangyun*: "originally the Spring-and-Autumn state of Kui; in the early Wude era the two counties Zigui and Badong of Kuizhou were split off to make a prefecture, named Guizhou (歸州) after the state of Gui." Guangyu ji: "now belonging to Jingzhou prefecture."
- *A surname.*
- *歸邪, a star-vapour.* Former Han, *Treatise on Astronomy*: "like a star yet not a star, like a cloud yet not a cloud, named Guixie (歸邪); when it appears, there will surely be one returning to his state; 邪 sounds 虵."
- *忘歸, an arrow-name (see the Gongsunzi); 姊歸, a bird-name; 當歸, a medicine-name.*
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 求位, sounding like 匱 (kuì) — same as 饋. *Shuowen*: "to send food"; also read as written. (The *zhou* contraction is 㱕; the Han steles write 歸.)

**Textual note.** "大占掌三易之法" should read "大卜掌三易之灋".
