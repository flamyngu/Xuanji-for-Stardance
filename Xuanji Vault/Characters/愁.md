## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁393第21【唐韻】士尤切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】鋤尤切，𠀤音㵞。【說文】憂也。从心秋聲。【廣韻】悲也。【增韻】慮也。　又【正韻】卽由切，音揫。【禮·鄕飮酒義】秋心爲言愁也。【註】愁讀爲揫。　又【集韻】將由切。與楢同。【說文】楢，聚也。或作愁。　又【集韻】財勞切，音曹。揚雄有畔牢愁。　又叶士交切，音巢。【道藏歌】椿齡會足衰，劫往豈足遼。虛刀揮至空，鄙滯五神愁。或書作𢝲。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.393 #21)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 士尤. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 鋤尤 — sounding like 㵞 (chóu).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "grief; it follows 心 (heart) with 秋 (autumn) as phonetic." *Guangyun*: "sorrowful." *Zengyun*: "anxious thought."
- *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 卽由, sounding like 揫 (jiū). Liji, *Xiang yinjiu yi*: "the 'autumn-heart' (秋心) as a word is 愁"; note: "愁 is read 揫 [to gather in]."
- *Same as 楢.* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 將由. *Shuowen*: "楢 means to gather; sometimes written 愁."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 財勞, sounding like 曹 (cáo). Yang Xiong wrote the *Panlao chou* (畔牢愁).

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 士交, sounding like 巢 (cháo).* The Daozang hymn: "...the empty blade swept to the void, base and clogged, the five spirits in grief (愁)." Sometimes written 𢝲.
