**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls еҜ…, lower; Shelter radical е№ҝ; p.351 #02)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie ж–№иӮә. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie ж”ҫеҗ  вҖ” sounding like зҷҲ (fГЁi).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "A house collapsed (еұӢй “)."

**Senses.**
- *To stop, cease (жӯў).* Erya, *Shi gu*; note: "to halt, to stay." Liji, *Qu li*: "in all sacrifices, where one has dropped (е»ў) a rite, none dare revive it; where one has taken up a rite, none dare drop (е»ў) it."
- *To set aside, lay down (иҲҚ).* Erya, *Shi gu*; note: "иҲҚ means to put aside." Zhouli, *Tian guan, Da zai*: "of the eight handles, the seventh is dismissal (е»ў), to control their crimes"; note: "е»ў is like to cast off (ж”ҫ)." Also: "of the eight rules, the third is dismissing and appointing (е»ўзҪ®), to control their officers."
- *Great (еӨ§).* Erya, *Shi gu*. Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "made into a ruinous levy (е»ўзҲІж®ҳиіҰ)"; tradition: "е»ў means excessive (еҝІ)"; *Shiwen*: "one text writes е»ў, 'great.'"
- *To fall (еў®).* Zuozhuan, Ding yr 3: "the Viscount of Zhu threw himself from the bed and fell (е»ў) onto the brazier-coals"; note: "е»ў means to fall."
- *Also* *Zhuanzhu guyin*: sounded зҷј (fДҒ). Former Han, *Suburban Sacrifice Songs*: "the western brightness, vast and boundless; the autumn breath, stern and slaying; holding their bloom and bowing their ears, the old is continued and not abandoned (дёҚе»ў)."
- *Interchangeable with зҷҲ.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 14: "Sikong Laoqi and LГј Gui feigned crippling sickness (зҷҲз–ҫ)."
- *Interchangeable with ж’Ҙ.* Zhouli, *Kao gong ji, Zi ren*: "starting up (ж’ҘзҲҫ) in anger"; note: "the old text writes ж’Ҙ as е»ў; Zheng Sinong says: е»ў is read as ж’Ҙ."

**Textual notes.** *Shuowen*, "a house toppled (еұӢеӮҫ)" вҖ” checked against the original, "еӮҫ" should read "й “." В· Zhouli, *Kao gong ji, Zi ren*, "еҝ…ж’Ҙ in anger" вҖ” examining the original sense, the word еҝ… may be dropped but зҲҫ may not; it is corrected to ж’ҘзҲҫиҖҢжҖ’.
