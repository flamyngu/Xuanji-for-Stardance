**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集下〉【辵字部】頁1260第06【唐韻】於爲切【集韻】【韻會】邕危切【正韻】烏爲切，𠀤委平聲。【說文】逶迤，衺去貌。【漢·郊祀歌】票然逝，旗逶蛇。【正字通】逶迤，別作逶迱、𨖿迆、委移、倭侇，載在史傳詩賦者，各家文畫雖異，其音義則同也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, lower; Walk radical 辵; p.1260 #06)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 於爲. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 邕危. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 烏爲 — the level tone of 委 (wēi).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "逶迤, the look of winding away aslant." Han, *Jiaosi ge*: "swiftly it passes, the banners winding (逶蛇)."

**On the graph.** *Zhengzitong*: "逶迤 is also written 逶迱, 𨖿迆, 委移, 倭侇 — recorded in the histories, poems, and rhapsodies; though the writers' graphs differ, the sound and sense are the same.
