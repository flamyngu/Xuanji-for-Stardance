## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈午集上〉【玉字部】頁731第19    〔古文〕：𤼬【廣韻】布還切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】逋還切，𠀤音頒。【書·堯典】班瑞于羣后。　又【爾雅·釋言】班，賦也。【註】謂布與。【書·洪範】武王旣勝殷，邦諸侯班宗彝。【傳】賦宗廟彝器酒罇賜諸侯。【左傳·襄二十六年】班荆相與食。【註】班，布也。【公羊傳·僖三十一年】晉侯執曹伯，班其所取侵地于諸侯。　又【博雅】班，秩序也。【左傳·文六年】趙孟曰：辰嬴賤班在九人。【註】班，位也。　又【集韻】次也。【左傳·桓六年】諸侯之大夫戍齊，齊人饋之餼，使魯爲其班。【註】班，次也。　又【集韻】別也。【左傳·襄十八年】有班馬之聲。【註】班，別也。夜遁馬不相見，故作離別聲也。　又徧也。【晉語】車班外內，順以訓之。【註】班，徧也。　又【揚子·方言】班，徹列也。北燕曰班，東齊曰徹。【易·屯卦】乗馬班如。【疏】六四應初，故乗馬也。慮二妨已路，故初時班如旋也。【書·大禹謨】班師振旅。　又雜色也。【禮·王制】班白者不提挈。【註】雜色曰班。　又班班，車聲。【後漢·五行志】車班班入河閒。　又姓。【風俗通】楚令尹鬪班之後。　又縣名。【前漢·地理志】班氏。【註】屬代郡。　又班茅，蟲名。【古今注】藥種有五物，五曰班茅，戎鹽解之。　又【集韻】或作辨。【前漢·王莽傳】辨社諸侯。　又【韻會】通作般。【左傳·成十三年】鄭公子班自訾求入于大宮。【釋文】班，本亦作般。【前漢·郊祀歌】先以雨，般裔裔。【註】先以雨，言神欲行，令雨先驅也。般，讀與班同，布也。裔裔，飛流之貌。　又【韻補】叶𤰞連切，音鞭。【何晏·景福殿賦】光明熠爚，文彩璘班，淸風萃而成響，朝日曜而增鮮。【廣韻】俗作𤦦。【正韻】亦作頒朌。    考證：〔又姓。【風俗通】楚令尹闕班之後。〕　謹照廣韻所引風俗通，闕班改鬪班。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, upper; Jade radical 玉; p.731 #19)*

**Ancient graph.** 𤼬.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 布還. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 逋還 — sounding like 頒 (bān). Shu, *Yao dian*: "he distributed (班) the auspicious tokens among the host of lords."

**Etymology and senses.** Erya, *Shi yan*: "班 means to bestow (賦)"; note: "to hand out, to give (布與)." Shu, *Hong fan*: "when King Wu had conquered Yin, he dealt out (班) the ancestral vessels among the feudal lords"; tradition: "he bestowed the ancestral-temple ritual vessels and wine-jars on the feudal lords." Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 26: "they spread brushwood (班荆) and ate together"; note: "班 means to spread out (布)." Gongyang zhuan, Xi yr 31: "the Marquis of Jin seized the Earl of Cao and parcelled out (班) the encroached lands he had taken among the feudal lords."

**Senses.**
- *Rank, order (秩序).* Boya. Zuozhuan, Wen yr 6: "Zhao Meng said: 'Chen Ying's low rank (班) is among the nine'"; note: "班 means position."
- *Order, sequence (次).* *Ji Yun*. Zuozhuan, Huan yr 6: "the grandees of the feudal lords garrisoned Qi, and the men of Qi sent them provisions, making Lu set their order (班)"; note: "班 means sequence."
- *To part, separate (別).* *Ji Yun*. Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 18: "there was the sound of parting horses (班馬)"; note: "班 means to part; fleeing by night, the horses could not see one another and so made the sound of separation."
- *Everywhere, all round (徧).* *Discourses of Jin*: "the carriages went all round (班), within and without, and so he instructed them"; note: "班 means everywhere."
- *Also* Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "班 means to draw up in rank (徹列). In Northern Yan it is 班, in Eastern Qi 徹." Yijing, *Zhun* hexagram: "mounting the horse, he draws back (班如)"; subcommentary: "the fourth six responds to the first, hence mounting the horse; fearing that the second blocks his path, at first he draws back and wheels round." Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "he withdrew the army (班師) and mustered the troops."
- *Mottled, of mixed colour (雜色).* Liji, *Wang zhi*: "the grey-streaked (班白) do not carry burdens"; note: "mixed colour is called 班."
- *班班, the sound of carriages.* Later Han, *Treatise on the Five Phases*: "the carriages rumbling (班班) enter Hejian."
- *Surname.* *Fengsu tong*: "descendants of Dou Ban, chief minister of Chu."
- *A county-name.* Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "Banshi (班氏)"; note: "it belongs to Dai commandery."
- *班茅 (banmao), the name of an insect.* *Gujin zhu*: "among the five medicinal kinds, the fifth is the banmao (班茅); rock-salt neutralizes it."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: sometimes written 辨. Former Han, *Biography of Wang Mang*: "he arrayed (辨) the altars of the feudal lords."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: "interchangeable with 般." Zuozhuan, Cheng yr 13: "Prince Ban (班) of Zheng, from Zi, sought to enter the great temple"; *Shiwen*: "班 is also written 般." Former Han, *Suburban Sacrifice Songs*: "first with rain, spreading and streaming (般裔裔)"; note: "'first with rain' means the spirit is about to move, and bids the rain run before it; 般 is read the same as 班, 'to spread'; 裔裔 is the look of flying and flowing."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 鞭 (biān), fanqie 𤰞連.* He Yan, *Jingfu dian fu*: "the light gleaming and glittering, the patterns shimmering and mottled (璘班); the clear wind gathers and forms an echo, the morning sun shines and adds freshness." *Guangyun*: commonly written 𤦦. *Zheng Yun*: also written 頒 or 朌.

**Textual note.** "Surname. *Fengsu tong*: descendants of Que Ban (闕班), chief minister of Chu" — checked against the *Fengsu tong* as cited in the *Guangyun*, "Que Ban (闕班)" should read "Dou Ban (鬪班)."
