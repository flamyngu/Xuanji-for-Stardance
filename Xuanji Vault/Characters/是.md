**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, upper; Sun radical 日; p.493 #26)*

**Ancient graph.** 𣆞.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 承紙. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 上紙 — sounding like 姼 (shì).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "written 昰; straight, upright (直). From 日 (sun) and 正 (correct)." *Shiming*: "是 is 嗜 (to relish) — people relish and delight in it." *Yupian*: "是, [as in] 是非 (right and wrong)." Liji, *Qu li*: "ritual is that by which to fix the near and far, settle doubts, distinguish the same and different, and make clear right and wrong (是非)."
- *This (此).* *Boya.* Yijing, *Qian*: "unapproved (是) yet without distress"; also, "therefore (是故) he dwells in high place yet is not proud."
- *Surname.* *Xingshi jijiu pian*: the Shi clan — Wu had Shi Yi, the Tang had Shi Guang.
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 田黎, sounding like 題 (tí). Gongyang zhuan, Xi yr 16: "what is '是月 (this month)'? barely reaching this month"; note: "是, 'the edge of the month' — a Lu expression"; *Shiwen*: "是 in usual sound, one reading fanqie 徒兮."
- *Interchangeable with 氏.* Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "氏 was Duke Zhuang"; note: "氏 same as 是; anciently interchangeable."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 市之.* Cai Yong, *Shihui*: "I only grieve — alas! how can it be thus (是)?"
