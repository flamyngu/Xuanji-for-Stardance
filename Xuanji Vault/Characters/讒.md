**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls й…ү, upper; Speech radical иЁҖ; p.1187 #09)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie еЈ«е’ё. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie йӢӨе’ё вҖ” sounding like йҘһ (chГЎn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to slander, defame (иӯ–)." *Yupian*: "to flatter perniciously (дҪһ)." *Zhengzitong*: "to embellish evil words, ruining the good and harming the able."
- *Slander, calumny.* Shu, *Shun dian*: "I detest slanderous (и®’) speech and pernicious conduct." Zhuangzi, *Yufu*: "to delight in speaking of others' faults is called slander (и®’)." Xunzi, *Xiushen*: "to injure the good is called slander (и®’)." Shuoyuan, *Chen shu*: "he who obscures the good is the slanderer (и®’) of the state."
- *A star-name.* Jin shu, *Treatise on Astronomy*: "among the six stars of Juanshe (Curled Tongue), one is called Heaven's Slanderer (еӨ©и®’), governing shamans and physicians."
- *A cauldron-name.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 3: "the inscription on the Chan cauldron (и®’йјҺ)"; subcommentary: "the Chan cauldron is the cauldron that loathes slander, the same as the Chong cauldron named in the *Mingtang wei*. One account: и®’ is a place-name вҖ” Yu cast the Nine Cauldrons at the land of Ganchan (з”ҳи®’), hence 'Chan cauldron.'"
- *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*: fanqie еЈ«жҮә. *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie еЈ«зӣЈ вҖ” the departing tone of йҘһ (chГ n); same sense.
- *Rhymes as еҙҮ (chГіng), fanqie йүҸеј“.* Chuci, *Jiu tan*: "I breathe in the pure essence and breathe out the murky vapours; crossing a crooked age, I will not court favour; I walk knocking at sincerity and do not fawn вҖ” and so am thrust aside and meet with slander (и®’)."
- *Rhymes as еҡҙ (yГЎn), fanqie йҠҖзӮҺ.* Liang Hong, *Poem on Going to Wu*: "I would mount and whip my horse and gallop far away, sick at my age's ways, which I make my reproach (и®’); they vie to raise the crooked and set aside the straight, all putting flattery first, fawning on and on (е”Ңе”Ң)" вҖ” е”Ң sounds like е»¶.

**On the graph.** The Sushu zhengwu: "[writing it] with two е…Қ is wrong." [Variant:] дңӣ.
