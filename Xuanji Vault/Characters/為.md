**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, middle; Claw radical 爪; p.689 #13)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𦥮, 𤓸.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 薳支. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 于嬀 — sounding like 潙 (wéi).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "A mother-monkey. As a beast it is fond of its claws; the claw-element images the mother-monkey, and the lower [part] is the mother-monkey's belly-shape. Wang Yu said: 爪 (claw) is the pictograph."

**Senses (level tone, *wéi*).**
- *To do, to make.* Erya, *Shi yan*: "作 (make), 造 (fashion) = 爲." Shu, *Yi ji*: "I would exert effort in the four quarters — do thou it (汝爲)." *Hong fan*: "have plans, have deeds (有爲), have guardianship."
- *To govern, manage (治).* Jinyu: "the illness cannot be treated (爲)"; note: "爲, to treat."
- *To cause (使).* Luyu: "it will make (爲) later ages illumine the fair fame of the past"; note: "爲 is like 使."
- *A final particle.* Former Han, *Annals of Wudi*: "why merely flee and hide in the cold bitter land north of the desert (爲)?"
- *Surname.* *Guangyun*, citing *Fengsu Tong*: "the Han had Wei Kun, Grand Administrator of Nanjun." *Yun Hui*: descendants of Gongwei, son of Duke Zhao of Lu.

**Departing tone (*wèi*).** *Guangyun, Ji Yun, Yun Hui*: fanqie 于僞, sounding like 䧦. *Guangyun*: "to assist (助)." *Zengyun*: "wherewith (所以); on account of (緣); for (被); to protect (護); to give (與)." Shu, *Xian you yi de*: "the minister, for (爲) those above, makes virtue; for (爲) those below, makes the people"; *Shiwen*: "the 爲 of 'for above, for below' is fanqie 于僞; Xu says all four are fanqie 于僞." *Duo shi*: "only we, the lowly people, hold to action (爲)." Shijing, *Daya*: "blessings and emolument come to aid (爲)"; *Jian*: "爲 is like 助"; *Shiwen*: fanqie 于僞, but read in the usual sound to fit the line.

**Rhyming variant.** *Rhymes as 莪 (é), fanqie 吾何.* Shijing, *Wang feng*: "the hare goes leisurely, the pheasant caught in the net; in my life's beginning there was yet nothing to do (爲); in my life's later days I meet these hundred griefs — would I might sleep and never stir."
