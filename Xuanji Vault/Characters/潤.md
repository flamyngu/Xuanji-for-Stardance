## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈巳集上〉【水字部】頁649第24【唐韻】如順切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】儒順切，𠀤音閏。澤也，滋也，益也。【易·說卦】雨以潤之。　又水名。【水經】淮水，又東北左會潤水。　又水名，出金城。【水經注】潤水，出令居縣西。　又州名。【韻會】漢丹陽郡，隋置潤州。　又黃潤，蜀布名。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.649 #24)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 如順. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 儒順 — sounding like 閏 (rùn).

**Etymology and senses.** "To moisten (澤); to enrich (滋); to benefit (益)." Yijing, *Shuogua*: "the rain moistens (潤) it."
- *A river-name.* Shuijing: "the Huai water, flowing northeast, joins on its left the Run water (潤水)."
- *A river-name, rising in Jincheng.* Shuijing zhu: "the Run water (潤水) issues west of Lingju county."
- *A prefecture-name.* *Yun Hui*: "the Han Danyang commandery; the Sui set up Runzhou (潤州)."
- *huangrun (黃潤), the name of a Shu cloth.*
