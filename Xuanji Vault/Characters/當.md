> 《康熙字典》〈午集上〉【田字部】頁764第18    〔古文〕：𤱭【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤都郞切，黨平聲。【說文】田相値也。从田，尚聲。又【玉篇】任也。【論語】當仁不讓於師。【晉語】夫幸，非福非德不當雍。【註】當，猶任也。　又【廣韻】敵也。【禮·王制】次國之上卿，位當大國之中，中當其下，下當其上大夫。【史記·廉頗藺相如傳】學兵法，言兵事，以天下莫能當。　又直也。【禮·內則】妻不在，妾御莫敢當夕。　又遇也。【左傳·昭七年】聖人有明德者，若不當世，其後必有達人。【禮·曲禮】當食不歎。　又適可也。【易·履卦】夬履貞厲，位正當也。【左傳·哀元年】逢滑當公而進。【註】當公，不左不右。【禮·學記】當其可之謂時。【史記·禮書】好惡以節，喜怒以當。　又主也。【左傳·襄二十七年】慶封當國。【註】當國，秉政。【儀禮·喪服】童子唯當室緦。【註】當室者爲父，後承家事者爲家主。　又偶也。【前漢·司馬相如傳】及飮，卓氏弄琴文君，竊從戸窺，心說而好之，恐不得當也。【註】師古曰：當謂對偶也。　又抵也。【史記·屈賈傳】以一儀而當漢中地，臣請往如楚。　又【增韻】蔽也。【左傳·昭二十年】使祝鼃寘戈於車薪以當門。【李白·蜀道難】一夫當關，萬夫莫開。　又斷罪曰當，言使罪法相當也。【史記·張釋之傳】廷尉奏當，一人犯蹕當罰。【註】當謂處其罪也。【路溫舒緩𠛬書】奏當之成。　又【正韻】猶合也。理合如是也。【儀禮·特牲饋食禮】佐食當事，則戸外南面。【註】當事，將有事而未至。【史記·萬石君傳】內史慶醉歸，入外門不下車，萬石君讓曰：內史貴人入閭里，里中長老皆走匿，而內史坐車中，自如固當。【註】固當者，反言之，以見其不當如是，責讓之甚也。　又過當。【史記·霍去病傳】斬首捕鹵過當。【註】言不啻相等。　又勾當。【歐陽修·歸田錄】曹彬平江南，詣閣門求見。其榜子云：奉敕江南勾當公事回。又【職官分紀】奏舉京朝官，知縣資序二人，充本司勾當。　又官名。【前漢·宣帝紀】詔單于毋謁，其左右當戸之羣皆列觀。【註】左右當戸，匈奴官名。　又排當，宋宮中宴飮名。　又州名。【韻會】本羌地，唐置當州，蓋取燒當羌以名之。【後漢·明帝紀】秋九月，燒當羌𡨥隴西。　又縣名。【正字通】鳳州兩當縣。【後漢·郡國志】南郡有當陽。又【劉隆傳】遣隆屯田武當。【註】武當，今均州縣也。　又馬當，武當，𠀤山名。　又當康，獸名。【山海經】欽山有獸焉，其狀如豚而有牙，其名曰當康。【註】牙豚也。　又當魱，魚名。【爾雅·釋魚】鯦當魱。【註】海魚也。　又姓。　又【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤丁浪切，黨去聲。事理合宜也。【禮·樂記】古者天地順而四時當。【韓詩外傳】君子行不貴苟難，惟當之爲貴。　又底也。【韓非子·外儲說】堂谿公見韓昭侯曰：人主漏泄羣臣語，譬猶玉巵之無當。　又【正字通】凡出物質錢，俗謂之當。【後漢·劉虞傳】虞所賚賞，典當胡夷，瓚復抄奪之。【註】當，音丁浪反。    考證：〔【晉語】夫幸，非福非德不當。〕　謹照原文當字下增雍字。〔【左傳·襄二十六年】慶封當國。〕　謹照原文二十六年改二十七年。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, upper; Field radical 田; p.764 #18)*

**Ancient graph.** 𤱭.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 都郞, the level tone of 黨 (dāng).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Fields matching each other (田相値); it follows 田 (field) with 尚 as phonetic." Also *Yupian*: "to bear, take on (任)." Lunyu: "facing benevolence (當仁), one does not yield even to one's teacher." *Discourses of Jin*: "good fortune that is neither blessing nor virtue does not befit (當) harmony"; note: "當 is like to bear."

**Senses.**
- *To match, be a match for (敵).* Liji, *Wang zhi*: "the senior minister of a second-rank state ranks with (當) the middle minister of a great state, the middle with the lower, the lower with the upper grandee." Shiji, *Biography of Lian Po and Lin Xiangru*: "he studied the art of war and discoursed on military matters, holding that none in the realm could match (當) him."
- *Directly, opposite (直).* Liji, *Nei ze*: "when the wife is away, the concubine in waiting dares not take the night-turn opposite (當夕)."
- *To meet with, fall upon (遇).* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 7: "a sage of bright virtue, if he does not meet his age (當世), his descendants will surely have an eminent man." Liji, *Qu li*: "meeting (當) the meal, one does not sigh."
- *Fitting, allowable (適可).* Yijing, *Lü* hexagram: "resolute treading, upright but perilous — the position is just right (正當)." Zuozhuan, Ai yr 1: "Feng Hua advanced facing (當) the duke"; note: "facing the duke, neither left nor right." Liji, *Xue ji*: "to meet its fit moment is called timely (當其可)." Shiji, *Treatise on Rites*: "liking and loathing in due measure, joy and anger fitting (當)."
- *To be in charge of (主).* Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 27: "Qing Feng was in charge of the state (當國)"; note: "當國 means to hold power." Yili, *Sang fu*: "a boy only if he heads the household (當室) wears the three-month mourning"; note: "the one heading the household, for his father, becomes the household's master, carrying on the family affairs."
- *A mate, counterpart (偶).* Former Han, *Biography of Sima Xiangru*: "as they drank, the Zhuo woman toyed at the zither for Wenjun; she peeped from behind the door, and her heart delighted in him, fearing she could not be his match (當)"; note: Yan Shigu, "當 means a counterpart, a mate."
- *To offset, be worth (抵).* Shiji, *Biographies of Qu Yuan and Jia Yi*: "with one Yi to offset (當) the Hanzhong land, I beg to go to Chu."
- *To screen, block (蔽).* *Zengyun*. Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 20: "he had Zhu Wa set a dagger-axe in the cartload of firewood to block (當) the gate." Li Bai, *Hard Roads to Shu*: "one man holding the pass (當關), ten thousand cannot force it."
- *To sentence (斷罪).* Shiji, *Biography of Zhang Shizhi*: "the Commandant of Justice memorialized the sentence (當): one man, having violated the imperial passage, should be fined"; note: "當 means to settle his crime." Lu Wenshu, *Memorial on easing punishments*: "the completing of the sentence (奏當)."
- *To accord with (合).* *Zheng Yun*. Yili, *Te sheng kui shi*: "when the assistant is to be on duty (當事), he faces south outside the door"; note: "當事 means about to have a task but not yet at it." Shiji, *Biography of Lord Wanshi*: "the Clerk Qing came home drunk and, entering the outer gate, did not alight; Lord Wanshi reproached him: 'when a clerk, a man of rank, enters the wards, the elders all flee and hide, yet the clerk sits in his carriage as if it were simply his due (固當)'"; note: "固當 is said ironically, to show it was not fitting — a stern reproach."
- *過當 (more than a match).* Shiji, *Biography of Huo Qubing*: "the heads taken and captives seized more than offset his own losses (過當)"; note: "meaning not merely equal."
- *勾當 (to manage, take charge).* Ouyang Xiu, *Guitian lu*: "when Cao Bin pacified Jiangnan, he came to the palace gate seeking audience; his card read: 'returned from managing (勾當) the affairs of Jiangnan by edict.'" Also *Zhiguan fenji*: "to memorialize the recommendation of two capital court officials of magistrate's standing to fill the office's managing post (勾當)."
- *An official title.* Former Han, *Annals of Emperor Xuan*: "an edict that the Chanyu need not pay homage, and his throng of Left and Right Danghu (當戸) all stood in attendance"; note: "Left and Right Danghu are Xiongnu official titles."
- *排當 (paidang), a name for a Song palace banquet.*
- *A prefecture-name.* *Yun Hui*: "originally Qiang land; the Tang set up Dangzhou (當州), taking the name from the Shaodang Qiang." Later Han, *Annals of Emperor Ming*: "in autumn, the ninth month, the Shaodang Qiang raided Longxi."
- *A county-name.* *Zhengzitong*: "Liangdang (兩當) county in Fengzhou." Later Han, *Treatise on Commanderies and Kingdoms*: "Nan commandery has Dangyang (當陽)." Also *Biography of Liu Long*: "Long was sent to farm-garrison at Wudang (武當)"; note: "Wudang is now a county of Junzhou." Also Madang (馬當) and Wudang (武當), both mountain-names.
- *當康 (dangkang), a beast-name.* Shanhai jing: "Mount Qin has a beast shaped like a pig but with tusks, named the dangkang (當康)"; note: "the tusked pig."
- *當魱 (danghu), a fish-name.* Erya, *Shi yu*: "the jiu (鯦) is the danghu (當魱)"; note: "a sea-fish."
- *Surname.*

**Departing tone (dàng).**
- *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 丁浪, the departing tone of 黨 (dàng). The fitting of affairs to reason (事理合宜). Liji, *Yue ji*: "of old, Heaven and Earth complied, and the four seasons were apt (當)." *Han shi waizhuan*: "the gentleman does not prize doing what is needlessly hard, but only the fitting (當)."
- *The bottom of a vessel (底).* Hanfeizi, *Wai chu shuo*: "Tangxi Gong saw Marquis Zhao of Han and said: 'a ruler's leaking out his ministers' words is like a jade cup with no bottom (無當).'"
- *Also* *Zhengzitong*: "in general, to pawn goods for cash is commonly called 當." Later Han, *Biography of Liu Yu*: "what Yu bestowed in reward and pawned (典當) to the barbarians, Zan again plundered"; note: "當, fanqie 丁浪."

**Textual notes.** *Discourses of Jin*, "good fortune that is neither blessing nor virtue does not befit (不當)" — checked against the original, the word 雍 is added after 當. · Zuozhuan, "Xiang yr 26: Qing Feng was in charge of the state" — checked against the original, "year 26" should read "year 27."
