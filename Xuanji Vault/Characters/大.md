## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集下〉【大字部】頁248第01    〔古文〕：𠘲【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】徒蓋切。【正韻】度柰切，𠀤音汏。小之對。【易·乾卦】大哉乾元。【老子·道德經】域中有四大，道大，天大，地大，王亦大。【莊子·天地篇】不同同之謂大。【則陽篇】天地者，形之大。隂陽者，氣之大。　又初也。【禮·文王世子】天子視學，大昕鼓徵。【註】日初明，擊鼓徵召學士，使早至也。　又徧也。【禮·郊特牲】大報天而主日。　又肥美也。【儀禮·公食大夫禮】士羞，庶羞皆有大，贊者辨取庶羞之大，以授賓。【註】大，以肥美者特爲臠，所以祭也。　又過也。【戰國策】無大大王。　又長也。【爾雅·釋器】珪大尺二寸謂之玠。【疏】大，長也。　又都大，官名。宋制有兩都大，一提舉茶馬，一提點坑冶鑄錢與提𠛬序官。　又措大，士也。【書·言故事】窮措大，眼孔小，與錢十萬貫，塞破屋子矣。　又唐大，弓名，見【周禮·夏官】。　又四大，地、水、火、風也，見【梵書·圓覺經】。　又姓大。廷氏之後，見【風俗通】。　又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤他蓋切，音忲。易：大和大極。書、詩：大王大師。禮：大羹大牢。𠀤音泰。　又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤唐佐切，音䭾。【杜甫·天狗賦】不愛力以許人兮，能絕目以爲大。　又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤吐臥切，音拕。猛也，甚也。【禮·童子不衣裘裳註】鄭康成爲大溫也。徐邈大音唾。　又叶徒計切，音第。【詩·大雅】戎雖小子，而式弘大，叶厲泄愒敗。【正字通】楊愼曰：大無音一駕切者，韻書二十二禡不收。考淮南子，宋康王世，有雀生鸇。占曰：小而生大，必霸天下。大叶下，古亦有一駕切之音。【說文】天大，地大，人亦大。象人形。【徐曰】本古文人字。一曰他達切，經史大太泰通。    考證：〔【儀禮·公食大夫禮】士羞，庶羞皆有大，贊者辨取庶羞之大，以治賓。〕　謹照原文治賓改授賓。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, lower; Great radical 大; p.248 #01)*

**Ancient graph.** 𠘲.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 徒蓋. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 度柰 — sounding like 汏 (dà). The opposite of small.

**Etymology and senses.** Yijing, *Qian* hexagram: "great (大) is the primal power of Qian!" Laozi, *Dao de jing*: "within the realm there are four great ones: the Way is great, Heaven is great, Earth is great, and the king too is great." Zhuangzi, *Tian di*: "to make like what is unlike is called great (大)." *Ze yang*: "Heaven and Earth are the greatest of forms; yin and yang are the greatest of breaths."

**Senses.**
- *The beginning, first (初).* Liji, *Wen wang shizi*: "when the Son of Heaven inspects the school, at first light (大昕) the drum summons"; note: "at the day's first brightening, they beat the drum to summon the scholars, making them come early."
- *Everywhere, all (徧).* Liji, *Jiao te sheng*: "to make full requital (大報) to Heaven, with the sun as chief."
- *Rich and choice (肥美).* Yili, *Gong shi dafu li*: "for the officers' dishes, the sundry dishes all have a 大 (choice morsel); the assistant selects the choice morsel (大) of the sundry dishes and presents it to the guest"; note: "大 means a special slice of the fat and choice part, used for the offering."
- *To exceed (過).* Zhanguo ce: "do not overreach the great king (無大大王)."
- *Long (長).* Erya, *Shi qi*: "a gui-tablet a foot two inches long (大) is called a 玠"; subcommentary: "大 means long."
- *都大 (duda), an official title.* Under the Song there were two Duda offices, one the Intendant of Tea and Horses, one the Supervisor of Mines, Smelting, and Coinage, ranking with the Judicial Intendant.
- *措大 (cuoda), a scholar.* *Shu yan gushi*: "the poor scholar (窮措大) has narrow eye-holes; give him a hundred thousand strings of cash, and it bursts his hovel."
- *唐大 (tangda), a bow-name.* Seen in Zhouli, *Xia guan*.
- *The Four Great Elements (四大): earth, water, fire, and wind.* Seen in the Buddhist *Yuanjue jing*.
- *Surname.* Da, descendants of the Ting clan; seen in the *Fengsu tong*.
- *Also* *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 他蓋, sounding like 忲 (tài). In the Yijing, 大和 (great harmony), 大極 (great ultimate); in the Shu and Shijing, 大王 (great king), 大師 (grand preceptor); in the Liji, 大羹 (great broth), 大牢 (great sacrifice) — all sounded 泰 (tài).
- *Also* *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 唐佐, sounding like 䭾 (duò). Du Fu, *Tian gou fu*: "grudging no strength to pledge to men, it could outstrip all sight to be the greatest (大)."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 吐臥, sounding like 拕 (tuò). "Fierce (猛); excessive (甚)." Liji, "a boy does not wear fur or skirt," note: "Zheng Kangcheng took it as 大溫 (too warm)"; Xu Miao read 大 as 唾 (tuò)."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 第 (dì), fanqie 徒計.* Shijing, *Daya*: "you, though a little one, yet are greatly (大) employed" — rhyming with 厲, 泄, 愒, 敗. *Zhengzitong*: Yang Shen says, "大 has no reading fanqie 一駕; the rhyme-books' twenty-second 禡-rhyme does not include it. But considering the Huainanzi: in the time of King Kang of Song, a sparrow bore a falcon, and the diviner said: 'the small bearing the great (大), it will surely overlord the realm' — 大 rhyming with 下; anciently too it had a fanqie-一駕 sound." *Shuowen*: "Heaven is great, Earth is great, and man too is great; it images a human form." Xu (Kai): "it is properly the old-script graph for 人 (man)." One reading: fanqie 他達 — in the classics and histories 大, 太, and 泰 run together.

**Textual note.** Yili, *Gong shi dafu li*, "the assistant selects the choice morsel and tends the guest (治賓)" — checked against the original, "治賓" should read "授賓" (presents it to the guest).
