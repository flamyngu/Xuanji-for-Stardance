## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集下〉【儿字部】頁124第06    〔古文〕：炗、𤎫、𠈑、𤉭【唐韻】古黃切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】姑黃切，𠀤廣平聲。【說文】从火在人上。本作灮，今作光。【徐曰】光明意也。【易·需卦】有孚光亨。【程傳】有孚則能光明而亨通。【正韻】輝光，明耀華彩也。又【集韻】太歲在辛曰重光。又諡法，能紹前業曰光。又姓。【廣韻】田光後，秦末子孫避地，以光爲氏。晉有光逸。又【集韻】古曠切，廣去聲。飾色也。或作䊯。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Legs radical 儿; p.124 #06)*

**Ancient graphs.** 炗, 𤎫, 𠈑, 𤉭.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 古黃. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 姑黃 — the level tone of 廣 (guāng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "from 火 (fire) above 人 (man); originally written 灮, now written 光." Xu [Kai]: "it carries the sense of brightness." Yijing, *Xu*: "having sincerity, it shines and prospers (光亨)." Cheng's tradition: "with sincerity one can be bright and attain success." *Zheng Yun*: "輝光 (radiance) means bright shining and splendid colour."
- *A year-name.* *Ji Yun*: when the Grand Year [Jupiter] stands in 辛 (xīn), the year is called 重光 ("doubled brightness").
- *A posthumous title.* In title-law, one able to continue the work of his forebears is titled 光 (Bright).
- *A surname.* *Guangyun*: descendants of Tian Guang, who at the end of Qin fled and took 光 as their clan-name; the Jin had Guang Yi.

**Further reading.**
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 古曠, the departing tone of 廣 — "to colour, ornament (飾色)"; sometimes written 䊯.
