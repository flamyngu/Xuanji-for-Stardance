> 《康熙字典》〈申集下〉【行字部】頁1108第31【唐韻】戸庚切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】何庚切，𠀤音蘅。【說文】人之步趨也。【類篇】从彳从亍。【韻會】从彳，左步。从亍，右步也。左右步俱舉，而後爲行者也。【爾雅·釋宮】堂上謂之行，堂下謂之步。【釋名】行，伉也，伉足而前也。　又【廣韻】適也，往也，去也。　又【增韻】路也。【禮·月令】孟冬，其祀行。【註】行，在廟門外之西，爲軷壤，高二寸，廣五寸，輪四尺，設主軷上。　又道也。【晉語】下有直言，臣之行也。　又五行。【書·洪範】我聞在昔，鯀陻洪水，汨𨻰其五行。【韻會】五行，運于天地閒，未嘗停息，故名。　又行人，官名。【廣韻】周有大行之官。【論語】行人子羽修飾之。　又語也。【爾雅·釋詁】行，言也。【註】今江東通謂語爲行。　又歌行。【前漢·司馬相如傳】爲鼓一再行。【師古曰】行謂引，古樂府長歌行，短歌行，此其義也。　又【唐書·韓琬傳】器不行窳。【音義】不牢曰行，苦惡曰窳。　又【廣韻】下孟切，胻去聲。【玉篇】行，迹也。【周禮·地官·師氏】敏德以爲行本。【註】德行內外，在心爲德，施之爲行。　又姓。【後漢·光武紀】隗囂遣將行巡𡨥扶風。【註】行，姓。巡，名。漢行祐爲趙相。　又【集韻】寒岡切，音杭。【類篇】列也。【左傳·隱十一年】鄭伯使卒出豭，行出犬雞。【註】百人爲卒，二十五人爲行。行亦卒之行列。【吳語】吳王陳士卒百人以爲徹行百行。【註】以百人通爲一行，百行爲萬人，謂之方𨻰。　又中行，複姓。【通志·氏族略】中行氏，晉公族隰叔之後也，漢文時有宦者中行說。　又太行，山名。【書·禹貢】太行、恆山，至于碣石。【註】太行在河內山陽縣西。　又【廣韻】戸浪切，音笐。次第也。　又輩行也。【杜甫詩】豈如吾甥不流宕，丞相中郞丈人行。　又【韻會】行行，剛健貌。【論語】子路行行如也。　又【類篇】下朗切，音沆。義同。　又【韻補】叶先韻。【焦氏·易林】缺破不完，殘祭側偏。公孫幽遏，跛踦後行。　又【集韻】乎監切，音嗛。與銜同。    考證：〔【左傳·隱十一年】鄭伯使卒出豭，行出雞犬。〕　謹照原文雞犬改犬雞。〔【杜甫詩】豈知吾甥不流宕。〕　謹照原文豈知改豈如。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, lower; Walk radical 行; p.1108 #31)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 戸庚. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 何庚 — sounding like 蘅 (xíng).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "A man's pacing and hastening (步趨)." *Leipian*: "it follows 彳 and 亍." *Yun Hui*: "彳 is the left step, 亍 the right step; left and right step both raised, and thereafter there is walking (行)." Erya, *Shi gong*: "above the hall it is called 行, below the hall 步." *Shiming*: "行 means to stride (伉) — to stride the foot and go forward."

**Senses.**
- *To go to, to proceed, to depart (適, 往, 去).* *Guangyun*.
- *A road (路).* *Zengyun*. Liji, *Yue ling*: "in early winter, the sacrifice is to the Road-god (行)"; note: "the 行 is west of the temple-gate's outside: a road-altar mound (軷), two inches high, five inches broad, four feet around, with the tablet set on the altar."
- *A way, conduct (道).* *Discourses of Jin*: "that below there is straight speech is a minister's duty (行)."
- *五行 (the Five Phases).* Shu, *Hong fan*: "I have heard that of old, Gun dammed the flood-waters and threw into disorder the Five Phases (五行)." *Yun Hui*: "the Five Phases revolve between Heaven and Earth, never resting, hence the name."
- *行人 (xingren), an official title.* *Guangyun*: "the Zhou had the office of Grand Messenger (大行)." Lunyu: "the messenger (行人) Ziyu polished it."
- *To speak (語).* Erya, *Shi gu*: "行 means to speak (言)"; note: "today in Jiangdong they commonly call speaking 行."
- *歌行 (a song-ballad).* Former Han, *Biography of Sima Xiangru*: "he drummed it through once or twice (一再行)"; Yan Shigu: "行 means a strain — the old Yuefu's *Long Song Ballad* (長歌行) and *Short Song Ballad* (短歌行) are this sense."
- *Also* Tang shu, *Biography of Han Wan*: "the vessels were not firm (不行窳)"; *Yinyi*: "not solid is 行, crudely bad is 窳."

**Departing tone (xìng).**
- *Guangyun*: fanqie 下孟, the departing tone of 胻. *Yupian*: "行 means a track, a deed (迹)." Zhouli, *Di guan, Shi shi*: "quick virtue as the root of conduct (行本)"; note: "virtue and conduct, inner and outer: in the heart it is virtue (德), put into act it is conduct (行)."
- *Surname.* Later Han, *Annals of Guangwu*: "Wei Xiao sent the general Xing Xun to raid Fufeng"; note: "行 is the surname, 巡 the name. Xing You of the Han was Chancellor of Zhao."

**Further readings (háng).**
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 寒岡, sounding like 杭 (háng). *Leipian*: "a rank, a row (列)." Zuozhuan, Yin yr 11: "the Earl of Zheng had the companies (卒) put forth a boar, and the rows (行) put forth a dog and a fowl"; note: "a hundred men are a 卒, twenty-five men a 行; the 行 is also the ranks of a 卒." *Discourses of Wu*: "the king of Wu arrayed soldiers a hundred to a row (行), making a hundred files (徹行百行)"; note: "a hundred men together make one 行, a hundred 行 make ten thousand men — called a square array."
- *中行 (Zhonghang), a compound surname.* Tongzhi, *Monograph on Clans*: "the Zhonghang clan are descendants of Xishu of the Jin ducal house; in Emperor Wen of Han's time there was the eunuch Zhonghang Yue."
- *太行 (Taihang), a mountain-name.* Shu, *Yu gong*: "Taihang and Mount Heng, as far as Jieshi"; note: "Taihang is west of Shanyang county in Henei."

**Further readings (hàng).**
- *Guangyun*: fanqie 戸浪, sounding like 笐 (hàng). "Order, sequence (次第)."
- *A generation-rank, a peer-set (輩行).* Du Fu's poem: "how unlike my nephew, who does not drift idly — the chancellor and palace-gentleman are of his elders' rank (丈人行)."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: "行行, a firm and vigorous look." Lunyu: "Zilu was firm and bold (行行) in bearing."
- *Also* *Leipian*: fanqie 下朗, sounding like 沆 (hàng). Same sense.

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes in the 先-rhyme.* Jiao's *Yilin*: "broken and flawed, not whole; the leftover offering lopsided and askew; the duke's grandson darkly checked, lame and halting, walks behind (後行)."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 乎監, sounding like 嗛 (xián) — same as 銜 (to hold in the mouth).

**Textual notes.** Zuozhuan, Yin yr 11, "the rows put forth a fowl and a dog (雞犬)" — checked against the original, "雞犬" should read "犬雞" (a dog and a fowl). · Du Fu's poem, "how I know (豈知) my nephew" — checked against the original, "豈知" should read "豈如" (how unlike).
