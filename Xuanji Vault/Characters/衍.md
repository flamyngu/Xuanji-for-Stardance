## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集下〉【行字部】頁1108第35【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤以淺切，音演。水溢也。【說文】水朝宗于海也。　又【小爾雅】澤之廣者謂之衍。　又美也。【詩·小雅】釃酒有衍。　又布也。【前漢·司馬相如傳】離靡廣衍。　又游衍。自恣之意。【詩·大雅】昊天曰旦，及爾游衍。　又衍沃，平美之地。【左傳·襄二十五年】井衍沃。　又曼衍，無極也。【莊子·齊物論】和之以天倪，因之以曼衍。　又沙衍，水中有沙者。【穆天子傳】天子乃遂東征，南絕沙衍。　又篋衍，笥也。【莊子·天運篇】芻狗之未陳也，盛以篋衍，巾以文繡。　又【博雅】衍衍，行也。【謝朓詩】衍衍淸風爛。　又水名。【史記·荆軻傳】丹匿衍水中。【索隱曰】在遼東。　又朐衍，地名。【前漢·地理志】屬北地郡。　又姓。【通志·氏族略】衍，子姓，宋微仲衍之後。　又【廣韻】于線切，延去聲。義同。　又【集韻】夷然切，音延。進也。【周禮·春官】望祀望衍。【鄭註】讀爲延。【疏】衍，衍祭也。【劉歆·甘泉賦】高巒峻阻，臨眺曠衍。叶泉字韻。【直音】或作衍。衍字从彡，不从氵作。    考證：〔【周禮·春官】望祀望衍。【鄭註】讀平聲。〕　謹照原文讀平聲改讀爲延。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, lower; Walk radical 行; p.1108 #35)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui* all: fanqie 以淺, sounding like 演 (yǎn). "Water overflows (水溢)."

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Water paying court to the sea (水朝宗于海)."

**Senses.**
- *Also* *Xiao Erya*: "the broad part of a marsh is called 衍."
- *Also, fine, abundant (美).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the strained wine is plentiful (有衍)."
- *Also, to spread out (布).* Former Han, *Biography of Sima Xiangru*: "trailing and far-flung, broad and spreading (廣衍)."
- *游衍, the sense of giving free rein to oneself.* Shijing, *Daya*: "bright Heaven is radiant, and roams at ease (游衍) with you."
- *衍沃, level and fertile land.* Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 25: "they laid out fields on the level fertile ground (衍沃)."
- *曼衍, boundless, without limit.* Zhuangzi, *Qi wu lun*: "harmonize them with the boundary of Heaven, and follow them in their endless flux (曼衍)."
- *沙衍, water with sand in it.* Mu tianzi zhuan: "the Son of Heaven then marched east and crossed the sandy shallows (沙衍) to the south."
- *篋衍, a hamper, a box (笥).* Zhuangzi, *Tian yun*: "before the straw dog is set out, it is stored in a hamper (篋衍) and draped with patterned embroidery."
- *Also* Boya: "衍衍 means to go, to move (行)." Xie Tiao's poem: "moving on and on (衍衍), the fresh wind is brilliant."
- *A river-name.* Shiji, *Biography of Jing Ke*: "Dan hid in the Yan river (衍水)"; Suoyin: "it is in Liaodong."
- *朐衍, a place-name.* Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "it belongs to Beidi commandery."
- *Surname.* Tongzhi, *Monograph on Clans*: "衍 is of the Zi clan, descendants of Weizhong Yan of Song."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: fanqie 于線, the departing tone of 延 (yán); same sense.
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 夷然, sounding like 延 (yán). "To advance (進)." Zhouli, *Chun guan*: "the distant sacrifice and the 衍 sacrifice"; Zheng's note: "read as 延"; subcommentary: "衍 is the 衍 sacrifice." Liu Xin, *Ganquan fu*: "high ridges and steep barriers, looking out over the wide expanse (曠衍)" — it rhymes in the 泉-rhyme. *Zhiyin*: sometimes written 衍. The graph 衍 follows 彡, it is not written with 氵.

**Textual note.** Zhouli, *Chun guan*, "the distant sacrifice and the 衍 sacrifice," Zheng's note given as "read in the level tone (讀平聲)" — checked against the original, it should read "read as 延 (讀爲延)."
