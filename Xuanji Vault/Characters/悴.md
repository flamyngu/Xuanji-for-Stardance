## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁389第02【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤秦醉切，音萃。與顇通。【說文】憂也。从心卒聲。【楚辭·漁父】顏色憔悴。　又【集韻】徐醉切，音遂。【廣雅】困悴也。　又【集韻】昨律切，音崒。憂也。【劉向·九歎】覽屈氏之離騷兮，心哀哀而拂鬱。聲嗷嗷以寂寥兮，顧僕夫之憔悴。俗作忰。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.389 #02)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui* all: fanqie 秦醉 — sounding like 萃 (cuì). Interchangeable with 顇.

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to grieve, to be careworn (憂)." It follows 心 (heart) with 卒 as phonetic. Chuci, *Yufu*: "his face haggard and wan (憔悴)."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 徐醉, sounding like 遂 (suì). Guangya: "worn and haggard (困悴)."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 昨律, sounding like 崒 (zú). "To grieve." Liu Xiang, *Jiu tan*: "reading Master Qu's Lisao, my heart sorrows and sorrows, choked and oppressed; my voice cries out into the silent void, as I look back on my servant, haggard and wan (憔悴)."

**On the graph.** Vulgarly written 忰.
