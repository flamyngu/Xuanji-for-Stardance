## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈寅集中〉【山字部】頁323第07【唐韻】五銜切【集韻】魚銜切【韻會】疑銜切【正韻】魚咸切，𠀤音嵒。【說文】岸也。从山嚴聲。【增韻】石窟曰巖，深通曰洞。一曰險也。【左傳·隱元年】制巖邑也。【公羊傳·僖三十三年】殽之嶔巖，文王所避風雨也。　又嶃巖，高峻貌。【司馬相如·上林賦】嶄巖嵾嵳。　又巖廊，殿廡也。【漢武帝·策賢良制】虞舜之時，遊於巖廊之上。　又地名。【書·說命】說築傅巖之野。【傳】傅巖，在虞虢之閒。　又【集韻】魚杴切【韻會】疑杴切，𠀤音嚴。巖巖，高貌。【詩·魯頌】泰山巖巖，魯邦所詹。　又【正字通】本作嚴。亦作巖。通作礹嵒碞。○按巖與嵒同。《說文》嵒，五咸切，山巖也。巖，五緘切，岸也。同字分訓巖，專訓岸，𠀤非。嚴巖礹𠀤从𠭖作。    考證：〔【公羊傳·僖三十二年】殽之嶔巖，文王所避風雨處。〕　謹照原文三十二年改三十三年。處改也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 寅, middle; Mountain radical 山; p.323 #07)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 五銜. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 魚銜. *Yun Hui*: fanqie 疑銜. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 魚咸 — sounding like 嵒 (yán).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a bank, a crag (岸). It follows 山 (mountain) with 嚴 as phonetic." *Zengyun*: "a rock-cave is called 巖, a deep passage 洞; one says, precipitous (險)." Zuozhuan, Yin yr 1: "Zhi is a craggy town (巖邑)." Gongyang zhuan, Xi yr 33: "the towering crags (嶔巖) of Yao, where King Wen sheltered from wind and rain."
- *Also 嶃巖, the look of loftiness and steepness.* Sima Xiangru, *Shanglin fu*: "jagged and craggy (嶄巖), towering uneven."
- *Also 巖廊 (the lofty corridor), the palace galleries.* Emperor Wu of Han, *edict on examining the worthy*: "in the time of Yu Shun, the ruler roamed upon the lofty galleries (巖廊)."
- *Also a place-name.* Shu, *Yue ming*: "Yue toiled at building in the wilds of Fuyan (傅巖)"; tradition: "Fuyan lies between Yu and Guo."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 魚杴; *Yun Hui*: fanqie 疑杴 — sounding like 嚴 (yán). 巖巖, the look of loftiness. Shijing, *Lusong*: "Mount Tai, lofty and high (巖巖), which the state of Lu looks up to."

**On the graph.** *Zhengzitong*: "properly written 嚴; also written 巖; interchangeable with 礹, 嵒, 碞." A note: 巖 and 嵒 are the same. The *Shuowen*: "嵒, fanqie 五咸, a mountain crag (山巖); 巖, fanqie 五緘, a bank (岸)." To take them as one graph yet split the glosses — making 巖 mean only "bank" — is wrong. The graphs 嚴, 巖, 礹 all follow 𠭖.

**Textual notes.** In the Gongyang zhuan citation, 三十二年 should read 三十三年, and 處 should read 也, per the original.
