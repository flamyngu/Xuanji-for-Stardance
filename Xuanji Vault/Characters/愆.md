**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls еҚҜ, upper; Heart radical еҝғ; p.393 #35)*

**Ancient graph.** р җЁ.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie еҺ»д№ҫ. *Ji Yun*: fanqie дёҳиҷ”. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie иӢҰе … вҖ” sounding like йЁ« (qiДҒn).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "A fault (йҒҺ)." *Zengyun*: "an error, a slip (е·®рӨ•Ө); also a crime (зҪӘ); a failing (еӨұ)." Shu, *Yi xun*: "these are the three vices and ten faults (еҚҒж„Ҷ)"; note: "faults (йҒҺ)."

**Senses.**
- *A grievous sickness is called ж„Ҷ.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 26: "the king fell sick (ж„Ҷ) in his person."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as дө– (qГӯn), fanqie иө·е·ҫ.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "I am much wearied, yet in the rites I make no slip (ж„Ҷ); the master-invoker conveys the message, granting bounty to the filial descendant." Han Yu, *Sacrificial address for his brother*: "that he had no length of years, to make good my fault (ж„Ҷ)" вҖ” rhyming with еўі, жҒ©, еҺҹ, еҘ” above.
- *Rhymes as йҒЈ (qiЗҺn), fanqie иө·ж·ә.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "they fell wood on the slope, and the strained wine is plentiful; the dishes are set in rows, and the brothers are not far; when the people lose their virtue, it is over a bit of dry food (ж„Ҷ)." Yang Xiong, *Taixuan jing*: "the well without a frame, the water flowing straight on; neither stream nor valley, ending in waste (ж„Ҷ)." Sometimes written и«җ; also written рЁ“І. The *Shuowen* and *Ji Yun* also write гҘ¶; the seal-script form is рўқҗ.
