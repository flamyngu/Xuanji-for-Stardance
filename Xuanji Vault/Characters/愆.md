## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁393第35    〔古文〕：𠐨【廣韻】去乾切【集韻】丘虔切【正韻】苦堅切，𠀤音騫。【說文】過也。【增韻】差𤕤也。又罪也，失也。【書·伊訓】惟兹三風十愆。【註】過也。　又惡疾曰愆。【左傳·昭二十六年】王愆于厥身。　又叶起巾切，音䵖。【詩·小雅】我孔熯矣，式禮莫愆。工祝致告，徂賚孝孫。【韓愈·祭兄文】其不有年，以補我愆。叶上墳恩原奔。　又叶起淺切，音遣。【詩·小雅】伐木于阪。釃酒有衍。籩豆有踐，兄弟無遠。民之失德，乾餱以愆。【揚子·太𤣥經】井無幹，水直衍，匪溪匪谷，終於愆。或作諐。亦作𨓲。【說文】【集韻】又作㥶。籀作𢝐。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.393 #35)*

**Ancient graph.** 𠐨.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 去乾. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 丘虔. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 苦堅 — sounding like 騫 (qiān).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "A fault (過)." *Zengyun*: "an error, a slip (差𤕤); also a crime (罪); a failing (失)." Shu, *Yi xun*: "these are the three vices and ten faults (十愆)"; note: "faults (過)."

**Senses.**
- *A grievous sickness is called 愆.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 26: "the king fell sick (愆) in his person."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 䵖 (qín), fanqie 起巾.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "I am much wearied, yet in the rites I make no slip (愆); the master-invoker conveys the message, granting bounty to the filial descendant." Han Yu, *Sacrificial address for his brother*: "that he had no length of years, to make good my fault (愆)" — rhyming with 墳, 恩, 原, 奔 above.
- *Rhymes as 遣 (qiǎn), fanqie 起淺.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "they fell wood on the slope, and the strained wine is plentiful; the dishes are set in rows, and the brothers are not far; when the people lose their virtue, it is over a bit of dry food (愆)." Yang Xiong, *Taixuan jing*: "the well without a frame, the water flowing straight on; neither stream nor valley, ending in waste (愆)." Sometimes written 諐; also written 𨓲. The *Shuowen* and *Ji Yun* also write 㥶; the seal-script form is 𢝐.
