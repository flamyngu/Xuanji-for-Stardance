## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈午集下〉【立字部】頁871第08【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】諸良切【正韻】止良切，𠀤音彰。【說文】樂竟爲一章。从音从十。十，數之終也。　又采也。【書·臯陶謨】五服五章哉。【周禮·冬官考工記】畫繢之事，靑與赤謂之文，赤與白謂之章。　又明也。【易·垢卦】品物咸章。【書·洪範】俊民用章。　又文章也。【詩·小雅】維其有章矣。【箋】禮文也。　又篇章。【詩疏】詩有章句，總義包體，所以明情也。　又成事成文曰章。【孟子】不成章不達。【周語】將以講事成章。　又【周語】余敢以私勞，變前之大章。【註】表也，表明天子與諸侯異物也。　又條也，程也。【史記·高祖紀】約法三章。【太史公自序】張蒼爲章程。【註】章。歷數之章術也。　又大林木曰章。【史記·貨殖傳】千章金材。　又【爾雅·釋山疏】山形上平者名章。　又地名。【山海經】鮮山又東曰章山。又赤水之北有章尾山。【史記·楚世家】吳大敗楚于豫章。【前漢·地理志】勃海郡屬縣章武，章鄕，會稽郡屬縣句章，西河郡千章縣，廣平國斥章，東平國章縣。【後漢·光武紀】建武六年，改春陵鄕爲章陵縣。　又官名。【周禮·春官】保章氏。【前漢·王子侯表】千章侯。【百官志】東閣主章令丞。【師古註】主章，掌大材也。【前漢·宣帝紀】元康元年置建章衞尉。　又【爾雅·釋天】太歲在庚曰上章。　又樂名。【禮·記註】大章，堯樂名。　又印章。【漢官儀】吏秩比二千石以上，銀印龜鈕其文曰章，刻曰某官之章。　又章奏。【獨斷】凡羣臣書通於天子者四，曰章，曰奏，曰表，曰駁議。　又【左傳·僖五年】日南至。【疏】步曆之始，以朔旦冬至爲首，曆之上元，其年是十一月朔旦冬，至十九年閏月盡，復得十一月。朔旦冬至，故以十九年爲一章，積章成部，積部成紀。治曆者以此章部爲法，以知氣朔。　又章甫，殷冠名。【禮·郊特牲】章甫，殷道也。　又總章，舜明堂名。　又建章，漢宫名。在長安。城西周迴二十餘里。　又姓，秦將章邯。　又諡法。【逸書】温克令儀曰章。　又國名。【左傳註】謝，章，薛，舒，呂，祝，終，泉，畢，過，此十國皆任姓。　又俗或謂舅曰章。　又【六書音義】周章，怔營貌。又懼貌。亦作慞。　又【字彙補】與樟同。【司馬相如·子虚賦】楩楠豫章。　又與獐同。【周禮·冬官考工記】山以章。【註】讀爲獐。【韻會小補】又叶之風切，音中。【書·臯陶謨】天秩有禮，自我五禮有庸哉，同寅協恭和衷哉，天命有德，五服五章哉。　又之亮切。同障。【禮·雜記】四面有章。    考證：〔【司馬相如·上林賦】楩楠豫章。〕　謹照原文上林賦改子虚賦。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, lower; Stand radical 立; p.871 #08)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 諸良. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 止良 — sounding like 彰 (zhāng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a piece of music brought to its close is one 章 (movement); it follows 音 (sound) and 十 (ten); ten is the end of numbers."
- *Colour, emblem (采).* Shu, *Gaoyao mo*: "the five robes and five emblems (五章)!" Zhouli, *Dongguan kaogong ji*: "in painting and embroidery, blue with red is 文 (pattern), red with white is 章 (emblem)."
- *To make manifest (明).* Yijing, *Gou*: "the kinds of things all show forth (章)." Shu, *Hongfan*: "men of worth are thereby made manifest (章)."
- *Literary composition (文章).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "having its pattern (章)"; gloss: "the forms of rite."
- *A chapter, stanza.* Shi subcommentary: "poems have stanzas and lines (章句), gathering the sense and enclosing the form, so as to set forth the feeling."
- *A completed pattern.* Mengzi: "not forming a finished pattern (章), it does not reach through." The Zhouyu: "to plan affairs and complete the design (章)."
- *An insignia.* The Zhouyu: "shall I dare, for private merit, to alter the great emblem (大章) of old?"; note: "marking the distinction of the Son of Heaven from the lords."
- *An article, statute (條, 程).* Shiji, *Annals of Gaozu*: "he covenanted the law in three articles (三章)." The Grand Historian's Preface: "Zhang Cang made the rules and measures (章程)"; note: "章 is the article of calendrical reckoning."
- *A great timber-tree.* Shiji, *Biographies of the Money-makers*: "a thousand great trees (千章) of precious wood."
- *A flat-topped mountain.* Erya, *Shishan* subcommentary: "a mountain level on top is named 章."
- *Place-names.* Shanhaijing: "east of Xian Mountain is Zhang Mountain (章山); north of the Red Water is Zhangwei Mountain." Shiji, *House of Chu*: "Wu utterly defeated Chu at Yuzhang (豫章)." Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: Zhangwu and Zhangxiang in Bohai, Juzhang in Kuaiji, Qianzhang in Xihe, Chizhang in Guangping, Zhang county in Dongping. Later Han, *Annals of Guangwu*: "he changed Chunling village to Zhangling (章陵) county."
- *Office-names.* Zhouli, *Chunguan*: "the Baozhang (保章) officer." Former Han, *Table of Royal Marquises*: "the Marquis of Qianzhang." *Treatise on Officials*: "the Donge Zhuzhang chief and deputy"; Yan Shigu: "the Zhuzhang (主章) had charge of great timber." Former Han, *Annals of Emperor Xuan*: "he set up the Jianzhang (建章) Commandant of the Guard."
- *上章, the year of 庚.* Erya, *Shitian.*
- *A music-name.* Note to the Liji: "the Dazhang (大章) is the name of Yao's music."
- *A seal.* Han guanyi: "for officers of rank two-thousand *shi* or above, the silver seal with tortoise-knob — its inscription is called 章, cut as 'the seal (章) of such-and-such office.'"
- *A memorial.* Duduan: "of the writings ministers submit to the Son of Heaven there are four: the 章 (report), the 奏 (memorial), the 表 (statement), and the 駁議 (dissent)."
- *A calendrical cycle.* Zuozhuan, Xi yr 5 subcommentary: "...nineteen years make one 章 (cycle); piled 章 make a 部, piled 部 a 紀; calendar-makers take this 章 and 部 as their rule, to know the breaths and new moons."
- *章甫, a Yin cap.* Liji, *Jiao te sheng*: "the *zhangfu*, the way of Yin."
- *總章, Shun's Hall of Light; 建章, a Han palace in Chang'an.* (Its walls ran more than twenty *li* round.)
- *A surname:* the Qin general Zhang Han.
- *A posthumous title.* The Lost Documents: "mild, self-mastered, and of fair bearing" is titled 章.
- *A state-name.* Note to the Zuozhuan: "Xie, Zhang (章), Xue, Shu, Lü, Zhu, Zhong, Quan, Bi, Guo — these ten states were all of the Ren surname."
- *A mother's brother (colloquial).*
- *周章, a look of flustered alarm.* Liushu yinyi; also written 慞.
- *Same as 樟 (camphor-tree).* *Zihui bu.* Sima Xiangru, *Zixu fu*: "*pian*, *nan*, and camphor (豫章)."
- *Same as 獐 (roe-deer).* Zhouli, *Dongguan kaogong ji*: "for the mountain, the roe (章)"; note: "read 獐."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 之風, sounding like 中 (zhōng).* Shu, *Gaoyao mo*: "...Heaven charges the virtuous — the five robes and five emblems (章)!"
- *Fanqie 之亮 — same as 障 (a screen).* Liji, *Zaji*: "on all four sides there are screens (章)."

**Textual note.** "司馬相如·上林賦" should read "子虛賦".
