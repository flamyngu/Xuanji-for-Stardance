**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls з”і, upper; Grass radical иүё; p.1050 #16)*

**Ancient graphs.** рЎ¶Қ, рҰӯҶ.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie дёғеІЎ. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie еҚғеүӣ вҖ” sounding like еҖү (cДҒng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the color of grass." Yijing, *Shuogua*: "Zhen is the green bamboo (и’јзӯӨз«№)"; Mr. Wu of Linchuan's note: "и’ј is a deep blue-green." Shijing, *Wangfeng*: "O far, far azure Heaven (и’јеӨ©)!" Liji, *Yuzao*: "a grandee wears water-azure jade (ж°ҙи’јзҺү)"; subcommentary: "azure like water and flecked with markings."
- *Also* Boya: "luxuriant (иҢӮ)." Shu, *Yi Ji*: "as far as the sea's corners, the teeming life (и’јз”ҹ)"; tradition: "lushly (и’ји’ј) growing grass and trees."
- *Also, aged (иҖҒ).* Shijing, *Qinfeng*: "the reeds and rushes, grey (и’ји’ј)"; Shiwen: "the look of things grown old."
- *Also* Former Han, *Biography of Chen Sheng*: "the blue-caps (и’јй ӯ)"; note: "soldiers' blue silk head-cloths."
- *Also* Gangmu jilan: "и’јй»ғ is the look of haste and flurry."
- *Also a surname.* Tongzhi, *Shizu lГјe*: the Cang (и’ј) clan; note: "the Fengsu tong says: of the Eight Good counselors, descendants of Cang Shu (и’јиҲ’)."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: fanqie йҮҮжң— вҖ” the rising tone of еҖү (cЗҺng). иҺҪи’ј (mangcang), a cold appearance; one says, the color of the near suburbs. Zhuangzi, *Xiaoyao you*: "one going to the near-green wilds (иҺҪи’ј) returns with three meals' worth of food." Tang shu, *Biography of Wei Shu*: "in sudden haste (и’јеҚ’) he fled, hard-pressed." Bai Juyi's poem: "the cold melts in spring's vast haze (и’јиҢ«)." Also written еҖү. Liji, *Yue ling*: "he yokes the azure dragon (еҖүйҫҚ), wears azure jade (еҖүзҺү)." Shiji, *Biography of Xiao Wangzhi*: "blue-capped (еҖүй ӯ) household lads." The Zhiyin: written и’ј.

**On the graph.** The graph и’ј is written with the grass radical иүё.

**Textual notes.** The Yi Ji is not styled a "mo"; и¬Ё should read зҜҮ.
