## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)

> 《康熙字典》〈子集中〉【人字部】頁91第07    〔古文〕：忎、𡰥【唐韻】如鄰切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】而鄰切，𠀤音人。【釋名】忍也。【易·乾卦】君子體仁，足以長人。【禮·禮運】仁者，義之本也，順之體也。得之者尊。【程顥曰】心如穀種。生之性，便是仁。　又【方書】手足痿𤷒爲不仁。後漢班超妹昭，以兄老西域，請命超還漢土，上書云：兄年七十，兩手不仁。　又果核中實有生氣者亦曰仁。　又姓。　又【韻補】叶如延切，音然。【歐陽修·送吳子京南歸詩】我笑謂吳生，爾其聽我言。顏回不貳過，後世稱其仁。【六書正譌】元，从二从人。仁則从人从二。在天爲元，在人爲仁。人所以靈於萬物者，仁也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, middle; Person radical 人; p.91 #07)*

**Ancient graphs.** 忎, 𡰥.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 如鄰. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 而鄰 — sounding like 人 (rén).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shiming*: "to bear, endure (忍)." Yijing, *Qian*: "the gentleman embodies benevolence (仁), enough to lead men." Liji, *Li yun*: "benevolence is the root of right and the body of compliance; whoever attains it is honoured." Cheng Hao: "the heart is like a grain-seed — the nature of growth is just benevolence (仁)."
- *Medical sense.* Numbness or palsy of the limbs is 不仁 ("un-benevolent," i.e. insensible). Hou Han: Ban Chao's sister Zhao, her brother being old in the Western Regions, petitioned for his return, her memorial saying: "my brother is seventy, his two hands without feeling (不仁)."
- *The live kernel within a fruit-stone,* having vital *qi*, is also called 仁 (the "kernel").
- *Surname.*
- *Rhymes as 然 (rán), fanqie 如延.* Ouyang Xiu, poem seeing off Wu Zijing: "…later ages praise his benevolence (仁)." *Liushu zhengwei*: "元 is from 二 and 人; 仁 from 人 and 二. In Heaven it is 元 (the originating), in man it is 仁 (benevolence); what makes man more numinous than the myriad things is benevolence.
