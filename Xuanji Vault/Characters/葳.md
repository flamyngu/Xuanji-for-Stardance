## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1046第18【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤於非切，音威。【玉篇】葳蕤也。【東方朔·七諫】上葳蕤而防露兮。【註】葳蕤，盛貌。　又草名。【述異記】葳蕤草，一名麗草。又呼爲女草，江浙呼爲娃草。　又【博雅】茈葳，蘧麥也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1046 #18)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui* all: fanqie 於非 — sounding like 威 (wēi).

**Etymology and senses.** *Yupian*: "葳蕤 (luxuriant)." Dongfang Shuo, *Seven Remonstrances*: "above, luxuriant (葳蕤), it wards off the dew"; note: "葳蕤, the look of abundance."
- *A plant-name.* Shuyi ji: "the *weirui* plant (葳蕤草), also called the lovely plant (麗草); also called the maiden-plant (女草); in Jiang and Zhe called the *wa* plant (娃草)."
- *also.* Boya: "*ziwei* (茈葳) is *qumai* (蘧麥, the fringed pink)."
