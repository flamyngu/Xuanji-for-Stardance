## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集下〉【辵字部】頁1256第14    〔古文〕：𨓆、𨑧、𢓴、𢓇、𨔫【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤吐內切，推去聲。【玉篇】郤也。【禮·表記】君子三揖而進，一辭而退，以遠亂也。【左傳·僖二十五年】退一舍而原降。　又【玉篇】去也。【老子·道德經】功成名遂身退，天之道也。　又遜讓也。【禮·曲禮】君子恭敬撙節退讓以明禮。【後漢·鍾皓傳】好學慕古，有退讓風。　又進退人才，猶言用舍也。【禮·檀弓】君子進人以禮，退人以禮。　又返也。【屈原·離騷】退將復修吾初服。【前漢·董仲舒傳】臨淵羨魚，不如退而結網。臨政願治，不如退而更化。　又如柔貌。【禮·檀弓】其中退然如不勝衣。　又【正字通】吐困切。與褪同。【王建詩】粉光深紫膩，肉色退紅嬌。【註】淺紅也。又𨓤。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, lower; Walk radical 辵; p.1256 #14)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𨓆, 𨑧, 𢓴, 𢓇, 𨔫.

**Pronunciation.** *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 吐內 — the departing tone of 推 (tuì).

**Etymology and senses.** *Yupian*: "to draw back (郤)." Liji, *Biao ji*: "the gentleman, with three bows, advances, and with one word of leave, withdraws (退), so as to keep far from disorder." Zuozhuan, Xi yr 25: "he withdrew (退) one day's march, and Yuan surrendered."

**Senses.**
- *Also* *Yupian*: "to depart (去)." Laozi, *Daode jing*: "merit achieved and name made, the self withdraws (身退) — this is the way of Heaven."
- *To defer, yield (遜讓).* Liji, *Qu li*: "the gentleman, reverent and measured, yielding and withdrawing (退讓), thereby makes ritual clear." Later Han, *Biography of Zhong Hao*: "fond of learning and admiring the ancients, he had the air of yielding withdrawal (退讓)."
- *To advance or set aside men of talent, like saying to employ or to shelve (用舍).* Liji, *Tan gong*: "the gentleman advances a man by ritual, and sets aside (退) a man by ritual."
- *To return (返).* Qu Yuan, *Li sao*: "I shall withdraw (退) and mend again my first attire." Former Han, *Biography of Dong Zhongshu*: "facing the deep and craving the fish is not so good as withdrawing (退) to knot a net; facing government and wishing for order is not so good as withdrawing to renew the work."
- *A look of softness and yielding (柔貌).* Liji, *Tan gong*: "within, soft and shrinking (退然) as if unable to bear his robes."
- *Also* *Zhengzitong*: fanqie 吐困. Same as 褪 (to fade). Wang Jian's poem: "the powder's gloss deep purple and rich, the flesh's hue a faded-red charm (退紅)"; note: "a pale red." Also written 𨓤.
