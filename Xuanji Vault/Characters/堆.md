## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集中〉【土字部】頁232第04【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤都回切，對平聲。聚土也。【楚辭·九歎】陵魁堆以蔽視兮。　又舍也。【戰國策】鍾期堆琴。【註】猶論語舍瑟也。　又地名。【前漢·地理志】蜀守李冰，鑿離堆以息水患。堆，一作𡺾。又灩澦堆，在蜀夔州。【杜甫詩】故馮錦水將雙淚，好過瞿塘灩澦堆。　又鳥名。驪山有鳥，名阿濫堆。唐𤣥宗御玉笛，以其聲翻爲曲。【張祐詩】至今風俗驪山下，村笛猶吹阿濫堆。　又灰堆。【金坡遺事】吳俗，除夜將曉，婢僕持杖擊糞壤，致詞祈利市，謂之打灰堆。【史記】作碓。【漢書】作𡸠。俗作塠。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, middle; Earth radical 土; p.232 #04)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 都回, the level tone of 對 (duī). To heap up earth (聚土). Chuci, *Jiu tan*: "the mound heaped high (魁堆) to block the view."

**Senses.**
- *To set down (舍).* Zhanguo ce: "Zhong Qi set down his zither (堆琴)"; note: "like 'setting down the se-zither' in the Lunyu."
- *A place-name.* Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "Li Bing, governor of Shu, cut through the Lidui (離堆) to stop the flood-troubles." 堆 is also written 𡺾. Also the Yanyudui (灩澦堆), in Kuizhou of Shu. Du Fu's poem: "so I lean on the Brocade River with paired tears, that I may safely pass the Yanyudui of Qutang (瞿塘灩澦堆)."
- *A bird-name.* Mount Li has a bird named the Alandui (阿濫堆); Emperor Xuanzong of Tang played the jade flute and turned its song into a tune. Zhang Hu's poem: "to this day, by the custom below Mount Li, the village flutes still pipe the Alandui (阿濫堆)."
- *灰堆 (the ash-heap).* *Jinpo yishi*: "by Wu custom, on New Year's Eve at dawn, the maids and servants take staves and beat the dung-heaps, uttering words to pray for good fortune in trade — this is called 'beating the ash-heap (打灰堆).'" The Shiji writes 碓, the Hanshu writes 𡸠. Commonly written 塠.
