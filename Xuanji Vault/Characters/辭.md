## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集下〉【辛字部】頁1252第08    〔古文〕：𤔧【唐韻】似兹切【集韻】詳兹切，𠀤音詞。辭說也。【易·乾卦】修辭立其誠。【書·畢命】辭尚體要。　又【說文】訟辭也。【周禮·秋官·小司𡨥】以五聲聽其獄訟，一曰辭聽。【書·呂𠛬】明淸于單辭，罔不中聽獄之兩辭。【疏】單辭謂一人獨言也，兩辭謂兩人競理也。　又與辤同。【正韻】卻不受也。【書·大禹謨】稽首固辭。【中庸】爵祿可辭也。又謝也。【前漢·韓王信傳】溫顏遜辭。　又別去也。【楚辭·九歌】入不言兮出不辭。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, lower; Bitter radical 辛; p.1252 #08)*

**Ancient graph.** 𤔧.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 似兹. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 詳兹 — sounding like 詞 (cí).

**Etymology and senses.** "Words, diction (辭說)." Yijing, *Qian*: "he cultivates his words (辭) and establishes his sincerity." Shu, *Biming*: "in diction (辭) prize the essential."
- *The words of a lawsuit (訟辭).* *Shuowen.* Zhouli, *Qiuguan, Xiao sikou*: "by the five [signs of] voice he hears their suits; the first is 'listening to the plea' (辭聽)." Shu, *Lüxing*: "be clear and discerning in a one-sided plea (單辭), and let nothing fail to be just in hearing both pleas (兩辭) of a case"; subcommentary: "單辭 is one man speaking alone; 兩辭 is two men contending over the right."
- *Same as 辤 (to decline).* *Zheng Yun*: "to refuse, not accept." Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "he bowed his head and firmly declined (辭)." Zhongyong: "rank and emolument may be declined (辭)."
- *To take leave, excuse oneself (謝).* Former Han, *Biography of Han Xin (King of Han)*: "a warm face and humble words (遜辭)."
- *To part, depart.* Chuci, *Jiuge*: "he comes without a word, and goes without farewell (辭).
