## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集中〉【虍字部】頁1075第42【唐韻】去爲切【集韻】【韻會】驅爲切，𠀤音𡓰。【說文】氣損也。【徐曰】气闕，則其出舒遲，故字从亐。【廣韻】缺也。【史記·蔡澤傳】月滿則虧。【晉書·律歷志】月在外道，先交後會者，虧蝕西南角起。　又與羲通。【六書正譌】伏羲，古作虙虧。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, middle; Tiger radical 虍; p.1075 #42)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 去爲. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 驅爲 — sounding like 𡓰 (kuī).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the diminishing of breath." Xu [Kai]: "when the breath is wanting, its issuing is slow and drawn out, hence the graph follows 亐."
- *To be deficient, to wane (缺).* *Guangyun.* Shiji, *Biography of Cai Ze*: "the moon at the full then wanes (虧)." Jin shu, *Treatise on the Pitch-pipes and Calendar*: "when the moon is on the outer path, crossing before conjunction, the eclipse (虧蝕) begins from the southwest corner."
- *Interchangeable with 羲.* The Liushu zhengwei: "Fuxi (伏羲) was anciently written 虙虧.
