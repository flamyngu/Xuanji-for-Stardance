> 《康熙字典》〈巳集上〉【水字部】頁632第01    〔古文〕：𣸧【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤式針切，音𦸂。水名。【說文】水出桂陽南平，西入營道。　又邃也。【增韻】深者，淺之對。【詩·邶風】深則厲，淺則揭。　又深微也。【易·繫辭】惟深也能通天下之志。　又藏也。【周禮·冬官考工記】梓人必深其爪。　又衣名。【禮·深衣疏】衣裳相連，被體深邃，故謂之深。　又州名。【韻會】後漢博陸郡，隋置深州。【廣輿記】今屬眞定府。　又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤式禁切，音諗。度淺深曰深。【周禮·地官】以土圭測土深。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.632 #01)*

**Ancient graph.** 𣸧.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 式針 — sounding like 𦸂 (shēn).

**Etymology and senses.** A river-name. *Shuowen*: "the water rises at Nanping in Guiyang and runs west into Yingdao."
- *Deep, profound (邃).* *Zengyun*: "deep is the opposite of shallow." Shijing, *Beifeng*: "if deep (深), wade with your clothes on; if shallow, lift your hem."
- *Deep and subtle.* Yijing, *Xici*: "only being deep (深) can it penetrate the will of all the world."
- *To hide, sink deep (藏).* Zhouli, *Dongguan kaogong ji*: "the carpenter must sink deep (深) its claws."
- *A robe-name.* Liji, subcommentary on the "Deep Robe" (深衣): "upper and lower garment joined, draping the body deep and full, hence called the 'deep (深) robe.'"
- *A prefecture-name.* *Yun Hui*: "in Later Han the Bolu commandery; the Sui set up Shenzhou (深州)." Guangyu ji: "now belonging to Zhending prefecture."
- *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 式禁, sounding like 諗 (shèn) — "to measure shallow and deep is 深 (shèn)." Zhouli, *Diguan*: "with the earth-gnomon he measures the depth (深) of the ground.
