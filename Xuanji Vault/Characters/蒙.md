> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1048第15【唐韻】莫紅切【集韻】謨蓬切，𠀤音濛。【爾雅·釋草】蒙，王女也。【註】女蘿別名。　又【詩傳】唐蒙，菜名。　又大蒙，藥名。【管子·地員篇】羣藥安生，小辛大蒙。　又【易疏】蒙者，微昧闇弱之名。【書·洪範傳】蒙，隂闇也。　又【左傳·昭元年】又使圍蒙其先君。【註】欺也。　又【左傳·昭十三年】晉人執季孫意如，以幕蒙之。【註】裹也。　又【前漢·宣帝紀】雖有患禍，猶蒙死而存之。【註】冒也。　又【書·伊訓】具訓于蒙士。【疏】謂蒙稚，𤰞小之稱。　又【詩·秦風】蒙伐有苑。【傳】蒙，討羽也。【箋】畫雜羽之文于伐。　又縣名。【史記·老莊列傳】莊子者，蒙人也。【註】地理志，蒙縣屬梁國。　又山名。【書·禹貢】蒙羽其藝。【疏】蒙山在泰山蒙隂縣西南。【又】蔡蒙旅平。【註】蒙山在蜀郡靑衣縣。　又水名。【楚辭·天問】出自湯谷，次于蒙汜。【註】暮入西極蒙水之涯也。　又門名。【左傳·襄二十七年】宋公及諸侯之大夫盟于蒙門之外。【註】宋城門。　又姓。【風俗通】東蒙主以蒙山爲氏，秦有將軍蒙驁。　又【韻會】母總切，音懵。【柳宗元文】鴟夷蒙鴻。【註】二字俱上聲。　又【韻補】叶莫江切，音尨。《詩》狐裘蒙茸。徐邈讀爲厖。【陳琳·大荒賦】帝告我以至順兮，重訊我以童蒙。義混合于宣尼兮，理齊歸于文王。　又莫鳳切，音孟。與雺同。《漢書》引《易》傳：有蛻蒙霿，上下合也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1048 #15)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 莫紅. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 謨蓬 — sounding like 濛 (méng).

**Etymology and senses.** Erya, *Shi cao*: "蒙 is the wangnü (王女)"; note: "another name for the dodder (女蘿)."

**Senses.**
- *Also* the *Shi* tradition: "tangmeng (唐蒙), a vegetable-name."
- *Also* dameng (大蒙), a drug-name. Guanzi, *Diyuan*: "where the host of drugs safely grow — the small xin and the great meng (大蒙)."
- *Also* the *Yi* subcommentary: "蒙 is the name for the faint, dim, dark, and weak." The *Shu, Hong fan* tradition: "蒙 means yin-darkness (隂闇)."
- *Also* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 1: "and made Wei deceive (蒙) his late ruler"; note: "to deceive."
- *Also* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 13: "the men of Jin seized Jisun Yiru, and covered (蒙) him with a tent"; note: "to wrap."
- *Also* Former Han, *Annals of Emperor Xuan*: "though there be calamity, still I will brave (蒙) death to preserve him"; note: "to risk."
- *Also* Shu, *Yi xun*: "he set out instruction fully for the young scholars (蒙士)"; subcommentary: "it means the young and untaught, a term for the lowly and small."
- *Also* Shijing, *Qin feng*: "the feather-painted shield, well-wrought (蒙伐有苑)"; tradition: "蒙 means feather-trimming (討羽)"; Jian: "the figure of mixed feathers was painted on the shield."
- *A county-name.* Shiji, *Biographies of Laozi and Zhuangzi*: "Zhuangzi was a man of Meng (蒙)"; note: "by the *Treatise on Geography*, Meng county belonged to the kingdom of Liang."
- *A mountain-name.* Shu, *Yu gong*: "the Meng and the Yu were made tillable (蒙羽其藝)"; subcommentary: "Mount Meng is southwest of Mengyin county in Mount Tai." Also: "the Cai and the Meng were sacrificed-to and set in order (蔡蒙旅平)"; note: "Mount Meng is in Qingyi county of Shu commandery."
- *A water-name.* Chuci, *Tian wen*: "issuing from the Tang valley, it lodges at the Meng-shoal (蒙汜)"; note: "at dusk it enters the brink of the Meng water at the far west."
- *A gate-name.* Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 27: "the Duke of Song and the grandees of the feudal lords covenanted outside the Meng gate (蒙門)"; note: "a city-gate of Song."
- *Surname.* *Fengsu tong*: "the lord of Dongmeng took Mount Meng (蒙山) for his clan-name; Qin had the general Meng Ao."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: fanqie 母總, sounding like 懵 (měng). Liu Zongyuan's prose: "the leather-bag, vast and dim (蒙鴻)"; note: "both graphs are in the rising tone."
- *Also* fanqie 莫鳳, sounding like 孟 (mèng). Same as 雺 (mist). The *Hanshu* cites the *Yi* tradition: "there is the shedding mist and fog (蒙霿), above and below joining."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 莫江, sounding like 尨 (máng).* The *Shi*: "the fox-fur, rough and shaggy (蒙茸)"; Xu Miao read it as 厖. Chen Lin, *Da huang fu*: "the Emperor told me of utmost compliance, and charged me again with the child's ignorance (童蒙); the sense merges with Confucius, the principle returns alike to King Wen."

**Textual note.** Where the *Yunbu* reading is glossed "音尨" with the *Shi* line "狐裘蒙茸" — the *Shi* here is the *Mao shi*, *Bei feng* "Mao qiu," read against Xu Miao's gloss 厖.
