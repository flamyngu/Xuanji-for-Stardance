**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls дё‘, middle; Earth radical еңҹ; p.231 #12)*

**Ancient graph.** рЎүқ.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui* all: fanqie еұ…д№Ӣ, sounding like жңһ (jД«).

**Etymology and senses.** Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "еҹә means a footing (ж“ҡ); being below, it is what a thing rests on." Shijing, *Daya*: "they fixed the footing (еҹә) and so set it in order." Pan Yue, *Jitian fu*: "he knit the sacred footprints of the lofty base (еҙҮеҹә)."

**Senses.**
- *A field-tool.* Mengzi: "though one have a hoe (йҺЎеҹә)."
- *The site of a gate-school (й–ҖеЎҫ).* Shijing, *Zhou song*: "from the hall to the threshold-base (еҹә)."
- *A music-name.* The *Xiaojing wei*: "the music of Fuxi was called Liji (з«Ӣеҹә)."
- *A mountain-name.* Shanhai jing: "three hundred li east of Mount Tanyuan is Mount Ji (еҹәеұұ)."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie жё д№Ӣ. Interchangeable with жңһ. The *Xianyang Lingtai stele*: "in the base-year (еҹәе№ҙ) of tending the shrine, the carp came to life again."
- *Also* rhymes, fanqie еҸӨи©Ј, sounding like иЁҲ (jГ¬). Liu Xiang, *LienГј song*: "and made it the age's foundation (дё–еҹә)" вҖ” rhyming with the graph йҒҝ below.
