## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁390第34【廣韻】【正韻】戸國切【集韻】【韻會】穫北切。【說文】亂也。从心或聲。【廣韻】迷也。【增韻】疑也。　又【諡法】滿志多窮曰惑。　又熒惑，星名。　又或作掝。【荀子·不苟篇】誰能以己之僬僬，受人之掝掝。亦通作或。【孟子】無或乎，王之不智也。【史記·賈誼服鳥賦】衆人或或。本作或，後加心以別之。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.390 #34)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 戸國. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 穫北 (huò).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to be confused, disordered (亂)." It follows 心 (heart) with 或 as phonetic. *Guangyun*: "deluded (迷)." *Zengyun*: "to doubt (疑)."
- *Posthumous-title rule:* "self-satisfied yet oft at his wit's end is called 惑."
- *Yinghuo (熒惑), a star-name* [Mars].
- *Sometimes written 掝.* Xunzi, *Bugou*: "who can, with his own clear discernment, take on others' befuddlement (掝掝)?" Also used interchangeably with 或. Mengzi: "no wonder (無或) that the king is not wise." Shiji, Jia Yi, *Fu on the Owl*: "the multitude are all confused (或或)." It was originally written 或, and later 心 was added to distinguish it.
