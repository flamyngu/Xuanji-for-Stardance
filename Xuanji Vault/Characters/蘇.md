> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1068第07【唐韻】素姑切【集韻】【正韻】孫租切，𠀤音酥。【說文】桂荏也。【本草】紫蘇。【註】蘇，从穌，舒暢也。蘇性舒暢，行氣和血，故謂之蘇。蘇乃荏類，而味辛如桂，故《爾雅》謂之桂荏。　又【揚子·方言】蘇，芥草也，江淮南楚之閒曰蘇。　又木名。【詩·鄭風】山有扶蘇。【傳】扶蘇，扶胥，小木也。　又流蘇。【西京雜記】昭陽殿壁帶往往爲黃金釭，皆銜五色流蘇。【司馬相如·上林賦】【註】蘇，析羽也。　又息也。【書·仲虺之誥】后來其蘇。　又死更生。【戰國策】勃然乃蘇。　又取也。【屈原·離騷】蘇糞壤以充幃兮。【綱目集覽】取草曰蘇。　又【韻會】蘇蘇，氣索貌。【易·震卦】震蘇蘇。【註】恐懼不安之貌。【王註】躁動貌。　又臺名。【吳語】高高下下，以罷民于姑蘇。【註】姑蘇，臺也。　又亭名。【後漢·郡國志】襄國有蘇人亭。　又國名。【魏志·東夷傳】諸國各有別色，名之爲蘇塗。　又姓。【書·立政】司𡨥蘇公。【傳】忿生爲武王司𡨥，封蘇國。　又【集韻】山於切，音疋。詩扶蘇之蘇，徐邈讀疋。　又【字彙補】蘇故切，音傃。【荀子·議兵篇】蘇刃者死。【註】蘇讀作傃，謂相向格鬭者。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1068 #07)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 素姑. *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 孫租 — sounding like 酥 (sū).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Cassia-perilla (桂荏)." *Bencao*: "purple perilla (紫蘇)"; note: "蘇 follows 穌, to stretch out and ease (舒暢). Perilla's nature is relaxing — it moves the breath and harmonizes the blood, hence called 蘇. 蘇 is of the perilla kind, but pungent in flavour like cassia, so the *Erya* calls it cassia-perilla (桂荏)."

**Senses.**
- *Also* Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "蘇 is the mustard-herb (芥草); between the Jiang and Huai and in southern Chu it is called 蘇."
- *A tree-name.* Shijing, *Zheng feng*: "on the mountain is the fu-su (扶蘇)"; tradition: "扶蘇 is fuxu, a small tree."
- *流蘇 (liusu), a tassel.* *Xijing zaji*: "the wall-bands of the Zhaoyang Hall here and there had golden lamp-sockets, all holding five-coloured tassels (流蘇)." Sima Xiangru, *Shanglin fu*, note: "蘇 means split feathers."
- *To rest (息).* Shu, *Zhonghui zhi gao*: "when the lord comes, they will revive (蘇)."
- *To come back to life from death.* Zhanguo ce: "all at once he then revived (蘇)."
- *To gather, pluck (取).* Qu Yuan, *Li sao*: "they pluck (蘇) dung and soil to stuff their sachets"; *Gangmu jilan*: "to gather grass is called 蘇."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: "蘇蘇, a spent, exhausted look." Yijing, *Zhen* hexagram: "the thunder, faltering (蘇蘇)"; note: "the look of fear and unease"; Wang Bi's note: "an agitated look."
- *A terrace-name.* *Discourses of Wu*: "high upon high, low upon low, wearying the people at Gusu (姑蘇)"; note: "Gusu is a terrace."
- *A pavilion-name.* Later Han, *Treatise on Commanderies and Kingdoms*: "Xiangguo has the Suren pavilion (蘇人亭)."
- *A state-name.* Weizhi, *Account of the Eastern Yi*: "the several states each had a distinct mark, named the sutu (蘇塗)."
- *Surname.* Shu, *Li zheng*: "Lord Su (蘇公), the Minister of Crime"; tradition: "Fensheng was King Wu's Minister of Crime, enfeoffed in the state of Su (蘇國)."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 山於, sounding like 疋 (shū). The 蘇 of "扶蘇" in the Shijing — Xu Miao read it 疋.
- *Also* *Zihui Bu*: fanqie 蘇故, sounding like 傃 (sù). Xunzi, *Yi bing*: "those who face the blade (蘇刃) die"; note: "蘇 is read as 傃 (sù), meaning those who close and fight face to face."
