## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁398第38【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤七感切，音黲。痛也。【詩·小雅】憂心慘慘。　又毒也。【史記·酷吏傳贊】雖慘酷，斯稱其位矣。　又【集韻】楚錦切，音墋。義同。　又叶七各切，音錯。【詩·大雅】昊天孔昭，我生靡樂。視爾夢夢，我心慘慘。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.398 #38)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 七感 — sounding like 黲 (cǎn).

**Etymology and senses.** "To grieve, to pain (痛)." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the grieving heart, sorrowful and sore (慘慘)."
- *Cruel (毒).* Shiji, *Appraisal to the Biographies of the Harsh Officials*: "though cruel and harsh (慘酷), they yet fitted their posts."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 楚錦, sounding like 墋 (chěn); same sense.
- *Rhymes as 錯 (cuò), fanqie 七各.* Shijing, *Daya*: "vast Heaven is very bright, yet my life has no joy; I look on you, dim and heedless (夢夢), and my heart is sore and grieving (慘慘).
