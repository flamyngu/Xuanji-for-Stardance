## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集下〉【辵字部】頁1258第08【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤時制切，音誓。【說文】往也。【增韻】行也，去也。【詩·魏風】逝將去女。【楚辭·九歌】將騰駕兮偕逝。　又【正韻】亡也。【前漢·司馬遷傳】長逝者魂魄。　又發語辭。【詩·邶風】逝不古處。【集韻】或作遞。　又【集韻】征例切，音制。義同。或作遰。　又叶之列切，音折。【詩·大雅】言不可逝。叶上舌字。　又叶食列切，音設。【江淹·傷友賦】魂綿昧其若絕，泣縈盈其如潔。嗟妙賞之不留，歎知音之已逝。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, lower; Walk radical 辵; p.1258 #08)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui* all: fanqie 時制 — sounding like 誓 (shì).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to go (往)." *Zengyun*: "to go on (行); to depart (去)."
- *To go, to go off.* Shijing, *Weifeng*: "I will go (逝) and leave you." Chuci, *Jiu ge*: "I will mount my chariot and go off (逝) together [with you]."
- *To pass away (亡).* *Zheng Yun.* Former Han, *Biography of Sima Qian*: "that which long departs (長逝) is the soul and spirit."
- *An opening particle.* Shijing, *Beifeng*: "逝, you do not treat me as of old"; *Ji Yun*: sometimes written 遞.
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 征例, sounding like 制 (zhì); same sense. Sometimes written 遰.
- *Rhymes as 折 (zhé), fanqie 之列.* Shijing, *Daya*: "one cannot go (逝)" — rhyming with 舌 above.
- *Rhymes as 設 (shè), fanqie 食列.* Jiang Yan, *Fu Grieving a Friend*: "the soul, faint and dim, as though cut off; tears welling and brimming, as if pure; alas that fine discernment does not stay — I sigh that the friend who knew my music has already passed (逝)."
