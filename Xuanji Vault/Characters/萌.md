**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls з”і, upper; Grass radical иүё; p.1041 #05)*

**Ancient graph.** йӢӮ.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie иҺ«иҖ•. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie зңүеәҡ вҖ” sounding like ж°“ (mГ©ng).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "A grass-sprout (иҚүиҠҪ)." Also *Yun Hui*: "the first springing of plants." Boya: "the beginning (е§Ӣ)."

**Senses.**
- *A motionless look.* Zhuangzi, *Ying di wang*: "just now I showed him the patterns of earth, sprouting-still (иҗҢд№Һ), neither quaking nor upright."
- *To plough is also called иҗҢ.* Zhouli, *Qiu guan*: "the Tishi managed the killing of weeds: in spring, when they first grow, he sprouts them (иҗҢ)"; note: "it means ploughing to turn over their sprouts."
- *Surname.* *Zhengzitong*: "Meng LГј, a subordinate general of the Five Dynasties Shu."
- *Jiameng (и‘ӯиҗҢ), a place-name.* Shiji, *Biography of the Money-makers*, note: "belonging to Guanghan, now a county of Lizhou."
- *Interchangeable with ж°“ (the common people).* Guanzi, *Shan guo gui*: "he said to the people (иҗҢ) of the high fields: 'the money I lodged with you is so much'"; note: "иҗҢ means the people." Zhanguo ce: "extending even to the common folk and bondservants (иҗҢйҡё)."
- *Same as и•„.* Erya, *Shi xun*: "еӯҳеӯҳ, иҗҢиҗҢ, meaning to be present (еңЁ)."
- *Also* *Tang yun guyin*: read иҠ’ (mГЎng). Yang Xiong, *Admonition for the Governor of Youzhou*: "the righteous troops cross the desert, and our border-folk (йӮҠиҗҢ) are laid to rest" вҖ” rhyming with еә· below.
- *Also* the sense interchangeable with иҠ’. Liji, *Yue ling*: "the hooked sprouts all come out, the budding (иҗҢ) all break through." Guanzi, *Wu xing*: "grass and trees in tiny sprout (еҚҖиҗҢ)" вҖ” еҚҖиҗҢ is the hook-sprout (еҸҘиҠ’).
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie еҪҢзҷ», sounding like зһў (mГ©ng). Same sense.
- *Also* fanqie зңүе…ө, sounding like жҳҺ (mГӯng). и•ЁиҗҢ, a plant-name.
