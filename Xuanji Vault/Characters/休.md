> 《康熙字典》〈子集中〉【人字部】頁96第02【唐韻】許尤切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】虛尤切，𠀤朽平聲。美善也，慶也。【書·太甲】實萬世無疆之休。又【周官】作德心逸日休。【詩·商頌】何天之休。　又宥也。【書·呂𠛬】雖休勿休。【註】宥之也。我雖以爲宥，爾惟勿宥。　又【爾雅·釋訓】休休，儉也。【疏】良士顧禮節之儉也。【詩·唐風】良士休休。　又休沐，言休假也。一曰下沐，一曰旬休。唐法，旬休者一月三旬，遇旬則休沐，卽十日一洗沐也。　又致仕曰休。　又休息。【禮·月令】季秋之月，霜始降，則百工休。　又【揚子·方言】稱傀儡戲曰休，亦曰提休。　又地名。漢封楚元王子爲休侯。見【史記·年表】。　又【爾雅·釋木】休，無實李。【註】一名趙李。　又蚤休，藥名。　又姓。　又休休，人名。見【五代史】。　又【集韻】吁句切，音煦。氣以溫之也。【周禮·冬官考工記】弓人角之，本蹙於𠜶，而休於氣。　又同咻。【左傳·昭三年】民人痛疾，而或燠休之。【註】燠休，痛念聲。　又叶虚嬌切，音囂。【陸雲·贈鄭曼季詩】拊翼墜夕，和鳴興朝。我之思之，言懷其休。　又叶匈于切，音虛。【吳志·胡綜黃龍大牙賦】含契河洛，動與道俱。天贊人和，僉曰惟休。【說文】休在木部，人依木則休。《爾雅》庇䕃曰休，會止木庇息意。◎按今《爾雅·釋言》本作庇庥䕃也。    考證：〔【書·說命】實萬世無疆之休。〕　謹照原書說命改太甲。〔按今爾雅釋木本作庇庥䕃也。〕 謹照原書釋木改釋言。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, middle; Person radical 人; p.96 #02)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 許尤. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 虛尤 — the level tone of 朽 (xiǔ), i.e. **xiū**.

**Etymology and senses.** "Fair, good; a blessing." Shu, *Taijia*: "truly a blessing (休) of myriad ages without bound." Also *Zhouguan*: "in doing virtue the heart is at ease and daily blessed (日休)." Shijing, *Shangsong*: "[they bore] the blessing (休) of Heaven."
- *To pardon (宥).* Shu, *Lüxing*: "though [I would] pardon (休), pardon not"; note: "to forgive him: though I would forgive, you must not forgive."
- *休休, thrift, restraint.* *Erya, Shixun*; subcommentary: "the good gentleman mindful of the restraint of ritual measure." Shijing, *Tangfeng*: "the good gentleman, restrained and easy (休休)."
- *休沐, a holiday from duty (also 下沐, 旬休).* By Tang law: a month has three decades, and at each decade's end one takes leave to bathe — that is, one washing-leave every ten days.
- *To retire from office.* To leave office is called 休.
- *To rest.* Liji, *Yueling*: "in the last month of autumn, when frost first descends, the hundred artisans rest (休)."
- *The puppet-play.* Yangzi's *Fangyan*: "the puppet-play is called 休, also 提休."
- *A place-name.* The Han enfeoffed a son of King Yuan of Chu as Marquis of Xiu (休侯); see the Shiji chronological tables.
- *A fruitless plum.* *Erya, Shimu*: "休, a plum that bears no fruit"; note: "also called the Zhao plum."
- *蚤休, a medicinal-herb name.*
- *A surname.*
- *休休, a personal name.* See the *Wudai shi*.
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 吁句, sounding like 煦 (xù) — "to warm with one's breath." Zhouli, *Dongguan kaogong ji*: "the bow-maker's [work on] horn: the base is pressed at the joint and warmed (休) with breath."
- *Same as 咻 (a moan of comfort).* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 3: "the people are in pain and sickness, and someone soothes them (燠休)"; note: "燠休, a sound of pained sympathy."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 虛嬌, sounding like 囂 (xiāo).* Lu Yun, *Poem presented to Zheng Manji*: "...I think of him, cherishing his goodness (休)."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 匈于, sounding like 虛 (xū).* Wuzhi, Hu Zong's *Yellow-Dragon Great-Banner fu*: "...Heaven aids and men accord; all say, 'it is well (休).'"

**On the graph.** *Shuowen*: 休 is in the Wood radical — when a man leans on a tree he rests (休). The Erya: "shade and shelter is 休," catching the sense of stopping under a tree's shade to rest.

**Textual notes.** The "萬世無疆之休" line is from *Taijia*, not *Yueming*. The cited Erya passage is from *Shiyan*, not *Shimu*.
