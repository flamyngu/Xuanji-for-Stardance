## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集下〉【女字部】頁256第24【廣韻】【集韻】【正韻】𠀤彌笑切，音廟。神妙也。【易·說卦】神也者，妙萬物而爲言者也。【老子·道德經】衆妙之門。【莊子·寓言篇】自吾聞子之言，九年而大妙。【劉劭人物志】尤妙之人，含精于內，外無飾姿。　又少年也。【杜甫詩】明公獨妙年。　又纖媚也。【前漢·李夫人傳】妙麗善舞。　又姓。見【姓苑】。　又【集韻】弭沼切。與眇通。嫽妙，女貌。【漢·三老袁君𥓓】朕以妙身，襲表繼業。別作玅𢆷。    考證：〔【易·繫辭】神也者，妙萬物而爲言者也。〕　謹照原文繫辭改說卦。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, lower; Woman radical 女; p.256 #24)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 彌笑 — sounding like 廟 (miào).

**Etymology and senses.** "Wondrous, marvelous (神妙)." Yijing, *Shuogua*: "the spirit (神) is that which is spoken of as the marvel (妙) of all things." Laozi, *Daode jing*: "the gate of all marvels (衆妙)." Zhuangzi, *Yuyan*: "since I heard your words, after nine years I greatly attained the marvelous (大妙)." Liu Shao, *Renwu zhi*: "the most marvelous (尤妙) of men hold their essence within and have no adorned bearing without."
- *Also, youth (少年).* Du Fu's poem: "the illustrious lord, alone in his prime years (妙年)."
- *Also, delicately charming (纖媚).* Former Han, *Biography of Lady Li*: "delicately lovely (妙麗) and a fine dancer."
- *Also a surname*; see the Xingyuan.
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 弭沼; interchangeable with 眇. 嫽妙 is a woman's graceful look. The Han *Stele of the Sanlao Yuan*: "I, with my slight (妙) person, succeed to the office and carry on the work." Separately written 玅, 𢆷.

**Textual notes.** The source 繫辭 should read 說卦.
