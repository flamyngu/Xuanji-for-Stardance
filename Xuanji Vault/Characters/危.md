## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集下〉【卩字部】頁159第12    〔古文〕：𡵁【唐韻】魚爲切【集韻】【韻會】虞爲切，𠀤僞平聲。【說文】在高而懼也。从厃，人在厓上，自卪止之也。【徐曰】孝經，高而不危，制節謹度。故从卪。【玉篇】不安貌。【廣韻】疾也。隤也，不正也。又【禮·儒行】有比黨而危之者。【註】危，欲毁害之也。又屋棟上也。【禮·喪大記】升自東榮，中屋履危。【疏】踐履屋棟高危處。又【韻會】宿名。三星。【左傳·襄二十八年註】𤣥武之宿，虛危之星。又【書·禹貢】三危旣宅。【韻會】三峗，山名。通作危。    考證：〔升自東榮，中屋危。〕　謹照禮記原文，危上增履字。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Seal radical 卩; p.159 #12)*

**Ancient graph.** 𡵁.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 魚爲. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 虞爲 — the level tone of 僞 (wēi).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "To be on a height and afraid (在高而懼)." It follows 厃 — a man on a cliff-edge, stayed by his own 卪 (restraint). Xu (Xu Xuan) says: "the *Xiaojing*: 'high yet not endangered (不危), keeping measure and minding rule' — hence it follows 卪." *Yupian*: "an uneasy look (不安貌)." *Guangyun*: "sharp, swift (疾); toppling (隤); not upright (不正)."

**Senses.**
- *Also* Liji, *Ru xing*: "there are those who form cliques and would endanger (危) him"; note: "危 means wishing to ruin and harm."
- *The roof-ridge top (屋棟上).* Liji, *Sang da ji*: "they ascend by the east eaves, and at the mid-roof tread the ridge (履危)"; subcommentary: "treading the high, perilous place of the roof-ridge."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: a lodge-name (宿名), of three stars. Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 28, note: "a lodge of the Dark Warrior (𤣥武), the stars Xu and Wei (危)."
- *Also* Shu, *Yu gong*: "the Three Wei were now dwelt in (三危旣宅)." *Yun Hui*: "Sanwei (三峗) is a mountain-name; written interchangeably 危."

**Textual note.** "They ascend by the east eaves, and at the mid-roof the ridge (中屋危)" — checked against the original text of the *Liji*, the graph 履 (tread) is added before 危, giving 中屋履危.
