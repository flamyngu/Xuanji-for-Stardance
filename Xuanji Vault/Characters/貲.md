## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集中〉【貝字部】頁1206第13【廣韻】卽移切【集韻】【韻會】將支切【正韻】津私切，𠀤音髭。【說文】小罰以財自贖也。漢律，民不繇，貲錢二十二。【徐曰】卽今庸置。　又【玉篇】財也，貨也。【史記·仲尼弟子傳】子貢好廢舉，與時轉貨貲。　又與訾同。【前漢·景帝紀】今訾算以上乃得官。【註】師古曰：訾，讀與貲同。　又【六書故】資別作貲。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, middle; Shell radical 貝; p.1206 #13)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 卽移. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 將支. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 津私 — sounding like 髭 (zī).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a small penalty, redeeming oneself with goods." The Han statute: "a commoner not doing corvée pays a fine (貲) of twenty-two cash." Xu [Kai]: "this is the hired-substitute payment of today."
- *Wealth, goods (財, 貨).* *Yupian.* Shiji, *Biographies of the Disciples of Confucius*: "Zigong liked to buy cheap and sell dear, trading his goods and capital (貨貲) as the times shifted."
- *The same as 訾.* Former Han, *Annals of Emperor Jing*: "now only those rated at a certain property-assessment (訾算) and above may hold office"; note: Yan Shigu: "訾 is read the same as 貲."
- *The Liushu gu*: "資 (resources) is in another form written 貲."
