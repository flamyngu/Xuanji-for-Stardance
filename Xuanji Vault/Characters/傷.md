> 《康熙字典》〈子集中〉【人字部】頁115第01【唐韻】式羊切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】尸羊切，𠀤音商。痛也。【爾雅·釋詁】傷思也。【詩·周南】維以不永傷。【小雅】我心憂傷。　又創也，損也。【書·說命】若跣弗視地，厥足用傷。　又毀傷也。【禮·緇衣】心以體全，亦以體傷。　又戕害也。【左傳·僖二十二年】君子不重傷。　又姓。【左傳】傷省，宋人。　又【諡法】未家短折曰傷。　又叶式亮切，同慯。【張衡·四愁詩】路遠莫致倚愁悵，何爲懷憂心煩傷。    考證：〔【爾雅·釋訓】憂思也。〕　謹照原文釋訓改釋詁，憂思也改傷思也。〔【書·說命】若跣勿視地。〕　謹照原文勿視改弗視。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, middle; Person radical 人; p.115 #01)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 式羊. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 尸羊 — sounding like 商 (shāng).

**Etymology and senses.** "Pain (痛)." Erya, *Shi gu*: "傷, grieving thought (思)." Shijing, *Zhou nan*: "so that I grieve (傷) not forever." *Xiaoya*: "my heart is sorrowful and wounded (憂傷)."
- *A wound; to injure, diminish (創, 損).* Shu, *Yue ming*: "like one barefoot not watching the ground, whose foot is thereby hurt (傷)."
- *To mar, injure (毀傷).* Liji, *Zi yi*: "the heart is made whole through the body, and likewise wounded (傷) through the body."
- *To harm cruelly (戕害).* Zuozhuan, Xi yr 22: "the gentleman does not wound (傷) [a foe] twice."
- *Surname.* Zuozhuan: Shang Sheng, a man of Song.
- *Posthumous title.* "To die young, before founding a household, is 傷."
- *Rhymes as 慯 (shàng), fanqie 式亮* (same as 慯). Zhang Heng, *Four Sorrows*: "the road is far, none can reach — I lean in melancholy; why does this cherished grief so vex and wound (傷) my heart?"

**Textual notes.** The Erya citation should be from *Shi gu* (not *Shi xun*), reading "傷思也"; and the Shu citation should read "弗視" (not "勿視").
