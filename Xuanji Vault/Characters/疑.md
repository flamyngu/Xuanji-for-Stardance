**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls еҚҲ, upper; Bolt radical з–Ӣ; p.768 #03)*

**Ancient graph.** рҰ¬Ұ.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie иӘһе…¶. *Ji Yun*: fanqie йӯҡе…¶. *Yun Hui*: fanqie еҮқе…¶ вҖ” sounding like е®ң (yГӯ).

**Etymology and senses.** To doubt (жғ‘). *Guangyun*: "unsettled (дёҚе®ҡ)." Yijing, *Qian* hexagram: "the 'or' of it is the doubting (з–‘) of it." Liji, *Fang ji*: "ritual is that whereby what is doubtful is made clear and what is subtle distinguished, and made a dyke for the people"; subcommentary: "з–‘ means right and wrong undecided, which should be made plain by ritual."

**Senses.**
- *To gauge, reckon (еәҰ).* Yili, *Shi xiangjian li*: "in all private audiences with the lord, one must make out the lord's south-facing place; if one cannot, then one stands square and does not presume to gauge (з–‘) the lord"; note: "з–‘ means to gauge; one cannot beforehand reckon the lord's facing-place and stand aslant towards it."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "to fear (жҒҗ)."
- *Also* *Zengyun*: "to resemble (дјј); to be suspect (е«Ң)."
- *Also* Erya, *Shi yan*: "to transgress (жҲҫ)."
- *A mountain-name.* Huainanzi, *Yuandao xun*: "south of the Nine-doubts (д№қз–‘), affairs on land are few and affairs on water many"; note: "д№қз–‘ is a mountain-name, in Cangwu."
- *A god's name.* Shanhai jing: "Mount Futi вҖ” above is much palm and nan-wood, below much metal and jade; the god Jiangyi (жұҹз–‘) dwells there."
- *An office-name.* Liji, *Wen wang shizi*: "Yu, Xia, Shang, and Zhou had the Tutor and Guardian, and had the Yi and the Cheng (з–‘дёһ)"; subcommentary: "anciently the Son of Heaven had four neighbours: in front the Yi (з–‘), behind the Cheng (дёһ), on the left the Fu (иј”), on the right the Bi (ејј)."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: fanqie з–‘йҷө. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie йӯҡйҷө вҖ” sounding like еҮқ (nГӯng). "To settle, fix (е®ҡ)." Shijing, *Daya*: "no place to rest and settle (жӯўз–‘), whither shall we go?"; tradition: "з–‘ means to settle"; subcommentary: "the *Correct Meaning* says: з–‘ is sounded еҮқ; з–‘ carries the sense of calm and quiet, hence it means to settle." Zhuangzi, *Da sheng*: "using the will undivided, one merges (з–‘) with the divine."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie йӯҡд№ҷ. *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie йӯҡд№һ вҖ” the entering tone of йҠҖ (yГ¬). Yili, *Xiang she li*: "the guest ascends the western steps, and at the top stands erect (з–‘з«Ӣ)"; note: "з–‘ means to halt, with a grave and dignified look"; *Shiwen*: "з–‘, fanqie йӯҡд№ҷ." Also *Shi hun li*: "the bride stands erect (з–‘з«Ӣ) west of the mat"; note: "з–‘ is the look of standing upright, self-composed."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie еҒ¶иө· вҖ” same as ж“¬ (to liken, yЗҗ). Yijing, *Wen yan*: "the yin matches itself to (з–‘) the yang." Liji, *She yi*: "not taking the dukes and ministers as guests, but the grandees as guests, was a matching (з–‘)"; note: "з–‘ is a word for reaching from below up to the above"; subcommentary: "з–‘ is to liken; it is the lower likening itself to the upper, hence 'a word for reaching from below up to the above.'"

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as зҫ© (yГ¬), fanqie йӯҡиЁҳ.* Yijing, *Sheng* hexagram: "ascending to an empty town, with nothing to doubt (з–‘)."
- *Rhymes as зүӣ (niГә), fanqie йӯҡжұӮ.* Jia Yi, *Fu niao fu*: "the man of virtue has no tie, and knowing his fate does not grieve; a petty matter, a mote or a thorn вҖ” what is there in it to doubt (з–‘)?"
