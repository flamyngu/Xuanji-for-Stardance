> 《康熙字典》〈午集上〉【疋字部】頁768第03    〔古文〕：𦬦【唐韻】語其切【集韻】魚其切【韻會】凝其切，𠀤音宜。惑也。【廣韻】不定也。【易·乾卦】或之者，疑之也。【禮·坊記】夫禮者所以章疑別微。以爲民坊者也。【疏】疑謂是非不決，當用禮以章明之。　又度也。【儀禮·士相見禮】凡燕見于君，必辨君之南面，若不得，則正方不疑君。【註】疑，度也，不可預度君之面位，邪立嚮之。　又【廣韻】恐也。　又【增韻】似也，嫌也。　又【爾雅·釋言】戾也。　又山名。【淮南子·原道訓】九疑之南，陸事寡而水事衆。【註】九疑，山名也。在蒼梧。　又神名。【山海經】符惕之山，其上多椶柟，下多金玉，神江疑居之。　又官名。【禮·文王世子】虞夏商周有師保，有疑丞。【疏】古者天子必有四鄰，前曰疑，後曰丞，左曰輔，右曰弼。　又【韻會】疑陵切【正韻】魚陵切，𠀤音凝，定也。【詩·大雅】靡所止疑，云徂何往。【傳】疑，定也。【疏】正義曰：疑，音凝。疑者，安靜之義，故爲定也。【莊子·達生篇】用志不分，乃疑于神。　又【集韻】魚乙切【韻會】【正韻】魚乞切，𠀤銀入聲。【儀禮·鄕射禮】賓升西階，上疑立。【註】疑，止也。有矜莊之色。【釋文】疑，魚乙切。又【士昏禮】婦疑立于席西。【註】疑，正立自定之貌。　又【集韻】【韻會】𠀤偶起切，同擬。【易·文言】隂疑于陽。【禮·射義】不以公卿爲賓，而以大夫爲賓，爲疑也。【註】疑，自下上至之辭也。【疏】疑，擬也。是在下比擬於上，故云自下上至之辭也。　又【韻補】叶魚記切，音義。【易·升卦】升虛邑無所疑也。　又叶魚求切，音牛。【賈誼·鵩賦】德人無累，知命不憂。細故芥蔕，何足以疑。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, upper; Bolt radical 疋; p.768 #03)*

**Ancient graph.** 𦬦.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 語其. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 魚其. *Yun Hui*: fanqie 凝其 — sounding like 宜 (yí).

**Etymology and senses.** To doubt (惑). *Guangyun*: "unsettled (不定)." Yijing, *Qian* hexagram: "the 'or' of it is the doubting (疑) of it." Liji, *Fang ji*: "ritual is that whereby what is doubtful is made clear and what is subtle distinguished, and made a dyke for the people"; subcommentary: "疑 means right and wrong undecided, which should be made plain by ritual."

**Senses.**
- *To gauge, reckon (度).* Yili, *Shi xiangjian li*: "in all private audiences with the lord, one must make out the lord's south-facing place; if one cannot, then one stands square and does not presume to gauge (疑) the lord"; note: "疑 means to gauge; one cannot beforehand reckon the lord's facing-place and stand aslant towards it."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "to fear (恐)."
- *Also* *Zengyun*: "to resemble (似); to be suspect (嫌)."
- *Also* Erya, *Shi yan*: "to transgress (戾)."
- *A mountain-name.* Huainanzi, *Yuandao xun*: "south of the Nine-doubts (九疑), affairs on land are few and affairs on water many"; note: "九疑 is a mountain-name, in Cangwu."
- *A god's name.* Shanhai jing: "Mount Futi — above is much palm and nan-wood, below much metal and jade; the god Jiangyi (江疑) dwells there."
- *An office-name.* Liji, *Wen wang shizi*: "Yu, Xia, Shang, and Zhou had the Tutor and Guardian, and had the Yi and the Cheng (疑丞)"; subcommentary: "anciently the Son of Heaven had four neighbours: in front the Yi (疑), behind the Cheng (丞), on the left the Fu (輔), on the right the Bi (弼)."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: fanqie 疑陵. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 魚陵 — sounding like 凝 (níng). "To settle, fix (定)." Shijing, *Daya*: "no place to rest and settle (止疑), whither shall we go?"; tradition: "疑 means to settle"; subcommentary: "the *Correct Meaning* says: 疑 is sounded 凝; 疑 carries the sense of calm and quiet, hence it means to settle." Zhuangzi, *Da sheng*: "using the will undivided, one merges (疑) with the divine."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 魚乙. *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 魚乞 — the entering tone of 銀 (yì). Yili, *Xiang she li*: "the guest ascends the western steps, and at the top stands erect (疑立)"; note: "疑 means to halt, with a grave and dignified look"; *Shiwen*: "疑, fanqie 魚乙." Also *Shi hun li*: "the bride stands erect (疑立) west of the mat"; note: "疑 is the look of standing upright, self-composed."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 偶起 — same as 擬 (to liken, yǐ). Yijing, *Wen yan*: "the yin matches itself to (疑) the yang." Liji, *She yi*: "not taking the dukes and ministers as guests, but the grandees as guests, was a matching (疑)"; note: "疑 is a word for reaching from below up to the above"; subcommentary: "疑 is to liken; it is the lower likening itself to the upper, hence 'a word for reaching from below up to the above.'"

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 義 (yì), fanqie 魚記.* Yijing, *Sheng* hexagram: "ascending to an empty town, with nothing to doubt (疑)."
- *Rhymes as 牛 (niú), fanqie 魚求.* Jia Yi, *Fu niao fu*: "the man of virtue has no tie, and knowing his fate does not grieve; a petty matter, a mote or a thorn — what is there in it to doubt (疑)?"
