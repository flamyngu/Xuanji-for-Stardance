## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集下〉【刀字部】頁138第12    〔古文〕：𠔁【唐韻】方別切【集韻】【韻會】筆別切【正韻】必列切，𠀤鞭入聲。【說文】分解也。【玉篇】分別也。【增韻】辨也。【禮·曲禮】日月以告君，以厚其別也。【爾雅·釋山】小山別大山，鮮。【疏】謂小山與大山不相連屬者名鮮。【淮南子·齊俗訓】宰庖之切割分別也。【晉·仲長敖覈性賦】同稟氣質，無有區別。又傅別，謂券書也。【周禮·天官·小宰】八成，聽稱責以傳別。【註】鄭曰：爲大手書於一札，中字別之。又【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】皮列切【正韻】避列切，𠀤便入聲。【玉篇】離也。【增韻】解也，訣也。【江淹·別賦】黯然銷䰟者，惟別而已矣。【宋·謝惠連·夜集歎乖詩】詩人詠踟躕，搔首歌離別。【梁荀濟贈隂梁州詩】已作金蘭契，何言雲雨別。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Knife radical 刀; p.138 #12)*

**Ancient graph.** 𠔁.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 方別. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 筆別. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 必列 — the entering tone of 鞭 (biē).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "To divide and split apart (分解)." *Yupian*: "to distinguish, separate (分別)." *Zengyun*: "to discriminate (辨)." Liji, *Qu li*: "the sun and moon are reported to the lord, to deepen the distinctions (別)." Erya, *Shi shan*: "a small mountain set apart from a great mountain is 鮮"; subcommentary: "it means a small mountain not joined to a great one is named 鮮." Huainanzi, *Qi su*: "the butcher's cutting and dividing apart (分別)." Jin, Zhongchang Ao, *He xing fu*: "all alike endowed with breath and substance, with no division of kind (區別)."

**Senses.**
- *傅別 (fubie), a bond, a contract (券書).* Zhouli, *Tian guan, Xiao zai*: "the eighth of the settlements, hearing claims of debt by the tally-bond (傅別)"; note: Zheng (Xuan): "a large written character on a single slip, with the character split down the middle (別)."

**Further readings (bié).**
- *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 皮列. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 避列 — the entering tone of 便 (bié). *Yupian*: "to part (離)." *Zengyun*: "to take leave, to bid farewell (解, 訣)." Jiang Yan, *Bie fu*: "what darkens and melts the soul is parting (別), and parting alone." Song, Xie Huilian, *Night-gathering, lamenting the parting*: "the poet sings of his lingering steps, and scratching his head chants of separation (離別)." Liang, Xun Ji, *Poem presented to Yin of Liangzhou*: "we have already made the golden-orchid bond — how can we speak of a cloud-and-rain parting (別)?"
