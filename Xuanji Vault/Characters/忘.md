## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁376第24【集韻】【韻會】武方切【正韻】無方切，𠀤音亡。【說文】不識也。【增韻】忽也。又遺也。【書·微子之命】予嘉乃德，曰篤不忘。謂不遺也。又【儀禮·士冠禮】壽考不忘。【註】長有令名，不忽然而遽盡也。　又善忘，病也。【莊子·達生篇】氣下而不上，則使人善忘。　又坐忘，無思慮也。【莊子·大宗師】回坐忘。　又【廣韻】【正韻】巫放切【集韻】【韻會】無放切，𠀤音妄。【韻會】棄忘也。【增韻】遺忘也。【周禮·地官·司刺】三宥，一曰遺忘。　又志不在也。【左傳·隱七年】鄭伯盟，歃如忘。【註】志不在于歃血也。又【韓愈·別竇司直詩】中盤進橙栗，投擲傾脯醬。歡窮悲心生，婉戀不能忘。【說文】从心从亡。會意。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.376 #24)*

**Pronunciation.** *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 武方. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 無方 — sounding like 亡 (wáng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "not to recognize, to forget." *Zengyun*: "to be heedless of." Also "to leave out, lose." Shu, *Weizi zhi ming*: "I praise your virtue and say it is firm and not forgotten (不忘)" — meaning not let slip. Yili, *Shi guanli*: "long life and unforgotten (不忘)"; note: "long keeping a fair name, not suddenly and abruptly spent."
- *善忘, forgetfulness (a sickness).* Zhuangzi, *Dasheng*: "when the *qi* sinks and rises not, it makes one forgetful (善忘)."
- *坐忘, sitting in oblivion.* Zhuangzi, *Da zongshi*: "[Yan] Hui sat in oblivion (坐忘)."

**Departing tone (wàng).** *Guangyun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 巫放. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 無放 — sounding like 妄 (wàng). *Yun Hui*: "to cast off, forget." *Zengyun*: "to forget." Zhouli, *Diguan, Sici*: "the three pardons; the first is for forgetfulness (遺忘)."
- *The mind being elsewhere.* Zuozhuan, Yin yr 7: "the Earl of Zheng at the covenant sipped [the blood] as if absent (如忘)"; note: "his mind was not on the blood-smearing." Han Yu, *Parting from Dou the Sizhi*: "...joy spent, a grieving heart arose; tender and clinging, I could not forget (忘)."

**On the graph.** *Shuowen*: "it follows 心 (heart) and 亡 (lost); a compound-meaning graph.
