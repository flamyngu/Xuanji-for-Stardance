> 《康熙字典》〈子集上〉【丿字部】頁82第04    〔古文〕：㞢【唐韻】【正韻】止而切【集韻】【韻會】眞而切，𠀤音枝。【說文】出也，象艸過屮枝莖益大有所之。一者，地也。【玉篇】是也，適也，往也。【禮·檀弓】延陵季子曰：若䰟氣，則無不之也。　又於也。【禮·大學】之其所親愛而辟焉。【註】之，適也。【朱傳】猶於也。　又語助辭。【書·金縢】禮亦宜之。【禮·文王世子】秋冬亦如之。【正字通】凡之字，或句中，或語尾，或層出。如毛詩我之懷矣，共武之服，及女曰雞鳴章知子之來之，六句九之字，常華章左之左之，六句八之字，可以例推。　又此也。【詩·周南】之子于歸。【註】之子，是子也。　又變也。【易傳】辭也者，各指其所之。【孫奕示兒編】之字訓變，左傳，遇觀之否。言觀變爲否也。　又至也。【詩·鄘風】之死矢靡他。　又遺也。【揚子·法言】或問孔子，知其道之不用也。則載而惡乎之。曰：之後世君子。【註】言行道者貴乎及身，乃載以遺後世。　又姓。出【姓苑】。　又【郝敬讀書通】凡言之者，物有所指，事有所屬，地有所往，連屬之辭也。通作旃。【詩·唐風】舍旃舍旃。又【魏風】上愼旃哉。𠀤與之同。通作至。往彼曰之，到此曰至，音義互通。　又【韻補】叶職流切，音周。【楚辭·九章】呂望屠於朝歌兮，甯戚歌而飯牛。不逢堯舜與桓繆兮，世孰云而知之。叶上牛下求。【周伯琦曰】古人因物制字。如之本芝草，乎本吁氣，焉本鳶，後人借爲助語，助語之用旣多，反爲所奪，又制字以別之，乃有芝字吁字鳶字。    考證：〔【禮·文王世子】冬亦如之。〕　謹照原文改秋冬亦如之。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, upper; Slash radical 丿; p.82 #04)*

**Ancient graph.** 㞢.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 止而. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 眞而 — sounding like 枝 (zhī).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to issue forth (出); it pictures grass passing the sprout-stage, its branch and stalk growing ever larger and having somewhere to go (之). The 一 is the ground." *Yupian*: "this (是); to go to (適); to proceed (往)." Liji, *Tan'gong*: "Ji Zi of Yanling said, 'as for the soul-breath, there is nowhere it does not go (之).'"
- *Also, toward, in regard to (於).* Liji, *Daxue*: "in regard to (之) those one loves, one is partial"; note: "之 means 'toward' (適)"; Zhu's tradition: "like 於."
- *Also a grammatical particle.* Shu, *Jin teng*: "the rites too befit it (之)." Liji, *Wenwang shizi*: "in autumn and winter likewise (如之)." *Zhengzitong*: "the graph 之 may stand mid-sentence, at the end, or repeatedly. As in the Mao Shi 'wo zhi huai yi,' 'gong wu zhi fu,' and the 'Nü yue ji ming' stanza's 'zhi zi zhi lai zhi' — six lines with nine 之's; the 'Chang hua' stanza's 'zuo zhi zuo zhi' — six lines with eight 之's; by these the pattern may be inferred."
- *Also, this (此).* Shijing, *Zhounan*: "this maiden (之子) goes to her home"; note: "之子 is 'this one.'"
- *Also, to change (變).* The Yi commentary: "the line-statements each point to where the line goes (之)." Sun Yi's *Shi'er bian*: "the graph 之 glosses as 'change'; the Zuozhuan: 'he met Guan changing into (之) Pi' — it means Guan changes to Pi."
- *Also, until (至).* Shijing, *Yongfeng*: "unto (之) death I swear no other."
- *Also, to bequeath (遺).* Yangzi, *Fayan*: "someone asked: Confucius knew his Way would not be used; then he recorded it — and to whom did he send (之) it? The answer: he sent (之) it to the gentlemen of later ages"; note: "it says that for one who practises the Way the prize is to apply it in his own person, but Confucius recorded it to bequeath to later ages."
- *Also a surname*; from the Xingyuan.
- *Also* Hao Jing's *Dushu tong*: "whenever 之 is said, it is that things have something pointed to, affairs something they belong to, a place something gone toward — a connective word. Interchangeable with 旃. Shijing, *Tangfeng*: 'leave it (旃), leave it'; also *Weifeng*: 'be careful, oh (旃)!' — both the same as 之. Interchangeable with 至: going thither is 之, arriving here is 至, their sound and sense being mutually current."
- *Rhymes* (per the *Yunbu*) as fanqie 職流, sounding like 周 (zhōu). Chuci, *Jiu zhang*: "Lü Wang butchered at Chaoge, Ning Qi sang as he fed his oxen; had they not met Yao and Shun, Huan and Mu, who in the age would have known them (之)?" — rhyming with 牛 above and 求 below.

**On the graph.** Zhou Boqi says: "the ancients made graphs from things. Thus 之 was originally the magic-fungus (芝草), 乎 the exhaled breath (吁氣), 焉 the kite (鳶); later men borrowed them as particles, and as the particle-use grew abundant, the originals were displaced, so graphs were made to distinguish them — hence the graphs 芝, 吁, 鳶."

**Textual notes.** The Liji, *Wenwang shizi* citation is corrected to read 秋冬亦如之 (per the original).
