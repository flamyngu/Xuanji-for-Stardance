## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集中〉【人字部】頁109第03【唐韻】力迍切【韻會】【正韻】龍春切，𠀤音淪。常也。【書·洪範】彝倫攸敘。　又類也。【禮·曲禮】儗人必于其倫。　又義也。【禮·祭統】夫祭有十倫焉。又【周禮·冬官考工記】析幹必倫。【註】順其理也。　又比也。【儀禮·卽夕】凡絞紟用布，倫如朝服。【禮·中庸】毛猶有倫。【疏】毛雖細物，猶有形體可比也。　又等也。【前漢·甘延壽傳】投石超距，絕於等倫。　又擇也。【儀禮·少牢饋食禮】雍人倫膚九，實于一鼎。【註】擇其至美者。　又姓。伶倫之後。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, middle; Person radical 人; p.109 #03)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 力迍. *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 龍春 — sounding like 淪 (lún). Constant, the norm (常). Shu, *Hong fan*: "the constant relations (彝倫) are set in order."

**Senses.**
- *A kind, a class (類).* Liji, *Qu li*: "in comparing a man one must use his own kind (倫)."
- *Right principle (義).* Liji, *Ji tong*: "in sacrifice there are ten principles (十倫)." Also Zhouli, *Kao gong ji*: "in splitting the shaft, one must follow the grain (倫)"; note: "follow its grain (理)."
- *To match, rank with (比).* Yili, *Ji xi*: "for all the binding-cords, cloth is used, ranked (倫) like the court-robe." Liji, *Zhongyong*: "even hair still has its likeness (倫)"; subcommentary: "hair, though a fine thing, still has form and body to be compared."
- *A peer, equal (等).* Former Han, *Biography of Gan Yanshou*: "in casting the stone and leaping the distance, he surpassed all his peers (等倫)."
- *To select (擇).* Yili, *Shao lao kui shi li*: "the cook selects (倫) nine pieces of skin and lays them in one cauldron"; note: "selecting the choicest."
- *Surname.* Descendants of Ling Lun.
