> 《康熙字典》〈未集中〉【耳字部】頁969第18    〔古文〕：殸【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】書盈切【正韻】書征切，𠀤聖平聲。【說文】音也。【書·舜典】詩言志，歌永言，聲依永，律和聲。【傳】聲謂五聲，宮商角徵羽也。【禮·月令】仲夏之月，止聲色。【註】聲謂樂也。　又凡響曰聲。【張載·正蒙】聲者，形氣相軋而成。兩氣者，谷響雷聲之類。兩形者，桴鼓叩擊之類。形軋氣，羽扇敲矢之類。氣軋形，人聲笙簧之類。皆物感之良能，人習而不察耳。【韻會】韻書平上去入爲四聲。　又聲敎。【書·禹貢】東漸于海，西被于流沙，朔南曁聲敎，訖于四海。【左傳·文六年】樹之風聲。【註】因土地風俗，爲立聲敎之法。　又聲譽。【孟子】故聲聞過情，君子恥之。【註】聲聞，名譽也。　又宣也。【孟子】金聲而玉振之也。【註】聲，宣也。　又【諡法】不生其國曰聲。【註】生于外家。【春秋·經傳集解】繼室以聲子，生隱公。【註】聲，諡也。　又姓。　又【韻補】叶尸羊切，音商。【韓愈·贈張籍詩】嬌兒未絕乳，念之不能忘。忽如在其側，耳若聞啼聲。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 未, middle; Ear radical 耳; p.969 #18)*

**Ancient graph.** 殸.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 書盈. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 書征 — the level tone of 聖, i.e. **shēng**.

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "sound (音)." Shu, *Shun dian*: "poetry voices intent, song prolongs the words, sound (聲) follows the prolonging, the pitches harmonize the sound"; tradition: "聲 means the five notes — gong, shang, jue, zhi, yu." Liji, *Yue ling*: "in mid-summer, stop sound (聲) and beauty"; note: "聲 means music."
- *Any resonance.* Zhang Zai, *Zhengmeng*: "聲 is formed by form and *qi* grinding together — two *qi*, like the valley-echo and thunder; two forms, like drumstick and drum; form grinding *qi*, like feather-fan and whistling arrow; *qi* grinding form, like the human voice and the reed-pipe — all the innate powers of things, which men practise but do not notice." *Yun Hui*: "in the rhyme-books, level, rising, departing, and entering are the four tones (四聲)."
- *聲敎, the reach of civilizing influence.* Shu, *Yu gong*: "eastward reaching to the sea, westward to the moving sands, north and south extending the influence (聲敎), to the Four Seas." Zuozhuan, Wen yr 6: "to set up the influence of repute (風聲)"; note: "according to land and custom, to establish the method of moral teaching."
- *聲譽, reputation.* Mencius: "so when one's repute (聲聞) outruns the reality, the gentleman is ashamed"; note: "聲聞, reputation."
- *To proclaim (宣).* Mencius: "the bronze-bell sound (金聲) and the jade-chime finish"; note: "聲, to proclaim."
- *Posthumous title.* "Not born in one's own state is 聲"; note: "born in the maternal family." *Chunqiu jingzhuan jijie*: "the continuing wife Shengzi (聲子) bore Duke Yin"; note: "聲, a posthumous title."
- *Surname.*
- *Rhymes as 商 (shāng), fanqie 尸羊.* Han Yu, poem to Zhang Ji: "…as if I heard the crying voice (聲).
