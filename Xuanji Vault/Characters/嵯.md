## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈寅集中〉【山字部】頁317第42【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤倉何切，音磋。【說文】嵯峨，山石貌。【晉衞恆論書】山岳嵯峨而連岡。本作𡾑。　又【廣韻】楚宜切，音雌。㠁嵯，山不齊貌。通作參差。或作嵳。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 寅, middle; Mountain radical 山; p.317 #42)*

**Pronunciation.** *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 倉何 — sounding like 磋 (cuó).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "嵯峨, the look of mountain rocks." Wei Heng of Jin, *On Calligraphy*: "the mountain peaks tower craggy (嵯峨) and the ridges run on." Originally written 𡾑.
- *Guangyun*: fanqie 楚宜, sounding like 雌 (cī). 㠁嵯, the look of a mountain uneven; used interchangeably with 參差. Also written 嵳.
