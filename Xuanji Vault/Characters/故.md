## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集下〉【攴字部】頁469第25【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤古暮切，音顧。【說文】使爲之也。【註】徐鍇曰：故使之也。　又【廣韻】事也。【易·繫辭】是故知幽明之故。【疏】故，謂事也。　又【廣韻】舊也。【易·雜卦】革，去故也。鼎，取新也。【周禮·天官·大宰】以八統詔王馭萬民，二曰敬故。【註】不慢舊也。　又【書·大禹謨】𠛬故無小。【傳】故犯雖小，必𠛬。　又【禮·曲禮·疏】故者，承上起下之語。　又【禮·檀弓】非有大故，不宿於外。【註】大故，謂喪憂。　又【前漢·藝文志】魯故二十五卷。【註】師古曰：故者，通其指義也。今流俗毛詩，攺故訓傳爲詁字，失眞。　又【前漢·蘇武傳】前以降及物故，凡隨武還者九人。【註】師古曰：物故，謂死也。言其同於鬼物而故也。　又【荀子·王霸篇】不隆本行，不敬舊法，而好詐故。【註】故，巧也。　又【韻補】叶攻乎切，辜也。【賈誼·弔屈原文】般紛紛其離此尤兮，亦夫子之故也。歷九州而相其君兮，何必懷此都也。○按《史記》故作辜。　又叶果五切。【東方朔·客難】悉力慕之，困於衣食，或失門戸，使蘇秦，張儀與僕𠀤生於今之世，曾不得掌故。【說文】从攴作故。故字原从攴作。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, lower; Rap radical 攴; p.469 #25)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 古暮 — sounding like 顧 (gù).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to cause it to be done." Note: Xu Kai says, "故 is to make [someone] do it."
- *An affair, matter (事).* *Guangyun.* Yijing, *Xici*: "thus one knows the matters (故) of the dark and the bright"; subcommentary: "故 means affairs."
- *Old, former (舊).* *Guangyun.* Yijing, *Zagua*: "Ge (Revolution) is to remove the old (故); Ding (Cauldron) is to take the new." Zhouli, *Tianguan, Da zai*: "by the eight bonds he instructs the king to govern the myriad people; the second is 'to honour the old (敬故)'"; note: "not to slight the old [ministers]."
- *Deliberate, wilful.* Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "for the deliberate [crime] (故) punish though it be small"; tradition: "a wilful offence, though small, must be punished."
- *Therefore (a connective).* Liji, *Quli* subcommentary: "故 is a word that takes up what precedes and leads to what follows."
- *A great trouble (大故).* Liji, *Tangong*: "save for a great trouble one does not lodge outside"; note: "大故 means a death or grave sorrow."
- *Exegesis (故).* Former Han, *Treatise on Letters*: "the Lu Exegesis (魯故), twenty-five rolls"; note: Yan Shigu says, "故 means to make plain its purport and sense; the vulgar Mao Shi today alters 故訓傳 to the graph 詁, losing the truth."
- *物故, to die.* Former Han, *Biography of Su Wu*: "of those who earlier surrendered or had died (物故), in all nine returned with Wu"; note: Yan Shigu says, "物故 means to die; it says they have become as the ghost-things and passed away (故)."
- *Craft, cunning (巧).* Xunzi, *Wangba*: "they exalt not fundamental conduct, honour not the old laws, but love deceit and cunning (故)"; note: "故 means craft."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 攻乎, [sounding like] 辜 (gū).* Jia Yi, *Lament for Qu Yuan*: "in his confusion he met this blame — yet this too was the Master's own doing (故); had he passed through the Nine Provinces to choose his lord, why need he cling to this capital?" A note: in the Shiji 故 is written 辜.
- *Rhymes, fanqie 果五.* Dongfang Shuo, *Kenan*: "...had Su Qin and Zhang Yi been born together with me in this present age, they could not have gained even a clerkship of records (掌故)."

**On the graph.** *Shuowen*: it follows 攴 to make 故.
