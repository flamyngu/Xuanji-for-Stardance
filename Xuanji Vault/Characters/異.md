**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls еҚҲ, upper; Field radical з”°; p.763 #18)*

**Ancient graph.** рў„–.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie зҫҠеҗҸ. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie д»Ҙжҷә вҖ” the departing tone of з§» (yГ¬).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to divide"; it follows е»ҫ (two hands) and рўҢҝ, where рўҢҝ means "to hand over." *Boya*: "з•° is to divide."
- *To divide, live apart.* Shiji, *Biography of Shang Yang*: "households with two or more grown sons who did not divide and live apart (еҲҶз•°) had their levy doubled."
- *To be different.* Shu, *LГј ao*: "the king then made his virtue manifest, reaching even to states of other (з•°) surnames." Liji, *Ruxing*: "those who agree he does not merely side with, those who differ (з•°) he does not condemn"; subcommentary: "though that man be estranged and different (з•°) from himself, if what he does is good he will not disparage him."
- *Strange (жҖӘ).* Shiming: "з•° is what differs from the ordinary." Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 26: "the duke found something strange (з•°) in it"; note: "з•° is like 'strange.'" Shiji, *Biographies of Qu Yuan and Jia Yi*: "transformed into a strange thing (з•°зү©) вҖ” what is there to dread?"
- *Extraordinary (еҘҮ).* Zhouli, *Diguan, Zhiren*: "[the market-clerk] manages the wares of the completed market вҖ” goods, persons, oxen and horses, weapons, and rarities (зҸҚз•°)"; note: "зҸҚз•° are the foods of the four seasons." Shiji, *Biographies of the Disciples of Confucius*: "of those who received instruction and mastered it in person there were seventy-seven, all men of extraordinary (з•°) ability."
- *To go against (йҒ•).*
- *A surname.* In the Tang, Yi Mouxun (з•°зүҹе°Ӣ) submitted to the Tang and was invested as King of Nanzhao; the Baishui Man still bear this surname.
- *The name of a plant.* Erya, *Shicao*: "йҖЈ (lian) is *yiqiao* (з•°зҝ№)."
- *wumingyi (з„ЎеҗҚз•°), a drug-name,* used chiefly for metal-wounds and fractures.
- *Yunbu*: rhymes as жҖЎ (yГӯ), fanqie е»¶зҹҘ. Shijing, *Beifeng*: "truly fair, and rare (з•°)" вҖ” rhyming with иІҪ below.
- *Rhymes as йҖё (yГ¬), fanqie ејӢиіӘ.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "it only makes for estrangement (з•°)"; Zhu Xi's note: "fanqie йҖёз№”." An anonymous *Song of Joy in Virtue*: "what is seen is wondrous and strange (з•°)" вҖ” rhyming with "sweet, fine wine and food."

**Textual notes.** In Zhouli, *Diguan, Zhiren*, "иіһжҲҗеёӮд№ӢиІЁиі„" вҖ” checked against the original, иіһ should read жҺҢ.
