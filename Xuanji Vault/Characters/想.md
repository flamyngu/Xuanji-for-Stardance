## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁392第26【廣韻】【正韻】悉兩切【集韻】【韻會】寫兩切，𠀤音鯗。【說文】冀思也。註希冀而思之也。【後漢·王霸傳】夢想賢士。【晉書·謝安傳】悠然遐想。　又【增韻】意之也。物未至而意之也。　又【周禮·春官】眡祲掌十輝之灋，以觀妖祥，辨吉凶。十曰想。【註】鄭司農云：想者，輝光也。鄭康成曰：想雜氣有所似，可形想也。【六書精薀】心有所欲而思也。字意从心从相，言有所著也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.392 #26)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 悉兩. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 寫兩 — sounding like 鯗 (xiǎng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to long and think (冀思)." Note: "to hope for it and think of it." Later Han, *Biography of Wang Ba*: "he dreamed and longed (夢想) for worthy gentlemen." Jinshu, *Biography of Xie An*: "far-off and serene, he let his thoughts roam (遐想)."
- *To imagine.* *Zengyun*: "to imagine; to form an idea of a thing before it arrives."
- *A coloured radiance (one of the ten portents).* Zhouli, *Chunguan*: "the Diviner of Portents keeps the method of the ten radiances, to observe omens ill and good and tell fortune from misfortune; the tenth is 想"; note: Zheng [Zhong] the Junior Minister says, "想 is a radiance"; Zheng Kangcheng says, "想 is a mixed vapour resembling some shape, that one may picture (形想)."

**On the graph.** The *Liushu jingyun*: "to think of what the heart desires; in sense the graph follows 心 (heart) and 相, saying that the thought is fixed upon something.
