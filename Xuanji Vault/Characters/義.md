> 《康熙字典》〈未集中〉【羊字部】頁952第32【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤宜寄切，音議。【說文】己之威儀也。从我羊。【註】臣鉉等曰：與善同意，故从羊。【釋名】義，宜也。裁制事物，使各宜也。【易·乾卦】利物足以和義。又【說卦傳】立人之道，曰仁與義。　又【容齋隨筆】人物以義爲名，其別最多。仗正道曰義，義師，義戰是也。眾所尊戴曰義，義帝是也。與衆共之曰義，義倉，義社，義田，義學，義役，義井之類是也。至行過人曰義，義士，義俠，義姑，義夫，義婦之類是也。自外入而非正者曰義，義父，義兒，義兄弟，義服之類是也。衣裳器物亦然。在首曰義髻，在衣曰義襴，義領之類是也。合眾物爲之，則有義漿，義墨，義酒。禽畜之賢者，則有義犬，義烏，義鷹，義鶻。　又義渠，戎國地。【史記·秦本紀】伐義渠，虜其主。【註】寧廣二州，春秋及戰國時爲義渠，戎國之地也。　又姓。【前漢·酷吏傳】義縱，何東人。　又與誼同。【前漢·董仲舒傳】漸民以仁，摩民以誼。　又與儀通。【前漢·鄒陽傳】使東牟朱虛東褒義父之後。【註】應劭曰：邾儀父也。師古曰：義讀曰儀。　又與宜同。【韻補】周官凡殺人而義者，史記君義嗣，𠀤魚羈切。　又【韻補】叶牛何切。周官註：儀作義，古皆音俄。古文尚書：無偏無陂，遵王之義。陂音坡。◎按說文在我部。今從正字通倂入。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 未, middle; Sheep radical 羊; p.952 #32)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 宜寄 — sounding like 議 (yì).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "one's own dignified bearing; it follows 我 (self) and 羊 (sheep)." Note: Xu Xuan and the others say, "it shares sense with 善 (good), hence it follows 羊." *Shiming*: "義 is 宜 (fitting): to order and shape things so each is fit." Yijing, *Qian*: "to profit things suffices to harmonize with right (義)." Shuogua zhuan: "the Way set up for man is benevolence and right (義)."
- *The many senses of 義 in names.* The Rongzhai suibi: "to uphold the right way is 義 — 義師 (a righteous army), 義戰 (a just war); what the multitude revere is 義 — 義帝 (the Righteous Emperor); what is shared in common is 義 — 義倉 (charity-granary), 義社, 義田, 義學, 義役, 義井 and the like; conduct surpassing others is 義 — 義士 (a man of honour), 義俠, 義姑, 義夫, 義婦 and the like; what comes from outside and is not proper is 義 — 義父 (foster-father), 義兒, 義兄弟, 義服 and the like; so too of garments and wares — on the head 義髻 (a false topknot), on the dress 義襴, 義領 and the like; things of many parts have 義漿, 義墨, 義酒; and the worthy among beasts have 義犬, 義烏, 義鷹, 義鶻."
- *義渠, the land of a Rong state.* Shiji, *Annals of Qin*: "he attacked Yiqu and captured its lord"; note: "the two prefectures Ning and Guang were, in Spring-and-Autumn and Warring-States times, the land of the Rong state of Yiqu."
- *A surname.* Former Han, *Biographies of the Harsh Officials*: "Yi Zong, a man of Hedong."
- *Same as 誼 (right, duty).* Former Han, *Biography of Dong Zhongshu*: "to steep the people in benevolence, to rub them with right (誼)."
- *Interchangeable with 儀 (Yi).* Former Han, *Biography of Zou Yang*: "to send east to Moudongxu to honour the descendants of Yifu (義父)"; note: Ying Shao: "Zhu Yifu (邾儀父)"; Yan Shigu: "義 is read 儀."
- *Same as 宜.* *Yunbu*: "the Zhouguan's 'whoever kills a man yet fittingly (義),' and the Shiji's 'the lord rightly (義) succeeded,' are both fanqie 魚羈."

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 牛何.* The Zhouguan note: "儀 written for 義, anciently both sounding 俄." The Old-Script Shu: "without bias, without slant, follow the king's right (義)" (陂 sounds 坡).

**On the graph.** A note: in the Shuowen 義 is in the 我 radical; here, following the Zhengzitong, it is merged in under 羊.
