**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 戌, middle; Short-tailed-bird radical 隹; p.1366 #14)*

**Ancient graph.** 邕.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 於容, sounding like 廱 (yōng).

**Etymology (Yupian).** *Yupian*: "harmonious (和)." Shu, *Yao dian*: "the black-haired folk, ah, were transformed and made concordant (時雍)."

**Senses.**
- *Also* Erya, *Shi tian*: "the Grand Year standing in 戊 is called Zhuyong (著雍)."
- *A water-name.* *Shuijing*: "water on all four sides is called 雍."
- *A county-name.* Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "Yongnu county (雍奴縣) in Yuyang commandery."
- *Also* Former Han, *Biography of Prince Jing of Zhongshan*: "the Master of the Yong Gate (雍門子) chanted faintly"; note: "Zhang Yan says: a worthy of Qi who dwelt at the Yong Gate, taking it for his style. Su Lin says: a man of the Six-States time, named Zhou."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: a surname. Yong Jiu (雍糾), son-in-law of Zhai Zhong; seen in Zuozhuan, Huan yr 15.
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: used interchangeably with 雝. Ban Gu, *Liang du fu*: "and so flows the moat of the Hall of Learning (辟雍)." A note: the *Liji, Wang zhi* writes 辟雝.
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 委勇, sounding like 壅 (yǒng). Zhouli, *Qiu guan, Sikou*: "the Yong officers (雍氏)"; note: "it means those who embank and dam to stop water"; *Shiwen*: "雍, fanqie 於勇."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: "to aid (祐)." Yang Xiong, *Ganquan fu*: "the gods aid (雍) his weal"; note: "Jin Zhuo says: 雍 means to aid; Yan Shigu says: 雍 means to gather; 雍 is read as 擁."
- *Also* *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 於用, sounding like 㙲 (yòng). *Guangyun*: one of the Nine Provinces. 雍 means to enclose (擁): the Xiao on the east, the Han on the west, the marches on the south, Juyong on the north — enclosed and screened by the four ranges. Shu, *Yu gong*: "the Black Water and the western He make Yongzhou (雍州)"; *Shiwen*: "雍, fanqie 於用." A note: the *Erya, Shi di* writes 雝.
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: a state-name. Zuozhuan, Xi yr 24: "Gao, Yong, Cao, and Teng (郜雍曹滕)"; note: "the state of Yong (雍國), in Shanyang county of Henei"; *Shiwen*: "雍, fanqie 於用."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: a surname. *Yun Hui*: "descendants of Yongbo (雍伯), son of King Wen."
