**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, upper; Use radical 用; p.755 #24)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𤰆, 𠛁.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 余頌, the departing tone of 容 (yòng).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "What may be put into practice (可施行)." Yijing, *Qian* hexagram: "the first nine: the hidden dragon, do not act (勿用)"; subcommentary: "it is fit only to lie hidden, and may not be put to use." Shu, *Gao Yao mo*: "Heaven punishes the guilty, with the five punishments for the five uses (五用)."

**Senses.**
- *To employ (使).* *Guangyun*. Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 26: "though Chu has the talent, it is Jin that uses (用) it." Du Fu's poem: "since of old, great talent is hard to use (用)."
- *Function, efficacy (功用).* Yijing, *Xi ci*: "manifest in benevolence, hidden in its working (用)"; subcommentary: "it means the function lying hidden, not letting things know of it — that is 'hidden in its working.'" Lunyu: "in the practice (用) of the rites, harmony is to be prized."
- *Goods, wealth (貨).* Shu, *Da Yu mo*: "rectify virtue, make useful the goods (利用), enrich life"; subcommentary: "those above being thrifty and not wasteful, using things to advantage, so that wealth and goods abound." Liji, *Wang zhi*: "the Prime Minister fixes the state's expenditure (國用), always at year's end, when the five grains are all in, and then fixes the state's expenditure."
- *Therewith, by means of (以).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the planners are very many, and so (用) nothing is achieved." Old Yuefu: "by what (何用) shall I know my husband? the white horse following the dark colt."
- *To employ, as a task (庸).* Lunyu: "then the people of the four quarters will come, bearing their children on their backs — what need (焉用) to farm?"
- *A utensil, implement (器用).* *Zengyun*. Shu, *Weizi*: "now the people of Yin pilfer and steal the sacrificial victims of the spirits, the offerings and beasts (牲用), to harbour them"; tradition: "the substance of a vessel is called 用." Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 25: "our former kings relied on its useful implements (利器用)."
- *To penetrate, get through (通).* *Guangyun*.
- *Surname.* The Han had Yong Dou, magistrate of Gaotang.

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 容 (róng), fanqie 餘封.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the good counsel they do not follow, the bad they on the contrary use (用)." Lu Jia, *Xinyu*: "when the great transforming is cut off and does not pass through, the Way and its virtue are set out but not used (用)." Also: "standing, it is the ancestor of all the trunks of Mount Tai; fallen, it is the use (用) of myriad ages."

**On the graph.** *Liushu zheng e*: Zhou Boqi says, "用 is the old graph for 鏞 (a bell); in the ancient inscriptions, the graph 鐘 in the Shang bell-vessel and the Yin gui-vessel is everywhere written 用; later men borrowed it for the 'use' graph."

**Textual note.** Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 25, "our former kings relied on its implements (器用)" — checked against the original, the word 利 is added before 器用.
