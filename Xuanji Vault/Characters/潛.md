**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.649 #09)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 昨鹽. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 慈鹽 — sounding like 𤄵 (qián).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "To wade water (涉水)." Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "潛涵 means to sink (沉)." Also "to swim (游)"; note: "to go submerged in water is also called 游."

**Senses.**
- *To hide (藏).* Yijing, *Qian* hexagram: "the yang force lies hidden (潛藏)."
- *Deep (深).* Erya, *Shi yan*: "潛 means deep." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "though one lie hidden and sunk (潛雖伏矣)."
- *Another name for the Han river.* Erya, *Shi shui*: "the water issuing from the Jiang is the Tuo (沱), from the Han the Qian (潛)." Shu, *Yu gong*: "the Tuo and the Qian (潛) being now channeled."
- *A hill-name.* Erya, *Shi qiu*: "Jin has the Qian hill (潛丘)."
- *A place-name.* Chunqiu, Yin yr 2: "the duke met the Rong at Qian (潛)."
- *A town-name.* Shiji, *Hereditary House of Chu*: "Wu took Chu's Liu and Qian (六潛)."
- *Qianjiang (潛江), a county-name.* *Guangyu ji*: "belonging to Anlu prefecture."
- *An ox-name.* *Youyang zazu*: "in the great river of Goulou county there is the submerged ox (潛牛)."
- *Surname.* The Song Pacification Commissioner Qian Shuoyou.
- *Also* *Xiao Erya*: "where fish rest is called 潛." Shijing, *Zhou song*: "in the shelters (潛) are many fish"; tradition: "潛 is a fish-shelter of brushwood (糝)."
- *Also* *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*: fanqie 慈艷, sounding like 𥌳 (qiàn). Same sense. One account: an underground stream (伏流).
