**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls еҚҜ, upper; Heart radical еҝғ; p.375 #02)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie рӨ°һеҗү. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie з’§еҗү вҖ” sounding like з•ў (bГ¬).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "The dividing-limit (еҲҶжҘө)." It follows е…« (eight) and жҲҲ; жҲҲ also gives the sound. *Zhao Yiguang's gloss*: "ејӢ is like a marker-sign, and the dividing-limit is like a boundary; hence it follows е…« and ејӢ."

**Senses.**
- *A word of certainty (е®ҡиҫӯ).* Shijing, *Qi feng*: "in taking a wife, how does one do it? one must (еҝ…) tell one's father and mother."
- *Wholly, only (е°Ҳ).* Yang Xiong, *Tai xuan jing*: "the red stone is not robbed of its red вҖ” the resolve (еҝ…) of the man of principle"; note: "stone cannot be robbed of its hardness, cinnabar cannot be robbed of its red, like the man of principle's wholeness of resolve (еҝ…е°Ҳ)."
- *To insist on, count on (жңҹеҝ…).* Lunyu: "the Master cut off four things: he had no surmising, no insisting (еҝ…)."
- *To make sure (еҜ©).* Later Han, *Biography of Liu Tao*: "those he took as friends had surely (еҝ…) to be of one mind."
- *Sure, decisive (жһң).* Later Han, *Eulogy in the Annals of Emperor Xuan*: "the rule of the filial Xuan: rewards were trusted and punishments sure (еҝ…зҪ°)."
- *Biyu (еҝ…иӮІ), a personal name*, an aide of Suiren. *Qunfu lu*: "Biyu received the tax-customs"; note: "he received the levies, taxes, and corvГ©e, and what was fit to be done with them."
- *Also* *Zihui Bu*: иөӨзҠ®еҝ…еҠӣ, a mountain-name, where the He water issues. Seen in the monk Zongle's record.
- *Also* *Gujin zikao*: fanqie е№·еҲ—, sounding like зёӘ (bГ¬). A cord (зө„). Zhouli, *Dong guan, Kaogong ji*: "in the jade-worker's task, the Son of Heaven's gui-tablet has a mid-cord (дёӯеҝ…)"; note: "it means binding the centre with a cord, to guard against its slipping and falling." A note: by the *Shiwen* of the *Zhouli Kaogong*, еҝ… is just зө„ (a cord), and reading it as зёӘ is the vulgar reading; the дӘҗ of the bow-brace follows йҹӢ, and the *Zhengwei* would cite this to refute the *Shuowen* вҖ” which is wrong-headed; one should take the *Shuowen* as correct. Also a note: the graph еҝ… does not follow еҝғ; the *Zihui* placed it in the Heart radical, and the *Zhengzitong* followed suit, taking it by resemblance of form, for ease of lookup only.
