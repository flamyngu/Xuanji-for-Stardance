**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls жңӘ, middle; Silk radical зіё; p.937 #01)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie йҷ„иўҒ. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie з¬ҰиўҒ вҖ” sounding like з…© (fГЎn).

**Etymology and senses.** Shu, *Zhonghui zhi gao*: "truly his followers are many (з№Ғ)"; tradition: "з№Ғ means many."
- *Also* Liji, *Xiangyinjiu yi*: "the ceremonies of bowing on arrival, offering, pledging, and yielding are abundant (з№Ғ)"; note: "з№Ғ is like зӣӣ (profuse)."
- *Also* Preface to the *Xiaojing*: "how could one not prune away its rank profusion (з№Ғи•Ә)?"; note: "з№Ғ means tangled (йӣң)."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "з№Ғ means a general scope (ж§Ә)."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: fanqie и–„е®ҳ; *Ji Yun*: fanqie и’Іе®ҳ вҖ” sounding like йһ¶ (pГЎn). Liji, *Liqi*: "the Great Carriage has one set of girth-bands and breast-tassels (з№Ғзә“), the next carriage seven sets"; subcommentary: "з№Ғ is the horse's belly-band." Zuozhuan, Cheng yr 2: "he asked for the hanging bells and breast-tassels (з№Ғзә“) to attend court"; note: "з№Ғзә“ is a horse-trapping."
- *Also interchangeable with жЁҠ.* Zhouli, *Chunguan, Jinche*, note on жЁҠзә“: "жЁҠ is read like the йһ¶ of йһ¶её¶ вҖ” it means today's horse's great belt."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: fanqie и–„жіў; *Ji Yun*: fanqie и’Іжіў вҖ” sounding like е©Ҷ (pГі). *Yupian*: "a surname." Zuozhuan, Ding yr 4: "of the seven clans of the Yin people, there was the Po (з№Ғж°Ҹ) clan." Former Han, *Biography of Chen Tang*: "the Grandee Secretary Po Yanshou (з№Ғе»¶еЈҪ)."
- *Rhymes* (per the *Yunbu*) as fanqie з¬Ұзӯ . Lu Yun, *Xia fujun lei*: "the august robe fair and bright, its radiance teeming and dense; greatly endowed with abundant grace, his brilliance early flourishing (з№Ғ)."
- *Rhymes* (per the *Yunbu*) as fanqie жұҫжІ…. Lady Zuo, *Yanghou lei*: "Heaven's favor upright and blessed, able to prosper and to multiply (з№Ғ); a hundredfold these blessings, rearing sages, rearing worthies."

**Textual notes.** In the Xiangyinjiu yi citation, after жӢңиҮі the two graphs зҚ»й…¬ are added (per the original).
