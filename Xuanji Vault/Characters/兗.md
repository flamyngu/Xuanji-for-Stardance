## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集下〉【儿字部】頁125第15【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】以轉切【正韻】以淺切，𠀤沿上聲。【說文】兗州之兗，九州之渥地也。【書·禹貢】濟河惟兗州。【春秋·元命包】五星爲兗州。兗，端也，信也。【韻會】蓋取流水以名。後魏置南兗州於譙，置西兗州於定陶，隋改魯州，唐復兗州，宋襲慶府。又【韻會】姓也。【集韻】通作沇。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Legs radical 儿; p.125 #15)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 以轉. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 以淺 — the rising tone of 沿 (yǎn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the 兗 of Yanzhou (兗州), the well-watered land among the Nine Provinces." Shu, *Yu gong*: "between the Ji and the Yellow River lies Yanzhou (兗州)." The Chunqiu Yuanmingbao: "the five planets make Yanzhou (兗州); 兗 means uprightness (端) and good faith (信)." *Yun Hui*: "the name is taken from the flowing waters. The Later Wei set up Southern Yanzhou at Qiao and Western Yanzhou at Dingtao; the Sui changed it to Luzhou; the Tang restored Yanzhou; under the Song it was Xiqing prefecture."
- *A surname.* *Yun Hui.* *Ji Yun*: used interchangeably with 沇.
