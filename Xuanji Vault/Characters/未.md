## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集中〉【木字部】頁509第05【唐韻】【集韻】【類篇】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤無沸切，音味。【說文】未，味也。六月，百果滋味已具，五行木老於未，象木重枝葉之形。【爾雅·釋天】太歲在未曰協洽。【禮·月令註】季夏者，斗建未之辰也。　又【前漢·律歷志】昧薆於未。【釋名】未，昧也。日中則昃，向幽昧也。　又【玉篇】未猶不也，未有不，卽有也。　又未央，複姓。見【李淳風·乙巳占】。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, middle; Tree radical 木; p.509 #05)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Leipian*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 無沸 — sounding like 味 (wèi).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "未 means flavor (味). In the sixth month, the flavors of the hundred fruits are all formed; among the Five Phases, Wood grows old at wei (未); it pictures the form of a tree with doubled branches and leaves." Erya, *Shitian*: "when the Grand Year is at wei (未) it is called Xieqia (協洽)." Note to Liji, *Yue ling*: "late summer is the hour when the Dipper points to wei (未)."
- *Also* Former Han, *Treatise on Pitch and Calendar*: "dim and hidden (昧薆) at wei (未)." *Shiming*: "未 means dim (昧): the sun at noon declines, turning toward the dark and dim."
- *Also* *Yupian*: "未 is like 'not' (不); 'not without' (未有不) is just 'there is.'"
- *Also 未央 (Weiyang), a compound surname*; see Li Chunfeng's Yisi zhan.
