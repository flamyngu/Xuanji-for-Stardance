## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集下〉【又字部】頁166第05    〔古文〕：𠭧【唐韻】殖酉切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】是酉切，𠀤音壽。【說文】相付也。【玉篇】得也。【易·旣濟】實受其福。【詩·大雅】受天之祜。又承也。【李適之法觀禪師𥓓銘】孰承最上，密受居多。又盛也。【杜甫詩】野航恰受兩三人。又容納也。【論語】君子不可以小知，而可大受也。又【正字通】神呪切，收去聲。【詩·小雅】投畀有北，有北不受。叶下昊。昊，許𠋫切。○按受字《韻書》無去聲。又叶音暑。【張衡·誚靑衣賦】晏嬰潔志，不顧景女。乃雋不疑，奉霍不受。【韻會】毛氏曰：从𠬪从冖。俗或作𤓾，非。𤓾音胡到切，下从丈。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Again radical 又; p.166 #05)*

**Ancient graph.** 𠭧.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 殖酉. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 是酉 — sounding like 壽 (shòu).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to hand over to one another (相付)." *Yupian*: "to get (得)." Yijing, *Jiji*: "truly he receives (受) its blessing." Shijing, *Daya*: "receiving (受) Heaven's favor."
- *Also, to inherit, to take up (承).* Li Shizhi, *stele-inscription for Chan Master Faguan*: "who took up (承) the highest? — much was secretly received (受)."
- *Also, to hold (盛).* Du Fu's poem: "the wild ferry just holds (受) two or three men."
- *Also, to take in, to accommodate (容納).* Lunyu: "the gentleman cannot be known in small matters, but can be entrusted (大受) with great ones."
- *Also* *Zhengzitong*: fanqie 神呪 — the departing tone of 收 (shòu). Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "cast him to the north; the north will not receive (受) him" — rhyming with 昊 below, 昊 fanqie 許𠋫. A note: the rhyme-books give 受 no departing tone.
- *Rhymes as* 暑 (shǔ). Zhang Heng, *Qiao qingyi fu*: "Yan Ying kept his aims pure, heeding not the shadow-maid; so Jun Buyi, serving Huo, would not accept (受)."

**On the graph.** *Yun Hui*: Mr. Mao says it follows 𠬪 and 冖; vulgarly written 𤓾, which is wrong (𤓾 is fanqie 胡到, with 丈 below).
