## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集下〉【衣字部】頁1113第04【唐韻】去金切【集韻】【韻會】袪音切，𠀤音欽。【玉篇】大被也。【詩·召南】抱衾與裯。【孟子】謂棺槨衣衾之美也。【疏】凡衾皆三幅。【鄭註】衾，單被也。【陳子昂詩】旖旎光首飾，葳蕤爛錦衾。【類篇】作𧘭。【集韻】作𡘚。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, lower; Clothes radical 衣; p.1113 #04)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 去金. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 袪音 — sounding like 欽 (qīn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Yupian*: "a large quilt." Shijing, *Zhaonan*: "she carries her coverlet (衾) and bed-curtain." Mengzi: "he speaks of the fineness of coffin, shell, grave-clothes, and shroud (衾)." Subcommentary: "every 衾 is three breadths wide." Zheng Xuan's note: "衾 is a single-layer coverlet." Chen Zi'ang's poem: "softly gleam the head-ornaments, luxuriantly shines the brocade coverlet (錦衾)."

**On the graph.** Leipian writes it 𧘭; *Ji Yun* writes it 𡘚.
