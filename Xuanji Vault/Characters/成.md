> 《康熙字典》〈卯集中〉【戈字部】頁411第11    〔古文〕：𢦩【唐韻】是征切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】時征切，𠀤音城。【說文】就也。【廣韻】畢也。凡功卒業就謂之成。　又平也。【周禮·地官·調人】凡過而殺傷人者，以民成之。【疏】成，平也。非故心殺傷人，故共鄕里和解之也。【詩·大雅】虞芮質厥成。又【左傳·隱六年】鄭人來輸平。【公羊傳】輸平猶墮成也。【文七年】惠伯成之。　又終也。凡樂一終爲一成。【書·益稷】簫韶九成。【儀禮·燕禮】笙入三成。【註】三成謂三終也。　又善也。【禮·檀弓】竹不成用。【註】成，猶善也。　又【周禮·天官·大宰】八灋五曰官成。註官成，謂官府之成事品式也。又【秋官·士師】掌士之八成。【註】八成者，行事有八篇，若今時決事比也。【釋文】凡言成者，皆舊有成事品式。　又必也。【吳語】勝未可成。【註】猶必也。　又倂也。【儀禮·旣夕】俎二以成。【註】成，猶倂也。　又【禮·王制】司會以歲之成質于天子。【註】計要也。【周禮·天官·司會】以參互攷日成，以月要攷月成，以歲會攷歲成。　又【司馬法】通十爲成。【周禮·冬官考工記】方十里爲成。【左傳·哀元年】有田一成。　又重也。【爾雅·釋地】丘一成爲敦丘。【註】成，猶重也。周禮曰：爲壇三成。【疏】言丘上更有一丘，相重累者。　又【釋名】成，盛也。　又【諡法】安民立政曰成。　又州名。古西戎白馬氏國，西魏置成州，唐同谷郡。　又姓。周武王子成伯之後。又盆成，陽成，皆複姓。　又【集韻】辰陵切，音承。本作郕。或省作成。地名。　又【韻補】叶𨻰羊切，音常。【范蠡曰】得時不成，反受其殃。又【史記·龜筴傳】螟螽歲生，五穀不成。叶上祥。    考證：〔【周禮·天官·大宰】職有官成。【註】官成者，謂官府之有成事品式也。〕　謹照原文改爲八灋五曰官成。註官成，謂官府之成事品式也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, middle; Halberd radical 戈; p.411 #11)*

**Ancient graph.** 𢦩.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 是征. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 時征 — sounding like 城 (chéng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to accomplish (就)." *Guangyun*: "to finish (畢)." Whenever a work is brought to completion it is called 成.
- *To settle, reconcile (平).* Zhouli, *Diguan, Tiaoren*: "in all cases of killing or wounding by accident, the Reconciler makes peace (成) between the people"; subcommentary: "成 is to settle; since the killing was not deliberate, the village together reconciles them." Shijing, *Daya*: "Yu and Rui brought their dispute to settlement (成)." Also Zuozhuan, Yin yr 6: "the men of Zheng came to deliver a peace"; Gongyang zhuan: "'delivering peace' is like undoing a settlement (墮成)." Wen yr 7: "Huibo settled (成) it."
- *To conclude (終); a movement of music.* Whenever a piece of music ends once, that is one 成. Shu, *Yi Ji*: "the Xiao-shao music in nine movements (九成)." Yili, *Yan li*: "the reed-organ enters and plays three movements (三成)"; note: "three 成 means three endings."
- *Good, finished well (善).* Liji, *Tan Gong*: "the bamboo vessels are not made fit for full use (成用)"; note: "成 is like 'good [finished].'"
- *Settled precedents.* Zhouli, *Tianguan, Da zai*: "of the eight statutes, the fifth is the 'official precedents' (官成)"; note: "官成 means the settled cases and forms of the offices." Also *Qiuguan, Shishi*: "the Master of Officers has charge of the officers' eight precedents (八成)"; note: "the 八成 are eight rolls of precedents for conducting affairs, like the 'case-comparisons' of today"; the Shiwen: "whenever 成 is spoken of, it is in all cases the old settled cases and forms."
- *Certain, sure (必).* The Wuyu: "victory cannot yet be assured (成)"; note: "like 'certain.'"
- *To pair, combine (倂).* Yili, *Ji xi*: "the stands, two making a set (成)"; note: "成 is like 'to pair.'"
- *An account, a reckoning.* Liji, *Wangzhi*: "the Accountant submits the year's account (成) to the Son of Heaven"; note: "the budget-summary." Zhouli, *Tianguan, Sihui*: "by cross-checking he audits the daily account (日成), by the monthly summary the monthly account (月成), by the yearly assembly the yearly account (歲成)."
- *A measure of land.* The Sima fa: "ten *tong* make a 成." Zhouli, *Kaogong ji*: "ten *li* square make a 成." Zuozhuan, Ai yr 1: "he had a tract of land of one 成."
- *A tier, layer (重).* Erya, *Shidi*: "a mound of one tier (一成) is a *dunqiu*"; note: "成 is like 'a layer'; the Zhouli says 'making an altar of three tiers (三成)'"; subcommentary: "upon the mound is yet another mound, piled one on another."
- *Flourishing (盛).* Shiming: "成 is 'flourishing.'"
- *Posthumous-title rule:* "to bring peace to the people and establish good government is called 成."
- *A prefecture-name.* Anciently the state of the Baima clan of the western Rong; the Western Wei set up Chengzhou (成州), the Tang's Tonggu commandery.
- *A surname.* Descendants of Chengbo, a son of King Wu of Zhou. Also Pencheng (盆成) and Yangcheng (陽成) are compound surnames.
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 辰陵, sounding like 承 (chéng). Originally written 郕, sometimes abbreviated to 成. A place-name.
- *Yunbu*: rhymes as 常 (cháng), fanqie 𨻰羊. Fan Li said: "to gain the moment yet not bring it to fruition (成) is, on the contrary, to suffer its calamity." Also Shiji, *Biography of the Tortoise and Milfoil*: "the locusts breed year by year, and the five grains do not ripen (成)" — rhyming with 祥 above.

**Textual notes.** The entry "職有官成。註官成者，謂官府之有成事品式也" — checked against the original, corrected to "八灋五曰官成。註官成，謂官府之成事品式也."
