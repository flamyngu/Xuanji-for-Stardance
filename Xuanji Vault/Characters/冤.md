## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集下〉【冖字部】頁130第27【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤於袁切，音鴛。【說文】屈也。从兔从冖。兔在冖下不得走，益屈折也。【廣韻】枉曲也。【史記·于定國傳】張釋之爲廷尉，天下無冤民。定國爲廷尉，民自以不冤。又冤句縣。【韻會】在曹州。句音劬。【正韻】亦作寃。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Cover radical 冖; p.130 #27)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui* all: fanqie 於袁 — sounding like 鴛 (yuān).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to be bent, wronged (屈)." It follows 兔 (hare) and 冖 (cover): the hare under the cover cannot run, the more cramped and bent. *Guangyun*: "twisted, wronged." Shiji, *Biography of Yu Dingguo*: "when Zhang Shizhi was Commandant of Justice there were no wronged (冤) folk in the realm; when Dingguo was Commandant, the people held themselves unwronged (不冤)."
- *Yuanqu (冤句), a county.* *Yun Hui*: "in Caozhou; 句 sounds like 劬." *Zheng Yun*: also written 寃.
