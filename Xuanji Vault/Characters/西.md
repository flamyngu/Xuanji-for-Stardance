## Shuowen Jiezi (說文解字) — original
> 《西部》西：鳥在巢上。象形。日在西方而鳥棲，故因以為東西之西。凡西之屬皆从西。

## Guangyun (廣韻) — original
> 《廣韻·上平聲·齊·西》西：秋方。《說文》曰：鳥在巢上也，日在西方而鳥西故因以爲東西之西篆文作㢴象形亦州名本漢車師國之地至貞觀討平以其地爲西州亦姓又漢複姓十一氏左傳秦師西乞術宋大夫西鉏吾西郷錯出丗本又黃帝娶西陵氏爲妃名纍祖史記魏文侯鄴令西門豹周末分爲東西二周武公庶子西周爲氏晉有北海西郭陽何承天以爲西朝名士慕容廆以北平西方虔爲股肱何氏姓苑有西野氏西宫氏王符潛夫論姓氏志曰如有東門西郭南宫北郭皆是因居也。先稽切，十六。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, lower; Cover radical 襾; p.1128 #10)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𠧧, 㢴.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 先稽. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 先齊 — sounding like 粞 (xī).

**Etymology and senses.** *Leipian*: "the Metal direction (金方)." *Shuowen*: "a bird upon its nest. When the sun is in the west and the bird roosts, it was therefore taken as the 西 of 東西 (east-west); in seal written 㢴, a pictograph." Former Han, *Treatise on the Calendar*: "the Lesser Yin is the west; 西 means to shift, decline (遷): the yin *qi* makes things fall; in season it is autumn." *Shangshu dazhuan*: "what is the west (西方)? the 'fresh direction (鮮方)'…"
- *A place name.* Jinshu, *Treatise on Geography*: 西郡. *Yun Hui*: "the Tang set up Xizhou."
- *Surname.* *Tongzhi, Shizu lüe*: the Xi clan. *Xingyuan*: "descendants of Ximen Bao, changed to Xi."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 相咨, sounding like 私 (sī); same sense.
- *Pianhai*: fanqie 蘇前, sounding like 先 (xiān). Former Han, *Jiao si ge*: "the image-cart gleams, the white [bird] gathers in the west (西), it eats sweet dew and drinks from the glory-spring." Hou Han, *Biography of Zhao Yi*, *Rhapsody on the Trapped Bird*: "…now you rouse me in the west (西)."
- *Rhymes as 辛 (xīn), fanqie 斯人.* Wang Yanshou, *Rhapsody on the Lu Lingguang Hall*: "…orchid and iris swaying east and west (西)."
- *Leipian*: fanqie 乙却, sounding like 約 (yuē) — "to level off in measuring (平量)."
- *Guangyun*: the *zhou*-script form is 𠧧. — A note: in the *Yupian* and like works 西 is its own radical; here, following the *Zihui* and *Zhengzitong*, it is placed under the 襾 radical.

**Textual note.** 郊祀志 should read 郊祀歌.
