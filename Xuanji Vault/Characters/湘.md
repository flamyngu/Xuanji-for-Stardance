## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈巳集上〉【水字部】頁637第11【唐韻】【正韻】息良切【集韻】【韻會】思將切，𠀤音襄。水名。【說文】水出零陵陽海山，北入江。　又山名。【史記·黃帝本紀】黃帝南至于江，登熊湘。　又地名。【廣輿記】長沙府吳晉曰湘州。　又湖名。【廣輿記】在紹興府蕭山縣。　又烹也。【詩·召南】于以湘之，維錡及釜。　又【集韻】思莊切，音霜。義同。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.637 #11)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 息良. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 思將 — sounding like 襄 (xiāng).

**Etymology and senses.** A river-name. *Shuowen*: "the water issues from Mount Yanghai in Lingling and flows north into the Yangtze."
- *A mountain-name.* Shiji, *Annals of the Yellow Emperor*: "the Yellow Emperor went south to the Yangtze and climbed Xiong and Xiang (熊湘)."
- *A place-name.* Guangyu ji: "in Changsha prefecture, Wu and Jin called it Xiangzhou (湘州)."
- *A lake-name.* Guangyu ji: "in Xiaoshan county of Shaoxing prefecture."
- *To boil, cook (烹).* Shijing, *Zhaonan*: "wherewith to cook (湘) it? — the tripod and the cauldron."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 思莊, sounding like 霜 (shuāng); same sense.
