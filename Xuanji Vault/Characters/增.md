## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集中〉【土字部】頁238第25【唐韻】作滕切【集韻】【韻會】咨騰切【正韻】咨登切，𠀤音曾。【說文】益也。一曰重也。【詩·小雅】如川之方至，以莫不增。【史記·黃霸傳】戸口歲增。　又衆也。【詩·魯頌】烝徒增增。　又與層通。增城漢宮。見前城字註。【揚雄·甘泉賦】增宮參差。　又與曾通。　又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】子鄧切【正韻】子孕切，𠀤音橧。賸也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, middle; Earth radical 土; p.238 #25)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 作滕. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 咨騰. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 咨登 — sounding like 曾 (zēng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to increase, add (益); one says, to layer (重)." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "like a river just coming on, so that nothing fails to swell (增)." Shiji, *Biography of Huang Ba*: "the households and population increased (增) year by year."
- *Also, many, teeming (衆).* Shijing, *Lusong*: "the throng of followers, teeming and teeming (增增)."
- *Also interchangeable with 層 (tier).* The Zengcheng (增城) was a Han palace; see the note under the graph 城 above. Yang Xiong, *Ganquan fu*: "the tiered palaces (增宮) rise jagged and uneven."
- *Also interchangeable with 曾.*
- *Also* *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 子鄧; *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 子孕 — sounding like 橧 (zèng): a surplus, an excess (賸).
