## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈巳集上〉【水字部】頁624第23【唐韻】縛牟切【集韻】【韻會】房尤切【正韻】房鳩切，𠀤音罘。【說文】氾也。【論語】乗桴浮於海。　又順流曰浮。【書·禹貢】浮于濟漯。　又濟涉腰瓠曰浮。【淮南子·釋山訓】百人抗浮。【註】浮，瓠也。　又溢也，過也。【禮·坊記】君子與其使食浮于人也，寧使人浮于食。　又先時曰浮。【書·盤庚】鮮以不浮于天時。　又輕也。【楚語】疏其穢而鎭其浮。　又浮浮，氣烝貌。【詩·大雅】烝之浮浮。　又雨雪盛貌。【詩·小雅】雨雪浮浮。　又衆彊貌【詩·大雅】江漢浮浮。　又浮沉，無定之意。【詩·小雅】載沉載浮。　又【小爾雅】浮，罰也。謂罰爵也。【禮·投壷】無偝立，無踰言，若是者浮。　又水名。【水經注】靑河東北，浮水故瀆出焉。　又山名。【山海經】竹山西百二十里曰浮山。又【廣輿記】廣東高州府城東有浮山。又羅浮，二山名。【羅浮山記】在增城，博羅二縣境。　又竹名。【戴凱之·竹譜】浮竹亞節，虛軟厚肉。　又石名。【左思·吳都賦】浮石若桴。　又天浮，星名。【甘氏星經】天浮四星，在左旗南北列。主漏刻。　又【莊綽鷄肋編】釣竿之半，繫以荻梗，謂之浮子。　又【甄權脈經】浮爲風爲虛。　又【集韻】普溝切，音桴。漂也。　又【韻補】叶符非切，音肥。【楚辭·遠遊】指炎神而直馳兮，吾將往乎南疑。覽方外之荒忽兮，沛罔象而自浮。    考證：〔【詩·大雅】烝之浮之。〕　謹照原書改烝之浮浮。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.624 #23)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 縛牟. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 房尤. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 房鳩 — sounding like 罘 (fú).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to overflow, to float (氾)." Lunyu: "I would ride a raft and float (浮) on the sea."
- *To go with the current.* Shu, *Yu gong*: "floated (浮) on the Ji and Ta."
- *To ford with a waist-gourd.* Huainanzi, *Shishan xun*: "a hundred men carry gourd-floats (浮)"; note: "浮, a gourd."
- *To overflow, to exceed (溢, 過).* Liji, *Fang ji*: "the gentleman would rather let the man exceed (浮) the food than the food exceed the man."
- *Ahead of time.* Shu, *Pan geng*: "few are those who do not anticipate (浮) Heaven's seasons."
- *Light (輕).* Chu yu: "thin out its filth and weigh down its lightness (浮)."
- *浮浮, rising vapour.* Shijing, *Daya*: "steaming it up, billowing (浮浮)."
- *Heavy rain or snow.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the snow falls thick (浮浮)."
- *The look of a strong multitude.* Shijing, *Daya*: "the Jiang and Han roll mighty (浮浮)."
- *浮沉, floating and sinking (instability).* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "now it sinks, now it floats (浮)."
- *A penalty (罰), i.e. a forfeit-cup.* *Xiao Erya.* Liji, *Tou hu*: "no standing with back turned, no overstepping speech; such a one is penalized (浮)."
- *A water name.* Shuijingzhu: "northeast of the Qing River the old channel of the Fu (浮) waters issues."
- *Mountain names.* Shanhaijing: "120 *li* west of Mount Zhu is Mount Fu (浮山)." *Guangyu ji*: "east of Gaozhou city in Guangdong is Mount Fu." Also 羅浮 (Luofu), twin mountains in the bounds of Zengcheng and Boluo counties.
- *A bamboo.* Dai Kaizhi, *Zhupu*: "the 浮竹 has close joints, hollow-soft and thick-fleshed."
- *A stone.* Zuo Si, *Wudu fu*: "the pumice (浮石) like a raft."
- *天浮, a star.* Gan's *Xingjing*: "the four 天浮 stars lie north and south of the Left Banner, governing the water-clock."
- Zhuang Chuo, *Jilei bian*: "halfway up the fishing-rod, tied with reed-stem — called the 浮子 (float)."
- Zhen Quan, *Maijing*: "the floating (浮) pulse means wind, means deficiency."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 普溝, sounding like 桴 (fú) — "to drift (漂)."
- *Rhymes as 肥 (féi), fanqie 符非.* Chuci, *Yuan you*: "…wave-tossed and formless, I float (浮) of myself."

**Textual note.** 烝之浮之 should read 烝之浮浮.
