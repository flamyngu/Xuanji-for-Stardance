## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈丑集上〉【口字部】頁188第12【唐韻】丕飮切【集韻】【韻會】丕錦切，𠀤匹上聲。【說文】衆庶也。【廣韻】類也。【易·乾卦】品物流形。【疏】品類之物，流布成形。　又【書·舜典】五品不遜。【疏】品爲品秩，一家之內，尊𤰞之差，卽父母兄弟子是也。　又【增韻】物件曰品。【書·禹貢】厥貢惟金三品。【疏】鄭元以爲金三品者，銅三色也。【易·巽卦】田獲三品。【註】一曰乾豆，二曰賔客，三曰充君之庖。【禮·禮器】薦不美多品。又【少儀】問品味，曰：子亟食于某乎。【疏】品味者，殽饌也。【周禮·天官·膳夫】品嘗食。【註】品者，每物皆嘗之，道尊者也。　又【韻會】品格也。【禮·檀弓】品節斯，斯之謂禮。【疏】品，階格也，節制斷也。　又【玉篇】齊也。【周語】品其百籩。　又同也。【前漢·李尋傳】百里爲品。【註】孟康曰：品，同也，言百里內數度同也。　又【玉篇】官品。【周語】外官不過九品。【註】九卿也。　又【廣韻】式也，法也。　又【廣韻】二口則生訟，三口乃能品量。　又官名。【正字通】唐宦官曰品官。　又【廣韻】姓也。出何氏姓苑。【正字通】明有品嵒。　又地名。【左傳·文十六年】楚予乗馹，會師于臨品。　又【前漢·西域傳】戎盧國王治𤰞品城。    考證：〔【書·禹貢】厥貢惟金三品。【疏】鄭云以爲金三品者，銅三色也。〕　謹照原文鄭云改鄭元。〔【左傳·文十五年】楚予乗驛。〕　謹照原文十五年改十六年。驛改馹。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, upper; Mouth radical 口; p.188 #12)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 丕飮. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 丕錦 — the rising tone of 匹 (pǐn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the many, the multitude (衆庶)." *Guangyun*: "a kind, a class (類)." Yijing, *Qian*: "the kinds of things (品物) flow into form"; subcommentary: "things of every class spread abroad and take form."
- *Also* Shu, *Shun dian*: "the five relations (五品) were not docile"; subcommentary: "品 means rank-orders; within a single household, the gradations of high and low — namely father, mother, elder brother, younger brother, and son."
- *Also* *Zengyun*: "an item, an article (物件) is called 品." Shu, *Yu gong*: "their tribute was metal of three sorts (三品)"; subcommentary: "Zheng Xuan held the three sorts of metal to be copper of three colors." Yijing, *Xun*: "in the hunt he takes three sorts (三品)"; note: "the first for the sacrificial dried meats, the second for guests, the third to fill the ruler's kitchen." Liji, *Liqi*: "the offerings are not made fine by many dishes (多品)." Also *Shaoyi*: "in asking about the dishes (品味), one says, 'do you often eat at So-and-so's?'"; subcommentary: "品味 are the viands." Zhouli, *Tianguan, Shanfu*: "he tastes each of the dishes (品嘗食)"; note: "品 means tasting each thing — the way of serving the honored."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: a standard, a rank (品格). Liji, *Tan'gong*: "to grade and regulate this (品節) — this is what is called ritual"; subcommentary: "品 is a graded rank, 節 is to restrain and decide."
- *Also* *Yupian*: "to make even, to order (齊)." Zhou yu: "he orders (品) his hundred baskets."
- *Also, the same (同).* Former Han, *Biography of Li Xun*: "a hundred li make one 品"; note: Meng Kang says: "品 means the same; it says that within a hundred li the measures are uniform."
- *Also* *Yupian*: official rank (官品). Zhou yu: "the outer officials did not exceed nine ranks (九品)"; note: "the nine ministers."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "a model (式); a rule (法)."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: "two mouths give rise to dispute; three mouths can then weigh and judge (品量)."
- *Also an office-name.* *Zhengzitong*: "in the Tang, eunuchs were called rank-officers (品官)."
- *Also* *Guangyun*: a surname; from He's Xingyuan. *Zhengzitong*: "the Ming had Pin Yan (品嵒)."
- *Also a place-name.* Zuozhuan, Wen yr 16: "the Viscount of Chu, riding the post-carriage, joined his forces at Linpin (臨品)." Former Han, *Account of the Western Regions*: "the king of Ronglu governed at Beipin city (𤰞品城)."

**Textual notes.** In the Yu gong subcommentary, 鄭云 should read 鄭元 (Zheng Xuan); and the Zuozhuan citation should be Wen yr 16 (not 15), with 驛 corrected to 馹.
