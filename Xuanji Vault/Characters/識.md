_All senses recorded for this character (Unicode Unihan / CC-CEDICT-class definitions):_
- recognize, understand, know
- to distinguish; to discern; to tell apart
- to appreciate; to recognize the worth of
- knowledge; learning; experience; discernment (知識, 見識)

- (also read zhì) to remember; to keep in mind

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集上〉【言字部】頁1181第17    〔古文〕：𧨺【唐韻】賞職切【集韻】【韻會】設職切，𠀤音式。【說文】常也。一曰知也。【長箋】訓常無意義。【玉篇】識，認也。【增韻】能別識也。又見識也。【詩·大雅】不識不知，順帝之則。【左傳·襄二十九年】吳公子札聘于鄭，見子產如舊相識。【老子·道德經】前識者，道之華而愚之始。【莊子·繕性篇】道固不小行，德固不小識。【揚子·法言】多聞見而識乎正道者，至識也。多聞見而識乎邪道者，迷識也。　又草名。【大戴禮·夏小正】三月采識。識，草也。　又州名。【唐書·地理志】識利州屬高麗降戸州。　又姓。見【姓纂】。　又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤職吏切，音志。與誌同。記也。【易·大畜】君子以多識前言往行，以畜其德。【書·益稷】書用識哉。【論語】女以予爲多學而識之者與。　又【韻會】器之款鏤爲識。【史記·孝武紀】鼎文鏤無款識。【註】韋昭曰：款，刻也。師古曰：識，記也。楊愼曰：三代鐘鼎文，隱起而凸曰款，以象陽，中陷而凹曰識，以象隂。　又【韻會】通作志。【周禮·春官】保章氏掌天星，以志星辰日月之變動。【鄭註】志，古文識字，記也。　又【讀書通】通作幟。【後漢·虞詡傳】以采綖縫賊裾爲幟。【註】幟，記也。　又【集韻】【韻會】𠀤式吏切，音試。義同。又【韻會】表識，謂有所標表，令異日可識知也。【左傳·宣十二年杜註】楚以茅爲旌識，謂以旌旗爲表也。【前漢·劉向傳】不可不識也。【師古註】墓須表識。俱音試。　又【集韻】【類篇】𠀤昌志切，音熾。義同。又【釋名】識，幟也。有章幟可按視也。【前漢·王莽傳】旌旗表識。【師古註】識讀與幟同。　又叶式列切，音刷。【崔駰·達旨】傳序歷數，三代興滅。大庭尚矣，赫胥罔識。　又叶施灼切，音爍。【蘇軾·毛長官詩】宦遊逢此歲年惡，飛蝗來時半天黑。羨君封境稻如雲，蝗自識人人不識。【集韻】或書作𧬣。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, upper; Speech radical 言; p.1181 #17)*

**Ancient graph.** 𧨺.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 賞職. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 設職 — sounding like 式 (shí).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Constant (常); one account: to know (知)." *Changjian*: "the gloss 'constant' has no sense." *Yupian*: "識 means to recognize (認)." *Zengyun*: "to be able to distinguish and recognize (別識); also, discernment, insight (見識)." Shijing, *Daya*: "without knowing (識), without contriving, he follows the Lord's pattern." Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 29: "Prince Zha of Wu, on embassy to Zheng, met Zichan as an old acquaintance (相識)." Laozi, *Dao de jing*: "foreknowledge (前識) is the flower of the Way and the beginning of folly." Zhuangzi, *Shan xing*: "the Way is surely not for petty practice, virtue surely not for petty knowing (小識)." Yang Xiong, *Fa yan*: "to hear and see much and know (識) the right Way is the utmost knowing; to hear and see much and know the crooked Way is deluded knowing."

**Senses.**
- *A plant-name.* Da Dai li, *Xia xiao zheng*: "in the third month they gather 識; 識 is a plant."
- *A prefecture-name.* Tang shu, *Treatise on Geography*: "Shilizhou (識利州) belongs to the surrendered-household prefectures of Goguryeo."
- *Surname.* Seen in the *Xingzuan*.

**Further readings (zhì).**
- *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 職吏, sounding like 志 (zhì) — same as 誌, "to record (記)." Yijing, *Da xu*: "the gentleman thereby records (識) much of former words and past deeds, to nourish his virtue." Shu, *Yi ji*: "write to record (識) it." Lunyu: "do you take me for one who has learned much and records (識) it?"
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: "an inscription incised on a vessel is 識." Shiji, *Annals of Emperor Wu*: "the cauldron's writing was incised, but without inscription (款識)"; note: Wei Zhao, "款 means to carve"; Yan Shigu, "識 means to record"; Yang Shen, "in the bell-and-cauldron writing of the Three Dynasties, what stands out raised and convex is 款, imaging the yang; what sinks in hollow and concave is 識, imaging the yin."
- *Also* *Yun Hui*: "interchangeable with 志." Zhouli, *Chun guan*: "the Baozhang officer managed the heaven-stars, recording (志) the changes and motions of the stars, sun, and moon"; Zheng's note: "志 is the old-script form of 識, 'to record.'"
- *Also* *Dushu tong*: "interchangeable with 幟." Later Han, *Biography of Yu Xu*: "with coloured thread they sewed the rebels' hems as a mark (幟)"; note: "幟 means a mark."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 式吏, sounding like 試 (shì). Same sense. Also *Yun Hui*: "表識 (a marker) means something set up as a sign, so that on another day it may be known (識)." Zuozhuan, Xuan yr 12, Du's note: "Chu used reeds as banner-marks (旌識), meaning to use flags as a sign." Former Han, *Biography of Liu Xiang*: "it may not go unmarked (識)"; Yan Shigu's note: "a grave must have a marker (表識)." Both sounded 試 (shì).
- *Also* *Ji Yun*, *Leipian*: fanqie 昌志, sounding like 熾 (chì). Same sense. Also *Shiming*: "識 is 幟 (a banner); having an emblem-banner that can be looked to." Former Han, *Biography of Wang Mang*: "the banners and flags as markers (表識)"; Yan Shigu's note: "識 is read the same as 幟."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 刷 (shuà), fanqie 式列.* Cui Yin, *Da zhi*: "transmitting in sequence the calendrical reckonings, the three dynasties' rise and fall; Datin is of old, and Hexu none can know (識)."
- *Rhymes as 爍 (shuò), fanqie 施灼.* Su Shi, *Poem for Magistrate Mao*: "wandering as an official, I meet this evil year; when the flying locusts came, half the sky went dark; I envy you, whose bounds' rice is like clouds — the locusts of themselves know (識) men, though men know nothing." *Ji Yun*: sometimes written 𧬣.
