*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.403 #32)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*: fanqie 眉殞. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 美隕 — sounding like 閔 (mǐn).
