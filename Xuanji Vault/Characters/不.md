## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集上〉【一字部】頁76第15    〔古文〕：𠀚、𠙐【韻會】【正韻】𡘋逋沒切，補入聲。不然也，不可也，未也。【禮·曾子問】葬引至于堩，日有食之，則有變乎，且不乎。　又【周禮·夏官】服不氏，掌養猛獸而敎擾之。【註】服不服之獸者。　又【廣韻】【韻會】𡘋分物切。與弗同。今吳音皆然。　又【韻會】俯九切，音缶。與可否之否通。【說文】鳥飛上翔，不下來也。从一，一猶天也。象形。　又【玉篇】甫負切【廣韻】甫救切，𡘋缶去聲。義同。又【廣韻】甫鳩切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】方鳩切，𡘋音浮。夫不，䳡也。亦作鳺鴀。【爾雅·釋鳥】䳡其鳺鴀。【郉疏】陸璣云：今小鳩也。一名䳕鳩，幽州人或謂鷱鴡，梁宋閒謂之隹，揚州人亦然。　又未定之辭也。【陶潛詩】未知從今去，當復如此不。　又姓。【晉書】汲郡人不準。◎按《正字通》云：不姓之不，轉注古音，音彪。　又【正韻】芳無切。與柎通。花萼跗也。【詩·小雅】鄂不韡韡。【鄭箋】承華者，鄂也。不當作柎。鄭樵曰：不象萼蔕形。與旉通。【陸璣詩疏】柎作跗。【束皙·補亡詩】白華絳趺。【唐詩】紅萼靑趺皆因之。　又華不注，山名，在濟南城東北。【左傳·成二年】晉卻克戰于鞌，齊師敗績。逐之，三周華不注。【伏琛齊記】引摯虞畿服經，不，與詩鄂不之不同。李白詩：兹山何峻秀，彩翠如芙蓉。蓋因華跗而比擬之。胡傳讀不如卜，非。又【古詩·日出東南隅行】使君謝羅敷，還可共載不。羅敷前致辭，使君亦何愚。使君自有婦，羅敷自有夫。〇按愚當讀若吾，疑模切，與敷不夫叶。敷不夫本同模韻，《正字通》不改音符，叶夫愚，非是。　又與丕同。【書·大誥】爾丕克遠省。馬融作不。【秦·詛楚文】不顯大神巫咸。【秦·和鐘銘】不顯皇祖。𠀤與詩周頌不顯不承同。不顯不承，猶書云丕顯丕承也。　又【韻補】叶補美切，音彼。【荀子·賦篇】𥳑然易知，而致有理者與。君子所敬，而小人所不者與。所不謂小人所鄙也。【正字通】不字在入聲者，方音各殊，或讀逋入聲，或讀杯入聲。司馬光切韻圖定爲逋骨切，今北方讀如幫鋪切，雖入聲轉平，其義則一也。    考證：〔【禮·曾子問】葬引至于堩，日日有食之，〕　謹照原文省下日字。〔【荀子·賦論篇】〕　謹照原書省論字。〔【註】謂小人所鄙也。〕　謹按原文無此註，改為所不謂小人所鄙也。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, upper; One radical 一; p.76 #15)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𠀚, 𠙐.

**Pronunciation.** *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 逋沒, the entering tone of 補. Senses: "not so (不然); not permissible (不可); not yet (未)." Liji, *Zengzi wen*: "the funeral cortège reaches the embankment and the sun is eclipsed — is there a change, or not (不)?"

**Senses and readings.**
- *服不氏 (the Tamer).* Zhouli, *Xiaguan*: "the 服不氏 manages the rearing of fierce beasts and trains them tame"; note: "to subdue beasts that will not submit (服不服之獸)."
- *= 弗 (not).* *Guangyun, Yun Hui*: fanqie 分物 — same as 弗; in the Wu pronunciation it is all so today.
- *= the 否 of 可否.* *Yun Hui*: fanqie 俯九, sounding like 缶 (fǒu). *Shuowen*: "a bird flying up and wheeling, not coming down. From 一, 一 being like Heaven; a pictograph."
- *Departing tone.* *Yupian*: fanqie 甫負; *Guangyun*: fanqie 甫救, the departing tone of 缶; same sense.
- *夫不, a bird (the 䳡).* *Guangyun*: fanqie 甫鳩; *Ji Yun*: fanqie 方鳩, sounding like 浮 (fú). Also written 鳺鴀. Erya, *Shi niao*: "the 䳡 is the 鳺鴀"; Xing subcommentary, citing Lu Ji: "today's small dove; one name 䳕鳩; in Youzhou some call it 鷱鴡; between Liang and Song it is called 隹; in Yangzhou too."
- *A word of indeterminacy.* Tao Qian's poem: "I know not, going on from now, whether it will be thus again, or not (不)."
- *Surname.* Jinshu: Bu Zhun of Jijun. — Zhengzitong: "the 不 of the surname, by phonetic transfer, reads 彪 (biāo)."
- *= 柎 (the calyx-base).* *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 芳無. Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the calyces gleaming (鄂不韡韡)"; Zheng *Jian*: "what bears the flower is the calyx; 不 should be 柎." Zheng Qiao: "不 images the calyx-and-stem; interchangeable with 旉." Various poets follow this.
- *華不注 (Huafuzhu), a mountain* northeast of Jinan. Zuozhuan, Cheng yr 2: "Xi Ke of Jin fought at An; the Qi army was routed; pursuing it, they thrice circled Huafuzhu." Fu Chen, *Qi ji*, citing Zhi Yu: "this 不 is the same as the Shijing's '鄂不.'" Li Bai's poem: "how steep and fair this mountain, its kingfisher hues like lotus" — taking the flower-base (華跗) for the simile. Reading 不 as 卜 is wrong.
- *In the ancient ballad "The Sun Rises in the Southeast":* "the lord thanks Luofu — 'can you ride along with me, or not (不)?' Luofu came forward and said, 'how foolish (愚) the lord is! the lord has his own wife, Luofu her own husband.'" — A note: 愚 should be read like 吾 (fanqie 疑模) to rhyme with 敷, 不, 夫, all of the 模 rhyme.
- *= 丕 (great).* Shu, *Da gao*: "you greatly (丕) can examine afar" — which Ma Rong wrote 不. The Qin "Curse on Chu": "不顯大神巫咸"; the Qin He-bell inscription: "不顯皇祖" — both like the Shijing, *Zhou song*, "不顯不承," which is the Shu's "丕顯丕承."
- *Rhymes as 彼 (bǐ), fanqie 補美.* Xunzi, *Fu pian*: "…and what the small man scorns (不)"; 所不 means what the small man despises. — Zhengzitong: "where 不 stands in the entering tone, dialects differ — some read 逋 in entering tone, some 杯; Sima Guang's rhyme-chart fixed fanqie 逋骨; today the north reads it like fanqie 幫鋪 — though the entering tone shifts to level, the sense is one."

**Textual notes.** A doubled 日 in the Liji citation is dropped; 論 is dropped from the Xunzi title; the note is corrected to read 所不謂小人所鄙也.
