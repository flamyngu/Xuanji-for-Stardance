**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 戌, middle; Short-tailed-bird radical 隹; p.1370 #05)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𩁢, 𩁬, 𩀙, 𩁣, 𩁲, 𩁩, 𩀏, 𩁤.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 那干. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 那肝 — sounding like 䕿 (nán).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a bird; originally written 𪄿."
- *A term for what is not easy.* *Yupian.* Shu, *Gaoyao mo*: "even the Emperor found it hard (難)." *Xian you yi de*: "let it be hard, let it be heedful (其難其愼)."
- *A pearl-name.* Cao Zhi, *Beauty ballad*: "coral set among *munan* (木難)"; note: the Nanyue zhi says, "*munan* is a green pearl formed of the foam of the golden-winged bird."
- *A surname.* Zhengzitong: "in the Northern and Southern dynasties, Nan Congdang"; see the Xingyuan.
- *Same as 儺 (the exorcism).* *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 囊何; *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 奴何. *Ji Yun*: "難 is to drive off the malign and evil." Zhouli, *Chunguan, Zhanmeng*: "thereupon he orders the first exorcism (難) to drive out pestilence." *Xiaguan, Fangxiang shi*: "he leads the hundred bondsmen in the seasonal exorcism (難), searching the houses to drive out plague." Liji, *Yueling*: "in late spring, he orders the state exorcism (國難)"; and: "in late winter he orders the officers [to hold] the Great Exorcism (大難)."
- *Luxuriant.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "its leaves are luxuriant (有難)"; tradition: "難然 is the look of abundance"; *Shiwen*: fanqie 乃多.
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 乃可, sounding like 娜 (nuǒ) — same as 橠; see the note to 橠 in the Wood radical.

**Departing tone (nàn).** *Guangyun*: fanqie 奴案. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 乃旦 — sounding like 㬮 (nàn).
- *A calamity, trouble (患).* *Guangyun.* Yijing, *Pi*: "the gentleman by frugal virtue avoids calamity (難), and may not be glorified with emolument." Liji, *Quli*: "facing peril (難), do not seek escape unworthily." Zhouli, *Diguan, Tiaoren*: "he is in charge of the feuds (難) of the people and of reconciling them"; note: "難 means to be enemies with one another."
- *To challenge, dispute (詰辨).* Tangshi, *Biography of Wei Chuhou*: "when Zhang Pingshu proposed selling [state] salt, Wei Chuhou raised ten objections (十難) to refute him."
- *To bar, repel (拒).* Shu, *Shundian*: "be earnest in virtue, sincere to the good, and bar (難) the fawning man"; tradition: "難 means to repel"; *Shiwen*: fanqie 乃旦.
- *A turn of phrase (辭).* Gongyang, Yin yr 8, the "we entered Bing" passage: "why does it say 'entered'? — it is a [diplomatic] difficulty (難)"; note: "難 is a turn of phrase"; *Shiwen*: fanqie 乃旦, one reading as written.
- *To reproach, blame (責).* Mengzi: "with the birds and beasts, what is there to reproach (難)?"; note: "難 means to blame."
- *A personal name.* Zuozhuan, Wen yr 1: "Nan shall gather you up"; *Shiwen*: fanqie 乃多, one reading as written.

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, sounding like 臡 (ní).* The Jizhong Zhoushu: "...the old and weak left alone, and their counsel then is hard (難)."

**Textual note.** The Zuozhuan Wen-1 *Shiwen* reading 乃旦反 should be 乃多反.
