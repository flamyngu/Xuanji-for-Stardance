**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls жҲҢ, middle; Short-tailed-bird radical йҡ№; p.1370 #05)*

**Ancient graphs.** р©Ғў, р©Ғ¬, р©Җҷ, р©ҒЈ, р©ҒІ, р©Ғ©, р©ҖҸ, р©ҒӨ.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie йӮЈе№І. *Ji Yun*: fanqie йӮЈиӮқ вҖ” sounding like д•ҝ (nГЎn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a bird; originally written рӘ„ҝ."
- *A term for what is not easy.* *Yupian.* Shu, *Gaoyao mo*: "even the Emperor found it hard (йӣЈ)." *Xian you yi de*: "let it be hard, let it be heedful (е…¶йӣЈе…¶ж„ј)."
- *A pearl-name.* Cao Zhi, *Beauty ballad*: "coral set among *munan* (жңЁйӣЈ)"; note: the Nanyue zhi says, "*munan* is a green pearl formed of the foam of the golden-winged bird."
- *A surname.* Zhengzitong: "in the Northern and Southern dynasties, Nan Congdang"; see the Xingyuan.
- *Same as е„ә (the exorcism).* *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie еӣҠдҪ•; *Zheng Yun*: fanqie еҘҙдҪ•. *Ji Yun*: "йӣЈ is to drive off the malign and evil." Zhouli, *Chunguan, Zhanmeng*: "thereupon he orders the first exorcism (йӣЈ) to drive out pestilence." *Xiaguan, Fangxiang shi*: "he leads the hundred bondsmen in the seasonal exorcism (йӣЈ), searching the houses to drive out plague." Liji, *Yueling*: "in late spring, he orders the state exorcism (еңӢйӣЈ)"; and: "in late winter he orders the officers [to hold] the Great Exorcism (еӨ§йӣЈ)."
- *Luxuriant.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "its leaves are luxuriant (жңүйӣЈ)"; tradition: "йӣЈз„¶ is the look of abundance"; *Shiwen*: fanqie д№ғеӨҡ.
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie д№ғеҸҜ, sounding like еЁң (nuЗ’) вҖ” same as ж© ; see the note to ж©  in the Wood radical.

**Departing tone (nГ n).** *Guangyun*: fanqie еҘҙжЎҲ. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie д№ғж—Ұ вҖ” sounding like г¬® (nГ n).
- *A calamity, trouble (жӮЈ).* *Guangyun.* Yijing, *Pi*: "the gentleman by frugal virtue avoids calamity (йӣЈ), and may not be glorified with emolument." Liji, *Quli*: "facing peril (йӣЈ), do not seek escape unworthily." Zhouli, *Diguan, Tiaoren*: "he is in charge of the feuds (йӣЈ) of the people and of reconciling them"; note: "йӣЈ means to be enemies with one another."
- *To challenge, dispute (и©°иҫЁ).* Tangshi, *Biography of Wei Chuhou*: "when Zhang Pingshu proposed selling [state] salt, Wei Chuhou raised ten objections (еҚҒйӣЈ) to refute him."
- *To bar, repel (жӢ’).* Shu, *Shundian*: "be earnest in virtue, sincere to the good, and bar (йӣЈ) the fawning man"; tradition: "йӣЈ means to repel"; *Shiwen*: fanqie д№ғж—Ұ.
- *A turn of phrase (иҫӯ).* Gongyang, Yin yr 8, the "we entered Bing" passage: "why does it say 'entered'? вҖ” it is a [diplomatic] difficulty (йӣЈ)"; note: "йӣЈ is a turn of phrase"; *Shiwen*: fanqie д№ғж—Ұ, one reading as written.
- *To reproach, blame (иІ¬).* Mengzi: "with the birds and beasts, what is there to reproach (йӣЈ)?"; note: "йӣЈ means to blame."
- *A personal name.* Zuozhuan, Wen yr 1: "Nan shall gather you up"; *Shiwen*: fanqie д№ғеӨҡ, one reading as written.

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, sounding like иҮЎ (nГӯ).* The Jizhong Zhoushu: "...the old and weak left alone, and their counsel then is hard (йӣЈ)."

**Textual note.** The Zuozhuan Wen-1 *Shiwen* reading д№ғж—ҰеҸҚ should be д№ғеӨҡеҸҚ.
