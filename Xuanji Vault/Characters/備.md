## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集中〉【人字部】頁113第21    〔古文〕：𠈍【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】𠀤平祕切，音避。成也。【周禮·春官·樂師】凡樂成則告備。　又咸也，副也。【書·周官】官不必備惟其人。　又先具以待用也。【書·說命】惟事事，乃其有備，有備無患。【左傳·僖五年】凡分至啓閉，必書雲物，爲備故也。　又足也。【易·繫辭】易之爲書也，廣大悉備。　又【禮·祭統】福者，備也。備者，百順之名也。無所不順者之謂備。　又盡也。【禮·月令】季秋之月，乃命冢宰，農事備收。　又長兵曰備。【左傳·昭二十一年】用少莫如齊致死，齊致死莫如去備。　又搔也。【周禮·秋官】冥氏若得其獸，則獻其皮革齒須備。【註】須直謂頤下須，備謂搔也。搔音爪。　又姓。　又叶蒲必切，音弼。【詩·小雅】禮儀旣備，鐘鼓旣戒。戒音吉。    考證：〔【周禮·春官·大司樂】凡樂則告備。〕　謹照原書大司樂改樂師。凡樂下增成字。〔【禮·月令】季秋之月，命冢宰，農事備收。〕　謹照原文命字上增乃字。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, middle; Person radical 人; p.113 #21)*

**Ancient graph.** 𠈍.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui* all: fanqie 平祕, sounding like 避 (bì). "To complete (成)." Zhouli, *Chun guan, Yue shi*: "whenever the music is finished, it is announced as complete (備)."

**Senses.**
- *All, entire; an aide (咸, 副).* Shu, *Zhou guan*: "the offices need not be fully staffed (備), only the right men."
- *To make ready beforehand for use.* Shu, *Yue ming*: "in all your affairs, have your preparation; with preparation (備) there is no calamity." Zuozhuan, Xi yr 5: "at the equinoxes, solstices, and the openings and closings, one must record the cloud-signs, for the sake of preparation (備)."
- *Sufficient, complete (足).* Yijing, *Xi ci*: "as a book, the Yijing is broad and great and wholly complete (悉備)."
- *Also* Liji, *Ji tong*: "blessing (福) means completeness (備); completeness is the name for the hundred compliances; what is in nothing not compliant is called complete (備)."
- *Wholly, fully (盡).* Liji, *Yue ling*: "in the last month of autumn, the Prime Minister is charged that the farm-work be fully gathered in (備收)."
- *A long weapon is called 備.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 21: "for using the few, nothing is like committing all to death; for committing all to death, nothing is like casting off the long arms (備)."
- *To scratch (搔).* Zhouli, *Qiu guan*: "if the Mingshi catches the beast, he presents its hide, teeth, whiskers (須), and bei (備)"; note: "須 means the whiskers below the chin; 備 means the claws (搔). 搔 is sounded 爪 (zhǎo)."
- *Surname.*

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 弼 (bì), fanqie 蒲必.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the rites and ceremonies being now complete (備), the bells and drums being now in readiness (戒)." (戒 is sounded 吉, jí.)

**Textual notes.** "Zhouli, *Chun guan, Da sile*: whenever the music, it is announced complete" — checked against the original, "*Da sile*" should read "*Yue shi*," and the word 成 is added after 凡樂. · Liji, *Yue ling*, "in the last month of autumn, the Prime Minister is charged that the farm-work be fully gathered" — checked against the original, the word 乃 is added before 命.
