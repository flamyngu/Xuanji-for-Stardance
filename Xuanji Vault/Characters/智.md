## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集上〉【日字部】頁497第15    〔古文〕：𥏼【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤知義切，音置。同𣉻。或作智。【說文】識詞也。从白从亏从知。○按經典相承作智。【釋名】智，知也。無所不知也。【孟子】是非之心，智之端也。【荀子·正名篇】知而有所合謂之智。○按經典或通用知。　又姓。【廣韻】晉有智伯。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, upper; Sun radical 日; p.497 #15)*

**Ancient graph.** 𥏼.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 知義 — sounding like 置 (zhì). The same as 𣉻; also written 智.

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a word of knowing." It follows 白, 亏, and 知. A note: in the classics it is by tradition written 智. Shiming: "智 is 知 (to know): knowing all things." Mengzi: "the heart of right and wrong is the sprout of wisdom (智)." Xunzi, *Zhengming*: "knowledge that meets its object aright is called wisdom (智)." A note: in the classics it is sometimes used interchangeably with 知.
- *A surname.* *Guangyun*: "Jin had Zhi Bo (智伯)."
