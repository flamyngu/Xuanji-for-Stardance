> 《康熙字典》〈寅集下〉【彳字部】頁371第08【唐韻】【正韻】多則切【集韻】【韻會】的則切，𠀤登入聲。【廣韻】德行也。【集韻】德行之得也。【正韻】凡言德者，善美，正大，光明，純懿之稱也。【易·乾卦】君子進德修業。【詩·大雅】民之秉彝，好是懿德。【書·臯陶謨】九德，寬而栗，柔而立，愿而恭，亂而敬，擾而毅，直而溫，𥳑而廉，剛而塞，彊而義。又【洪範】三德，一曰正直，二曰剛克，三曰柔克。【周禮·地官】六德：知、仁、聖、義、中、和。　又【玉篇】德，惠也。【書·盤庚】施實德于民。【詩·小雅】旣飽以德。　又善敎也。【禮·月令】孟春之月，命相布德，和令，行慶，施惠。【註】德謂善敎。　又感恩曰德。【左傳·成三年】王曰：然則德我乎。【疏】德加於彼，彼荷其恩，故謂荷恩爲德。【後漢·樊曄傳】光武微時，曄餽餌一笥，帝德之不忘。　又【韻會】四時旺氣也。【禮·月令】某日立春，盛德在木。　又【諡法】綏柔士民，諫爭不威，執義揚善，曰德。　又【說文】升也。　又【玉篇】福也。　又星名。【前漢·郊祀志】望氣王朔言，後獨見塡星出如瓜。有司皆曰：陛下建漢家封禪，天其報德星云。【註】德星，旣填星也。　又【韻會】亦作悳。【前漢·賈誼傳】悳至渥也。　又州名。【廣韻】秦爲齊郡地，漢爲平原郡。武德初爲德州，因德安縣以名之。　又叶都木切，音篤。【謝惠連·雪賦】曹風以麻衣比色，楚謠以幽蘭儷曲。盈尺則呈瑞於豐年，袤丈則表沴於隂德。　又叶得各切，當入聲。【易林】酒爲歡伯，除憂來樂，福善入門，與君相索，使我有德。    考證：〔【書·大禹謨】九德，寬而栗。〕　謹照原書改臯陶謨。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 寅, lower; Step radical 彳; p.371 #08)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 多則. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 的則 — the entering tone of 登 (dēng), i.e. **dé**.

**Etymology and senses.** *Guangyun*: "virtuous conduct (德行)." *Ji Yun*: "the 'getting' (得) of virtuous conduct." *Zheng Yun*: "德, as a word, names the good and fair, the upright and great, the bright, the pure and admirable." Yijing, *Qian*: "the gentleman advances in virtue (德) and cultivates his work." Shijing, *Daya*: "the people hold to the norm, and love this fair virtue (懿德)." Shu, *Gaoyao mo*: "the nine virtues (九德): broad yet firm, mild yet standing, blunt yet respectful, capable yet reverent, docile yet bold, straight yet gentle, easy yet scrupulous, hard yet substantial, strong yet right." Also *Hongfan*: "the three virtues (三德): uprightness, mastery by firmness, mastery by softness." Zhouli, *Diguan*: "the six virtues (六德): wisdom, benevolence, sageliness, righteousness, balance, harmony."
- *Kindness, bounty (惠).* *Yupian.* Shu, *Pangeng*: "to bestow real bounty (德) on the people." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "already sated with [your] bounty (德)."
- *Good teaching.* Liji, *Yueling*: "in the first month of spring, the minister is charged to spread virtue (德), harmonize the ordinances, dispense rewards, and grant favours"; note: "德 means good teaching."
- *To feel gratitude.* Zuozhuan, Cheng yr 3: "the king said, 'then will you be grateful (德) to me?'"; subcommentary: "virtue is conferred on another, and he bears the kindness — hence bearing kindness is called 德." Later Han, *Biography of Fan Ye*: "when Guangwu was still obscure, Ye gave him a hamper of cakes; the Emperor was grateful (德) for it and never forgot."
- *The ascendant qi of a season.* *Yun Hui.* Liji, *Yueling*: "on such a day spring begins; the flourishing virtue (盛德) lies in Wood."
- *A posthumous title.* In title-law: "one who soothes the people, who in remonstrance is not overbearing, who holds to right and exalts the good" is titled 德 (Virtuous).
- *To ascend (升).* *Shuowen.*
- *Blessing (福).* *Yupian.*
- *A star-name.* Former Han, *Treatise on the Feng and Shan*: "the cloud-diviner Wang Shuo said that afterward he alone saw Saturn (塡星) come forth like a gourd; the officials all said, 'Your Majesty has established the Han *feng* and *shan* rites; Heaven repays with the Virtue-star (德星)'"; note: "the Virtue-star is Saturn."
- *Also written 悳.* *Yun Hui.* Former Han, *Biography of Jia Yi*: "the bounty (悳) was most rich."
- *A prefecture-name.* *Guangyun*: "under Qin it was part of Qi commandery, under Han the Pingyuan commandery; in the early Wude reign it became Dezhou (德州), named after De'an county."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 都木, sounding like 篤 (dǔ).* Xie Huilian, *Snow fu*: "...a foot deep, it shows an omen of plenteous years; a fathom deep, it marks the baleful breath of hidden virtue (隂德)."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 得各, the entering tone of 當.* Yilin: "...blessing and good come in at the gate and seek you out, making me to have virtue (德)."

**Textual note.** The "nine virtues" passage is from *Gaoyao mo*, not *Da Yu mo*.
