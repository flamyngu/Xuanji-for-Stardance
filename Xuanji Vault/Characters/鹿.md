## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈亥集下〉【鹿字部】頁1508第14【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】盧谷切，音祿。【說文】獸也。【玉篇】麞屬。【爾雅·釋獸】鹿牡麚牧麀，其子麛，其跡速，絕有力𪊑。【埤雅】仙獸也。牡者有角。【字統】鹿性驚防，羣居分背而食，環角向外以備人物之害。【易·屯卦】卽鹿無虞，惟入于林中。【疏】卽鹿若無虞官，虛入林木中，必不得鹿。【詩·小雅】呦呦鹿鳴。　又鹿蜀，獸名。見【山海經】。　又鉅鹿，郡名。【前漢·地理志】鉅鹿，卽禹貢大陸。○按今屬順德府。　又獲鹿、束鹿，𠀤縣名。【唐書·地理志】隋鹿泉，唐攺獲鹿。隋鹿城，唐攺束鹿。　又地名。【左傳·僖二十四年】出于五鹿。【註】在今衞縣西北。　又山名。涿鹿在上谷。白鹿在荆州。鹿門舊名蘇嶺山，在襄陽。沙鹿在晉平陽元城縣東。　又鹿門，關名。【左傳·襄二十四年】臧紇斬鹿門之關以出。【註】魯南城東門。　又臺名。【書·武成】散鹿臺之財。【薛瓚曰】在朝歌城。　又衡鹿，官名。【左傳·昭二十年】山林之木，衡鹿守之。　又囷鹿。【吳語】大荒薦饑，市無赤米，而囷鹿空虛。【註】先儒以圓曰囷，方曰鹿。囷，聚也，亦散也。鹿善聚善散。故囷謂鹿。俗作簏。　又鹿車小車也。【風俗通】鹿車小，裁容一鹿。　又酒器。【魯相韓敕修孔廟禮器𥓓】有雷洗觴觚，爵鹿柤梪。　又縷鹿，婦人冠名。見【蔡邕·獨斷】。　又鹿豆。【爾雅·釋草】蔨，鹿𧆑，其實莥。【註】今鹿豆也。　又活鹿，鹿蹄，鹿腸，鹿首，鹿麻，𠀤草名。見【本草綱目】。　又與麓通。【詩·大雅】瞻彼旱麓，棒楛濟濟。【周語】作旱鹿。　又姓。【正字通】漢鹿旗，明鹿麟。又五鹿，複姓。漢有五鹿充宗。　又鹿鹿，與錄錄通。【前漢·蕭曹傳贊】錄錄未有奇節。【註】錄錄，猶鹿鹿也。亦作碌碌。又作陸陸。　又【荀子·成相篇】剄以獨鹿，棄之江。【註】獨鹿，與屬鏤同。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 亥, lower; Deer radical 鹿; p.1508 #14)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 盧谷, sounding like 祿 (lù).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "A beast (獸)." *Yupian*: "of the roe-deer kind (麞屬)." Erya, *Shi shou*: "the deer — the male is 麚, the herd 麀, its young 麛, its tracks 速, and one of surpassing strength is 𪊑." *Piya*: "a transcendent beast; the male has antlers." *Zitong*: "the deer's nature is wary and guarded; grazing in herds, they feed back-to-back, ringing their antlers outward to ward off harm from men and creatures." Yijing, *Zhun* hexagram: "chasing the deer (鹿) without a forester, one only goes into the forest"; subcommentary: "to chase the deer with no forester-officer, going emptily into the woods, one surely will not get the deer." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "you-you cry the deer (鹿)."

**Senses.**
- *鹿蜀 (lushu), a beast-name.* Seen in the Shanhai jing.
- *Julu (鉅鹿), a commandery-name.* Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "Julu is the Great Land (大陸) of the *Yu gong*." A note: it now belongs to Shunde prefecture.
- *Huolu (獲鹿) and Shulu (束鹿), both county-names.* Tang shu, *Treatise on Geography*: "the Sui Luquan, the Tang renamed Huolu; the Sui Lucheng, the Tang renamed Shulu."
- *A place-name.* Zuozhuan, Xi yr 24: "they came out at Wulu (五鹿)"; note: "it is now northwest of Wei county."
- *Mountain-names.* Zhuolu (涿鹿) is in Shanggu; Bailu (白鹿) in Jingzhou; Lumen (鹿門), anciently named Suling Mountain, is at Xiangyang; Shalu (沙鹿) is east of Yuancheng county in Jin Pingyang.
- *Lumen (鹿門), a barrier-name.* Zuozhuan, Xiang yr 24: "Zang He hacked through the Lumen barrier (鹿門之關) to get out"; note: "the east gate of Lu's south wall."
- *A terrace-name.* Shu, *Wu cheng*: "he scattered the wealth of the Deer Terrace (鹿臺)"; Xue Zan: "it was in the city of Zhaoge."
- *衡鹿 (henglu), an official title.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 20: "the timber of the mountains and forests, the Henglu guarded it."
- *囷鹿 (qunlu), a granary.* *Discourses of Wu*: "great waste and recurrent famine, no red rice in the markets, and the granaries (囷鹿) stood empty"; note: "earlier scholars held the round to be 囷, the square 鹿. 囷 means to gather, and also to scatter; the 鹿 is good at gathering and scattering, hence 囷 is called 鹿." Commonly written 簏.
- *鹿車 (luche), a small cart.* *Fengsu tong*: "the deer-cart is small, just holding one deer (鹿)."
- *A wine-vessel.* The *Stele on the Ritual Vessels Repaired for Confucius's Temple by Han Chi, Chancellor of Lu*: "there were thunder-basins, goblets and cups, beakers, lu-vessels (鹿), trays and stands."
- *縷鹿 (lülu), the name of a woman's headdress.* Seen in Cai Yong, *Du duan*.
- *鹿豆 (ludou).* Erya, *Shi cao*: "蔨 is the lu-niao (鹿𧆑), its fruit the niu (莥)"; note: "today's deer-bean (鹿豆)."
- *活鹿, 鹿蹄, 鹿腸, 鹿首, 鹿麻 — all plant-names.* Seen in the *Bencao gangmu*.
- *Interchangeable with 麓 (a foothill).* Shijing, *Daya*: "look at that foothill of Han (旱麓), the hazels and arrow-thorns lush"; the *Discourses of Zhou* writes 旱鹿.
- *Surname.* *Zhengzitong*: "Lu Qi of the Han, Lu Lin of the Ming." Also Wulu (五鹿), a compound surname: the Han had Wulu Chongzong.
- *鹿鹿, interchangeable with 錄錄.* Former Han, *Eulogy on Xiao and Cao*: "common and undistinguished (錄錄), with no rare integrity"; note: "錄錄 is like 鹿鹿." Also written 碌碌, and 陸陸.
- *Also* Xunzi, *Cheng xiang*: "his throat cut with the dulu (獨鹿), he was cast into the river"; note: "獨鹿 is the same as 屬鏤 (a sword-name)."
