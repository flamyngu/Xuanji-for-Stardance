## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁391第17    〔古文〕：𢛧【唐韻】以追切【集韻】【韻會】夷隹切，𠀤音維。【說文】凡思也。从心隹聲。【玉篇】有也，爲也，謀也，伊也。　又語辭也。【毛晃曰】有是惟之惟，書濟河惟兗州之類。有思惟之惟，書視遠惟明，詩載謀載惟之類。有惟獨之惟。書惟王不邇聲色之類。　又姓。　又【正韻】無非切，音微。義同。◎按《說文》从心隹聲，則梁韻弋隹切，唐韻以追切，宋韻夷隹切，𠀤切喩母也。獨正韻竟作無非切，則切微母矣。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.391 #17)*

**Ancient graph.** 𢛧.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 以追. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 夷隹 — sounding like 維 (wéi).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "thinking in general (凡思)." It follows 心 with 隹 as phonetic. *Yupian*: "to have (有); to do/be (爲); to plan (謀); [the particle] 伊."
- *A particle.* **Mao Huang**: "There is the 惟 of '有是 (it is so),' as in Shu, '濟河惟兗州' and the like. There is the 惟 of '思惟 (to ponder),' as in Shu, '視遠惟明' and Shijing, '載謀載惟.' There is the 惟 of '惟獨 (only),' as in Shu, '惟王不邇聲色.'"
- *Surname.*
- *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 無非, sounding like 微 (wēi); same sense.

**Editorial note.** Per *Shuowen* ("from 心, 隹 phonetic"), the Liang-rhyme fanqie 弋隹, the Tang-rhyme 以追, and the Song-rhyme 夷隹 all spell the 喻 (*yu*) initial; only the *Zheng Yun* makes it 無非, which spells the 微 (*wei*) initial.
