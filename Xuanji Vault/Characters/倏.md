## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集中〉【人字部】頁107第22【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤式竹切，音叔。倏忽。又犬走疾也。下从犬。俗作倐，从火，非。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, middle; Person radical 人; p.107 #22)*

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 式竹 — sounding like 叔 (shū).

**Etymology and senses.** 倏忽, in a flash, suddenly.
- *also.* A dog running swiftly; below, it follows 犬 (dog).

**On the graph.** The vulgar form 倐, following 火 (fire), is wrong.
