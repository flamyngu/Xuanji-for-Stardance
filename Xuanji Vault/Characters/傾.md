## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)

> 《康熙字典》〈子集中〉【人字部】頁115第10【唐韻】去營切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】窺營切，𠀤音卿。【說文】側也。又伏也，敧也。【禮·曲禮】傾則姦。【註】視流則容側，必有不正之心存乎胷中，此君子所以愼也。　又圯也，空也。【淮南子·天文訓】天傾西北。　又西傾，山名。【書·禹貢】西傾因桓是來。　又通作頃。【詩·周南】不盈頃筐。　又【韻補】叶曲陽切，音匡。【黃庭經】羅列六腑生三光，心精意專內不傾，上合三焦下玉漿。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, middle; Person radical 人; p.115 #10)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 去營. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 窺營 — sounding like 卿 (qīng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to lean, tilt (側)." Also "to bow down; to slant." Liji, *Quli*: "to tilt [the gaze] is depravity (傾)"; note: "if the eyes wander, the bearing leans aslant — there must be a crooked heart within the breast; this is what the gentleman guards against."
- *To topple; to empty out.* Huainanzi, *Tianwen xun*: "Heaven tilts (傾) to the northwest."
- *Xiqing (西傾), a mountain-name.* Shu, *Yugong*: "from Xiqing, by way of the Huan [river], they come."
- *Interchangeable with 頃.* Shijing, *Zhounan*: "it does not fill the slanting basket (頃筐)."

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 曲陽, sounding like 匡 (kuāng).* The Huangting jing: "...the heart's essence and the will made one, within not swaying (傾); above it joins the Triple Burner, below the jade fluid.
