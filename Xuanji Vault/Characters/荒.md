**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1031 #08)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𠃤, 𠯚.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 呼光 — sounding like 肓 (huāng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "overgrown, waste (蕪); one says, grass covering the ground." Zhou yu: "the fields lay waste and overgrown (荒蕪)." Han shi waizhuan: "when the four grains do not ripen, it is called 荒 (famine)." Erya, *Shitian*: "when the fruits do not ripen it is 荒 (dearth)."
- *Also, to neglect, let lapse (廢).* Shu, *Cai Zhong zhi ming*: "do not neglect (荒) and cast off my charge"; tradition: "do not abandon my command."
- *Also, great (大).* Shijing, *Zhousong*: "Heaven made the high mountain, and King Tai greatly ordered (荒) it." Shu, *Yi Ji*: "he greatly (荒) measured the works of the land"; tradition: "he greatly governed and measured the works of land and water."
- *Also* Shu, *Yu gong*: "the five hundred li of the Wild Domain (荒服)." Erya, *Shidi*: "Guzhu, Beihu, Xiwangmu, and Rixia are called the Four Wilds (四荒)."
- *Also, to cover over (掩).* Shijing, *Zhounan*: "in the south is the drooping tree, the kudzu vines cover (荒) it."
- *Also, empty, void (空).* Wu yu: "an empty (荒) settlement, with no covenant."
- *Also, a covering (蒙).* Note to Liji, *Sang da ji*: "at the sides it is called the curtain (帷); over the top, the pall (荒)."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: the same as 慌. Chuci, *Ai Ying*: "vague and dim (荒忽), where is its limit?"
- *Also interchangeable with 肓.* Shiji, *Biography of Bian Que*: "he kneaded the marrow and brain and handled the diaphragm (荒)"; note: "荒 is the 膏肓 (the vitals)."
- *Also a place-name.* Shuijing zhu: "on the east bank of Huanggu (荒谷) stands Yefu city." Emperor Yuan of Liang, *Xuanlan fu*: "at evening I gaze on the temple of Huanggu (荒谷)."
- *Also a year-name.* Erya, *Shitian*: "when the Grand Year stands in 巳 it is called Dahuangluo (大荒落)."
- *Also a surname*; see Tongzhi, *Shizu lüe*.

**Textual notes.** In the Wu yu citation, 荒城 should read 荒成 (per the original).
