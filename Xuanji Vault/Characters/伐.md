> 《康熙字典》〈子集中〉【人字部】頁96第01【唐韻】【集韻】【類篇】【韻會】𠀤房越切，音罰。征伐。【詩·小雅】薄伐玁狁。【周禮·夏官】大司馬以九伐之灋正邦國。【左傳·莊二十九年】凡師有鐘鼓曰伐，無曰侵。　又【小爾雅】伐，美也。　又伐閱，與閥閱同。【史記·功臣侯表】古者人臣功有五等，明其功曰伐，積日曰閱。【左傳·莊二十八年】且旌君伐。【註】伐，功也。　又自稱其功曰伐。【老子·道德經】不自伐，故有功。　又斫木也。【詩·周南】伐其條枚。　又考擊鐘鼓也。【禮·郊特牲】孔子曰：二日伐鼓何居。　又攻殺擊刺也。【書·牧誓】不愆于四伐五伐六伐七伐，乃止齊焉。【註】少不下四五，多不過六七而齊，所以戒其貪殺也。　又兵器。【詩·秦風】蒙伐有苑。【註】蒙，雜文。伐，干也。亦作瞂。　又星名。【周禮·冬官考工記】熊旗六斿。以象伐也。【註】熊虎爲旗，師都之所建，伐屬白虎宿，與參連體而六星。　又與垡同。【周禮·冬官考工記】一耦之伐。【疏】畎上高土謂之伐。　又叶許竭切，音歇。【詩·商頌】韋顧旣伐。叶上截下桀。　又叶扶廢切，音吠。【徐幹·西征賦】奉明辟之渥德，與游軫而西伐。過京邑以釋駕，觀帝居之舊制。伐字从人从戈，戍字亦从人从戈。【留靑日札】人坐臥則爲戍守，人立行則爲征伐。    考證：〔【周禮·冬官考工記】一耦之伐。【疏】畝上高土謂之伐。〕　謹照原文畝上改畎上。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, middle; Person radical 人; p.96 #01)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Leipian*, *Yun Hui* all: fanqie 房越, sounding like 罰 (fá). To campaign against, to chastise (征伐).

**Etymology and senses.** Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "briskly we smite (伐) the Xianyun." Zhouli, *Xia guan*: "the Grand Marshal by the law of the nine chastisements (九伐) sets the states right." Zuozhuan, Zhuang yr 29: "in all, a campaign with bells and drums is called 伐 (an open war); without, it is 侵 (a raid)."

**Senses.**
- *Also* *Xiao Erya*: "伐 means fine, admirable (美)."
- *伐閱, the same as 閥閱 (merit and service record).* Shiji, *Table of Meritorious Marquises*: "anciently a subject's merit had five grades; to make plain one's achievement is 伐, the accumulation of days is 閱." Zuozhuan, Zhuang yr 28: "and to display the lord's merit (伐)"; note: "伐 means achievement."
- *To boast of one's own merit is 伐.* Laozi, *Dao de jing*: "he does not boast (伐) of himself, hence he has merit."
- *To fell trees (斫木).* Shijing, *Zhou nan*: "they fell (伐) its branches and trunks."
- *To strike bell and drum (考擊鐘鼓).* Liji, *Jiao te sheng*: "Confucius said: 'striking the drum (伐鼓) on the second day — what is the meaning of that?'"
- *To assault, slay, strike, stab (攻殺擊刺).* Shu, *Mu shi*: "do not exceed four strokes, five strokes, six strokes, seven strokes (四伐五伐六伐七伐), then halt and dress ranks"; note: "fewest not below four or five, most not above six or seven, then dress ranks — to warn against greed for killing."
- *A weapon.* Shijing, *Qin feng*: "the patterned shield (蒙伐), how fine"; note: "蒙 means mottled pattern; 伐 is a shield (干); also written 瞂."
- *A star-name.* Zhouli, *Kao gong ji*: "the bear-banner with six streamers, imaging Fa (伐)"; note: "bear and tiger make the banner, raised by the army's commander; 伐 belongs to the White Tiger lodge, joined in body with Shen, six stars in all."
- *Same as 垡 (a clod, furrow-ridge).* Zhouli, *Kao gong ji*: "the 伐 of a single pair (of ploughmen)"; subcommentary: "the high soil above the furrow is called 伐."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 歇 (hē), fanqie 許竭.* Shijing, *Shang song*: "Wei and Gu being smitten (伐)" — rhyming with 截 above and 桀 below.
- *Rhymes as 吠 (fèi), fanqie 扶廢.* Xu Gan, *Xizheng fu*: "bearing the rich virtue of the bright sovereign, with the touring carriages I campaign westward (西伐); passing the capital I unhitch the team, and view the old order of the imperial seat." The graph 伐 follows 人 (man) and 戈 (dagger-axe); the graph 戍 (to guard) likewise follows 人 and 戈. *Liuqing rizha*: "a man sitting or lying is a guard (戍守); a man standing and marching is a campaign (征伐)."

**Textual note.** Zhouli, *Kao gong ji*, "the 伐 of a single pair," subcommentary "the high soil above the field (畝上) is called 伐" — checked against the original, "畝上" should read "畎上" (above the furrow).
