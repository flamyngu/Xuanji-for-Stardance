**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls й…ү, middle; Shell radical иІқ; p.1206 #15)*

**Ancient graphs.** рЎӯҷ, р§ёӢ.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie еұ…иғғ. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie жӯёи¬Ӯ вҖ” sounding like йӨҪ (guГ¬).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Written дқҝ; a thing not cheap (зү©дёҚиіӨ)." Yijing, *Xi ci*: "the low and the high being set out, the noble and base (иІҙиіӨ) have their places." Shu, *LГј ao*: "do not prize (иІҙ) outlandish things, nor make light of useful things."

**Senses.**
- *High; honoured (й«ҳ, е°Ҡ).* *Yupian*. Yijing, *Xi ci*: "for loftiness and height, nothing is greater than wealth and rank (еҜҢиІҙ)." Zhouli, *Tian guan, Da zai*: "by the eight statutes he counsels the king in governing the myriad people; the sixth is honouring the noble (е°ҠиІҙ)." Also Liji, *Ji yi*: "of old the Youyu clan prized virtue (иІҙеҫ·) and honoured age"; note: "иІҙ means feasting and bestowal beyond the other ministers." Also Mengzi: "for the lower to revere the higher is called honouring the noble (иІҙиІҙ)."
- *To desire (ж¬І).* Zhanguo ce: "he wished (иІҙ) to join with Qin to attack Qi"; note: "иІҙ is like to desire."
- *Of high price (еӨҡеғ№).* *Yupian*. Former Han, *Treatise on Food and Money*: "the wares being shoddy, the prices high (иіҲиІҙ)."
- *Also* *Shiming*: "иІҙ means to look up to (жӯё); what all things turn and look up to."
- *Surname.* *Fengsu tong*: "Gui Qian, Grand Administrator of Lujiang."
- *A prefecture-name.* *Yun Hui*: "in the Chunqiu the land of the Luoyue; the Sui Yuzhou; the Tang renamed it Guizhou (иІҙе·һ)."
