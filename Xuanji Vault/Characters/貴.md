> 《康熙字典》〈酉集中〉【貝字部】頁1206第15    〔古文〕：𡭙、𧸋【唐韻】居胃切【集韻】【韻會】歸謂切，𠀤音餽。【說文】作䝿，物不賤也。【易•繫辭】𤰞高以𨻰貴賤位矣。【書•旅獒】不貴異物，賤用物。　又【玉篇】高也，尊也。【易•繫辭】崇高莫大乎富貴。【周禮•天官•大宰】以八統詔王馭萬民，六曰尊貴。又【禮•祭儀】昔者有虞氏貴德而尚齒。【註】貴謂燕賜有加於諸臣也。又【孟子】用下敬上，謂之貴貴。　又欲也。【戰國策】貴合於秦以伐齊。【註】貴，猶欲也。　又【玉篇】多價也。【前漢•食貨志】器苦惡賈貴。　又【釋名】貴，歸也，物所歸仰也。　又姓。【風俗通】廬江太守貴遷。　又州名。【韻會】春秋駱越地，隋鬰州，唐攺貴州。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, middle; Shell radical 貝; p.1206 #15)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𡭙, 𧸋.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 居胃. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 歸謂 — sounding like 餽 (guì).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Written 䝿; a thing not cheap (物不賤)." Yijing, *Xi ci*: "the low and the high being set out, the noble and base (貴賤) have their places." Shu, *Lü ao*: "do not prize (貴) outlandish things, nor make light of useful things."

**Senses.**
- *High; honoured (高, 尊).* *Yupian*. Yijing, *Xi ci*: "for loftiness and height, nothing is greater than wealth and rank (富貴)." Zhouli, *Tian guan, Da zai*: "by the eight statutes he counsels the king in governing the myriad people; the sixth is honouring the noble (尊貴)." Also Liji, *Ji yi*: "of old the Youyu clan prized virtue (貴德) and honoured age"; note: "貴 means feasting and bestowal beyond the other ministers." Also Mengzi: "for the lower to revere the higher is called honouring the noble (貴貴)."
- *To desire (欲).* Zhanguo ce: "he wished (貴) to join with Qin to attack Qi"; note: "貴 is like to desire."
- *Of high price (多價).* *Yupian*. Former Han, *Treatise on Food and Money*: "the wares being shoddy, the prices high (賈貴)."
- *Also* *Shiming*: "貴 means to look up to (歸); what all things turn and look up to."
- *Surname.* *Fengsu tong*: "Gui Qian, Grand Administrator of Lujiang."
- *A prefecture-name.* *Yun Hui*: "in the Chunqiu the land of the Luoyue; the Sui Yuzhou; the Tang renamed it Guizhou (貴州)."
