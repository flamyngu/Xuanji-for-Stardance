> 《康熙字典》〈巳集中〉【火字部】頁683第16【唐韻】於甸切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】伊甸切，𠀤音宴。【說文】𤣥鳥也。籋口布翄枝尾，象形。【爾雅·釋鳥】燕燕，𩾐。【疏】燕燕，又名𩾐。古人重言之。【詩·邶風】燕燕于飛。【玉篇】俗作鷰。【集韻】亦作䴏。　又【禮·學記】燕朋逆其師，燕辟廢其學。【註】燕，猶褻也。　又與醼通。【廣韻】醼飮，古無酉，今通用。【詩·小雅】我有旨酒，嘉賔式燕以敖。【儀禮】燕禮第六。　又【集韻】與宴通。安也，息也。【易·中孚】初九虞吉，有他不燕。【疏】燕，安也。【詩·小雅】悉率左右，以燕天子。【傳】以安待天子。【又】或燕燕居息。【傳】燕燕，安息貌。【齊語】昔聖王之處士也，使就閒燕。【註】閒燕，猶淸淨也。　又【廣韻】烏前切【集韻】【韻會】因蓮切【正韻】因肩切，𠀤音煙。【玉篇】國名。【爾雅·釋地】燕曰幽州。【詩·大雅】溥彼韓城，燕師所完。【朱傳】燕召公之國。◎按鄭箋云：燕，安也。讀去聲。今从朱傳。　又【左傳·隱五年】衞人以燕師伐鄭。【註】南燕國今東郡燕縣。【疏】燕有二國，一稱北燕，故此註言南燕，以別之。世本燕國姞姓。地理志：東郡燕縣南燕國姞姓，黃帝之後也。　又姓。【廣韻】邵公奭封燕，爲秦所滅，子孫以國爲氏。漢有燕倉。〇按史記仲尼弟子傳：燕伋字思。是春秋時，卽有燕姓也。　又【集韻】於殄切，音蝘。本作宴。引爾雅宴宴，居息也。〇按爾雅疏引小雅北山，或燕燕居息。考爾雅釋文及北山釋文，俱無作上聲讀者，惟大雅韓奕：韓姞燕譽。釋文云：燕，于遍反。又於顯反。可証燕之有上聲也。    考證：〔【齊語】昔先君之處事也，使就閒燕。〕　謹照原文先君改聖王。處事改處士。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, middle; Fire radical 火; p.683 #16)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 於甸. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 伊甸 — sounding like 宴 (yàn).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the dark bird [the swallow]; tweezer-beak, spread wings, forked tail — a pictograph." Erya, *Shiniao*: "*yanyan* (燕燕) is the *yi* (𩾐)"; subcommentary: "*yanyan*, also named *yi*; the ancients doubled the word." Shijing, *Beifeng*: "the swallows (燕燕) in flight." (*Yupian*: "vulgarly 鷰"; *Ji Yun*: "also 䴏.")
- *Wanton, over-familiar (褻).* Liji, *Xueji*: "wanton (燕) friends defy one's teacher; wanton intimacies ruin one's study"; note: "燕 is like 'lewd.'"
- *Interchangeable with 醼 (a feast).* *Guangyun*: "醼飮 (banqueting); anciently without the 酉 element, now used interchangeably." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "I have good wine; my fine guests feast (燕) and make merry." Yili: "the Banquet Rite (燕禮), the sixth."
- *Interchangeable with 宴: at ease, at rest.* *Ji Yun.* Yijing, *Zhongfu*: "the first nine: foresight is lucky; if there is distraction, no ease (燕)"; subcommentary: "燕 means at ease." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "leading all his men left and right, to give the Son of Heaven ease (燕)"; tradition: "to wait on the Son of Heaven in peace." Also: "or at rest, taking their ease (燕燕居息)"; tradition: "燕燕 is the look of restful ease." The Qiyu: "anciently, in placing the gentlemen, the sage kings let them dwell at quiet ease (閒燕)"; note: "閒燕 is like 'calm and clean.'"

**Level tone (yān).** *Guangyun*: fanqie 烏前. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 因蓮. *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 因肩 — sounding like 煙 (yān). *Yupian*: "a state-name."
- *The state of Yan.* Erya, *Shidi*: "Yan (燕) is Youzhou." Shijing, *Daya*: "broad is that city of Han, walled by the host of Yan (燕)"; Zhu's tradition: "Yan, the state of the Duke of Shao." (A note: Zheng's gloss reads 燕 as 'ease,' departing tone; here we follow Zhu.)
- *Southern Yan.* Zuozhuan, Yin yr 5: "the men of Wey, with the host of Yan (燕), attacked Zheng"; note: "the state of Southern Yan, now Yan county in Dong commandery"; subcommentary: "there are two Yan states, one called Northern Yan, so this note says Southern Yan to distinguish it." The Shiben: "the state of Yan was of the Ji (姞) surname"; the Treatise on Geography: "Yan county in Dong commandery, Southern Yan, of the Ji surname, descendants of the Yellow Emperor."
- *A surname.* *Guangyun*: "Shi, Duke of Shao, was enfeoffed at Yan, which Qin destroyed; his descendants took the state as surname; the Han had Yan Cang." A note: the Shiji, *Disciples of Confucius*, has "Yan Ji, styled Si" — so the Yan surname existed already in the Spring-and-Autumn.
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 於殄, sounding like 蝘 (yǎn) — originally written 宴; it cites the Erya "宴宴, to dwell at rest." (A note: the rising tone of 燕 is confirmed by the Daya, *Hanyi*, "韓姞燕譽," whose *Shiwen* gives fanqie 于遍 and 於顯.)

**Textual note.** "昔先君之處事也" should read "昔聖王之處士也".
