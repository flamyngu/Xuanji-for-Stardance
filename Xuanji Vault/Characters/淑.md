## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈巳集上〉【水字部】頁629第22【唐韻】殊六切，音孰。【說文】淸湛也。　又【爾雅·釋詁】淑，善也。【詩·曹風】淑人君子。　又【集韻】昌六切，音俶。淑沑，水貌。　又與俶通。【儀禮·聘禮】俶獻無常數。【註】古文俶作淑。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.629 #22)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 殊六 — sounding like 孰 (shú).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "clear and limpid (淸湛)."
- *Good (善).* *Erya, Shigu.* Shijing, *Caofeng*: "the good (淑) man, the gentleman."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 昌六, sounding like 俶 (chù) — 淑沑, the look of water.
- *Interchangeable with 俶.* Yili, *Pinli*: "the first offerings (俶獻) are of no fixed number"; note: "the old text writes 俶 as 淑.
