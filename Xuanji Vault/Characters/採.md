## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集中〉【手字部】頁438第12【唐韻】倉宰切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】此宰切，𠀤同采。摘也，取也，㩜也。【戰國策】芻牧薪採，莫敢闚東門。【註】大者薪，小者採。【晉書·劉琨傳】古語云：山有猛獸，藜藿爲之不採。【唐書·地理志】開元二十一年，置十五採訪使檢察，如漢刺史之職。◎按採本从爪从木，爪即手也。後人又加手作採。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, middle; Hand radical 手; p.438 #12)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 倉宰. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 此宰 — the same as 采 (cǎi).

**Etymology and senses.** "To pluck (摘); to take (取); to gather (㩜)." Zhanguo ce: "for fodder and pasture, firewood and gathering (採), none dared peer at the eastern gate"; note: "the larger is firewood (薪), the smaller is gathered brush (採)." Jin shu, *Biography of Liu Kun*: "an old saying runs, 'where the hills have fierce beasts, the goosefoot and bean-leaf go ungathered (不採).'" Tang shu, *Treatise on Geography*: "in the twenty-first year of Kaiyuan, fifteen Investigating Commissioners (採訪使) were set up to inspect, like the Han office of regional inspector."

**On the graph.** A note: 採 originally followed 爪 (claw) and 木 (wood); 爪 is just the hand. Later men added 手 (hand) to write 採.
