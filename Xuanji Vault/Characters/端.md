> 《康熙字典》〈午集下〉【立字部】頁872第32    〔古文〕：耑【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤多官切，音偳。【說文】直也，正也。【禮·曲禮】振書端書于君前。【註】端，正也。【玉藻】目容端。【前漢·賈誼傳】選天下之端士，孝悌博聞，有道術者，以衞翼之。　又【篇海】萌也，始也，首也。【禮·禮運】人者，天地之心，五行之端也。【公羊傳·隱元年註】上係天端。【疏】天端，卽春也。春秋說云：以元之深，正天之端。以天之端，正王者之政也。【左傳·文元年】先王之正，時也，履端于始。【疏】履，步也，謂推步曆之初始，以爲術曆之端首。【孟子】惻隱之心，仁之端也。【註】端者，首也。人皆有仁義禮智之首，可引用之。　又【廣韻】緒也，等也。【揚子·方言】緒，南楚或曰端。　又【增韻】審也。【戰國策】郤疵對智伯曰：韓魏之君，視疵端而趨疾。　又專也。【戰國策】敢端其願。【註】端，猶專也。　又布帛曰端。【禮·記疏】束帛，十端也。丈八尺爲端。【小爾雅】倍丈謂之端，倍端謂之兩，倍兩謂之疋。　又【周禮·春官】其齊服有𤣥端，素端。【鄭司農註】衣有襦裳者爲端。【穀梁傳·僖三年】桓公委端搢笏，而朝諸侯。【註】端，𤣥端之服。【疏】其色𤣥，而制正幅無殺，故謂之𤣥端。　又地名。【山海經】號山，端水出焉，東流注于河。又國在流沙中者，墩端璽㬇。【史記·趙世家】與韓魏分晉，封晉君以端氏。【註】端氏，澤州縣也。【前漢·地理志】蒼梧郡有端溪。　又姓。孔子弟子端木賜。　又宮門名。【後漢·黃瓊傳】舉吏，先試之于公府，又覆之于端門。【註】端門，太微宮南門也。　又獸名。【後漢·鮮𤰞傳】禽獸異于中國者，野馬，原羊，角端牛，以角爲弓，俗謂之角端弓。　又【正韻】尺兗切。與喘同。【荀子·勸學篇】端而言。【註】端，讀爲喘。喘，微言也。　又【韻會小補】美辨切。同冕。大夫以上冠也。【禮·玉藻】諸侯𤣥端以祭，天子𤣥端以朝，日于東門之外。【註】端，皆音冕。　又【韻補】叶都元切。【陸機·文賦】罄澄心以凝思，眇衆慮而爲言。籠天地于形內，挫萬物于筆端。　又叶多汪切。【楊戲贊秦子敕】正方受遣，豫聞後綱。不𨻰不僉，造此異端。    考證：〔【禮·月令】諸侯玄端以祭，天子玄端以朝，日于東門之外。〕　謹照原文月令改玉藻。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, lower; Stand radical 立; p.872 #32)*

**Ancient graph.** 耑.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun* all: fanqie 多官, sounding like 偳 (duān).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Straight (直); upright (正)." Liji, *Qu li*: "to dust the documents and set them straight (端書) before the lord"; note: "端 means upright." *Yu zao*: "the eyes upright (端) in bearing." Former Han, *Biography of Jia Yi*: "select the upright gentlemen (端士) of the realm — filial, fraternal, broadly learned, possessed of the Way's arts — to guard and assist him."

**Senses.**
- *To sprout; beginning; head, source (萌, 始, 首).* *Pianhai* gives these. Liji, *Li yun*: "man is the heart of Heaven and Earth, the budding (端) of the Five Phases." Gongyang zhuan, Yin yr 1, note: "above, it ties to the Heaven-beginning (天端)"; subcommentary: "the Heaven-beginning is spring. The *Chunqiu shuo* says: by the depth of the Primal, the beginning of Heaven is set right; by the beginning of Heaven, the governance of the king is set right." Zuozhuan, Wen yr 1: "the regulation of the former kings was the seasons: they trod the beginning (履端) at the start"; subcommentary: "履 means to pace off — to reckon the very start of the calendar and make it the head of the calendrical art." Mengzi: "the heart of compassion is the sprout (端) of benevolence"; note: "端 means head — all men have the head of benevolence, righteousness, propriety, and wisdom, which can be drawn out and used."
- *A thread-end; a grade, class (緒, 等).* *Guangyun*. Yang Xiong, *Fangyan*: "緒 (a thread-end), in southern Chu sometimes called 端."
- *To scrutinize (審).* *Zengyun*. Zhanguo ce: "Xi Ci said to Zhi Bo: 'the lords of Han and Wei watched me closely (端) and hurried off in haste.'"
- *To be sole, exclusive (專).* Zhanguo ce: "I venture to fix solely on his wish (端其願)"; note: "端 is like 專, sole."
- *Of cloth and silk, a bolt (端).* Liji subcommentary: "a bundle of silk is ten bolts (十端); eighteen feet make a 端." *Xiao Erya*: "twice a 丈 is called a 端, twice a 端 a 兩, twice a 兩 a 疋."
- *A ceremonial robe.* Zhouli, *Chun guan*: "its fasting-robes include the dark 端 (𤣥端) and the plain 端 (素端)"; Zheng Sinong's note: "a robe with jacket and skirt is the 端." Guliang zhuan, Xi yr 3: "Duke Huan, in the dark 端-robe (委端) with tablet tucked in his sash, gave audience to the feudal lords"; note: "端 is the dark-端 robe"; subcommentary: "its colour is dark, and it is made of straight widths without tapering, hence called the dark 端."
- *Place-names.* Shanhai jing: "Mount Hao — the Duan river (端水) issues from it and flows east into the He." Also a state within the moving sands, 墩端璽㬇. Shiji, *Hereditary House of Zhao*: "when they partitioned Jin with Han and Wei, they enfeoffed the lord of Jin with Duanshi (端氏)"; note: "Duanshi is a county of Zezhou." Former Han, *Treatise on Geography*: "Cangwu commandery has the Duanxi (端溪)."
- *Surname.* Confucius's disciple Duanmu Si (端木賜).
- *A palace-gate name.* Later Han, *Biography of Huang Qiong*: "in promoting officials, they first tested them at the ducal courts, and again reviewed them at the Duan gate (端門)"; note: "the Duan gate is the south gate of the Taiwei palace."
- *A beast's name.* Later Han, *Biography of the Xianbei*: "among beasts unlike those of the Central States are the wild horse, the plains-sheep, and the horn-Duan ox (角端牛), whose horn is made into bows — commonly called the horn-Duan bow."
- *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 尺兗 — same as 喘 (chuǎn). Xunzi, *Quan xue*: "to speak faintly (端)"; note: "端 is read as 喘; 喘 means faint speech."
- *Yun Hui Xiaobu*: fanqie 美辨 — same as 冕 (miǎn), the cap of grandees and above. Liji, *Yu zao*: "the feudal lords in dark 端 sacrifice, the Son of Heaven in dark 端 holds court, greeting the sun outside the east gate"; note: "端 is here all sounded 冕 (miǎn)."

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes (per the Yunbu), fanqie 都元.* Lu Ji, *Wen fu*: "drain the clear mind to fix the thought, and survey the host of cares to make the words; cage Heaven and Earth within the form, and crush the myriad things at the brush-tip (筆端)."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 多汪.* Yang Xi, *Eulogy for Qin Zichi*: "Zhengfang received his charge and foreknew the later bonds; neither displaying nor concurring, he wrought this heterodoxy (異端)."

**Textual note.** Liji passage cited as "*Yue ling*" — "the feudal lords in dark 端 sacrifice…" — checked against the original, "*Yue ling*" should read "*Yu zao*."
