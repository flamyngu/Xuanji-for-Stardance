**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.646 #17)*

**Ancient graphs.** 㵄, 𣶔.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 呼旰. *Ji Yun*: fanqie 虛旰. *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 虛汗 — sounding like 熯 (hàn).

**Etymology and senses.** A river-name. Shu, *Yugong*: "from Mount Bozhong he led the Yang, which flowing east becomes the Han."
- *The River of Heaven (天河).* Erya, *Shitian*: "between the Winnowing-Basket and the Dipper is the ford of the Han (漢津)." Shiji, *Treatise on the Heavens*: "the Han is also the scattered *qi* of Metal." Yang Quan, *Wuli lun*: "the Han is the essence of water." Shijing, *Daya*: "bright is that Cloudy Han (雲漢)."
- *漢中, a commandery (set up by King Hui of Qin).* Guangyu ji: "now Hanzhong prefecture."
- *漢口, a place-name.* Guangyu ji: "Hanyang prefecture, originally the Spring-and-Autumn state of Yun, under Han part of Jiangxia, under Tang Mianzhou, also Hanyang."
- *A prefecture-name (under Chengdu).* *Yun Hui*: "[Hanzhou] under Han belonged to Guanghan commandery; the Tang set up the prefecture."
- *半漢, half-aloft (descriptive).* Zhang Heng, *Nandu fu*: "the heavenly horses half-aloft (半漢)."
- *A base fellow.* The Chuogeng lu: "people now call a base fellow a *hanzi* (漢子)."
- *White (Goryeo dialect).* In the Goryeo dialect 'white' is *han* (漢); see Sun Mu's Jilin leishi.
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 他干, sounding like 攤 (tān). "When the Grand Year is in 申, the year is *ruihan* (汭漢); also written *tuntan* (涒灘)."

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 詩建, sounding like 扇 (shàn).* Fan Qin's fu: "...coiling and towering aloft, it pricks the Cloudy Han (雲漢)."

**Textual note.** "六金之散氣" should read "亦金之散氣".
