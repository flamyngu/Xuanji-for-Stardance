> 《康熙字典》〈子集中〉【人字部】頁96第16【唐韻】【集韻】【正韻】𠀤博陌切，音百。【說文】長也。【釋名】父之兄曰伯父。伯，把也，把持家政也。　又兄曰伯。【詩·小雅】伯氏吹塤。　又第三等爵曰伯。又【周禮·春官】大宗伯之職，以九儀之命，正邦國之位，九命作伯。【註】上公有功德者，加命爲二伯，得征五侯九伯者。【疏】公羊傳，自陝以東，周公主之。陝以西，召公主之。是東西二伯也。言九伯者，九州有十八伯，各得九伯，故云九伯也。　又婦人目其夫曰伯。【詩·衞風】伯也執殳。　又馬祖，天駟，房星之神曰伯。【詩·小雅】旣伯旣禱。【註】以吉日祭馬祖而禱之。　又鳥名。【左傳·昭十七年】伯趙氏，司至者也。【註】伯趙，伯勞也。　又姓。益之後。春秋時有伯宗，伯州犂。　又同陌。【史記·酷吏傳】置伯格長。【註】言阡陌村落皆置長也。　又【正韻】必駕切。同霸。五伯，齊桓，晉文，秦繆，宋襄，楚莊也。伯叔伯長之義，後人恐與侯伯字溷，故借霸字別之。　又叶蒲各切，音博。【詩·大雅】王錫申伯。叶下蹻濯。　又叶壁益切，音必。【史記·敘傳】維弃作稷，德盛西伯。　又叶博故切，音布。【揚雄·解嘲】子胥死而吳亡，種蠡存而越伯。五羖入而秦喜，樂毅出而燕懼。    考證：〔【周禮·春官】大宗伯之職，九命作伯。【疏】公羊傳，自陝以東周公主之。陝以西召伯主之。是東西二伯也。言九伯，九州有十八伯，各得九伯，故云九伯也。〕　謹照原文召伯之伯改公。言九伯下增者字。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, middle; Person radical 人; p.96 #16)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Ji Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 博陌 — sounding like 百 (bó).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the eldest (長)." *Shiming*: "the father's elder brother is the 伯父; 伯 is 把 (to grasp), grasping the household's governance."
- *An elder brother.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "elder brother (伯氏) blows the ocarina."
- *The third rank of nobility (earl).* Zhouli, *Chunguan*: "the Grand Director of Rites: by the commands of the nine dignities he sets right the ranks of the states; at the ninth command [a noble] is made a chief (伯)"; note: "a high duke of merit and virtue is raised to be one of the Two Chiefs (二伯), empowered to punish the five [ranks of] marquis and the nine earls"; subcommentary: "the Gongyang zhuan: 'east of Shan, the Duke of Zhou governed; west of Shan, the Duke of Shao' — these are the East and West Chiefs. The 'nine earls': the Nine Provinces have eighteen earls, each [chief] having nine, hence 'nine earls.'"
- *A husband (as a wife names him).* Shijing, *Weifeng*: "my lord (伯) grasps his lance."
- *The Horse-Ancestor.* The Heavenly Team, the spirit of the 房 (Room) constellation, is called 伯. Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "having sacrificed to the Horse-Ancestor (伯) and prayed"; note: "on a lucky day they sacrifice to the Horse-Ancestor and pray to it."
- *A bird-name.* Zuozhuan, Zhao yr 17: "the Bo-Zhao officer (伯趙氏) is in charge of the solstices"; note: "伯趙 is the shrike (伯勞)."
- *A surname.* Descendants of [Bo] Yi; in the Spring-and-Autumn there were Bo Zong and Bo Zhouli.
- *Same as 陌.* Shiji, *Biographies of the Harsh Officials*: "they set up 伯-and-村 headmen (伯格長)"; note: "headmen set over the field-paths (阡陌) and villages."
- *Same as 霸 (hegemon).* *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 必駕. The Five Hegemons (五伯): Duke Huan of Qi, Duke Wen of Jin, Duke Mu of Qin, Duke Xiang of Song, King Zhuang of Chu. 伯 has the sense of 'elder' as in 伯叔 and 伯長; later men, fearing confusion with the 伯 of 侯伯, borrowed the graph 霸 to distinguish it.

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 蒲各, sounding like 博 (bó).* Shijing, *Daya*: "the king bestowed [a charge] on the Earl of Shen (申伯)" (rhyming below with 蹻濯).
- *Rhymes, fanqie 壁益, sounding like 必 (bì).* Shiji, *Author's Postface*: "Qi became Lord of Millet, virtue flourished in the Earl of the West (西伯)."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 博故, sounding like 布 (bù).* Yang Xiong, *Jiechao*: "...Wen Zhong and Fan Li survived, and Yue won mastery (伯)."

**Textual notes.** "召伯主之" should read "召公主之"; and "言九伯" should add 者 ("言九伯者").
