## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈巳集上〉【水字部】頁648第40【唐韻】古屑切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】吉屑切，𠀤音結。淸也。【廣韻】經典用絜。【儀禮·宗人】舉鼎鼏告絜。　又【新書·道術篇】厚志隱行謂之潔。【集韻】或作洯。亦省作𣳱。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 巳, upper; Water radical 水; p.648 #40)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 古屑. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 吉屑 — sounding like 結 (jié).

**Etymology and senses.** "Clean, pure (淸)." *Guangyun*: "in the classics 絜 is used for it." Yili, *Zongren*: "raising the tripods and their covers, he announces them clean (絜)."
- *also.* Jia Yi, *Xinshu, Daoshu*: "deep in purpose and hidden in conduct is called purity (潔)." *Ji Yun*: sometimes written 洯; also abbreviated 𣳱.
