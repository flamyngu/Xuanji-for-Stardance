## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈酉集下〉【辵字部】頁1263第12    〔古文〕：𨖸、𢕱【廣韻】雲阮切【集韻】【韻會】雨阮切，𠀤爰上聲。【說文】遼也。【廣韻】遙遠也。【正韻】指遠近定體也。　又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】于願切【正韻】于怨切，𠀤爰去聲。【正韻】遠之也。遠近之遠上聲，如《詩》其人則遠之類。遠離之遠去聲，如《論語》敬鬼神而遠之之類是也。　又叶于員切，音淵。【詩·小雅】爾之遠矣，民胥然矣。俗作逺。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 酉, lower; Walk radical 辵; p.1263 #12)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𨖸, 𢕱.

**Pronunciation (rising tone, *yuǎn*).** *Guangyun*: fanqie 雲阮. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 雨阮 — the rising tone of 爰.

**Senses.** *Shuowen*: "far (遼)." *Guangyun*: "remote and far (遙遠)." *Zheng Yun*: "indicating far and near — a stative sense."

**Departing tone (*yuàn*).** *Guangyun, Ji Yun, Yun Hui*: fanqie 于願; *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 于怨 — the departing tone of 爰. *Zheng Yun*: "to keep at a distance (遠之)." The 遠 of 'far/near' is the rising tone, as in the Shijing "其人則遠"; the 遠 of 'to keep away' is the departing tone, as in Lunyu "敬鬼神而遠之 (revere the spirits but keep them at a distance)."

**Rhyming variant.** *Rhymes as 淵 (yuān), fanqie 于員.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "you being far off (遠), the people are all so." Colloquially written 逺.
