## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1027第12    〔古文〕：𠷜【唐韻】【廣韻】古厚切【集韻】【類篇】【正韻】舉后切，𠀤音垢。【說文】草也。　又誠也。【魯語】夫苟中心圖民，知雖不及，必將至焉。　又且也。【晏子·雜上篇】行廉不爲苟得，道義不爲苟合。　又但也。【揚子·法言】非苟知之。　又【韓詩外傳】指緣謬辭謂之苟。　又磯名。【水經注】苟磯，亦曰南陽磯。　又姓。【廣韻】漢有苟參。【急就篇】苟貞夫。【註】苟，草名也。所居饒之，因以爲氏。　又【集韻】居侯切，音鉤。苟吻，草名。　又【類篇】果羽切。【唐韻古音】讀矩。【蔡邕·述行賦】登高斯賦，義有取兮。則善戒惡，豈云苟兮。翩翩獨征，無疇與兮。    （茍）【廣韻】紀力切【篇海】訖逆切，𠀤音殛。急也。通亟。與苟異。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1027 #12)*

The head graph here is 茍 (jì, "circumspect"), to be distinguished from 苟 (gǒu); the entry first treats the readings and senses of 苟.

**Ancient graph.** 𠷜.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Guangyun*: fanqie 古厚. *Ji Yun*, *Leipian*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 舉后 — sounding like 垢 (gǒu).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a plant."
- *Also, truly, sincerely (誠).* Lu yu: "if (苟) one sincerely at heart plans for the people, though his wisdom fall short, he will surely get there."
- *Also, carelessly, heedlessly (且).* Yanzi, *Zashang*: "upright in conduct, he does not seek gain heedlessly (苟得); in the way of righteousness, he does not conform carelessly (苟合)."
- *Also, only, merely (但).* Yangzi, *Fayan*: "not merely (苟) to know it."
- *Also* Han shi waizhuan: "to twist words to a false purpose is called 苟."
- *Also a rock-name.* Shuijing zhu: "Gou Rock (苟磯), also called Nanyang Rock."
- *Also a surname.* *Guangyun*: "the Han had Gou Can (苟參)." Jijiu pian: "Gou Zhenfu (苟貞夫)"; note: "苟 is a plant-name; where they dwelt abounded in it, hence the clan-name."
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: fanqie 居侯, sounding like 鉤 (gōu). 苟吻 (gouwen), a plant-name.
- *Also* *Leipian*: fanqie 果羽; the Tang yun guyin reads it 矩 (jǔ). Cai Yong, *Shuxing fu*: "climbing high I make this fu, that there be a meaning to take; commending good and warning against evil, how could it be heedless (苟)? Fluttering, I journey alone, with no companion."
- *The graph 茍 proper:* *Guangyun*: fanqie 紀力; Pianhai: fanqie 訖逆 — sounding like 殛 (jí): urgent, pressing (急); interchangeable with 亟; distinct from 苟.
