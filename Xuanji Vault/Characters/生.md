> 《康熙字典》〈午集上〉【生字部】頁754第26    〔古文〕：𤯓【唐韻】所庚切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】師庚切，𠀤音甥。【說文】進也。【玉篇】起也。【莊子·外物篇】凡道不欲壅，壅則哽，哽而不止則跈，跈則衆害生。【註】生，起也。　又【玉篇】產也。【博雅】人十月而生。【穀梁傳·莊二年】獨隂不生，獨陽不生，獨天不生，三合然後生。　又出也。【易·觀卦】上九觀其生，君子無咎。【註】生，猶動出也。　又養也。【周禮·天官·大宰】五曰生以馭其福。【註】生，猶養也。賢臣之老者，王有以養之。【左傳·哀元年】越十年生聚，而十年敎訓。　又【韻會】死之對也。【孟子】生，亦我所欲也。【前漢·文帝紀】世咸嘉生而惡死。　又造也。【公羊傳·桓八年】遂者何，生事也。【註】生，猶造也。專事之詞。　又性也。【書·君𨻰】惟民生厚，因物有遷。【傳】言人自然之性敦厚，因所見所習之物，有遷變之道。　又【左傳·僖二十七年】於是乎出定襄王，入務利民，民懷生矣。【疏】懷生者，謂有懷之心。　又【詩·衞風】旣生旣育，比予于毒。【箋】生，謂財業也。【前漢·高帝紀】不事家人，生產作業。　又【周禮·冬官考工記·矢人】凡相笴，欲生而摶。【註】相猶擇也，生謂無瑕蠹也。摶謂圜也。　又不熟也。【史記·項羽紀】與一生彘肩。　又語辭。【李白·戲杜甫詩】借問別來太瘦生。【歐陽修詩】問向靑州作麼生。　又平生，疇昔也。【阮藉詩】平生少年時，趙李相經過。【杜甫詩】平生爲幽興，未惜馬蹄遙。　又【正字通】凡事所從來曰生。宋高宗朝，孫楙入覲，嘗論公生明。上問何以生公。曰：廉生公。問何以生廉。曰：儉生廉。上稱善。　又所生，祖父也。【詩·小雅】夙興夜寐，毋忝爾所生。【疏】當早起夜臥，行之無辱汝所生之父祖也。　又友生，朋友也。【詩·小雅】矧伊人矣，不求友生。　又先生，師之稱。諸生，弟子之稱。【韓愈·進學解】國子先生晨入太學招諸生。【史記·酈生傳】高祖謂酈食其，以萬戸封生。【註】師古曰：生猶言先生。文穎曰：諸生也。　又先生，父兄也。【論語】有酒食，先生饌。　又【儀禮·士冠禮】遂以摯見於鄕大夫鄕先生。【註】鄕先生，鄕中老人爲卿大夫致仕者。【史記·五帝紀】薦紳先生難言之。　又【詩·商頌】以保我後生。【朱註】我後生，謂後嗣子孫也。　又門生。【裴皡詩】三主禮闈年八十，門生門下見門生。　又蒼生，民也。【晉書·謝安傳】安石不出，其如蒼生何。【張協雜詩】冲氣扇九垠，蒼生衍四垂。　又【前漢·郊祀志】故神降之嘉生。【註】師古曰：嘉生謂衆瑞。　又【楚語】滯則不震，生乃不殖。【註】生，人物也。　又水名。【山海經】北二百二十里曰盂山。生水出焉，而東流注于河。【註】卽奢延水也。水西出奢延縣西南赤沙阜，東北流。　又姓。【正字通】漢生臨，明生甫申。又微生，浩生，俱複姓。　又與牲同。【前漢·昭帝紀】令破烏桓。斬虜獲生有功。【註】獲生口也。　又與狌同。【汲冢周書】郭都生生。　又【廣韻】【正韻】所敬切【集韻】【韻會】所慶切，𠀤音貹。產也。【字彙】俗謂雞生卵。　又【集韻】【韻會】𠀤所景切，音眚。育也。【論語註】四乳生八子。【陸德明·音義】生，所幸反。又如字。　又【正韻】息正切，音性。【周禮·地官·司徒】以土會之灋，辨五土之物生。【註】杜子春讀爲性。　又【韻補】叶師莊切，音商。【傅毅·舞賦】在山峨峨，在水湯湯，與志遷化，容不虛生。　又叶桑經切，音星。【詩·小雅】雖有兄弟，不如友生。叶寧平。【東方朔·七諫】觀天火之炎煬兮，聽大壑之波聲。引八維以自道兮，含沆瀣以長生。　又叶尸連切，音羶。【黃庭經】內養三神可長生，魂欲上天魄入淵，還魂反魄道自然。    考證：〔【韓愈·進學解】諸生弟子事先生於兹有年矣。〕　謹按原文無諸生弟子句。今改國子先生晨入太學招諸生。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 午, upper; Life radical 生; p.754 #26)*

**Ancient graph.** 𤯓.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 所庚. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 師庚 — sounding like 甥 (shēng).

**Senses.** *Shuowen*: "to advance (進)." *Yupian*: "to rise (起)." Zhuangzi, *Wai wu*: "the Way does not wish to be blocked; blocked, it chokes; choking unceasing, then disorder; disorder, and the host of harms arise (生)"; note: "生, to arise."
- *To give birth, produce (產).* *Yupian.* *Boya*: "a person is born after ten months." Guliang zhuan, Zhuang yr 2: "yin alone gives no life, yang alone gives no life, Heaven alone gives no life — only when the three combine is there life."
- *To come forth (出).* Yijing, *Guan*: "the nine-at-top observes its coming-forth (生); the gentleman has no blame"; note: "生 is like moving forth."
- *To nourish (養).* Zhouli, *Da zai*: "the fifth is '生,' by which to manage their blessing"; note: "生 is like to nourish — the king has means to support the aged worthy ministers." Zuozhuan, Ai yr 1: "[Yue] in ten years bred and gathered (生聚), and in ten years taught and trained."
- *The opposite of death.* Mencius: "life (生) too is what I desire." Former Han, *Annals of Wendi*: "the age all prized life and hated death."
- *To create (造).* Gongyang zhuan, Huan yr 8: "what is 遂? the creating (生) of an affair"; note: "生 is like to create — a term for acting on one's own."
- *Nature, disposition (性).* Shu, *Jun chen*: "the people's nature (生) is generous, but with things it shifts"; tradition: "man's natural disposition is generous, yet according to what he sees and practises there is change."
- Zuozhuan, Xi yr 27: "…the people cherished life (懷生)"; subcommentary: "懷生 means having a cherishing heart."
- *Property, livelihood.* Shijing, *Wei feng*: "born and reared, [yet] you liken me to poison"; *Jian*: "生 means property." Former Han, *Annals of Gaozu*: "he did not tend the household's livelihood and work (生產)."
- *Free of flaw.* Zhouli, *Kaogong ji*, arrow-maker: "in choosing shafts, want them sound (生) and round"; note: "相, to choose; 生 means free of flaw and worm; 摶, round."
- *Uncooked (不熟).* Shiji, *Annals of Xiang Yu*: "[gave him] a raw (生) pork shoulder."
- *A particle.* Li Bai, teasing Du Fu: "may I ask — so very gaunt (太瘦生) since we parted?" Ouyang Xiu: "I ask, off to Qingzhou, how goes it (作麼生)?"
- *平生, one's whole life / former days.* Ruan Ji: "in my youth (平生少年時)…" Du Fu: "all my life (平生) given to secret delights…"
- *Whatever a thing springs from.* Zhengzitong: in Emperor Gaozong of Song's court, Sun Mao discussed "impartiality begets clarity (公生明)"; asked what begets impartiality, he said "incorruptness begets it (廉生公)"; asked what begets incorruptness, "frugality begets it (儉生廉)"; the Emperor praised him.
- *所生, father and grandfather.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "rise early, sleep late, disgrace not those who bore you (所生)."
- *友生, friends.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "how much more these men — does he not seek his friends (友生)?"
- *先生, a teacher; 諸生, disciples.* Han Yu, *On Advancing in Learning*: "the Master of the Directorate (國子先生) entered the Academy at dawn and called his students (諸生)." Shiji, *Biography of Master Li (Yiji)*; note: Shigu, "生 is like 先生."
- *先生, fathers and elder brothers.* Lunyu: "when there is wine and food, the elders (先生) eat." Yili, *Shi guan li*: "鄕先生"; note: "village elders, ministers and grandees retired from office." Shiji, *Annals of the Five Thearchs*: "the gentry and elders (先生) found it hard to speak of."
- *後生, later descendants.* Shijing, *Shang song*: "to guard our later-born (後生)"; Zhu note: "我後生, later descendants."
- *門生, a protégé.* Pei Hao: "…the protégé (門生) sees, under his own gate, a protégé."
- *蒼生, the people.* Jinshu, *Biography of Xie An*: "if Anshi does not come forth, what of the common folk (蒼生)?"
- *嘉生, auspicious crops/omens.* Former Han, *Treatise on Sacrifices*; note (Shigu): "嘉生 means the many auspicious [growths]."
- *People and creatures.* Chu yu: "stagnant, it does not stir; [then] living things (生) do not breed."
- *A river.* Shanhaijing: north 220 *li* is Mount Yu, whence the 生 waters issue, flowing east into the He; note: this is the Sheyan River.
- *Surname.* Zhengzitong: the Han Sheng Lin, the Ming Sheng Fushen. Also 微生, 浩生, compound surnames.
- *Same as 牲 (sacrificial beast).* Former Han, *Annals of Zhaodi*: "…taking captives alive (獲生)"; note: "capturing live prisoners."
- *Same as 狌 (civet).* *Jizhong Zhoushu*: "郭都生生."

**Further readings.**
- *Guangyun, Zheng Yun*: fanqie 所敬; *Ji Yun, Yun Hui*: fanqie 所慶, sounding like 貹 — "to bear young (產)." *Zihui*: "colloquially, the hen 生 (lays) eggs."
- *Ji Yun, Yun Hui*: fanqie 所景, sounding like 眚 (shěng) — "to rear (育)." Note to Lunyu: "in four sucklings she reared eight young"; Lu Deming: "生 fanqie 所幸, or the usual sound."
- *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 息正, sounding like 性 (xìng). Zhouli, *Situ*: "辨五土之物生"; note: Du Zichun read it as 性 (nature).

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 商 (shāng), fanqie 師莊.* Fu Yi, *Dance Rhapsody*: "…the form is not born (生) in vain."
- *Rhymes as 星 (xīng), fanqie 桑經.* Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "though I have brothers, they are not like a friend (生)" (rhyming with 寧, 平). Dongfang Shuo, *Seven Remonstrances*: "…breathing the night-dew for long life (生)."
- *Rhymes as 羶 (shān), fanqie 尸連.* *Huangting jing*: "nourish within the three spirits for long life (生)…"

**Textual note.** The Han Yu citation is corrected to read 國子先生晨入太學招諸生.
