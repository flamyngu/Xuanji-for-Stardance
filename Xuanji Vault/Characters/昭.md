> 《康熙字典》〈辰集上〉【日字部】頁493第22【唐韻】止遙切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】之遙切，𠀤音招。【說文】日明也。【爾雅·釋詁】昭，見也。【博雅】明也。【玉篇】光也。【廣韻】著也，覿也。【易·晉卦】君子以自昭明德。【書·堯典】百姓昭明。【詩·大雅】於昭于天。　又【禮·王制】天子七廟，三昭三穆與太祖之廟而七。又【祭統】夫祭有昭穆。昭穆者，所以別父子、遠近、長幼、親疏之序而無亂也。【魯語】明者爲昭，次者爲穆。　又【禮·樂記】蟄蟲昭蘇。【註】昭，曉也。蟄蟲以發生爲曉，更息曰蘇。　又姓。【戰國策】楚有昭奚恤。【屈原·離騷註】三閭之職，掌王族三姓，曰：昭、屈、景。　又【集韻】時饒切【正韻】時昭切，𠀤音韶。廟中佋穆，或作昭。【佩觽集】說文自有佋穆之字，以佋爲昭，蓋借音耳。李祭酒涪說爲晉諱昭，改音韶，失之也。　又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】𠀤止少切，音沼。【詩·魯頌】其馬蹻蹻，其音昭昭。【釋文】昭，之繞反。　又【集韻】【正韻】𠀤之笑切。照，或省作昭。　又【韻補】叶蚩於切。【前漢·敘傳】受命之初，贊功剖符。奕世弘業，爵土乃昭。　又叶之由切。【楚辭·九章】臨沅湘之𤣥淵兮，遂自忍而沈流。卒沒身而絕名兮，惜壅君之不昭。    考證：〔爵士乃昭。〕　謹照前漢敍傳原文，爵士改爵土。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, upper; Sun radical 日; p.493 #22)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 止遙. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 之遙 — sounding like 招 (zhāo).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the sun's brightness (日明)." Erya, *Shi gu*: "昭, to appear (見)." *Boya*: "bright (明)." *Yupian*: "light (光)." *Guangyun*: "manifest (著); to behold (覿)." Yijing, *Jin*: "the gentleman thereby makes bright (昭) his bright virtue." Shu, *Yao dian*: "the hundred clans were made bright (昭明)." Shijing, *Daya*: "oh, bright (昭) in Heaven!"
- *昭穆, the ancestral-temple order.* Liji, *Wang zhi*: "the Son of Heaven has seven temples — three 昭, three 穆, with the Grand Ancestor's, making seven." *Ji tong*: "in sacrifice there is 昭穆; 昭穆 is what distinguishes the order of father and son, far and near, elder and younger, close and distant, without confusion." Luyu: "the bright is 昭, the next is 穆."
- *To awaken (曉).* Liji, *Yue ji*: "hibernating insects awake and revive (昭蘇)"; note: "昭, to awaken — the hibernating insects take coming-to-life as their dawn; renewed breath is 蘇."
- *Surname.* Zhanguo Ce: Chu had Zhao Xixu. Qu Yuan, *Li sao* note: "the office of the Three Lü managed the three royal-clan surnames — 昭, 屈, 景."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 時饒; *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 時昭 — sounding like 韶 (sháo). In temple order 佋穆, sometimes written 昭. *Peixi ji*: "the Shuowen has its own graph 佋穆; using 佋 for 昭 is a phonetic loan; Libation-Steward Li of Fu's claim that it was [taboo] for the Jin Emperor Zhao's name and changed to the sound 韶 is mistaken."
- *Ji Yun, Yun Hui, Zheng Yun*: fanqie 止少, sounding like 沼 (zhǎo). Shijing, *Lu song*: "his horses sturdy, his music ringing clear (昭昭)"; *Shiwen*: "昭 fanqie 之繞."
- *Ji Yun, Zheng Yun*: fanqie 之笑 — 照 (to illumine) is sometimes abridged to 昭.

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 蚩於.* Former Han, *Author's Postface*: "…in generation upon generation expanding the work, rank and land then made manifest (昭)."
- *Rhymes, fanqie 之由.* Chuci, *Jiu zhang*: "…grieving that the blinded lord is not enlightened (昭)."

**Textual note.** 爵士 should read 爵土.
