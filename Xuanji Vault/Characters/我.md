## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集中〉【戈字部】頁412第01    〔古文〕：𠨂、𢦓、𢦠【唐韻】五可切【集韻】【韻會】語可切，𠀤俄上聲。【說文】施身自謂也。【廣韻】已稱也。　又稱父母國曰我，親之之詞。【春秋·隱八年】我人祊。　又姓。古賢人，著書名我子。　又【說文】或說我，頃頓也。○按頃頓，義與俄同。然字書從無作俄音者，存考。　又【韻補】叶與之切，音台。【揚子·太𤣥經】出我入我，吉凶之魁。【註】我音如台小子之台。　又叶阮古切，音五。【張衡·鮑德誄】業業學徒，童蒙求我。濟濟京河，實爲西魯。𢦐，篆文我。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, middle; Halberd radical 戈; p.412 #01)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𠨂, 𢦓, 𢦠.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 五可. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 語可 — the rising tone of 俄, i.e. **wǒ**.

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "applied to oneself — the way one names oneself." *Guangyun*: "a term of self-reference."
- *One's own [parents'] state.* To call one's own state 我 is a word of intimacy. Chunqiu, Yin yr 8: "我人祊 (our men at Beng)."
- *Surname.* An ancient worthy wrote a book titled *Wozi* (我子).
- *Shuowen, alternate gloss:* "我 means to topple, to tilt (頃頓)." — A note: 頃頓 has the sense of 俄, yet no dictionary reads 我 as *é*; recorded for the record.

**Rhyming variants.**
- *Rhymes as 台 (tái), fanqie 與之.* Yang Xiong, *Taixuanjing*: "out from me, into me (出我入我) — the chief of fortune and misfortune"; note: "我 read like the 台 of '台小子 (this little one).'"
- *Rhymes as 五 (wǔ), fanqie 阮古.* Zhang Heng, *Dirge for Bao De*: "diligent the band of students, the young and unlit seek me (我)… teeming by capital and river, truly the western Lu." 𢦐 is the seal-script form of 我.
