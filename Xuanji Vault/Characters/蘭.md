> 《康熙字典》〈申集上〉【艸字部】頁1070第11【唐韻】落干切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】郞干切，𠀤音闌。【說文】香草也。【陸甸云】闌艸爲蘭，闌不祥也。【陸璣詩疏】其莖似藥草澤蘭，廣而長節，漢諸池苑及許昌宮中皆種之。【易·繫辭】同心之言，其臭如蘭。【左傳·宣三年】鄭文公妾燕姞，夢天與蘭，曰：蘭有國香，人服媚之。【屈原·離騷】紉秋蘭以爲佩。【爾雅翼】一榦一花而香有餘者蘭。　又【本草】木蘭。【屈原·離騷】朝搴阰之木蘭兮。　又【管子·小匡篇】輕罪入蘭盾、鞈革二戟。【註】蘭卽所謂蘭錡，兵架也。　又脈也。【史記·扁鵲傳】夫以陽入隂支蘭藏者生。【註】支者順節，蘭者橫節。隂支蘭，膽藏也。　又布名。【華陽國志】蘭干細布。蘭干，獠言紵也。　又人名。【列子·說符篇】宋有蘭子。【張堪註】凡物不知生謂之蘭。【殷敬順曰】《史記》無符傳出入謂之闌。此蘭子謂以技妄遊，義與闌同。　又姓。【通志·氏族略】漢有太守蘭廣。　又萑蘭。【前漢·息夫躬傳】涕泣流兮萑蘭。【註】萑蘭，淚闌干也。　又芄蘭。【揚子·太𤣥經】陽氣親天，萬物芄蘭。【註】芄蘭，茂密也。　又通欄。【後漢·東夷傳】徙于馬蘭。【註】蘭卽欄。　又通斕。【吳志·孫權傳】童謠曰：黃金車，斑蘭耳。　又【韻補】陵延切，音連。【宋玉·招䰟】川谷徑複流潺湲，光風轉蕙氾崇蘭，經堂入奧塵朱筵。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 申, upper; Grass radical 艸; p.1070 #11)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 落干. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 郞干 — sounding like 闌 (lán).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "a fragrant herb." Lu Dian says: "lan-grass (闌艸) makes 蘭; 闌 [means] to ward off the inauspicious." Lu Ji's *Shi shu*: "its stem resembles the medicinal herb ze-lan (澤蘭), broad with long joints; the Han ponds and parks and the palace at Xuchang all planted it." Yijing, *Xici*: "words of one heart — their fragrance is like the orchid (蘭)." Zuozhuan, Xuan yr 3: "Duke Wen of Zheng's concubine Yan Jie dreamed that Heaven gave her an orchid (蘭), saying: 'the orchid has the fragrance of the realm; men wear it and are charmed by it.'" Qu Yuan, *Lisao*: "I string autumn orchids (秋蘭) to make my girdle-pendant." Erya yi: "one stalk, one flower, and fragrance to spare — that is the orchid (蘭)."
- *Also* Bencao: 木蘭 (magnolia). Qu Yuan, *Lisao*: "at dawn I pluck the magnolia (木蘭) on the slope."
- *Also* Guanzi, *Xiaokuang*: "for light offenses one submits a 蘭-shield, a leather buckler, and two halberds"; note: "蘭 is what is called the 蘭錡, a rack for weapons."
- *Also a body-channel.* Shiji, *Biography of Bian Que*: "when the yang enters the yin's branching and crossing reservoir (支蘭藏), the patient lives"; note: "支 is the lengthwise vessel, 蘭 the crosswise; the yin's 支蘭 is the gall-bladder reservoir."
- *Also a cloth-name.* Huayang guozhi: "langan (蘭干) fine cloth; 蘭干 is the Liao word for ramie (紵)."
- *Also a personal name.* Liezi, *Shuofu*: "in Song there was a Lanzi (蘭子)." Zhang Kan's note: "whatever thing whose origin is unknown is called 蘭." Yin Jingshun says: "in the Shiji, to come and go without a tally-pass (符傳) is called 闌; this 蘭子 means one who roamed at large by his tricks — the sense is the same as 闌."
- *Also a surname.* Tongzhi, *Shizu lüe*: "the Han had the prefect Lan Guang (蘭廣)."
- *Also 萑蘭.* Former Han, *Biography of Xifu Gong*: "tears flow streaming (萑蘭)"; note: "萑蘭 is tears coursing down (闌干)."
- *Also 芄蘭.* Yangzi, *Taixuan jing*: "the yang energy draws near to Heaven, and the myriad things grow lush (芄蘭)"; note: "芄蘭 means luxuriant and dense."
- *Also interchangeable with 欄 (railing).* Later Han, *Account of the Eastern Yi*: "they moved to Malan (馬蘭)"; note: "蘭 is 欄."
- *Also interchangeable with 斕 (variegated).* Wu zhi, *Biography of Sun Quan*: "a children's rhyme ran, 'a chariot of yellow gold, with dappled (斑蘭) ear-flaps.'"
- *Rhymes* (per the *Yunbu*) as fanqie 陵延, sounding like 連 (lián). Song Yu, *Zhao hun*: "through valleys winding back the waters murmur and flow; the bright breeze turns the basil and floods the towering orchids (崇蘭); past the hall and into the recess, dust lies on the vermilion mats."
