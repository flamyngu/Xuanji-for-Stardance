## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈卯集上〉【心字部】頁403第19【唐韻】【正韻】房吻切【集韻】【韻會】父吻切，𠀤墳上聲。【說文】懣也。从心賁聲。【周語】陽癉憤盈。【註】積也。鬱積而怒滿也。　又【集韻】或作𢤬。亦作賁。【禮·樂記】廣賁之音作，而民剛毅。【音義】依註讀爲憤，扶粉反。　又作馮。【莊子·盜跖篇】侅溺於馮氣，郭象讀。　又【韻會】【正韻】𠀤房問切，音與分同。滿也。【論語】不憤不啓。【又】發憤忘食。◎按註𠀤上聲，字書有上去二音。又叶符筠切，音焚。【蘇轍·祭范鎭文】力諫不從，遂致爲臣。開門接士，不怨不憤。【說文】憤本字。憤字从芔作。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 卯, upper; Heart radical 心; p.403 #19)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 房吻. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 父吻 — the rising tone of 墳 (fèn).

**Etymology (Shuowen).** "Vexed, choked with feeling (懣)." It follows 心 (heart) with 賁 as phonetic. *Discourses of Zhou*: "the yang-heat swells and brims (憤盈)"; note: "to accumulate; pent-up and filled with anger."

**Senses.**
- *Also* *Ji Yun*: sometimes written 𢤬; also written 賁. Liji, *Yue ji*: "the broad, swelling sound (廣賁之音) arises, and the people grow firm and resolute"; the *Yinyi*: "following the note, read as 憤, fanqie 扶粉."
- *Also* written 馮. Zhuangzi, *Dao zhi*: "choked and drowning in pent-up breath (馮氣)" — so read by Guo Xiang.
- *Also* *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 房問, the sound being the same as 分. "Full (滿)." Lunyu: "not till he is worked up (憤) do I open the way for him." Also: "in his ardour (發憤) he forgets to eat." A note: the gloss gives both readings in the rising tone, and the character-books give both rising and departing sounds.
- *Also* fanqie 符筠, sounding like 焚 (fén). Su Che, *Sacrificial text for Fan Zhen*: "his strong remonstrance unheeded, he came at last to be a subject; opening his door to receive scholars, he neither grudged nor chafed (不怨不憤)."

**On the graph.** *Shuowen*: this is the original graph 憤; the 憤 graph is formed with 芔 (huì).
