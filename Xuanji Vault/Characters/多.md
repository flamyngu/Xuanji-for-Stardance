> 《康熙字典》〈丑集下〉【夕字部】頁246第11    〔古文〕：𡖇、𡖈【廣韻】【正韻】得何切【集韻】【韻會】當何切，𠀤朵平聲。【爾雅·釋詁】衆也。【詩·小雅】謀夫孔多。【增韻】不少也。【易·謙卦象傳】君子以裒多益寡。【禮·表記】取數多者，仁也。　又勝也。【禮·檀弓】曾子曰：多矣乎予出祖者。【註】曾子聞子游喪事有進無退之言，以爲勝于已之所說出祖也。【史記·高帝紀】臣之業所就，孰與仲多。　又刻求也。【左傳·僖七年】後之人將求多于汝，汝必不免。　又稱美也。【前漢·袁盎傳】諸公聞之皆多盎。【後漢·馮異傳】諸將皆言願屬大樹將軍，帝以此多之。　又戰功曰多，見【周禮·夏官·司勲】。　又荒俗呼父爲阿多。【唐書·德宗紀】正元六年，回紇可汗謝其次相曰：惟仰食于阿多。　又姓。漢多軍，多卯，宋多岳。　又梵語吃栗多，華言賤人。底栗多，華言畜生。　又樹名。貝多樹，出摩伽陀國，長六七丈，冬不凋，見【酉陽雜俎】。又南印建那補羅國北有多羅樹，株三十餘里，其葉長廣，其色光潤，諸國書寫采用之。見【西域記】。　又叶都牢切，音刀。【蘇轍·巫山廟詩】歸來無恙無以報，山下麥熟可作醪。神君尊貴豈待我，再拜長跪神所多。又【詩·魯頌】享以騂犧，是饗是宜，降福旣多。【正字通】朱傳，犧，虛宜虛何二反，宜，牛奇牛多二反，多，章移當何二反。字彙專叶音趨，不知詩有二反也。○按朱子意，若从上虛宜切之犧，牛奇切之宜，則當何切之多宜叶章移切，音貲。若从下多字叶，則犧叶虛何切，音呵，宜叶牛多切，音哦。一在支韻止攝，一在歌韻果攝，《字彙》叶逡須切，音趨，錯入虞韻遇攝，《正字通》譏《字彙》不知二反，殊不知其錯入虞韻，幷不知一反也。【說文】多，重也，从重夕。夕者，相繹也，故爲多。重夕爲多，重日爲曡。    考證：〔【禮·坊記】取數多者，仁也。〕　謹照原書坊記改表記。〔【左傳·僖七年】後之人必求多于汝。〕　謹照原文必求改將求。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 丑, lower; Evening radical 夕; p.246 #11)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𡖇, 𡖈.

**Pronunciation.** *Guangyun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 得何. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 當何 — the level tone of 朵, i.e. **duō**.

**Etymology and senses.** *Erya, Shigu*: "many (衆)." Shijing, *Xiaoya*: "the planners are very many (多)." *Zengyun*: "not few." Yijing, *Qian* image: "the gentleman accordingly reduces the much (多) and adds to the little." Liji, *Biaoji*: "to take the larger (多) number is benevolence."
- *To surpass (勝).* Liji, *Tangong*: "Zengzi said, 'mine surpasses (多) — the road-sacrifice I gave'"; note: "Zengzi, hearing Ziyou's words on funeral matters, deemed his own better." Shiji, *Annals of Gaozu*: "the estate I have built — which is the greater (多), mine or Zhong's?"
- *To demand much of.* Zuozhuan, Xi yr 7: "men after you will demand much (多) of you, and you will surely not escape."
- *To praise, extol.* Former Han, *Biography of Yuan Ang*: "the nobles hearing it all praised (多) Ang." Later Han, *Biography of Feng Yi*: "the generals all wished to serve under the General of the Great Tree, and the Emperor for this esteemed (多) him."
- *War-merit.* See Zhouli, *Xiaguan, Sixun*.
- *A father (in the wild southern custom, 阿多).* Tangshu, *Annals of Emperor Dezong*: "in the sixth year of Zhengyuan, the Uyghur khan thanked his second minister, saying, 'I look only to A-duo (阿多) for my food.'"
- *A surname.* The Han had Duo Jun and Duo Mao; the Song, Duo Yue.
- *Sanskrit terms.* *jilieduo* (吃栗多) means "a base person," *dilieduo* (底栗多) "a beast."
- *Tree-names.* The *beiduo* tree (貝多, palmyra) grows in Magadha, six or seven *zhang* tall, unwithering in winter; see the Youyang zazu. North of the Konkanapura kingdom in South India is the *duoluo* tree (多羅, tala), groves over thirty *li*, with long broad glossy leaves used for writing; see the Xiyu ji.

**Rhyming variant.**
- *Rhymes, fanqie 都牢, sounding like 刀 (dāo).* Su Zhe, *Wushan shrine poem*: "...the god, noble and high, does it wait for me? — twice bowing, long kneeling, what the god esteems (多)." Shijing, *Lusong*: "...feasted and fitting, the blessing sent down already much (多)."

**On the graph.** *Shuowen*: "多 is 'doubled,' from a doubled 夕 (evening); 夕 is 'drawing on one after another,' hence 'much.' Doubled 夕 is 多, doubled 日 (sun) is 曡."

**Textual notes.** "禮·坊記" should read "禮·表記"; "必求多于汝" should read "將求多于汝".
