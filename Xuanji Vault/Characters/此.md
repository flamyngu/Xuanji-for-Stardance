## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集下〉【止字部】頁574第10【唐韻】【正韻】雌氏切【集韻】【韻會】淺氏切，𠀤音佌。【說文】止也。从止从匕。匕，相比次也。【徐曰】匕，近也。近在此也。【爾雅·釋詁疏】此者，彼之對。【詩·周頌】在彼無惡，在此無斁。【老子·道德經】去彼取此。　又【六書故】此猶兹也，斯也。【大學】此謂知本。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, lower; Stop radical 止; p.574 #10)*

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 雌氏. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 淺氏 — sounding like 佌 (cǐ).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to stop; to be here (止)." It follows 止 (stop) and 匕; 匕 means things ranged in order beside one another. Xu [Kai]: "匕 means 'near'; what is near is 'here' (此)." Erya, *Shigu* subcommentary: "此 is the opposite of 彼 (that, yonder)." Shijing, *Zhou song*: "yonder (彼) none hates him, here (此) none is weary of him." Laozi, *Daodejing*: "he discards that (彼) and takes this (此)."
- *also.* The Liushu gu: "此 is like 兹 and 斯 (this)." Daxue: "this (此) is called knowing the root."
