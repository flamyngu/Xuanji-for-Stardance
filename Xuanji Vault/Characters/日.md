**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls иҫ°, upper; Sun radical ж—Ҙ; p.489 #01)*

**Ancient graph.** рЎҶё.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie дәәиіӘ. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie е…ҘиіӘ вҖ” sounding like рҰЁҷ (rГ¬).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the full, the solid (еҜҰ); the essence of the Great Yang, never waning." *Boya*: "the image of the ruler (еҗӣиұЎ)." *Shiming*: "ж—Ҙ is еҜҰ (full): its light bright and abundantly full." Yijing, *Qian*: "with sun and moon it joins its brightness"; *Xici*: "of hanging images displaying brightness, none is greater than sun and moon"; *Shuogua*: "Li is fire, is the sun." Liji, *Ji yi*: "the sun rises in the east." Shiji, *Tianguan shu* note: "the sun is the ancestor of the yang essence." Huainanzi, *Tianwen xun*: "the essence of the fire-*qi* is the sun."
- *The civil day.* Shu, *Shun dian*: "he harmonised the seasons and months and rectified the days (жӯЈж—Ҙ)"; tradition: "harmonise the four seasons' terms, the months' length, the days' [stems], making them uniform." *Hong fan*: "of the Five Reckonings, the third is ж—Ҙ"; subcommentary: "from midnight to the next midnight, round the twelve [double-]hours, is one ж—Ҙ."
- *Hard and soft days.* Liji, *Qu li*: "outer affairs on the hard days (еүӣж—Ҙ), inner affairs on the soft (жҹ”ж—Ҙ)"; subcommentary: "the ten stem-days have five odd and five even вҖ” *jiЗҺ, bЗҗng, wГ№, gД“ng, rГ©n* (odd) are 'hard'; *yЗҗ, dД«ng, jЗҗ, xД«n, guЗҗ* (even) are 'soft.'" *Jiao te sheng*: "the suburban sacrifice welcomes the coming of the lengthening day (й•·ж—Ҙ)"; note: "at [the position] *mЗҺo*, day and night divide evenly, and thereafter the day lengthens."
- *In days past.* Zuozhuan, Wen yr 7: "of late (ж—Ҙ) Wei was not at peace"; note: "ж—Ҙ, in days past."
- *Calendar officers.* Zuozhuan, Huan yr 17: "the Son of Heaven has the Day-officer (ж—Ҙе®ҳ), the lords the Day-driver (ж—ҘеҫЎ)"; note: "both keepers of the calendar." Shiji, *Biographies of Diviners* note: "diviners who watch the seasons and days are commonly called ж—ҘиҖ…."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie иҖҢеҠӣ, sounding like зңІ; same sense.
- *Rhymes, fanqie иҖҢиҮі.* Li Song, *Shuzhi fu*: "вҖҰlodging sincere devotion in the white sun (ж—Ҙ)." *Leipian*: "Empress Wu made the graph рЎҶ ."
