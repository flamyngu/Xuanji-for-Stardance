## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈辰集上〉【日字部】頁489第01    〔古文〕：𡆸【唐韻】【正韻】人質切【集韻】【韻會】入質切，𠀤音𦨙。【說文】實也。太陽之精不虧。【博雅】君象也。【釋名】日，實也，光明盛實也。【易·乾卦】與日月合其明。又【繫辭】縣象著明，莫大乎日月。又【說卦】離爲火爲日。【周禮·天官·九𡣕註】日者天之明。【禮·祭義】日出於東。【史記·天官書註】日者，陽精之宗。【前漢·律歷志】日合於天統。【後漢·荀爽傳】在地爲火，在天爲日。【淮南子·天文訓】火氣之精者爲日。　又【書·舜典】協時月正日。【傳】合四時之氣節，月之大小，日之甲乙，使齊一也。又【洪範】五紀，三曰日。【傳】紀一日。【疏】從夜半以至明日夜半，周十二辰爲一日。又【禮·曲禮】外事以剛日，內事以柔日。【疏】十日有五奇五偶，甲丙戊庚壬五奇爲剛，乙丁己辛癸五偶爲柔也。又【郊特牲】郊之祭也，迎長日之至也。【註】迎長日者，建卯而晝夜分，分而日長也。　又【左傳·文七年】日衞不睦。【註】日，往日也。　又【左傳·桓十七年】天子有日官，諸侯有日御。【註】皆典歷數者。　又【史記·日者傳註】卜筮占𠋫時日，通名日者。　又【集韻】而力切，音眲。義同。　又【韻補】叶而至切。【李嵩·述志賦】審機動之至微，思遺餐而忘寐。表略韻於納素，託精誠於白日。【類篇】唐武后作𡆠。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 辰, upper; Sun radical 日; p.489 #01)*

**Ancient graph.** 𡆸.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 人質. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*: fanqie 入質 — sounding like 𦨙 (rì).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "the full, the solid (實); the essence of the Great Yang, never waning." *Boya*: "the image of the ruler (君象)." *Shiming*: "日 is 實 (full): its light bright and abundantly full." Yijing, *Qian*: "with sun and moon it joins its brightness"; *Xici*: "of hanging images displaying brightness, none is greater than sun and moon"; *Shuogua*: "Li is fire, is the sun." Liji, *Ji yi*: "the sun rises in the east." Shiji, *Tianguan shu* note: "the sun is the ancestor of the yang essence." Huainanzi, *Tianwen xun*: "the essence of the fire-*qi* is the sun."
- *The civil day.* Shu, *Shun dian*: "he harmonised the seasons and months and rectified the days (正日)"; tradition: "harmonise the four seasons' terms, the months' length, the days' [stems], making them uniform." *Hong fan*: "of the Five Reckonings, the third is 日"; subcommentary: "from midnight to the next midnight, round the twelve [double-]hours, is one 日."
- *Hard and soft days.* Liji, *Qu li*: "outer affairs on the hard days (剛日), inner affairs on the soft (柔日)"; subcommentary: "the ten stem-days have five odd and five even — *jiǎ, bǐng, wù, gēng, rén* (odd) are 'hard'; *yǐ, dīng, jǐ, xīn, guǐ* (even) are 'soft.'" *Jiao te sheng*: "the suburban sacrifice welcomes the coming of the lengthening day (長日)"; note: "at [the position] *mǎo*, day and night divide evenly, and thereafter the day lengthens."
- *In days past.* Zuozhuan, Wen yr 7: "of late (日) Wei was not at peace"; note: "日, in days past."
- *Calendar officers.* Zuozhuan, Huan yr 17: "the Son of Heaven has the Day-officer (日官), the lords the Day-driver (日御)"; note: "both keepers of the calendar." Shiji, *Biographies of Diviners* note: "diviners who watch the seasons and days are commonly called 日者."
- *Ji Yun*: fanqie 而力, sounding like 眲; same sense.
- *Rhymes, fanqie 而至.* Li Song, *Shuzhi fu*: "…lodging sincere devotion in the white sun (日)." *Leipian*: "Empress Wu made the graph 𡆠."
