## Kangxi Dictionary (康熙字典)
**Original entry (verbatim):**

> 《康熙字典》〈子集下〉【刀字部】頁141第11    〔古文〕：𠜛、𠇝、𠇙【唐韻】古郞切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】居郞切，𠀤音岡。【說文】彊斷也。从刀，岡聲。【增韻】堅也，勁也。【易·乾卦】大哉乾乎，剛健中正。【司馬光·潛虛】剛，天之道也。又作㓻。【史記·樂書】而民㓻毅。

**Full English translation:**

*(Kangxi: rolls 子, lower; Knife radical 刀; p.141 #11)*

**Ancient graphs.** 𠜛, 𠇝, 𠇙.

**Pronunciation.** *Tang Yun*: fanqie 古郞. *Ji Yun*, *Yun Hui*, *Zheng Yun*: fanqie 居郞 — sounding like 岡 (gāng).

**Etymology and senses.** *Shuowen*: "to cut through by force; firm-cutting." It follows 刀 (knife) with 岡 as phonetic. *Zengyun*: "hard (堅); strong (勁)." Yijing, *Qian* hexagram: "great indeed is Qian! — firm (剛), vigorous, central, and correct." Sima Guang, *Qianxu*: "the firm (剛) is the Way of Heaven."
- *also written 㓻.* Shiji, *Treatise on Music*: "and the people grow firm and resolute (㓻毅).
